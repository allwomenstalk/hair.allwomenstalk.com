# The 7 Best Color Combos for Ombre Hair ...

Ombre hair is still huge, even though it's been popular for quite some time already. That's why you should jump on board and try the trend, even though you'll be late to the game. After all, sexy looks never go out of style. Of course, there are plenty of different ombre looks you can try, so you need to do your research. To help you out, here are a few of the best color combos for ombre hair:

## 1. Black to Grey

We have this adorable ombre look thanks to the granny trend. If you're too timid to dye your whole head grey, because you're worried it'll actually make you look like a little old lady, then you can dye half of your hair grey. Meanwhile, you can keep the other half of your hair black. The colors transition into each other smoothly, which is why this look is so hot.

## 2. Brown to Blonde

When you picture ombre hair, this is probably the color combination that first pops into your mind. Plenty of girls, like Zoella the beauty guru, have rocked this look over the past few years. If you just can't choose between dying your hair brown or blonde, then this is the perfect solution.

## 3. Black to Purple

Do you want to look a little rebellious without going overboard? Then you should think about dying three quarters of your hair purple. It won't look all that shocking, since the top will still be black. However, it'll give you that rocker look that you've always wanted to try. You'll feel like a real star with this color combo.

## 4. Dark Blue to Light Blue

Maybe you aren't afraid of dying your hair a bright color, but you don't want to look like every other girl you pass on the street. Here's your solution: get ombre hair in two shades of your favorite unnatural color. Even though you'll see some other women with blue hair, you won't see anyone else with this exact look nearby.

## 5. Red to Orange

If you're artsy, then this is an amazing color combination for you to try. It'll make your head look like a sunset. If you have a fiery personality, then this hair will match it pretty damn well.

## 6. Red to Black

How sexy does this look? The dark red works perfectly well with the accompanying shade of black. Another great thing about this look is that the black doesn't climb too high up the woman's hair. It strikes a balance between ombre hair and dip dyed hair. If you want to be unique, then you should try to do something similar to it.

## 7. Blonde to Brown

If you like the idea of having both brown and blonde hair at the same time, but don't want to look like a clone, then you can get reverse ombre hair! The only difference is that the blonde will be at the top and the brown will be at the bottom. It's a tiny change, but it'll make you stand out from the crowd.
