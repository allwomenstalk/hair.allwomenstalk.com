# These Athletes Show Us How to Wear Our Hair for Sports ...

Sometimes that cute side braid from Pinterest just doesn't get the job done for your soccer, tennis, or running workout - and when it's time to get serious about a hairstyle for when you're working on your fitness, it's all about keeping your hair back and off your face and neck, without looking like a wreck. Let's get some inspo from some butt-kicking female athletes, shall we?

## 1. Venus Williams

![Venus Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/ki/eacytmg555a3d7dee4f65789636076_600x849.jpg)

Sleek low-bun and visor: the perfect combination for any athlete who's got to keep the sun out of her eyes, but who won't be doing any distance running.

## 2. Megan Rapinoe

![Megan Rapinoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dz/qw/lfpqi6jw55a3d7e201566023122989_600x847.jpg)

Short and sweet, with maybe a small headband to keep it in place, this style is ideal for soccer players (obviously). Her style is pixie-perfect!

## 3. Alex Morgan

![Alex Morgan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/cc/pjusvicz55a3d7e0be15e410937119_600x900.jpg)

Alex occasionally braids her bangs back into a ponytail, but she usually wears the ponytail-with-elastic-headband to keep her hair out of her eyes and her focus on the ball.

## 4. Jessica Ennis-Hill

![Jessica Ennis-Hill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/it/s92cjkxx55a3d7dfe84fa155254138_400x600.jpg)

A sleek low ponytail is perfect for a runner, with or without a headband to keep wispy fringe pieces out of your eyes.

## 5. Nicola Adams

![Nicola Adams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mn/c2/a231uzwt55a3d7e013ebb625511484_600x450.jpg)

This boxer keeps her hair out of play by braiding it back. Smart move!

## 6. Mo'ne Davis

![Mo'ne Davis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ck/ff/smsx6jvm55a3d7e35642c972517862_600x450.jpg)

If your sport comes with headwear (like, say, a baseball cap for baseball), make sure your style can be worn with it, comfortably.

## 7. Laura Trott

![Laura Trott](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/rr/rr07orxh55a3d7e148468238959578_309x465.jpg)

Laura keeps her hair away from her face with two braids. It might not make sense for a runner or soccer player, but for a cyclist, it's perfect!
