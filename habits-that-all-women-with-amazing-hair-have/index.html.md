# 10 Wonderful  Habits of Women with Ahh\-mazing Hair ...

We all know that one girl whose hair is absolutely amazing. As in, so fabulous that you wonder why she hasn’t been discovered by a modeling agency yet. While it’s true that some of having great hair comes from genetics and plain old luck, there’s a lot you can do to determine if your hair is awesome or not. These’re 7 habits you’ll find in women with amazing hair.

## 1. They Have a Great Relationship with Their Hairdresser

![hair, hairstyle, blond, long hair, brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yb/iz/mdqiss5e58ff8db5ebdb4350625803_600x714.jpg)

Girls with great hair don’t just walk in at any salon. They’re very selective in who they trust their hair to. They find a hairdresser they love and develop a good relationship with her. They don’t mind to pay for great service at the salon as well as including a tip. Because of this, their hairdresser is always willing to do their best work and go the extra mile.

## 2. They Get Regular Trims

![white, clothing, woman, dress, wedding dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hm/q4/ad0zplrn58ff8d5603205459648726_600x749.jpg)

Another habit women with great hair have is that they get regular trims. Yes, it’s a pain to go in every 6-8 weeks but the payoff is huge. They don’t have split ends that go halfway up the shaft of their hair. Their hair always looks fresh and healthy which is a big component of what it takes to look amazing. We all know that having a trim won’t actually make your hair grow faster but it helps your hair stay in great condition so any new growth you have looks fabulous.

## 3. They Don’t Skimp on Hair Products

![photo shoot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o4/qv/kmronzzw58ff8d89c4d14655455208_600x750.jpg)

This doesn’t mean that they always pay outrageous prices for hair products. They may have found a great drugstore brand that works for them. But they’re willing to make sure they have great products, whether that means driving to a specific store to get them or paying a little more. They see their hair as an investment. They do what it takes to keep it gorgeous.

## 4. They Don’t Wash Their Hair Daily

![hair, clothing, blue, beauty, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pl/uc/zonnqkl558ff8d1af0b8e736155977_600x749.jpg)

Girls with amazing hair rarely wash their hair daily. They’re much more likely to wash it every other day or even every third day. They know that washing your hair too much can dry it out. Hair needs some natural oil to stay in good condition. If you’re new at this, dry shampoo can be a lifesaver at getting you through between washes.

## 5. They Treat Their Hair Gently

![hair, face, hairstyle, long hair, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ww/q8/ep1m1frb58ff8d46423e4041980374_600x600.jpg)

You won’t see a girl with amazing hair treating it roughly. They usually comb and style it very carefully. They’re also good about deep conditioning their hair regularly. They don’t use higher heat than is necessary when they style their hair. They always remove hair accessories carefully, too.

## 6. They Experiment with Products

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, long hair, fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fr/x2/n0y0zky058ff8e02a074c155356919_600x721.jpg)

Someone with amazing hair has her favorites but also isn’t afraid to try something new. They know they may be on the verge of discovering the next life-changing hair product. If it doesn’t work they’ll just pass it along to someone else. They’re interested in new hair products and love experimenting with them. After all, this’s how you discover stuff you love!

## 7. They’re Always up for Learning New Hair Techniques and Tricks

![hair, face, clothing, hairstyle, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/ng/gtxorce058ff8e3d494ab606443920_600x749.jpg)

I don’t know about you but new hair styling techniques and tricks intimidate me. But for a girl with amazing hair, this’s just a matter of fun! This kind of girl loves to learn new ways of styling her hair. They’ve got the patience and interest for it. You can be the same way with an effort.

## 8. They Go Easy on Heat

![hair, photography, hairstyle, beauty, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k2/cz/ww49en7o58ff8d0333b94561643196_600x750.jpg)

Of course women with amazing hair use heat styling tools, but it's how often they use them that keeps their hair gorgeous. By avoiding overly heating your hair, you're keeping it from drying out. If you're going to use heat tools, be sure to always use a heat protectant to save your hair from damage.

## 9. They Sleep on Silk

![clothing, lingerie, undergarment, beauty, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y2/dc/pqaa2lgk58ff8e5568d9a898706015_600x749.jpg)

As if women with gorgeous hair didn't already seem glamorous, now you can keep in mind they probably sleep on a Sill pillow case. This protects the cuticles from getting too roughed up in your sleep and allows you to wake with smoother hair; this equates to fewer frizzies in the morning.

## 10. She Eats Right

![meal, dish, lunch, sense, food,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/xw/zk8ox8jo58ff8e99a4de1015700908_600x749.jpg)

Whether it's a daily vitamin or a diet high in iron, zinc, protein, and Omega-3s, a girl with gorgeous hair will always make sure she's eating healthy to promote healthy hair growth. Experts recommend alternating between chicken, red meat, and fish throughout the week to optimize beautiful hair benefits from each.

These'er 10 habits of women that have amazing hair. What’s your best hair tip? What’s hair product works miracles for you?
