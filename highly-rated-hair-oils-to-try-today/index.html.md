# 7 Highly Rated Hair Oils to Try Today ...

Have you discovered the wonder of hair oils for your hair? No matter what type of hair you have, you too can reap the incredible benefits of using hair oil to add moisture, tame frizzies and give your scalp a relaxing and beneficial treatment. Hair oils don’t have to be super greasy or oily. I want to show you seven great hair oils that can deliver unbelievable results to your hair with just a few uses!

## 1. Josie Maran Argan Oil

![Josie Maran Argan Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/02/1_josie-maran-argan-oil_250x250.jpg)

One of the most popular hair oils and multipurpose oils around is Argan oil! This can be used on your skin, hair and nails and is 100% natural. Argan oil is said to be best for medium to thick hair, but this formula is pretty light and you can use smaller amounts to benefit finer hair, also. Use this oil to treat split ends, moisturize dry areas and bring back some shine to your tresses!

## 2. Weleda Rosemary Hair Oil

![Weleda Rosemary Hair Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/02/2_weleda-rosemary-hair-oil_410x410.jpg)

If you’re looking for oils for hair that’s damaged, Weleda’s rosemary oil might be just the thing to help rejuvenate stressed tresses. Use this hair oil weekly to repair and revitalize a dry scalp or damaged hair from using heat tools. This hair oil will nourish both your hair and scalp and help restore shine and health to your hair!

## 3. Desert Essence Organic Jojoba Oil

![Desert Essence Organic Jojoba Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/02/3_desert-essence-organic-jojoba-oil_300x300.jpg)

Here’s another multipurpose lubricant that can be used as both a skin and hair oil. Jojoba oil is said to be the goof-proof hair oil for all hair types and is very lightweight. Use this to massage into your hair and scalp, use it as a leave-in hair treatment or use it to cleanse or moisturize skin!

## 4. Ojon Rare Blend Oil Total Hair Therapy

![Ojon Rare Blend Oil Total Hair Therapy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/02/4_ojon-rare-blend-oil-total-hair-therapy_250x250.jpg)

Ojon’s Rare Blend Oil combines three types of exotic oils to make one amazing hair oil to heal dry and damaged hair as well as restore shine, gloss and tame frizzy hair. Ladies love this lightweight yet highly moisturizing hair oil that is easy to use and absorbs quickly into hair.

## 5. Macadamia Healing Oil Treatment

![Macadamia Healing Oil Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/02/5_macadamia-healing-oil-treatment_410x410.jpg)

Macadamia makes some fabulous oils for dry hair like their Deep Repair Masque, but their Healing Oil Treatment comes in at a close second! Use this emollient oil to nourish and repair dry, tangled and frizzy hair. It’s also said to extend the life of color-treated hair and reduce drying time by 40-50%! Don’t worry, even if you don’t have dry hair, all hair types can benefit from this hair oil!

## 6. Biosilk Silk Therapy Oil

![Biosilk Silk Therapy Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/02/6_biosilk-silk-therapy-oil_410x410.jpg)

Biosilk is another popular hair product company that makes a pretty extraordinary hair oil. Their silk therapy oil has helped my hair come back to life after using tons of heat tools and hair products! This hair oil helps prevents split ends, conditions hair, provides shine and replenishes hair with vitamins and botanical extracts!

## 7. Hollywood Beauty Castor Oil Hair Treatment

![Hollywood Beauty Castor Oil Hair Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/02/7_hollywood-beauty-castor-oil-hair-treatment_224x209.jpg)

When you think of Castor Oil you might not automatically think of a hair treatment, but it makes an awesome scalp and hair oil! Use this hair oil to prevent breakage, condition natural, relaxed or color-treated hair and restore shine and moisture to all types of hair. A bonus use for this product is to use it condition baby’s hair and skin!

Using a hair oil has so many benefits that you won’t want to pass up! If you’re worried about it leaving you with heavy hair that has an oily look, try using just a dime-sized drop on the ends of hair or the scalp and experiment with different types of oils until you find the type that’s most compatible with your hair type!
