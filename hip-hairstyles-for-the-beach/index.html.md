# 7 Hip Hairstyles for the Beach ...

When the temperature rises, there are hip hairstyles for the beach you can try wearing. Instead of putting your hair into a simple bun or ponytail like everyone else around you, you can test out something new. You don't have to be confined to a certain set of styles, due to your location. There are plenty of great hairstyles for the beach that you could also wear on a night out to dinner.

## 1. Braided Pony

![Braided Pony](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/me/eg/g9ahz2de_596x880.jpg)

If you can't decide whether you want to wear braids or a simple ponytail, then mix them together. Start out by braiding one section of your hair, and then stop, allowing the rest of your hair to take the shape of a normal ponytail. Just make sure you put your elastic band beneath the braided section, so that it doesn't come loose. This is one of the easiest hairstyles for the beach that won't take you too long to create.

## 2. Sides Back

![Sides Back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/li/ts/n9ffrxmt_600x901.jpg)

Push the sides of your hair back so that they're out of your face. If you have any cute clips to use, now's the time! You can leave the rest of your hair down, so that everyone else on the beach can admire your gorgeous locks.

## 3. Beachy Waves

![Beachy Waves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/fo/ie9mgm6z_600x1065.jpg)

This gorgeous style will make you look like you belong at the beach. You can clip your hair in whatever way you'd like. The most important piece to this look is the cute waves. If you don't want to worry about styling your hair early in the morning, do it before you go to bed. That way, you can touch it up quick when you wake up, and then run straight to the sand.

## 4. Braided Bun

![Braided Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b0/uu/yd9bkhfs_580x871.jpg)

If you have a bit of time to make yourself look fancy, try this style. First, section off your hair so that you're able to make two separate, small braids. Once you're finished, you can put your hair up in a bun the way that you normally would.

## 5. Multiple Braids Back

![Multiple Braids Back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qd/qv/v3puy1zt_600x753.jpg)

If you're a fan of braids, make as many as you can, and clip them all together at the back of your head. It looks difficult to do, but it's easy to get the hang of. The time you invest in it will be worth it, because you'll be getting compliments thrown at you all day.

## 6. Side Ponytail

![Side Ponytail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/dx/j0wwrepr_500x751.jpg)

You've probably worn your hair like this before, so it won't take you long to create the look. All you have to do is push your hair to the side and secure it in place. It's almost effortless to do, but will make you look gorgeous.

## 7. Add Accessories

![Add Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/zp/mnds7kj9_600x741.jpg)

Bringing a hat or bandana to the beach can help protect you from sunburn, and will keep you looking fabulous. It doesn't matter how you wear your hair when you add the perfect accessory. Everyone will be too busy looking at your cute bandana to notice what your hair looks like once it gets wet.

The next time you spend a day at the beach, try wearing your hair in one of these [sexy styles](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-sexy-styles-to-try-this-weekend/). Just don't take too long making sure it's perfect, because the water and wind could ruin all of your hard work. How do you usually wear your hair when you go to the beach?
