# 7 Products That Will Keep Your Rainbow Hair Healthy ...

Rainbow hair is gorgeous, but all the chemicals it takes to get that colorful look can cause stress to your tresses. But no worries! You can be a pretty rainbow princess as long as you use products designed to protect your hair. Here are a few, for starters.

## 1. Shu Uemara Color Lustre Brilliant Glaze Shampoo + Conditioner

![Shu Uemara Color Lustre Brilliant Glaze Shampoo + Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m8/gy/eu1x763r55fc22c9ce1e9813087227_600x444.jpg)

Made with rose musk oil, this lush shampoo and conditioner leave even the most processed rainbow hair soft and manageable, not brittle and course.

## 2. Bed Head Colour Goddess Shampoo + Conditioner

![Bed Head Colour Goddess Shampoo + Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/zc/hvs9ixoz55fc22c9ac559016199594_600x559.jpg)

Maybe your budget won't allow the weekly rainbow hair touch-ups AND the spendy Shu Uemara shampoo & conditioner. That's okay! This sugar-vanilla scented set can do the trick, too, and it's not as pricey.

## 3. Garnier Fructis Instant Melting Conditioner

![Garnier Fructis Instant Melting Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h2/hi/usxla3ku55fc22c91237a361915753_410x472.jpg)

I can't stress enough how important it is to keep your rainbow hair hydrated with a deep condition you know you can trust... like this one!

## 4. Kerastase Masque CHROMA RICHE

![Kerastase Masque CHROMA RICHE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/en/za954h6z55fc269c07634368879056_600x346.jpg)

If there's a twice-weekly masque for color-treated hair that's better at keeping damage at bay, I haven't tried it. It smells so good, too!

## 5. Josie Maran Argan Oil Hair Serum

![Josie Maran Argan Oil Hair Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/md/mu/mf2npyqb55fc269d1935d737453569_600x588.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is my all-time favorite hair product, and it's ideal for [rainbow hair](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/are-you-really-ready-for-rainbow-hair/) or any other hair that's dry or under stress. Rub a few drops into you palms, then smooth your hair, paying special attention to your ends. It's also great for taming those little hairs along your part that want to stick up!

## 6. Living Proof Restore Instant Protection Spray

![Living Proof Restore Instant Protection Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a3/rk/igfbjc6155fc269d4a767853569698_600x592.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you love your rainbow hair and your flat iron, you'll want to consider using this light-weight but ultra-protective-against-heat-damage spray.

## 7. Verb Ghost Oil

![Verb Ghost Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ng/dz/fj6zp4xj55fc269da4e8e242770671_600x582.jpg)

[sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
While some styling products can mess with your brilliant rainbow hair, this one won't. It also helps prevent frizz, which is nice for those days when you let your hair air-dry and don't use your flat iron.
