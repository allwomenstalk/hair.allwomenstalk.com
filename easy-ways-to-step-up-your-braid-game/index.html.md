# 5 Easy Ways to Step up Your Braid Game ...

Braids are my go-to style whenever I need something quick and easy to do with my hair. It's a style that looks good with anything you decide to wear! Even though you can never go wrong with a classic braid, here are some ways that you can step things up:

## 1. Flower Power

![Flower Power](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/tk/l5ph0c3r_500x750.jpg)

Just because summer's over, that doesn't mean you can't wear the floral trend anymore. Adding floral pieces to your braid is a subtle and feminine touch that'll have everyone talking!

## 2. Chalk It up

![Chalk It up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/l2/uli91z1x_450x639.jpg)

Hair chalk is a fun and easy way to experiment with your style without committing to anything. You can buy an ombre hair chalk set from [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)!

## 3. Ribbons

![Ribbons](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vo/s7/p1p3tjph_429x640.jpg)

For a boho feel, add some ribbons into your braid! You can use it as the third strand in your braid or just wrap it around when you're finished!

## 4. Braids on Braids

![Braids on Braids](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jn/w2/tfnrqscd_600x900.jpg)

Add some texture to your look by incorporating braids into other braids! It's eye-catching and an easy look to accomplish!

## 5. Faux Hawk

![Faux Hawk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/n8/si2ge4sh_500x750.jpg)

You can achieve the faux hawk look by braiding the side of your hair! It's a sleek and edgy look that's perfect for back to school.

How do you like to add some pizazz to your braid?
