Choosing the best hair brushes based on your hair or the style you want to achieve is an investment every girl needs to make if she wants to reduce hair damage and have a perfect hairstyle. It’s also a huge save as it will help you get salon-quality results at home for only the fraction of the price, whenever you want, using handpicked hair styling tools that haven’t touched other people’s hair and are as clean as you’ve left them. Sounds good? Well, check out this list and find the best hair brush for your hair:

Well, my ladies, that’s where this brush fits in! Use it to gently detangle your tresses and it will leave them shiny, static-free and most importantly – whole and healthy.

Definitely something worth having!

One of the best hair brushes for all of you who can’t stomach a rat tail comb or simply feel the need to advance to a better tool.

## 5. Boar Bristle Paddle Brush

![Boar Bristle Paddle Brush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/05/5_boar-bristle-paddle-brush_300x116.jpg)

Natural boar bristles aren’t only gentle to your delicate strands but help spread the essential oils evenly giving your scalp a soothing, invigorating massage in the process! And, in case a paddle brush is something you can’t live without, I would advise you to opt for something like this instead of your regular plastic one. It won’t do much to detangle thick, voluminous hair, but it’s a great all-in-one brush to use on daily basis.

## 6. Extra Small round Brush

Use it to straighten and smooth out new growth to prolong time between two chemical straightening treatments, style those stubborn cowlicks or let it help you style your Bettie Page fringe to perfection! The choices are numerous and this is the best hair brush to use!

## 7. Thermo Ceramic Straightening Brush

![Thermo Ceramic Straightening Brush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/05/7_thermo-ceramic-straightening-brush_263x250.jpg)
