# 7 Absolutely Clever Ways to Make Your Hair Thicker ...

**How to Make Your Hair Thicker** is something most women want to know, even if it's as a preventative measure. Thin limp hair can be such a drag as well, so if you have fine hair, you likely also want to know **how to make your hair thicker**. Fortunately I have put together these 7 absolutely clever ways to make your hair thicker. These tips are sure to give your locks that thick look you are wanting, so if you want to learn **how to make your hair thicker**, keep reading!

## 1. Volumizing Shampoos and Conditioners

Find a good quality volumizing shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair. You may have to try a few different brands before you find the right one with that ideal formula that simply knows how to make your hair thicker. They work by plumping the hair shaft to create fuller looking hair. Finding that perfect combo will create luscious, full base is necessary, so that you'll be able to work with your newly luscious locks.

## 2. Blow out

The next step on how to make your hair thicker is to combine your shampoos and conditioners with a good blow out. Use a large round brush to dry sections of your hair one by one using light, rolling movements while keeping the hairdryer on a safe distance. Try this easy, effective trick and you will have great full looking hair in no time!

## 3. Rollers

If you don't have a lot of free time and you would still love to know how to make your hair thicker rely on rollers to give you those luscious, full locks. Place rollers in your hair and dry with the blow dryer. Leave the rollers in after the hair is dry for a little longer, take them out really carefully, fix with some hairspray and voila!

## 4. See Your Stylist

While products and good styling techniques can help you get thicker looking hair, it's always best to consult your stylist. He or she can give you tips on how to make your hair thicker with the right cut. Your stylist may be able to suggest a style that will give you the volume and fullness you are looking for while helping you get the most out of your favorite volumizing products.

## 5. Extensions

Adding extension is always a good trick to instantly add volume and, if your budgets allows it, you can give it a shot as well. Your stylist can give you that full bodied, Hollywood look you want by updating your current style with a few carefully prepared extensions.

## 6. Hair Color

A good colorist can use hair dyes to give the illusion of thickness and that's why you should definitely consult your stylist if you want to know how to make your hair thicker with nothing more than just a new dye-job . One tone doesn’t offer the same dimension as color with highlights and low-lights and that's why professional stylists always use more than one color to give their respected customers the most natural-looking multi-toned look possible.

## 7. Body Wave

A soft perm or body wave is another step in how to make your hair thicker. A simple body wave adds volume without creating heavy curls thus helping your hair look fuller every day. Combine it with good color and a good cut and you'll get to enjoy a hairstyle that doesn't only look divine but needs no special styling gadgets to upkeep.

You see, it's easy to learn **how to make your hair thicker**. With the help of your stylist and the right products you can turn your limp hair into luscious luxurious locks; better still, most of these tips on **how to make your hair thicker** don't cost a lot at all. Let me know how these 7 absolutely clever ways to make your hair thicker have worked for you. If you know of any other tips on **how to make your hair thicker**, please share those too!
