# Roll out of Bed Hairstyles for Days You Need the Extra Zzzs ...

If you're running late and think there's not enough time to style your hair, don't panic! You might not have enough time to wash your hair, but you won't need to with these fast looks. All you need is a couple minutes to look pretty and put together. No one will even know how crazy of a morning you've had with these hairstyles!

## 1. Messy Bun

This is the easiest messy bun tutorial ever. No experience is needed to create this perfectly messy bun. Throw your hair up and out of your face, but still look flawless by spraying dry shampoo at the roots, massage in, and use your fingers to create a high pony tail. Twist hair, tuck under the elastic, and fix any pieces hanging out. Loosen the top to create more body, pin in place, spray with hairspray, and you're done!

## 2. Elegant Bun

Twist sections of hair away from your face. Fan out the twist and pin in place with a couple of bobby pins in the back of your head. Use the remainder of your hair and separate into two sections. Continue twisting and create a circle. Pin any loose layers in place, and you have an elegant bun in minutes!

## 3. Fishtail Braid

This tutorial is perfect for beginners and is so quick and easy! It will show you step by step to master this look for a rushed morning. For such an intricate braid, this is a very simple tutorial. Section hair into two sections, transferring hair back and forth and crossing over. Continue the entire length of your hair. Tie at the end, and you've got yourself a fishtail braid!

## 4. Dutch Braided Ponytail

A ponytail is obviously a quick hairstyle, but spend another extra couple of minutes to create a Dutch braid into your ponytail for a look that's far from ordinary! Braid a Dutch braid on the side of your head, pull pieces out, and continue braiding all the way down your hair. Braid the same way on the opposite side of your head. Backcomb the top section of your hair for extra volume. Shape the ponytail and take out any pieces to frame your face. Add the braid to your ponytail with an elastic. Finish with hairspray, and you're left with a big, messy Dutch braided ponytail!

## 5. Two Strand Braid

Simplify your braid with just two strands! You'll need a teasing comb, wax, hair elastics, and a curling iron. Tease the crown of your hair for volume, and quickly smooth out the tease. Leave out some layers of your hair for a messy look. Split hair into two equal sections, grabbing one section and tying with an elastic. Grab section two and pull through section one. Continue all the way down your hair for the rest of the braid. This is such an easy, elegant look and is very simple to master.

## 6. 2 MINUTES SPRING TWIST HAIR TUTORIAL

Mist hairspray all over and start twisting the hair by your bangs. Continuing twisting and adding hair. Tie the end with a hair tie and secure with bobby pins. This beautiful look frames your face and only takes minutes!

## 7. Sleek up-do

Start with dry shampoo and tease hair at the crown. Put hair in a low ponytail, keeping bangs or side layers out. Twist ponytail, wrap around ponytail, and pin in place. Pull side sections around your bun and keep it tight on the sides. You'll have a sleek up-do in minutes!

If you need the extra Zzzz's, you can get all the sleep you need, roll out of bed, and still style your hair with one of these looks. What's your favorite look? Comment below!
