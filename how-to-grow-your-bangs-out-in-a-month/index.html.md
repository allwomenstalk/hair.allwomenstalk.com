# How to Grow Your Bangs out in a Month ...

When I got bangs two years ago, it was so hard to grow them out again, I remember thinking: 'are they ever going to grow out completely?'. If you are in the same boat as me, don't worry, I got you covered. Below are some tips and tricks to help you grow out your bangs beautifully.

## 1. Snip Bangs in the Center

![Snip Bangs in the Center](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/yt/xaxkri7c56eccaa8d309b220397540_600x600.jpg)

It helps spread the hair a bit and allows you to separate the bangs a bit!

## 2. Schedule Regular Trim Ups

![Schedule Regular Trim Ups](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/io/ze/wbxhuo5356ecca3a54002471005561_600x604.jpg)

Why? It'll help your fringe grow longer and more evenly and quicker!

## 3. Keep Your Hair Really Moisturized

![Keep Your Hair Really Moisturized](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zi/ho/mqiut5w556ecca4669409816970092_600x600.jpg)

Not only will this keep your hair conditioned beautifully, but it'll hydrate your hair.

## 4. Pick Your Part

![Pick Your Part](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/ca/ryctnuc256eccacc77bbc733860901_600x600.jpg)

When it comes to that time to stop parting your bangs in the middle, you've got to pick a side; angle it to the left or right.

## 5. Embrace Accessories

![Embrace Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a6/om/domouj2y56eccb1b1a192028257017_600x779.jpg)

Seriously, bobby pins, combs, headbands ... they all work and they are all necessary!

## 6. Lightweight Hair Products Are Key

![Lightweight Hair Products Are Key](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o6/xr/saqfrc8e56eccb34befba986977989_600x600.jpg)

You don't need a lot of pomade or hairspray,; just a little bit.

## 7. Twist, Braid, Tease Your Braids

![Twist, Braid, Tease Your Braids](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rj/iv/cm6beguq56eccb5c54fa2440882735_520x785.jpg)

It helps keep them out of your eyes and it'll make them look chic and styled!

## 8. Create Some Fun Updos!

![Create Some Fun Updos!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c1/fs/ny87spmn56eccb6bb37d2596028900_600x600.jpg)

This will allow you to pin your bangs back and nobody will see them.

## 9. Embrace the Layered Look

![Embrace the Layered Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/qn/mg65yhiw56eccb795c923809471356_600x900.jpg)

Shaggy, slightly messy looks with bangs are very in right now, so during that awkward phase, embrace it.

## 10. Part Your Hair on the Side

![Part Your Hair on the Side](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fx/tn/b6yslrrd56eccba3ab592312358005_600x575.jpg)

Once your bangs are long enough, you probably should be able to tuck them behind your ear, but remember, choose a part!

## 11. Slick 'em Back

![Slick 'em Back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/yz/p6x9umr956eccbad3fe09677522088_594x547.jpg)

I love this look! It's chic and so fashionable.

## 12. Feather It up

![Feather It up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rv/ge/e8qzmgo956eccbc6ae6d5376522814_600x611.jpg)

A feathered style is so in, so just use your curling iron and do it Farrah Fawcett style.

## 13. Do NOT CUT

![Do NOT CUT](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pg/ty/xznjbjt556eccbe22d118579575759_600x484.jpg)

I'm being serious, don't cut your bangs more and more, especially during that awkward phase.

## 14. Steer Clear of Harsh Damaging Treatments

![Steer Clear of Harsh Damaging Treatments](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/he/lo3a8acz56eccbf6e0282425267149_600x900.jpg)

Heat, bleaching, tons of chemicals ... all of that can stunt the growth of your bangs.

## 15. Hydrate Your Hair

![Hydrate Your Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/om/nl/jh7nd4p556eccc0a24644380777314_600x801.jpg)

Drink tons and tons of water so that your hair doesn't dry out.

## 16. Eat up

![Eat up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e2/az/u0x8qxxb56eccc346311b392446808_600x600.jpg)

Plenty of eggs, leafy greens and olive oil. It'll keep your hair focused.

## 17. Be Very Patient

![Be Very Patient](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/vp/ctqd44bp56eccc43ea351874097640_400x400.jpg)

I know that it's hard to be patient when you are growing out your bangs, but it's worth it in the end.
