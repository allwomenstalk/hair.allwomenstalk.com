# 15 Drugstore Hair Care Products for Hot Hair on a Budget ...

Drugstore hair care products don’t always get the recognition they deserve. I mean, they’re affordable, easily available and many of them are really good quality products! Sure, there are a few bad apples here and there, but for the most part, I’ve been quite pleased. I’ve tried my share of my hair care products from high end to drugstore, but I always go back to the more familiar drugstore brands. So whether you’re a drugstore diva or only swear by high-end hair care, this list of drugstore hair care products is worth taking a look at!

## 1. Neutrogena Triple Moisture Deep Recovery Hair Mask

![Neutrogena Triple Moisture Deep Recovery Hair Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12210249_1332_499x495.jpg)

Neutrogena is one of the most popular brands of drugstore hair care products. This deep recovery hair mask makes for an ideal weekly treatment to hydrate and baby your hair from a week of the sun, heat styling and whatever else it might have gone through! Get yours at drugstore.com for $6.99.

## 2. Sexy Hair Concepts Big Sexy Hair Spray & Play Volumizing Hairspray

![Sexy Hair Concepts Big Sexy Hair Spray & Play Volumizing Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12210249_7675_409x408.jpg)

Who doesn’t want big, sexy hair? This Spray & Play hairspray allows you do to just that. Spray this on to lock in the style and forget about it. No need to keep checking your hair to see it if it’s still in place, this stuff works. It’s a got a super strong hold but it's still lightweight and helps control frizz. Get your can at Target; price varies by store.

## 3. Garnier Fructis Sleek & Shine anti-Frizz Serum

![Garnier Fructis Sleek & Shine anti-Frizz Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12210249_8206_409x398.jpg)

Do you have a head of wild hair that needs some taming? Garnier’s Anti-Frizz Serum seeks to tame frizz, hydrate and add luster to your locks. It’ll help make styling much easier and more manageable. It’s noted to control frizz in even 97% humidity with its blend of oils and apricot. Try this out at Walmart for a mere $4.97!

## 4. Dove Nourishing Oil Hair Care Detangler

![Dove Nourishing Oil Hair Care Detangler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12210249_1270_409x387.jpg)

If one of your hair care concerns is having hair that’s prone to tangles like me, you’ll love this Dove detangler! All hair types can benefit from this detangling spray that helps get the tangles out, smooths, softens and adds shine. Many people report their hair being much easier to comb through and manage, and it leaves behind a great scent! Find this at Target for $3.94.

## 5. Shea Moisture Raw Shea Butter Moisture Retention Shampoo

![Shea Moisture Raw Shea Butter Moisture Retention Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12210249_8383_409x389.jpg)

Ladies looking for a gentle, moisturizing shampoo for dry hair, look no further! Shea Moisture makes some of the best all-natural hair care products around, and their Moisture Retention Shampoo is one of the best. If you have dry, damaged hair, this shampoo will help restore moisture and help repair over-processed hair. Get yours at Target for $9.99.

## 6. Motions at Home Nourish Leave-in Conditioner

## 11. John Frieda Root Awakening Strength Restoring Smooth Lotion

![John Frieda Root Awakening Strength Restoring Smooth Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/26172319_7229_599x501.jpg)

## 12. Desert Essence Organic Jojoba Oil
