7 Side Braid

Via Beautiful Braids...
Messy side braid meet low pony, fastened with a daisy band. Love it!

***

The side braid is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for centuries. It's a great way to keep hair out of your face and can be dressed up or down. For a summer look, try a messy side braid with a low ponytail. To add a touch of femininity, fasten the ponytail with a daisy band. This look is perfect for a day out in the sun or a night out on the town.

The side braid is easy to do and requires only a few minutes to complete. To start, brush your hair to one side and separate a section of hair from the top of your head. Begin braiding the section of hair and continue until you reach the end. Secure the braid with a clear elastic band. Next, gather the remaining hair into a low ponytail and fasten with a daisy band. To finish, gently tug at the braid to loosen it and give it a more relaxed look.

20 Braids

Via beauty
French braid on each side, then a side fishtail braid to finish.

***

Braids are one of the most popular hairstyles for summer. Whether you’re headed to the beach or a night out, a braid is a great way to keep your hair looking stylish and put together. The French braid on each side and the fishtail braid at the end of this look are great for creating an eye-catching style.

The French braid is a classic style that is easy to create and looks elegant. Start by dividing your hair into three sections. Take the right section of hair and cross it over the middle section. Then take the left section and cross it over the middle section. Continue this pattern until you reach the end of your hair. Once you’ve reached the end, secure the braid with a clear elastic band.

The fishtail braid is another popular style. This look is created by splitting the hair into two sections. Take a small strand from the outside of the right section and cross it over to the left section. Then take a small strand from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the right section. Continue this pattern until you reach the end of your hair. Secure the braid with an elastic band.

Beach waves are the perfect summer look for those seeking a laid-back yet stylish style. The long, loose waves and blonde highlights create an effortless, sun-kissed look that is perfect for the summer months. To achieve this look, start with damp hair and use a curling wand to create loose curls. Once the curls have cooled, run your fingers through them to loosen them up.
