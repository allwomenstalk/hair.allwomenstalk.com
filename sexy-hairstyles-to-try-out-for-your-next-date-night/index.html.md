# 7 Best Sexy Hairstyles for Women: Date Night Ideas for Long & Natural Hair

Finding the perfect **hot hairstyles** for a special evening can be a game-changer for your confidence. Whether you're looking for **date night hairstyles for natural hair** or something sleek and modern, these **sexiest womens hairstyles** are sure to impress. We've rounded up the top looks that are timeless, feminine, and perfect for any setting, from a casual dinner to a black-tie event.

Have you been wondering what **sexy hairstyles for women** your partner would love to see you wear? Here's the scoop. These are the top hairstyles that people enjoy seeing their dates wear. Of course, everyone has their own taste, so you may want to ask them what their favorite styles are. ## 1. Big Curls

![woman with big curls in a wedding dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/c9/p9go2_clothing_dress_gown_wedding-dress_supermodel_553x692.jpg)

Big curls definitely make the list of sexy hairstyles. This is a perfect style for date night. You can create big curls in a couple of different ways. You can use a one-and-a-half-inch curling iron to spiral curl your hair or use hot rollers. Both methods offer lots of curl and body, making it one of the most **romantic date night hairstyles for long hair**.

## 2. Sleek, Straight Hair

![woman with sleek straight hair in a red dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/ny/yfadh_clothing_red_dress_hairstyle_long-hair_600x1000.jpg)

Sleek, straight hair is sexy because it looks so healthy. It has a shine that can sometimes be lost in other hairstyles by the time you add in teasing and tons of hair spray. A high-quality [flat iron](https://www.ghdhair.com/) can help you achieve the sleek look you're dreaming of. Even if you have naturally straight hair, you'll probably still want to use a flat iron for that extra polish. If you need a bit more shine, rub a few drops of hair oil onto your hair before you blow it dry. It may take a few tries before you get this exactly right for your unique hair type.

## 3. A Ponytail

![woman with a polished ponytail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y0/lx/cx3hl_hair_clothing_blond_hairstyle_photography_505x626.jpg)

Most people love the look of a ponytail. It's fresh and youthful. For a bit of extra polish, you can leave a strand of hair out to wrap around the ponytail to hide your hair band. You can also tie a ribbon around your ponytail, which always looks adorable. If you want a bit of volume in your crown, tease the top of your hair before you pull it into the ponytail.

- 1.5-inch Curling Iron

- Rat-tail Comb (for teasing)

- Heat Protectant Spray

- Boar Bristle Brush

- Texturizing Sea Salt Spray

## 4. A Polished Updo

![woman with a polished updo on a runway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/mk/dewhc_hair_runway_wedding-dress_fashion_hairstyle_600x902.jpg)

A polished updo is the perfect choice for date nights when you need something a bit more glamorous. If you're going to a swanky new restaurant, then this is the hairstyle you want to wear. There are different ways you can style your hair into a polished updo, but a French twist is probably the easiest. I like to tease my hair a lot at the top before I actually do the French twist. When you do this, you can enjoy all that volume by adding in a jeweled headband to show contrast. For more maintenance tips, see our latest [hair care tips](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-healthier-hair/).

## 5. Beachy Waves

![woman with beachy waves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sz/m1/qjh3y_hair_human-hair-color_face_black-hair_nose_600x900.jpg)

Beachy waves are easier for some women to achieve than others. If you have naturally wavy hair, then this is the sexy hairstyle for you. This is a great choice for casual summer dates when you're having a picnic or attending a barbecue. Add a bit of [texturizing spray](https://www.bumbleandbumble.com/product/19055/221/style/preps-primers/surf-spray) and let nature do its job. You'll be the envy of everyone you meet.

## 6. A Side Braid

![woman with a side braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nc/mg/bzvxu_hair_human-hair-color_face_hairstyle_blond_518x778.jpg)

Side braids are sexy because they're also very feminine. If you have long hair, try a side braid for your next date. They usually look best when they aren't overly tight; a bit of looseness adds to the appeal. If you want something different, try a fishtail braid instead. This is often cited as one of the **sexiest hairstyles for long hair**.

## 7. A Messy Bun

![woman with a styled messy bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/te/jh/lo3ik_clothing_sleeve_hairstyle_spring_fashion_467x700.jpg)

There are messy buns that are the result of not wanting to devote time to your hair, and there are messy buns that are intentionally styled that way. It's not hard to tell the difference! A styled messy bun is when you take time to make sure that your hair looks undone but it really isn't. You take pains to make sure it looks beautifully undone.
