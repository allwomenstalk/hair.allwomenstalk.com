# 7 Best Hair Colors for Summer: Stunning Ideas to Try Now

Summer is the perfect season to refresh your look and swap out those muted winter tones for something vibrant and full of life. Whether you're dreaming of a bold transformation or a subtle shift from your natural shade, the right summer hair color can elevate your style and give your outfits that extra pop. If you're looking for ways to [fix damaged colored hair](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-fix-damaged-colored-hair/) or just want a fresh start, these 7 stunning hair colors will make you stand out and feel fabulous.

## 1. Fiery Red

![Fiery Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/1_fiery-red_600x755.jpg)

Fiery Red remains an iconic choice for anyone looking to make a statement. While it's a bit more refined than the ultra-bright shades of years past, the version seen on stars like Scarlett Johansson is still incredibly striking. This hue brings a beautiful warmth to your face without washing you out, making it one of the most versatile [vibrant hair colors](https://www.lorealparisusa.com/hair-color/red) to explore. It looks stunning on all skin tones and adds an undeniable touch of allure to your summer aesthetic.

## 2. Chocolate Brown

![Chocolate Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/2_chocolate-brown_600x955.jpg)

While dark hair might seem like an unusual choice for the heat, a rich Chocolate Brown offers a sophisticated contrast, especially when paired with light eyes. Even for those with darker eyes, this shade—famously rocked by Mila Kunis—adds incredible depth and richness. It's far from dull; in fact, it looks exceptionally glossy and healthy under the bright summer sun. To keep your brunette locks looking their best, you might want to learn about the [best ways to condition your hair](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/best-ways-to-condition-your-hair/) to maintain that salon-fresh shine.

## 3. Strawberry Blonde

![Strawberry Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/3_strawberry-blonde_375x500.jpg)

Strawberry Blonde is a playful, warm-toned twist on traditional blonde. It’s the perfect middle ground for those who want a hint of red without going full copper. Take a cue from Blake Lively's signature style and embrace this radiant color. It’s ideal for standing out at the beach or a summer garden party, reflecting the sunlight beautifully.

## 4. Platinum Blonde

![Platinum Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/4_platinum-blonde_540x494.jpg)

Platinum Blonde is an intense, high-fashion color that's always in style. It’s flirty, brilliant, and edgy, much like the bold looks often seen on trendsetters like Miley Cyrus. This vivacious color can instantly brighten your complexion and make your eyes pop. However, it requires some maintenance—be sure to check out [official coloring guides](https://www.garnierusa.com/tips-and-how-tos/how-to-dye-hair-platinum-blonde) to ensure your hair stays healthy during the process.

## 5. Ombre Hair

![Ombre Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/5_ombre-hair_500x650.jpg)

Ombre is still a fantastic way to enjoy two colors at once without the commitment of a full dye job. It’s a low-maintenance trend that shows off your personality. Jessica Alba has long been a fan of this look, with her dark roots seamlessly melting into a lustrous blonde. The best part? You can customize this transition with any colors you like to suit your [highlights](#{7}) or base shade.

## 6. Pieces of Bright Colors

![Pieces of Bright Colors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/6_pieces-of-bright-colors_600x973.jpg)

Nothing adds a pop of excitement like a few well-placed bright accents. If you're feeling adventurous, adding pieces of pink, aqua, or purple can make your hair look playful and lively. It’s a bold move, but even a few hidden peek-a-boo strands can transform your look for festival season. To keep these colors vibrant, incorporating [best hair oils](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/best-hair-oils/) into your routine can help maintain hair health.

## 7. Highlights

![Highlights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/7_highlights_500x650.jpg)

If you’re not ready for a total transformation, highlights are the way to go. Consulting a professional stylist at a top salon like [Madison Reed](https://www.madison-reed.com/) can help you find the perfect tones to complement your natural color. Highlights add dimension and movement to your hair, creating a sun-kissed effect that Jennifer Lawrence has mastered perfectly.

Summer is the ultimate time to experiment and find the look that makes you feel most confident. Whether you want to become a "new you" or simply enhance your current style, changing your hair color is a great way to match the energy of the season. What are your favorite summer hair color ideas? Let us know!

### Quick Summer Hair Care Tips:

- Use a UV protection spray to prevent fading.

- Rinse hair with fresh water before and after swimming.

- Deep condition weekly to combat dryness from the heat.
