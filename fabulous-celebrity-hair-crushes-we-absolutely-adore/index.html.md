# 7 Fabulous Celebrity Hair Crushes We Absolutely Adore ...

No matter how perfect your hair is, you're still going to have celebrity hair crushes and looks you want to borrow. The women in Hollywood are so gorgeous that it can make you jealous, but don't fret. Instead of getting envious, try to take aspects of their look and incorporate them into your own style. Your celebrity hair crushes will be flattered that you're modeling your locks after them.

## 1. Mila Kunis

![Mila Kunis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/02160320_3276_333x500.jpg)

No matter what role she plays or what red carpet she appears on, Mila Kunis always looks stylish. She's found a way to make her hair appear soft with a sheen. It's difficult to get the perfect amount of curls and bounce to your hair, but she manages to do so. One of the celebrity hair crushes you should model yourself after is Mila. There's never a time when she doesn't look perfect.

## 2. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/02160320_0149_375x500.jpg)

She's tried every hair color and looks fantastic with every single one of them. She's able to pull off any style and look as sweet or sexy as she wants. It's hard not to be jealous when you look at Emma and think of all she's accomplished. Not only is she beautiful, but she gets to kiss Andrew Garfield whenever she wants. Who has a better life than she does?

## 3. Ginnifer Goodwin

![Ginnifer Goodwin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/02160555_7604_600x800.jpg)

Long hair doesn't always equal beautiful, ladies. If you can pull off a short hairdo, you can look just as fabulous as anyone else. Ginnifer can make anyone want to go for a drastic change and chop off their locks. She looks amazing with short hair that makes it easier to see her gorgeous face. If you think you can pull off this look, go for it!

## 4. Zoey Deschanel

![Zoey Deschanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/02155526_9656_500x600.jpg)

If you like bangs, you'll love Zoey's look. She's managed to find the perfect hairstyle for her face shape. Sometimes bangs are correlated with childishness, but she shows that that's not at all true. Depending on the day, she can look professional, sexy, or just plain adorable. So take a hint from Zoey and don't be afraid to stick to a signature style.

## 5. Jennifer Aniston

![Jennifer Aniston](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/02155526_0470_496x594.jpg)

She's been around for decades and our love for her hair hasn't changed. From the 'Rachel cut' to her current style, she's always making us jealous. Who can compete with Jen? She can cut her hair to length and still look amazing. It seems like it's impossible for her to have a bad hair day.

## 6. Christina Hendricks

![Christina Hendricks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/02155525_5602_448x581.jpg)

Her hair color is absolutely stunning. At times, her style is reminiscent of the 50s and 60s, which ironically gives her a fresh look. She stands out from whoever she's with. If you're looking for a new hair color, you might want to borrow Christina's look. Trust me, she won't mind.

## 7. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/02155526_1456_486x750.jpg)

She can dye her hair an unnatural color and still look perfect. It's difficult for some people to pull off such a unique look, but she does it with flair. She can wear her hair long or short, dark or light, and still look incredible. Wouldn't you love to have such versatility with your hair? It's fun to change things up as much as possible.

If you're looking for a new hairdo, look to your favorite stars for inspiration. What celebrity do you admire for their hair? Who else should be added to this list?
