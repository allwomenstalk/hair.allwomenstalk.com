# 7 Top Hair Products of 2012 Every Woman Should Own ...

Top hair products 2012 listed below are hot, much-loved and definitely something worth checking out. I know every woman has some holy grail hair products that they absolutely adore, but if you haven’t found your must-have hair product yet, here's your chance to do so. There are tons of hair products available out there, of course, yet some simply aren’t worth our time or money so do yourself a favor, skip the trial-and-err method and check out these 7 top hair products of 2012 every woman should own:

## 1. Ojon Damage Reverse Instant Restorative Hair Serum

![Ojon Damage Reverse Instant Restorative Hair Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/1_ojon-damage-reverse-instant-restorative-hair-serum_144x448.jpg)

**Price:** $25, available at: [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Let’s face it, our hair takes a beating from styling, blow drying and using heated hair tools so one of my top hair products of 2012 is, of course, a restorative hair serum. Treat your tresses to this lightweight serum that will tame even the wildest frizzies, strengthen your hair and give your locks luscious shine! Don’t worry, this gloss won’t leave your hair feeling oily or weighed down, although it’s an oil, it’s non-greasy and absorbs quickly!

## 2. Pantene Pro-V Relaxed and Natural for Women of Color Shampoo, Dry to Moisturized

![Pantene Pro-V Relaxed and Natural for Women of Color Shampoo, Dry to Moisturized](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/2_pantene-pro-v-relaxed-and-natural-for-women-of-color-shampoo-dry-to-moisturized_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $ 6.99, available at: [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)
This is THE top hair product 2012 for African-American women and women with very thick or curly hair! This highly rated shampoo is extremely moisturizing, it strengthens weak hair, thoroughly cleansing your scalp and strands and even prevents color fading! Whoa -what more could we ask for?! This baby is affordable and effective so go on and most certainly give it a try!

## 3. SERGE NORMANT Meta Revive Dry Shampoo

![SERGE NORMANT Meta Revive Dry Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/3_serge-normant-meta-revive-dry-shampoo_128x448.jpg)

**Price:** $25, available at: [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Dry shampoos are a lifesaver and this one is definitely one of the top hair products 2012! It is a bit on the pricy side, I will give you that, yet a little bit goes a long way and only a few sprays of it will leave your hair smelling great, feeling clean and packed with volume and texture! You'll need only a small amount of it to get amazing results so use sparingly and your day-old hair will thank you for this decadent delight!

## 4. L'Oreal Elnett Satin Hairspray, Extra Strong Hold, Unscented

![L'Oreal Elnett Satin Hairspray, Extra Strong Hold, Unscented](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/4_loreal-elnett-satin-hairspray-extra-strong-hold-unscented_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $14.99, available at: [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)
Now what list of top hair products 2012 could be considered complete without the infamous Elnett?! This bad boy is far from new but it always tops the lists of hair stylists’ must-have items! I love the hold that Elnett provides but I was never a fan of the fragrance, yet even that has been taken cared of now that L’oreal has introduced an unscented version of their famed hairspray! Time for our olfactory receptors to finally take a break without us having to sacrifice that superb hold!

## 5. John Frieda Luxurious Volume Lavish Lift Root Booster

![John Frieda Luxurious Volume Lavish Lift Root Booster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/5_john-frieda-luxurious-volume-lavish-lift-root-booster_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $5.99 available at: [drugstore.com](https://goo.gl/Ru7q9z)
This top hair product of 2012 is for my ladies who love volume! This product is best for fine-haired foxes who want lots of lift without the added weight. Some ladies like using this with wet hair while others swear by spritzing it on dry hair so try out both and see which method works best for you. And remember, if you have fine hair you'd like to see looking thick and voluminous and have a very strong policy about not accepting any greasy residue or stickiness, this is the product to check out.

## 6. Tresemmé Thermal Creations Heat Tamer Spray

![Tresemmé Thermal Creations Heat Tamer Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/6_tresemm-thermal-creations-heat-tamer-spray_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $4.75, available at: [drugstore.com](https://goo.gl/hbyYLT)
One of the top hair products 2012 and probably the year before, Tresemme’s Heat Tamer spray is an essential for ladies who heat style their hair! This lightweight spray smells great, is easy to use and apply and really helps protect your hair from the ravages of blow dryers and flat irons! The best part about this hair rescuer is, of course, the price and less than $5 can, in this case, buy your tender tresses protection and help you get great-looking hair!

## 7. L’oreal Paris EverCreme Deep Nourishing Masque

![L’oreal Paris EverCreme Deep Nourishing Masque](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/7_loreal-paris-evercreme-deep-nourishing-masque_200x200.jpg)

**Price:** $8.99, available at: [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
L’oreal’s EverCreme hair masque has to be one of the best 2012 hair care items out there! This deep conditioning treatment is sulfate-free, moisturizing and works fast! Keep this jar in your shower, apply to your hair for 5 minutes to allow the treatment to penetrate strands and voila! Soft, shiny and nourished hair is yours! I absolutely love this treatment because it's so effective yet sulfate-free and quite affordable for a deep conditioner!

So ladies, do you own any of these top hair products 2012? If not, I highly suggest trying some of these out! Take it from your fellow hair care lovers; these products are popular and loved for a reason! What are some of your must-have hair care products? Do you have a top hair product of 2012 so far?
