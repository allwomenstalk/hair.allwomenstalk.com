# 7 Bow Hair Tutorials for an Elegant Look ...

You've seen Lady Gaga rock a bow hairstyle, but how often do you see your peers wear the style? It's not something you see around all that often, which is exactly why you should learn how to create them. You'll be getting compliments left and right, as soon as you learn how to master the following bow hair tutorials:

## 1. Bow Bun

It doesn't matter if you have long hair or short hair, because this tutorial will show you how to create a bow bun, depending on the length of your hair. All you need is a hair tie that matches your hair color, a brush, and a bunch of bobby pins. Once you have all of your supplies ready, then it's time to create this fabulous look.

## 2. Diagonal Bow Braid

Although the woman in this tutorial is creating a diagonal bow braid on a child, it looks amazing on girls of any age. You need to start off by sectioning your hair correctly, which is the trickiest part of the entire hairdo. Once you have that figured out, the rest should be easy!

## 3. Half-up Hair Bow

If you hate pushing all of your hair away from your face, then you can create a half-up hair bow.This hairdo looks best with thick hair, but anyone can pull it off if it's done correctly. All you need is a hairbrush, bobby pins, an elastic, and two mirrors in order to see the front and back of your hair. You should already have all of those items in your bathroom, so it's time to try this look yourself!

## 4. Sock Bun Bow Updo

This bow bun is entirely different than the first bun on this list. This time, instead of the bow consisting of your entire updo, the bow will just be an accent that appears beneath your bun. The final product looks complicated, but once you see the tutorial that's under two minutes long, you'll realize that it's not nearly as difficult as it appears at first glance.

## 5. Ponytail Bow

You're going to start off by creating a simple side ponytail, like you've probably done a million times before. Then you're going to grab a one-inch section of your hair and twist it until it forms a pretty little bow. Just make sure that you use elastics that match your hair color, so that the bow appears as natural as it could possibly look.

## 6. Bow Braid Headband

You're going to start by creating a regular french braided headband. Then you're going to grab a hairpin in order to create the little bows that make this hairdo so impressive. You can choose how big you want each bow to be, so you can go as tiny or as large as you want. It's all up to you!

## 7. Natural Bow Puff

If you have natural hair that you don't feel like straightening, you can create this hairdo. All you need to do is pull your hair up as far as you'd like the bow to be and then start the process. Like the other styles, all you really need is a hairbrush and some ties.

After a few attempts, you'll be a pro at creating each of these elegant styles. Which of these tutorials are you going to try first?
