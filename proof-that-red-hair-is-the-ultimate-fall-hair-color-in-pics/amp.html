Frequently Asked Questions

Why is red hair such a great choice for fall?
Red hair is perfect for fall because it matches the cozy and warm vibes of the season. The fiery shades emulate the autumn leaves and can brighten up any gloomy day!
Can I mix red with other colors for fall hair?
Absolutely! Mixing red with blonde highlights or even a hint of deeper auburn can create a fabulous, multidimensional look that's super trendy for the fall.
What shade of red works best for redheads in the fall?
Well, it really depends on your style! Some love a rich, deep mahogany or burgundy, while others go for a brighter copper or auburn. It’s all about finding what feels right for you.
How do I maintain my red hair color during the fall?
Maintaining red hair is all about using color-safe shampoos and conditioners, minimizing heat styling, and keeping your hair moisturized. Consider doing gloss treatments to keep that color vibrant!
I'm blonde. Can I try red shades for fall without a drastic change?
For sure! You can always incorporate red undertones or soft balayage to add a hint of red. It’s a subtle way to switch things up without committing to a full head of red hair.
