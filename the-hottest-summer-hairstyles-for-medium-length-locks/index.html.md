# The Hottest Summer Hairstyles for Medium\-Length Locks ...

Medium-length hair doesn't get enough attention. Plenty of us have hair that's stuck somewhere between long and short. Your hair's not necessarily stuck in an in-between stage, either – who says you can't simply like medium-length hair? It just really doesn't get enough attention, and that's a shame, but it's one I'm here to rectify. The season's changing, summer's on its way, so it's the perfect time to try one – or more – of the hottest, trendiest hairstyles for those luscious, mid-length locks of yours.

## 1. A Tousled Bob

![A Tousled Bob](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z1/uj/kz1h66kq574776661e5e3259618282_600x600.jpg)

Can't go wrong with bedhead, and medium-length hair lends itself incredibly well to the look. The best part is that a bob this length doesn't need a ton of maintenance.

## 2. Blunt Bangs and Layers

![Blunt Bangs and Layers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/t0/dojb0wro57477666b2640621618349_600x600.jpg)

With or without colorful hair, blunt bangs and soft layers go well together. I have to say, though, I'm loving the blue ombre.

## 3. Braided up

![Braided up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/p6/ty9fzm5857477667d0988281169131_600x602.jpg)

This has been trendy for years, you just didn't know it.

## 4. Crowning Braid

![Crowning Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ox/il/aoy57f5k574776bb6a49f151338681_500x669.jpg)

I don't think I've ever seen this style before. I kind of dig it. I also kind of don't. I'm torn here.

## 5. A Little Wave

![A Little Wave](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h7/v6/cho5fw9w5747766787da9840178113_600x600.jpg)

Add some waves and texture to make your mid-length tresses really pop. It's as easy as braiding your hair overnight, really.

## 6. A Waterfall Braid

![A Waterfall Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/hk/kyburc4057477667a2808909241760_600x600.jpg)

Oh, yeah. You can do this when your hair is medium-length. I mean, if you can do it at all. I cannot do a waterfall braid.

## 7. Twists and Waves

![Twists and Waves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/hz/gancnfb7574776685650d048970391_600x600.jpg)

If you love half up hairstyles, you'll adore this. It's almost formal, but not quite, so you could still rock this style on the daily.

## 8. Beach-ready Waves

![Beach-ready Waves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xa/ri/ohqq93u7574776e5b314e444280306_500x666.jpg)

As we've already established, I know. Simple waves look amazing in mid-length hair, though.

## 9. A Cotton Candy Braid

![A Cotton Candy Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/a9/hzor9wh757477667f39fe765781047_599x800.jpg)

Want a hairstyle with a little whimsy? Look no further than the cotton candy braid!

## 10. A Simple Tousle

![A Simple Tousle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qz/mu/c219f6it574776689ff9c656670591_481x471.jpg)

Off topic, but I actually really liked Kylie's hair like this. I think the color and the style suited her.

## 11. A Vintage Summer Style

![A Vintage Summer Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/x1/iz8rao8957477669832cd770370375_600x586.jpg)

Oh, this is gorgeous! This kind of easy elegance is a must-have for sultry summer evenings.

## 12. Kinky Curls

![Kinky Curls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zu/ut/m8bc6qz4574776697743c955239475_600x601.jpg)

Let your curls shine! Step out with your natural coily cuteness and rock the summer.

## 13. A Sleek Middle Part

![A Sleek Middle Part](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n8/qe/q4zx1phq5747770ee1481006344026_500x753.jpg)

Medium-length hair with a middle part is lovely. You can work a look that falls on the sleeker side of boho.

## 14. An Extreme Part

![An Extreme Part](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yw/y7/w64prx6d5747766abb943886272172_594x594.jpg)

Then again, you can also go for an extreme side part. This has the added benefit of giving you tons of volume.

## 15. A Messily Braided Bun

![A Messily Braided Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ce/u6/xskh2d315747766a41eb9191257016_600x901.jpg)

Medium-length hair, don't care! Keep it casual and gorgeously messy by mixing braids and buns.

## 16. All the Volume

![All the Volume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fd/mx/f9s9usaq5747766b5bc62163310677_600x600.jpg)

All of it! Every single bit of volume ever.

## 17. Choppy Bob

![Choppy Bob](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/fv/g9jt004q5747766b4ca24922904438_600x596.jpg)

You really can't go wrong with a bob. This is another style that's easy to maintain and quick to style.
