# 12 Fab & Easy Bun Hairstyles ...

Easy bun hairstyles are so in right now and the best part is that many of them are super quick to do! Whether you are running out the door to school or you are trying to get ready for work, these easy bun hairstyles are quick and so, so simple! These styles work great if you have super long hair, or even if you have medium length hair! So give it a try, girls, and let us know how fun and easy it was!

## 1. Knot Bun

The knot bun is by far one of the easiest easy bun hairstyles out there! It is all about the technique and this tutorial gives a fantastic technique. It's also super simple to follow, so you'll never have to worry about not pulling off the knot bun! Knot buns are definitely in right now, so just remember that!

## 2. Tuck & Wrap Bun

The tuck and wrap bun is actually really easy to accomplish, if you have the right hair for it! In this tutorial, the woman has a ton of hair, but she does prove how easy it is to do a tuck and wrap bun. This is a great hairstyle if you just feel like pulling your hair back, but still want it a little sexy!

## 3. Donut/Sock Bun

I swear, everywhere I turn around now, I find someone that is wearing a donut/sock bun on their head! I think the reason for that is because it's so easy to pull together. All you need is a sock, rolled into a donut shape and your hair is almost rolled into it. Take a look at the tutorial above!

## 4. Spiral Twisty Bun

This is a great and uncomplicated bun that is ideal for any girl or woman that is looking for something unique to do with her hair but also wants something easy. I've done this hairstyle and I have shorter hair. It's super easy to pull together in the morning and it only take a few minutes once you get the hang of it!

## 5. Gibson Tuck Hair

I love, love this look for anyone that is looking for something super chic, totally easy and so stylish! This look is so awesome for the beach or even for a night out on the town! All you do it just pull a ponytail in and tuck!

## 6. Chignon

I swear that the chignon is a look that so many celebs are pulling off now and it's so, so glamorous – and easy to do! I love how versatile a this style is! You can make it loose and casual, or clean-cut and elegant. This may take a little bit longer than some other styles, but it is absolutely worth it!

## 7. Side Chignon

If you want something ideal for date night, something sweet for the office and something awesome for a girls' night, this is the hairstyle for you! I actually think that this look is great for a shorter hair length too, as long as you have enough to pull it into a bun.

## 8. Messy Top Knot

Finally, a messy top knot (which is seen on the Kardashian girls all of the time!) is awesome for the office, for a lazy day off or even for house cleaning! If you do it right, it can also be great to go out with – just make sure it's not too messy.

## 9. Ballet Bun

Who doesn’t love the classic look of a ballet bun? It’s sweet and chic, plus you can’t really go wrong with a ballerina bun! Dress down this style with jeans and a T-shirt, or dress it up for a night on the town with a nice dress! I love how fresh-faced and youthful this hairstyle makes you look!

## 10. Messy Braided Bun

The messy braided bun is a really unique look that’s perfect for any occasion! You can wear it out to any event, from running errands to attending a wedding! It’s not one you’ll see every day, but it’s surprisingly easy to achieve! It will only take you about ten minutes max, and is perfect for girls with long, luscious locks!

## 11. Bow Bun

For a really fun look try a hair bow! In the video above, you’ll see that you can achieve this style whether you have short or long hair! However, you may need to practice a few times before you go out with this style. This look is darling especially if you have bangs! If you’re a teenager, you should certainly try out this style, then your friends are bound to love it!

## 12. Upside down French Braid Bun

This one is definitely one of my favorites! If you're good with braids, I'm confident you can master this style. Even though this look isn't easy for everyone, once you get the hang of it, it can become second nature! Just try not to get too dizzy braiding your hair upside down! Once you master this style, rock it as often as you please; it will look fabulous!

Now that you've seen all of my easy, fabulous, and awesome buns for lazy days, do you have any more to add? What is your favorite hairstyle for a day off?
