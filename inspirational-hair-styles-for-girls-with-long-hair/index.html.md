# 8 Inspirational Hair Styles for Girls with Long Hair ...

If you have long hair, it means you have numerous amazing ways to braid it and style it, so, do not go for the boring old bun and try these styles instead! If you already have a favourite hairstyle for long hair that's not mentioned here, do tell us about it in the comments! Now, let's get to it:

## 1. Double Braid

![Double Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y4/ka/hbp6io4f5701ce05dd9ba974748516_500x500.jpg)

Create two braids on either side of your face and pull them back, securing with a couple of small bobby pins.

## 2. Messy Cuteness

![Messy Cuteness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/pp/t3cpirll5701ce1195392198160028_550x910.jpg)

Pull back the front part of your hair and secure it to make a small loose pony tail.

## 3. Long and Sexy

![Long and Sexy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h4/t2/v1huhz3s5701ce202ae19613153973_500x500.jpg)

Run a light hand through your hair until it looks mildly dishevelled.

## 4. Sun-kissed Locks

![Sun-kissed Locks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j0/yf/ep3rednv5701ce28483c0040359910_486x472.jpg)

Transition your hair from dark to light or vice versa.

## 5. Effortless and Chic

![Effortless and Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/ow/pzcrxhb95701ce306ec35278721030_500x500.jpg)

Take the front part of your hair and make a loose braid at the back of your head.

## 6. Ombre, Baby

![Ombre, Baby](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ca/xy/yzt0zr9o5701ce3c37b51088756527_500x500.jpg)

With an ombre look, gently curl the ends of your hair so the bottom strands lightly overlap each other.

## 7. Front Braid

![Front Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f3/jg/jkiaebu95701ce4c7a9d4561704424_600x600.jpg)

Braid one side of your hair and flip it over your shoulder so it's at the front.

## 8. Relaxed Chic

![Relaxed Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/c1/kh3jynzp5701ce564eb07487345288_456x585.jpg)

Create a loose, relaxed braid around the back of your head over the top of the rest of your hair.
