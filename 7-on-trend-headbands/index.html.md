# 7 on\-Trend Headbands ...

Headbands have been enjoying renewed popularity lately, most likely due to a certain series called Gossip Girl. While bow headbands have enjoyed their time in the spotlight, it’s time to look at other styles and designs. From gem stones to studs, there’s a style of headband to suit a range of fashion aesthetics. Take a look at a few on-trend headbands below.

## 1. Topshop Black Gem Stone Embellished Headband

![Topshop Black Gem Stone Embellished Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bb/x0/bpezby4j54c8fc5201a72584198900_300x435.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Je6IOl)
Gem stones make a great embellishment for an evening look. This black satin headband comes with a gem stone embellished panel. The gem stones range in size and shape. This headband would easily complement a little black dress ensemble.

## 2. Eugenia Kim Feather-Embellished Headband

![Eugenia Kim Feather-Embellished Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ry/b6/jcp7c6ox54c8fc52da240971591945_300x373.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/cG7k9z)
Feather embellishments are on-trend for this season. This Eugenia Kim headband is a refined, sophisticated take on the commonly boho trend. The wide satin headband is covered with black and dark green feather embellishments. If you have dark hair this headband may blend in or add texture to your hairstyle. This headband would really stand out against lighter hair.

## 3. Forever21 Satin Rosette Headband

![Forever21 Satin Rosette Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/wz/hb3ycymq54c8fc53b133a704158966_300x356.jpg)

**Price:** $5.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This headband features three satin roses on a satin wrapped headband. A large rose embellishment sits in between two smaller ones. This headband would make a great statement piece. On-trend headbands such as this rosette one would be well suited for formal occasions or even a day at the races.

## 4. Topshop Chain Headband

![Topshop Chain Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q8/wt/f0yze0tt54c8fc548ed32948857367_300x399.jpg)

**Price:** $25.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/99Ldiz)
On-trend headbands can vary in shape and size. This gold-look chain headband is a simple and chic option. It consists of a flat chain attached to an elastic headband. Metallic accents are suitable for both day and night. Simply pull your hair back into a pony tail or bun and slide this headband on.

## 5. Louis Mariette Jardin Crystal-Embellished Headband

![Louis Mariette Jardin Crystal-Embellished Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r5/kb/fj9pxryg54c8fc556d4ec795963677_300x440.jpg)

**Price:** $300.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oh9idW)
This delicate looking headband just bursts with colour. This headband features crystal flower embellishments atop a silver metal band. It also features a designer logo at the side. A headband such as would be well suited for special occasions. And at that price, it should almost be treated as fine jewellery.

## 6. Forever21 Stud and Satin Headband

![Forever21 Stud and Satin Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/g3/wikbqtro54c8fc56630dd416326510_300x353.jpg)

**Price:** $3.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Stud embellishments make for on-trend headbands. This headband features subtle square studs on a slim satin band. It comes is a sleek grey and silver colourway. This headband would be a great accessory for updating a relaxed, casual outfit.

## 7. Joomi Lim Headband with Single Spikes

_ **Price:** $205.00 at openingceremony.us_
If you’re a fashion risk-taker, and like your accessories to make a statement, then take a look at this spiked headband. It features bright blue spikes attached to a silver metal chain. The end result is a punk-inspired headband. To play down the punk aspect, try pairing it with a more refined, feminine outfit.

Trends are fickle things - they come and go at the speed of light and are not to everyone’s tastes. For now, anyway, which of the above on-trend headbands do you like best?
