# Get Ready to Add Some Serious Glamour to Your Hair ...

There are some simple ways to add some glamour to your hair and with the festive season coming up, it's a great way of making your look shimmer and shine. Here are my favorite ways to add some glamour to your hair for the festive season and beyond.

## 1. Gorgeous Glitter

![Gorgeous Glitter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iy/z7/c2u86bd7_500x700.jpg)

Hair glitter is one of the simple ways to add some glamour to your hair. It depends on whether the event calls for it and you do need to be careful that you don't end up looking like you belong on the top of the christmas tree! Hair glitter can literally make you shimmer and glow during the festive season. You can even make your own hair glitter. Just mix some water, glitter and a little hair spray in a spray bottle and you have your very own way to sparkle for those nights on the town!

## 2. Simple Slides

![Simple Slides](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/er/c5/h15s84vu_570x854.jpg)

Slides are another simple way to add some sparkle to your hair style. There are so many slides on the high street ranging from big, bold and bright to the more subtle ones that can still make an impact. It depends on the event and whether big and bold or more understated pieces are suitable. And of course, it depends on the outfit. If your outfit is simple then you can add some overstated glamour to your hair. However, if your outfit is already bold, you may not want to take the attention away from it with lively hair accessories.

## 3. Fabulous Flowers

Flowers aren't just for hippies in the sixties and they're not just for the spring and summer months. Flowers can look great in the hair at any time of the year. There are some beautiful floral accessories that can make you look super feminine and chic.

## 4. Head Bands

![Head Bands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/pi/gouhar4s_550x719.jpg)

Hair bands can look fabulous too. Whether you have your hair in a pony tail or not, an accessorized hair band can add some much needed glamour to an otherwise plain hair style. Again, they range from the more understated and simple to the bold and beautiful with fabulous embellishments. The choice is yours!

## 5. Scarf Saviors

![Scarf Saviors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oy/db/avpwec0m_500x750.jpg)

Scarves look great in the hair too and are wonderful if you're between washes. There are so many ways you can wear scarves in the hair and if you're having a bad hair day or you're in a rush, a hair scarf can become your best friend. Satin and silk are best for your hair and there are some fabulous tutorials on YouTube if you're a hair scarf novice.

## 6. Pop of Color

![Pop of Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v8/nc/i745qrvr_600x900.jpg)

How about adding a pop of color to your hair. Adding some color can add some interest to an otherwise plain outfit and hair style. There are so many ways to add a pop of color to your hair. For example, if you're blonde, go for a rosy pink ombre and if your hair is a dark brown, navy looks fabulous and alluring. A touch of fuchsia looks beautiful with brown hair. For more ideas on how to add a pop of color to your hair, have a look at the following article on AWS
[hair.allwomenstalk.com](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/stylish-ways-to-add-pops-of-color-to-your-hair/)

## 7. Amazing Accessories

There are so many different types of imaginative hair accessories to choose from. Whether you want to go for something inspired by Greek Goddesses or Egyptian princesses, or something simple like a scrunchie or bulldog hair clip, all you need is a little imagination!

These are just a few of my favorite ways to add glamour to your hair. Can you think of any more ways?
