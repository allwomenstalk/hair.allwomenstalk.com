# Celebrity Hair Transformations \- the Most Dramatic Haircuts ...

Celebrities are always looking to reinvent themselves and it usually starts by cutting their hair. It's true what they say - getting a new hairstyle can easily make you feel like a brand new woman! Here some of the most dramatic celebrity hair transformations:

## 1. Keke Palmer

![Keke Palmer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/uu/da8oanx6_600x400.jpg)

Earlier this year, Keke Palmer debuted a pixie cut at New York Fashion Week. I think she looks super fierce with the new do'!

## 2. Jennifer Hudson

![Jennifer Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lz/hn/lv8ikgyl_600x400.jpg)

Jennifer has been sporting the pixie cut for some time now and during a recent appearance on The Real, J.Hud revealed that it was because she was tired of having to deal with her bangs!

## 3. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pb/rb/al60tc2w_600x400.jpg)

Vanessa is constantly changing up her do' (as documented on her Instagram account). When she started off 2014, her hair was already reaching her lower back! Then, earlier this summer, she cut into an ombre bob.

## 4. Kelly Osbourne

![Kelly Osbourne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o2/uv/miud34ri_600x400.jpg)

It's clear that Kelly's never had a problem with experimenting with her hair. She's had it purple for quite some time (maybe even before it was considered "cool") but earlier this summer, Kelly was sporting a buzzed mohawk!

## 5. Kaley Cuoco

![Kaley Cuoco](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uo/hq/p7kbkre5_600x400.jpg)

Recently, this Big Bang Theory actress decided to get a pixie cut! I love the way that she styles it.

## 6. Whitney Port

![Whitney Port](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/ue/zunjc0sx_600x400.jpg)

Earlier this summer, Whitney was seen sporting a bob cut... much different than her long wavy locks.

## 7. Anne Hathaway

![Anne Hathaway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/is/s2l5lbgf_600x400.jpg)

Anne proved her commitment to Les Miserables when she cut her hair (on camera) for the movie.

## 8. Hayden Panattiere

![Hayden Panattiere](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yu/up/xzs2x81c_600x400.jpg)

In 2010, Hayden went from having long and dark hair to chopping it all off and dying it blonde. She looked great though, right?

## 9. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/w9/zzsenkwx_600x400.jpg)

Jennifer got a lot of attention after her decision to cut her hair. Most people were worried about what she'd do when it came to filming for The Hunger Games but there was an easy fix... a wig!

## 10. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pp/gj/zfy5h4z5_600x400.jpg)

After Emma finished filming the Harry Potter movies, she revamped her look with a pixie cut in 2010. Her hair has grown pretty long since then but I wonder if she'll ever chop it all off again!

## 11. Julianne Hough

![Julianne Hough](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/z4/uhukwf4f_600x400.jpg)

Earlier this year, Julianne cut her blonde tresses into an adorable pixie cut. I think it looks great on her!

## 12. Shailene Woodley

![Shailene Woodley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zr/m4/qi76zab5_600x400.jpg)

Shailene was known for her long and luscious locks but she had no problem chopping it all off for her role in The Fault In Our Stars.

## 13. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lc/au/egtr6y57_600x400.jpg)

With Miley's new music and attitude, came a bold platinum blonde pixie cut. The singer is still wearing her hair short and I have to say, I think she pulls it off fabulously!

Who do you think had the most dramatic transformation?
