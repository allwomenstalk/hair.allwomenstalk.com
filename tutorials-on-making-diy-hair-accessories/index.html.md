# 7 Tutorials on Making DIY Hair Accessories ...

**DIY accessories** are THE most budget-friendly, totally creative way to always rock some pretty awesome hair accessories! Oh, and don’t get me started on that silly rule that says that you can never find a matching bow or a headband when you really and desperately need one! Well, experiment with DIY accessories and all that is going to change! You can even use fabric that’s identical to the one your dress is made of or old jewelry to create matching, stunning and completely unique hairpieces whenever you need them! And here are some pretty cool videos on DIY accessories to help you get started:

## 1. DIY Princess Bow

This totally adorable bow might not be something big girls can wear but it’s going to make your favorite little princess very and I mean VERY happy! This super-cute princess bow looks waay better than a lot of store bought hair accessories, and considering the price of kiddy stuff these days, I’m sure it doesn’t cost as nearly as much! So stock up on various colorful ribbons and your girl could have a new **DIY accessory** whenever she wants!

## 2. Glitzy Bohemian Headband

Next on my list of great [videos to watch](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/must-watch-youtube-videos-for-when-youre-bored/) in order to get some fresh ideas for **DIY accessories** is this one that will show you how to make an awesome, eye-catching headband to wear this summer! It’s easy, it’s fun to do and it’s a very nice way to use rhinestones, flowers or feathers that have either fallen off something or simply left forgotten in your arts and crafts treasure chest.

## 3. Feather Fascinator

Advanced crafters and all of you who don’t mind getting their hands super-busy – this video is going to blow your mind! It’s not exactly easy to do and you will need to visit a few shops to find feathers but it’s going to help you make something really, really extraordinary! Don’t you just LOOOVE fascinators?! I’ve always had a soft spot for them!

## 4. DIY Pill Box Hat

There are many ways to obtain vintage or vintage-looking hairpieces and making them on your own is both budget-friendly and sanitary enough to convince anyone to give it a shot! This video right here will show you how to make one of the most awesome vintage-inspired **DIY accessories** ever – a lady like pill box hat! Enjoy, take notes and do let me know how your hat turned out!

## 5. Feather Head Piece

Those chic feather hairpieces don’t come cheap…unless you make them yourself of course! Heeey, now there’s an idea! Well, I might not be the first one to think of it but I’ll definitely make it easy for you by showing you this super-creative video! And it won’t only show you how to make your own **DIY accessories**, but also inspire you to use everything you can find to get them to look super fabulous!

## 6. DIY Tiara

Yaaay Threadbanger! And yes, I totally agree – you should always be able to feel a little fancy! And whether it’s Halloween or any other event you need a tiara for, there is really no need to spend your money on one! In fact, a bit of creativity, some wire, a pair of cutters and the beads of your choice are all the material you are going to need to make your own, absolutely unique tiara!

## 7. Shabby Chic Lace Flowers

Lace flowers are a perfect thing to add to your boring old bobby pins, plain metal hair clips or other **DIY accessories** such as headbands, hairpieces etc. And this last video I meant to show you today will help you create individual flowers you can later use for your hair, clothes or even scrapbooks. Pretty awesome, isn’t it? Check it out!

Even I feel up for an arts & crafts project after watching these unbelievably creative videos on DIY hair accessories! How about you? Ever made DIY hair accessories before? Well, give them a shot- you might discover that you have a new talent!
