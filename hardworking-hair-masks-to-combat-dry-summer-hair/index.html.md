# 7 Hardworking Hair Masks to Combat Dry Summer Hair ...

If your hair is starting to show signs of dryness and damage from swimming or spending a lot of time out in the sun, it’s time you tried some of these incredible hair masks. Hair masks can help get some moisture back in your locks and breathe some life back into dry, dull hair. There are lots of fab hair masks at various price points so you have lots of options in reviving your hair. If your hair is in need of some extra TLC, don’t wait to pick up one of these helpful hair masks.

## 1. L'orÉAL Everpure Moisture Restorative Mask

![L'orÉAL Everpure Moisture Restorative Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ec/ql/jje7vary_400x400.jpg)

Lots of people color their hair, especially in the summer, so one of the best bargain hair masks that hydrates hair while preventing color fading is L'Oreal's Restorative Mask. L’Oreal’s EverPure line doesn’t contain any sulfates which can cause frizziness in some hair types and weaken hair, so it’s a good all-around choice. This rich hair mask will restore shine and softness and improve the texture of your hair. Available at Ulta for $8.99.

## 2. Garnier Fructis Damage Eraser Strength Reconstructing Butter Hair Mask

![Garnier Fructis Damage Eraser Strength Reconstructing Butter Hair Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m8/jx/k5fami22_410x368.jpg)

Rejuvenate your hair with Garnier’s Damage Eraser. This hair mask contains plant based proteins, active fruit concentrate and cupuaçu butter to offer an intense conditioning treatment that’s said to reconstruct 90% of your hair’s strength, in addition to providing 3 times the damage resistance. As a bonus, Garnier Fructis products pretty much all smell heavenly so your hair will be soft, fragrant and healthy! Available at Walmart for $4.97.

## 3. Macadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masque

![Macadamia Natural Oil Deep Repair Masque](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/tg/mxssynbe_400x394.jpg)

This particular hair mask is a holy grail product for a lot of people. It has a great scent and it’s thick and creamy so it feels like it’s really penetrating your hair. Formulated with algae extracts along with macadamia, argan, chamomile and tea tree oil, your hair will be deeply strengthened and nourished with lots of softness and shine. Available at Ulta for $33.

## 4. Kiehl’s Olive Fruit Oil Deeply Repairative Hair Pak

![Kiehl’s Olive Fruit Oil Deeply Repairative Hair Pak](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/kr/b87zeg20_600x504.jpg)

Olive fruit and Avocaodo oils work together to bring you a deeply repairative hair pak. Perfect for dry, damaged hair from the sun, heat styling and chemical processing; Kiehl’s hair mask will strengthen, moisturize and restore hair. With continued use, this product will protect your hair from environmental stressors and future damage. Available at Kiehls.com for $25.

## 5. Alterna Bamboo UV+ Color Protection Rehab Deep Hydration Masque

![Alterna Bamboo UV+ Color Protection Rehab Deep Hydration Masque](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ts/ju/gn9s5bye_450x352.jpg)

We spend a lot of money getting our hair colored, so it would be a shame to see it fade away just because we didn’t take the proper precautions. Alterna’s Deep Hydration Masque contains organic bamboo extract, Kalahari melon extract and Color Hold UV+ technology to hydrate, nourish and protect hair from sun and oxidative damage. Available at Sephora for $26.

## 6. Suave Professionals Moroccan Infusion Deep-Conditioning Shine Mask

![Suave Professionals Moroccan Infusion Deep-Conditioning Shine Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ec/mn/xft0ik8x_500x499.jpg)

You might be thinking a bargain brand like Suave won’t have any good quality products, but their Deep Conditioning Shine Mask is highly rated for its shine-enhancing, smoothing and deeply moisturizing properties. The Moroccan Argan oil conditions hair so it’s touchably soft, moisturized and noticeably healthier looking. Available at Walmart for $2.88.

## 7. Living Proof Restore Mask Treatment

![Living Proof Restore Mask Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f5/zg/dvfn1k2v_450x300.jpg)

This lightweight hair treatment provides intense hydration and increases your hair’s resistance up to 9 times after 5 uses! This hair mask is said to heal dry, damaged hair that’s porous and often misbehaves back to healthy, more manageable, virgin-like hair. Available at Sephora for $42.

Spend the rest of your summer with envy-worthy, gorgeous hair. Just because you have fun in the sun and sand, doesn’t mean your hair has to take a beating. With these beneficial hair masks, you can enjoy summer activities and still have the beautiful, silky strands you desire!
