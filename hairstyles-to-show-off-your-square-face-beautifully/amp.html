1 Body Perm

Via 19 Styles To Choose From ...
This type of perm looks best on people with a square shaped face and shoulder length hair, as it adds a lot of volume.

***

A body perm is an excellent choice for those who desire voluminous waves that flow effortlessly down their shoulders. It's especially flattering for square faces, as the added texture softens the angular jawline and enhances the cheekbones. Ideal for medium-length locks, this style balance gives a romantic and feminine charm, making it perfect for various occasions, from casual outings to more formal events. With the right care, a body perm can give your hair a bouncy and full-bodied look that's both beautiful and manageable.
