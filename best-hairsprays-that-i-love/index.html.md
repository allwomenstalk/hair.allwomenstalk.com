# 7 Best Hairsprays That I Love ...

Best Hairspray can be hard to find, especially if you aren't an avid user of hairspray. Recently, I have accepted my curly hair (it's been hard, but worth it) and I have went on a rapid buying trip to find the best hairspray to keep my curls in check. That inspired me to make a list of the best hairspray brands that I have used so that you won't have to waste your money (like I did) on a hairspray that doesn't keep your curls in check or that expensive do up!

## 1. Fructis Style Volumizing anti-Humidity Hairspray

![Fructis Style Volumizing anti-Humidity Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/kg/sjlty02u54c92f6b114cc492655443_400x400.jpg)

**Price:** $3.33 at [walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I am a huge, huge fan of volume. If I am going to have curly hair, I want it to be big and I want it to have a lot of volume. That is what lead me to this little gem! Not only does it protect my curls from humidity but it's actually a great hairspray for anyone looking for a little volume and lift in their hair!

## 2. Herbal Essences Set Me up Max Hold

![Herbal Essences Set Me up Max Hold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/y6/v15vv3wb54c92f6bdd0bf381537579_400x400.jpg)

**Price:** $2.97 at [walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Okay, I have to admit that I put this on the best hairspray list because of the smell. It smells SO good and I actually have this sitting in my bathroom right now. It will hold your hair, don't get me wrong on that but you'll fall in love with the smell before you fall in love with what it can do for your hair!

## 3. Dove Extra Hold W/Natural Movement Hairspray

![Dove Extra Hold W/Natural Movement Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ed/bu/dwr687qa54c92f6cd1283605159060_400x397.jpg)

**Price:** $6.24 at [walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
One of the main reasons that I like this one is because it doesn't leave my hair sticky and unmoveable. The spray is flexible and it's a great way to make sure that my curls stay in check – but are not rock hard. Just a couple spritzs ladies and I promise, it'll make all of the difference – plus is smells good!

## 4. Pantene Pro-V Classic Style Extra Strong Hold Unforgettable Hairspray

![Pantene Pro-V Classic Style Extra Strong Hold Unforgettable Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vf/nw/ffoc6lqt54c92f6d9f33b301234776_400x398.jpg)

**Price:** $3.97 at [walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I actually am guilty of loving anything Pantene. I have used it for years and years and while I never got the hairspray until now, I was bound to like it before I brought it. This hairspray is just classic, it is a little flexible and a little sticky at the same time. I like it because it is a simple and no frills hairspray. You looking for something that works? This is it!

## 5. Herbal Essences Body Envy Volumizing Hairspray

![Herbal Essences Body Envy Volumizing Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yb/i6/krp4g3az54c92f6e5d19d463143970_400x400.jpg)

**Price:** $2.97 at [walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I mentioned that I love volume right? Well, this is my second choice of volumizing hairspray. I love it because again, it smells awesome (everything that Herbal Essences does!) but I use it because it works. It is a little sticky and does make your hair a little stiff, but when I want real curls and I want them to stick and stay in place, this is the hairspray for you!

## 6. Herbal Essences: Tousle Me Softly Violet Splash Hairspray

![Herbal Essences: Tousle Me Softly Violet Splash Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fd/ii/qbgh28o554c92f6f31ec8385953579_400x400.jpg)

**Price:** $2.97 at [walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
So the Tousle Me Softly collection as a whole is one of the best that I have used, but when you start with the shampoo and finish with the hairspray, you'll fall in love. Not only it is light, but honestly – just little bit of his best hairspray goes a very long way. Definitely recommend it!

## 7. John Frieda Luxurious Volume All-out Hold Hairspray

![John Frieda Luxurious Volume All-out Hold Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/xt/km1umg3c54c92f702dc13227442649_400x384.jpg)

**Price:** $6.49 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I think that I have tried every single John Freida product out there and while I might not use the shampoo or conditioner anymore, the hairspray is still a staple. Not only does it provide a ton of volume to my hair, but it doesn't matter the weather, my hair stays in check! This is definitely the best hairspray for the fashionista that wants perfect hair all of the time!

Trust me, I have went through a ton of different bad hairsprays to get to the best hairspray. I love each and every best hairspray on this list but for all different reasons. What ones have you ladies tried – do you think I missed some? Tell me a little bit about it!
