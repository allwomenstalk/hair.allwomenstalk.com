# Beautiful Brunettes Should Try These Hair Styling Rules ...

If you’re a brunette, you probably want to enhance the great color of your hair, while also keeping it healthy and lush at the same time. Turns out there are some things you can do for your mane that will make you the most gorgeous brunette in town. Add these rules to your hair styling routine and you’ll have the best hair you’ve ever had. These rules are easy to follow and should give you the results you want in no time at all.

## 1. Choose Products That Are Specialized for Brunettes

![Choose Products That Are Specialized for Brunettes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jw/mv/y6q85mgn575201cf11826133003543_600x600.jpg)

Whether you’re a natural brunette or you have some help getting the color you love, choose products that are designed for that hair color and it will look gorgeous every single day. Choose products that enhance the lovely brown color you have and use them on a regular basis. Experts also suggest limiting the amount of time you use heat stylers, which can mess with your color.

## 2. Get the Right Hair Cut

![Get the Right Hair Cut](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/fd/j1jzdb6d5751fe2e8ff41260303163_600x600.jpg)

Your hair color can look better or worse depending on your hair cut. Surprised? I was too. However, hair experts say that girls with a lighter and softer shade of brown look great with a shorter cut. On the other hand, girls with darker brown hair should grow their strands out a bit longer. What shade are you? Do you have the right hair cut?

## 3. Highlights Are Perfect

![Highlights Are Perfect](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ib/qe/p0x72id95751feff8fddd220505743_500x500.jpg)

Highlights add dimension and interest to your head of brunette hair so don’t be afraid to give them a try. Have highlights added around your face to create a framing look that is trendy and pretty at the same time. You can also get highlights all over your hair to help add some visual appeal and give your hair some depth.

## 4. Try a Gloss Rather than a Color

![Try a Gloss Rather than a Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/zo/uu62vdvw5752007b375f7167249352_500x500.jpg)

If you don’t want to get full highlights, or any highlights for that matter, consider a glaze instead. Experts say that this can give your brunette strands the lift you’re after without the commitment of a coloring treatment. The glaze will brighten and boost your hair color, but won’t fade out like highlights will, leaving your roots showing. Talk to your stylist about the right glaze for your hair.

## 5. Use Hydrating Products on a Regular Basis

![Use Hydrating Products on a Regular Basis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a6/i1/ccio9zkn5751ffe296b03807161585_500x500.jpg)

In addition to using hair care products for brunettes, you should also use those that hydrate your strands. Hydrated hair is lush, smooth and shiny so you definitely want to make sure you keep it healthy. On the other hand, dried out brunette hair won’t look that great. Look for products that are designed to add moisture back into your hair so that it always looks great.

## 6. Use SPF Products to Preserve Your Color

![Use SPF Products to Preserve Your Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o3/w2/fjingk5a5752004369144041560750_600x600.jpg)

Whether it’s natural or not, the sun can really wash out brunette hair, leaving you with an icky orange or brassy color. That’s why you should use SPF products to protect your hair and keep the color looking fabulous. You can find these products at drugstores and will be so glad you added them to your hair styling routine.

## 7. Consider Your Makeup

![Consider Your Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/nl/h58mb5jr5751fd991bdb0955656654_540x540.jpg)

Your hair color plays a role in what types of makeup you should choose. You want those that blend with your skin tone as well. This may require a bit of experimentation and practice, but finding the right color of makeup for you will totally enhance your lovely brown hair and get you loads of compliments at the same time.

Are you a natural brunette or not? What other hair tips can you share?
