# 10 Cool Fall Fashion Week Hair Styles ...

Fall fashion week hairstyles are a great source of inspiration for your new season hairdo. Right now, the spring/summer fashion week season is wrapping up, but that doesn’t mean you can’t use them to inspire your current fall looks. While the clothes from the current fashion weeks won’t be available for some time, the hair and beauty looks are achievable right now! Stay ahead of the trends with these examples of fall style fashion week hair.

## 1. Beehives at Moschino

![Beehives at Moschino](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/1_beehives-at-moschino_554x831.jpg)

All things sixties inspired are trending right now, even the hairstyles! When it comes to fall fashion week hairstyles, try rocking a quirky beehive to add some retro flair to your new season look.

## 2. Glitter Dipped Hair at Markus Lupfer

![Glitter Dipped Hair at Markus Lupfer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/2_glitter-dipped-hair-at-markus-lupfer_554x832.jpg)

How many times can you reinvent ombré hair? At London fashion week, Markus Lupfer took this hair trend to the next level with glitter dipped ponytails! Are you daring enough to try this disco look this fall?

## 3. Skinny Braids at Marchesa

![Skinny Braids at Marchesa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/3_skinny-braids-at-marchesa_554x832.jpg)

Braids are forever a favourite when it comes to fall fashion week hairstyles. This fall, take inspiration from the latest fashion week runways and try skinny braids looped around your head for a part Renaissance, part warrior princess look.

## 4. Faux Hawks at Clover Canyon

![Faux Hawks at Clover Canyon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/4_faux-hawks-at-clover-canyon_333x500.jpg)

For a seriously slick ‘do, you can’t go past the faux hawk ponytails at Clover Canyon during New York fashion week. It was a clever and stylish way of updating the basic ponytail.

## 5. Turbans at Marc by Marc Jacobs

![Turbans at Marc by Marc Jacobs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/5_turbans-at-marc-by-marc-jacobs_400x600.jpg)

This fall, tie your hair up in a bun and accessorise with a patterned turban-style headscarf. Instead of the summery hues on the Marc by Marc Jacobs runways, try rich reds and golden yellows for a more appropriate fall style fashion week hair.

## 6. Neat Ponytails at Jil Sander

![Neat Ponytails at Jil Sander](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/6_neat-ponytails-at-jil-sander_600x900.jpg)

Neat, pulled back ponytails are a firm favourite not only for spring/summer but for fall as well. It’s an easy look that you can recreate in the comfort of your own home, yet can transform your look into something more high fashion.

## 7. Banded Hair at Michael Kors

![Banded Hair at Michael Kors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/7_banded-hair-at-michael-kors_333x500.jpg)

This hairstyle seen at Michael Kors is a cool and contemporary take on the ponytail. It’s the perfect example of fall style fashion week hair. Simply keep sleek, straightened hair in place with a wide barrette at the nape of the neck.

## 8. Slick Side Parts at Rodarte

![Slick Side Parts at Rodarte](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/8_slick-side-parts-at-rodarte_333x500.jpg)

Rodarte took side parts to the extreme this season. You could almost say they were more comb over than side part. However, the deep side part ruled the runways and less extreme versions were spotted at Altuzarra and Michael Kors.

## 9. Messy Knots at Marni

![Messy Knots at Marni](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/9_messy-knots-at-marni_600x900.jpg)

Knotted buns were another popular hairstyle trend spotted during the recent fashion weeks. At Marni, the buns were knotted and messy – perfect for creating a casual, off-duty look. The Row and Bottega Veneta also favoured the messy chignon this season.

## 10. Contrasting Textures at Monique Lhuillier

![Contrasting Textures at Monique Lhuillier](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/10/10_contrasting-textures-at-monique-lhuillier_333x500.jpg)

Are you indecisive when it comes to your hair? Can’t decide whether to pick up the flat iron or curling iron? Then these fall fashion week hairstyles are for you. Keep the roots straight and work in some blended waves towards the ends.

New York, London, Milan, Paris - there are plenty of spring/summer fashion week hairstyles that can easily work with your current fall looks. Spring, summer or fall fashion week hairstyles are all the perfect motivation to try a new ‘do. What was your favourite fall style fashion week hair?
