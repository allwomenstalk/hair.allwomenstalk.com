# 7 Signs Its Time for a Hair Makeover ...

The signs it's time for a hair makeover are different for everyone, but the below reasons are pretty good indicators. I don’t know about you, but I feel so beautiful after a fresh haircut and color. Sometimes a great haircut is the best thing for your self esteem and confidence. These are my signs it's time for a hair makeover.

## 1. You Have Lots of Split Ends

![You Have Lots of Split Ends](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10205728_5318_600x883.jpg)

Every time I see I have some split ends, it’s the surest of all the signs it’s time for a hair makeover. Rather than just getting the same old trim, I try and get something new to add some flair and fun. This is a great time to switch it up, but if you’re afraid of that, just get maybe a couple more inches off and go for a shorter 'do!

## 2. Your Hair Has No Volume

![Your Hair Has No Volume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10210723_5895_467x700.jpg)

If you find your hair growing out and losing all its volume, this is a sign you might want to try a new style. If you have longer hair, try getting more layers and a good trim. This will help bring the volume back and make it easier to blow dry.

## 3. Your Highlights Are More Noticeable than Your Main Color

![Your Highlights Are More Noticeable than Your Main Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10210732_2609_469x699.jpg)

This is a sign that your highlights have become too heavy and unattractive. Try going back to a single process to cover the streaks and lay off new highlights for a while. Not only will this bring your hair back to being healthy, but some of the lighter bits will naturally show through.

## 4. Your Clothes Have Evolved

![Your Clothes Have Evolved](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10210811_1279_500x750.jpg)

Make sure if you are dressing a new way or evolving your wardrobe, that your hair keeps up. Altering your clothing style is the perfect excuse to update your 'do. This way, your new look will work from head to toe. If you have lost weight, you might want to go for a sleeker look that accentuates your face.

## 5. You Broke up with Your Boyfriend

![You Broke up with Your Boyfriend](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10210840_0236_400x483.jpg)

Don’t fret girl, there are many many guys that would love to be with you! Show your ex that with a brand new hair 'do and stylish color. This is a great time to go into the new world of being single and show him what he’s missing. Also, it’s a great way to regain confidence and get back to feeling great about you.

## 6. You Are Always Putting Your Hair up

![You Are Always Putting Your Hair up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10210902_6038_553x805.jpg)

Unless of course you love your hair up, it's time for a new hairstyle. If you find yourself not having time to do your hair, it's not the right style for you. Try going for an easier to manage style that is simple and stylish.

## 7. You’re Missing the Compliments

![You’re Missing the Compliments](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10210912_9853_467x700.jpg)

If you have always had amazing hair and people always complimented you, and now they don’t, it's time for a new style. Look at a new style and get inspired to change your hair and get that confidence back. Even if it takes just a couple tweaks, like shorter bangs or shorter layers, make sure you do it for you, and love it!

Whatever your hair style is, make sure you work it! If you want to buzz half your head, do it and love it. To each their own ladies! What is your go to hairstyle? Are you into long or short hair?
