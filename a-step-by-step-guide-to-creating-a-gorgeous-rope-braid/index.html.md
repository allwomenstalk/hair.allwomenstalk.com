# A Step\-by\-Step Guide to Creating a Gorgeous Rope Braid ...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/ke/vic6f71y565c54eab8569330432949_600x647.jpg)

If you want to braid your hair in a new way, try creating a rope braid. [Self](https://www.self.com/story/rope-braid-tutorial) can show you how to do it. Just follow their advice:

## 1. Prep the Hair with a Body-boosting Product

![Prep the Hair with a Body-boosting Product](https://img.allw.mn/content/en/l2/gqm9ghaw565c54ecf15be952230618_480x321.gif)

After you do that, make sure you blowdry your hair until it's straight.

## 2. Gather Strands into a High Ponytail

![Gather Strands into a High Ponytail](https://img.allw.mn/content/lm/jr/gk1oct9a565c54ead7c97322014223_500x214.gif)

This is the easiest part!

## 3. Divide the Pony into Two Sections

![Divide the Pony into Two Sections](https://img.allw.mn/content/xo/oc/dyaufi1s565c5565c16f7228025047_800x450.gif)

Make sure that both sides have an equal amount of hair.

## 4. Wind the Strands into a Rope Twist

![Wind the Strands into a Rope Twist](https://img.allw.mn/content/sx/p4/wbaeqs6r565c54ec1bc27382694260_768x384.gif)

"Tightly twist each individual section using a clockwise twirling motion. Then twist the two sections around each other, moving in the opposite direction. Secure with an elastic; smooth ends with styling cream."
