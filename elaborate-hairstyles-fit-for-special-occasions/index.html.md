# Glamorous and Elaborate Hairstyles for Girls Having a Special Occasion ...

Maybe you're making your prom preparations early. Maybe you have a wedding to attend. Maybe you're just going on a special anniversary date. It doesn't matter what you're prepping for, because these elaborate hairstyles are perfect for any special occasion:

## 1. Braided Updo

You should begin prepping this hairstyle the night before you're going to wear it by sleeping in large curlers. When you wake up, you should remove the curlers and start creating a [dutch braid](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/awesome-video-tutorials-on-dutch-braids/) on the side of your head. Then you can tease your hair, pull it back, and create a unique type of loose bun. It's a bit difficult to create this look, so make sure you listen closely to each step of this tutorial.

## 2. Elaborate Bun

This is another updo that's fit for special occasions, but it's a little easier to create than the first style. You should start by creating a lace braid and then add in some curls, secure all of your hair into a ponytail, backcomb your hair to create texture, and finally twist your ponytail to create your bun.

## 3. Evening Updo

For this style, you're going to leave out a piece of hair on your left and your right sides, and then make a ponytail with the rest of your hair. Then you should secure a hair donut to your hair and place your ponytail over it. Tuck away any stray pieces and secure them with bobby pins. Then you should crisscross the two pieces of hair you left down earlier in order to complete the look.

## 4. Big Bouffant

This is another hairstyle that requires a hair donut. First, you're going to create a high ponytail and slide it through the donut. Then you're going to wrap strands of hair around the donut and secure them with bobby pins. Do that over and over again until you have your finished product.

## 5. Twisted Curls

This is one of the easiest styles to create that looks elaborate. All you really have to do is take different sections of your hair and twist them together. Then you should tug on the twisted pieces to make them appear thicker, roll the piece up, and pin it onto the top of your head. You'll do that several times until the hairstyle is complete.

## 6. Half-up Half-down Hairdo

Like most of the other styles, you should begin by curling your hair in one way or another. You can either leave in curlers overnight or use a curling iron in the morning. Once you're finished with the curls, you should create a small braid on both sides of your head and then pin them together in the back. Once you're done, you'll have a beautiful hairstyle that looks fancy and fun.

## 7. Bun for Short Hair

If you have short hair, it can be hard to create elaborate hairstyles, because you don't have too much to work with. If that's been a problem for you in the past, this tutorial is perfect for you. It'll show you how to create a bun that is meant specifically for ladies with shoulder length hair.

During big events, you want to look your best. That's why you should use these elaborate hairstyles to impress everyone you see during that special day. What's your favorite way to wear your hair for a special occasion?
