# Take Some Fall Hair Color Inspiration from These Celebs!

Celebrity hair colorist Marie Robinson (whose clients include Emma Stone, Michelle Williams, Scarlett Johansson, Rose Byrne, etc) shared the top 3 autumn hair colors with E's Fabulist! Check them out and see which celebs are rocking these fab colors:

## 1. Platinum Blonde

![Platinum Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xc/x3/fcdmkkr1_600x600.jpg)

Although people tend to go lighter in the warmer seasons, it's becoming quite the trend for the fall! Stars like Kaley Cuoco-Sweeting, Lauren Conrad, and Glee's Dianna Agron have all recently dyed their hair a platinum shade. "Platinum color can look elegant or edgy depending on how you wear it," says Robinson. "It's a lot of upkeep but it's totally worth it."

## 2. Strawberry Blonde

![Strawberry Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/lh/wro0bce5_600x600.jpg)

Strawberry blonde leaves your hair with a super vibrant tint! I prefer this color over a deep red shade, but both look fabulous. Jessica Chastain and Emma Stone have both perfected this look.

## 3. Dark Chocolate

![Dark Chocolate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zw/c1/ol44r77n_600x588.jpg)

Big-name stars like Kerry Washington, Kim Kardashian, and Lea Michele have opted for dark hues. The trend calls for "rich chocolate brown roots and mid-length with slightly softer golden tones on the ends and around the face," says Robinson.
