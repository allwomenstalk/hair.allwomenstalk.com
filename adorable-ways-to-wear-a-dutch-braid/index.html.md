# 26 Adorable Ways to Wear a Dutch Braid ...

When you have those days when your hair just doesn't want to cooperate or you're short on time but still want to look cute, a braid is an easy solution. Braids are sporty but chic, perfect for busy days, and you get gorgeous waves when you take your braid out. Fishtails braids are popping out all over blogs and Insta but I implore you to try the understated yet equally stylish Dutch braid. Check out some of the fun ways to wear it below!
