# Mermaid Hair Tutorials for Beach Babes ...

If you’re going to be hanging out at the beach, you might as well look like a mermaid while you’re there. Mermaid hair is a thing and it’s not all that hard to master either. If you want the perfect hairstyle for your time in the sun and surf, you are going to love this. It’s easier to get the look down pat when you can watch someone else do it so check out any or all of these videos and you’ll have the best hair at the beach.

## 1. Total Color Transformation

Nothing is better than the perfect color for your mermaid style. This purple and pink color combination is wonderful and is surprisingly easy to master. You’ll get step by step directions for copying the look in your own hair. But hey, you could always use it as inspiration and have a pro do the coloring. Beach days just got a whole lot more colorful!

## 2. The Mermaid Braid Combo

You are going to get a total kick out of watching this tutorial. It’s fun to see and you’ll wind up with a braid that’s perfect for keeping you stylish and cooled off while you’re lying in the sunshine. The girls in the video might be young, but that doesn’t mean you can’t learn to copy a perfect mermaid braid from them. Have fun!

## 3. Curly Mermaid Hair

This is the video I need since my hair is super curly. Curly hair doesn’t mean you’re excluded from the mermaid look. In fact, it might make it easier for you to get right since you already have the texture thing going for you. This tutorial will show you how to get the curl just right and then add the mermaid style for a day of fun in the sun.

## 4. Perfect Mermaid Waves

Part of the allure of mermaid hair is the natural wavy look you get from hanging out in the salt water. If you have straight hair, you’ll have to mimic those waves to get the look. Fortunately, this tutorial makes it super easy to get those waves of perfection on your own head. It’s easy to do and will totally change the way you look at the beach and will make anything you’re doing this summer even more fun.

## 5. Overnight Beach Hair without Heat

You love mermaid hair, but you hate what heat styling does to your strands. That’s why you need this video to teach you how to get epic [mermaid hair](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/mesmerizing-photos-of-mermaid-hair/) without any heat at all. You’ll start before you go to bed, sleep peacefully all night long, then wake up to a head of glorious beach waves that rival any mermaid in the sea.

## 6. DIY Sea Salt Spray for Mermaid Hair

Like I said before, salt water really helps make your hair do what you want it to do so this tutorial for making your own salt spray is invaluable. It’s perfect for when you want mermaid waves for something other than a day at the beach, but it’s also great for getting your perfect style in place before you even hit the sand. Yay!

## 7. Twisted Mermaid Braid

Here’s something you’re going to love! It’s a braid that keeps your hair off your neck so you aren’t all hot and sweaty, but it’s also the perfect choice if you want to show off your inner mermaid. Easy directions gets you ready in no time at all. Get ready to turn some heads!

Which one are you going to watch first? Do you want colored mermaid hair or a natural look?
