# 15 Positively Effective Ways to Make Your Hair Grow Faster ...

Ways to make your hair grow faster really do exist, and they don't involve buying magical miracle products out of the back of fashion magazines! However, although there are some great ways to make your hair grow faster, it does take time. Keep in mind, making your hair grow faster can be a complicated process, but these ways will put you on the right track. As women we often change our minds about how we want our hair. Once we cut it short, we can’t wait to have it long again, hence the reason we are often left pondering ways to make our hair grow faster.

## 1. Keep up with Trims

![Keep up with Trims](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/28133307_7312_415x560.jpg)

It may seem a little backwards to keep cutting when you want to make your hair get longer, but frequent cuts are actually one of the best ways to make your hair grow faster. Trims help keep the hair healthy and eliminate split ends caused from heat damage. Split ends can stunt the growth of your hair, so keeping the ends trimmed is good.

## 2. Hot Oil Scalp Massages

![Hot Oil Scalp Massages](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/28133307_4051_372x560.jpg)

Some believe that hot oil scalp massages will make your hair grow faster. I’m not completely sold on the idea, but it sounds so relaxing. Even if it doesn’t increase the growth of the hair it will help keep the hair healthy. Healthy hair generally does grow much faster.

## 3. Egg Whites

![Egg Whites](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13004218_0962_600x598.jpg)

Many of the best ways to make your hair grow faster can be done right at home -- like this method. Whisk together the whites of 3 eggs. Massage the egg whites into the hair and let it sit for 5 to 10 minutes. The nutritional vitamins of the egg whites will seep into the hair, making it stronger and healthier. Having strong healthy vitamin rich hair is the trick to making your hair grow faster.

## 4. Drink Water

![Drink Water](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13004305_6859_500x700.jpg)

Water makes everything grow! It is a quick and natural method that will make your short hair grow and become fuller a lot faster. In the event you drink about 8 cups of water a day, not only will your body feel better, but your hair will get stronger too. The stronger the hair, the faster that it grows.

## 5. Limit Blow Drying

![Limit Blow Drying](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13004349_8593_400x600.jpg)

Sometimes the best ways to make your hair grow faster are to avoid certain things. If you can’t go a day without blow drying your hair, at least try to let it dry as much as possible on its own. Too much heat exposure can damage and slow the growth of your hair. Blow drying your hair less will make your hair grow faster.

## 6. Eat Protein

![Eat Protein](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/28133307_2461_373x560.jpg)

Protein benefits your hair. It provides your hair with important nutrients to grow. Eating a protein rich diet will help your hair to grow faster. Fill up on lean red meats, chicken and fish, nuts, dairy and eggs to get plenty of protein.

## 7. Vitamin B

![Vitamin B](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13004628_4380_525x700.jpg)

Eating the right foods is actually one of the best ways to make your hair grow faster. Your hair needs vitamin B to grow strong and healthy. Without it your hair will feel dry and brittle, and it will break easily. Make sure your multivitamin has a good amount of vitamin B. You can also get it from eating bananas and peppers.

## 8. Avoid Frequent Washes

![Avoid Frequent Washes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13004743_5309_600x720.jpg)

There is no actual need to wash your hair every day, unless it is absolutely necessary. You hair is basically fiber and the more you wash it and apply shampoo, the more unhealthy it can become. Think of your hair as wool fiber, the more times you wash it, the worse it appears. So if you want to grow your hair, stop getting rid of your natural oils!

## 9. Take Supplements

![Take Supplements](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13004811_7980_600x843.jpg)

Your inner health can always be seen on your exterior no matter how much you try to hide it. If you have a complete imbalance on the inside: your hair will loose its shine, your nails will become weaker and you skin might appear worse than before. Therefore in order to take care of your appearance, first you have to start your reparations on the inside. Many people with thinning and breaking hair possibly have biotin deficiency, which is why it would be important to introduce biotin supplements to your daily routine.

## 10. Avoid Stress, Promote Relaxation

![Avoid Stress, Promote Relaxation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13004825_4179_600x804.jpg)

Don't be surprised to see chunks of your hair falling out after weeks of stress because there is a definite correlation between the two factors. Stress can demand a lot from you physically and mentally, which is why you often observe a temporary case of hair loss. In order to protect your hair, avoid stress and find stress-relieving techniques that work best for you.

## 11. Say Goodbye to Tight Ponytails

![Say Goodbye to Tight Ponytails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13005056_8103_600x900.jpg)

Do you ever come home, take your hair out of a ponytail and ask yourself: How did I spent the whole day wearing my hair that tight? You are not alone! Tight ponytails can deter hair growth by creating tensions in your hair causing it to break and appear thin. For your hair to grow to its optimal length, avoid wearing hair styles that pull on your hair and instead use fabric ties instead of harsh hair bands.

## 12. Exercise

![Exercise](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13005116_8164_600x802.jpg)

The best way to stimulate your hair follicles and make your hair grow is by increasing your blood circulation. One of the most effective ways to do that is by exercising! The more blood flow your scalp receives that faster and thicker your hair will grow! Anything that promotes your health, promotes a great appearance!

## 13. Deep Condition

![Deep Condition](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13005519_3845_600x750.jpg)

The more hydrated your hair is the less likely it is to split and break, which is why it is important to take an extra minute in your shower and nourish your hair! Conditioning will help you fight split ends and avoid tangles which can have detrimental breakage effects on your hair.

## 14. Use a Wide-toothed Comb

![Use a Wide-toothed Comb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13005607_0466_600x799.jpg)

If you have thin and easily tangled hair, it is important to purchase a wide-toothed comb. You don't want to be pulling on your hair and harshly brushing through tangles because that can break the hair you have been growing out for so long. Wide-toothed combs enable you to safely brush through your knots with minimal pulling and tugging.

## 15. Nourish Dry Ends

![Nourish Dry Ends](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/13005635_1636_592x888.jpg)

Aside from conditioning, it is important to give extra hydration to the driest parts of your hair, which are the tips. Find the best moisturizing butters or oils that suit your needs the most and generously apply it to the tips religiously. Not only will you notice how easy it is to move your fingers through your hair but you will easily promote hair growth!

There are lots of ways to make your hair grow faster, and many of them don't require that much effort. Some of the most effective ways to make your hair grow faster are completely natural. That's always good. Please let me know if you have any tricks besides these ways to make your hair grow faster that have worked for you. I always love to hear advice from others.
