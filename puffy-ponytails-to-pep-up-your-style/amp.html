1 Double Ponytail

Via Double Ponytail | Promise Tangeman
Doubling up seriously increases the volume of your ponytail.

***

The double ponytail is a great way to add volume and texture to your hairstyle. It’s a simple look that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To achieve the double ponytail look, start by gathering your hair into two sections at the crown of your head. Secure each ponytail with an elastic band. Then, use a teasing comb to add volume to the roots of your hair. This will help create the illusion of a fuller ponytail. Next, wrap a strand of hair around the elastic band of each ponytail. This will help hide the elastic and give the look a more polished finish. To add even more volume, use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair. Finally, use a light-hold hairspray to keep your look in place. With the double ponytail, you can create a look that’s perfect for a night out or a day at the office. The double ponytail is an easy way to add volume and texture to your hairstyle without too much effort.
