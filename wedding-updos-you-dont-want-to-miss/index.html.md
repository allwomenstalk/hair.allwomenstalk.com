# 7 Wedding Updos You Dont Want to Miss ...

It's wedding season, y'all, and with that comes amazing and beautiful wedding updos! Wedding updos are so important and these styles are very in this season – and you won't want to miss them! If you are a bride and you're still deciding exactly what updo you want, take a look below so that you can see exactly what is on-trend, what styles you should watch out for and what styles you should be aware of!

## 1. Retro Curls

![Retro Curls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/1_retro-curls_600x890.jpg)

Oh, this style is oh-so-'20s and oh-so-chic! This is one of those wedding updos that I would love to have for my wedding day. It's all about the pin curls in this particular style and they look absolutely amazing! If you are having a '20s themed wedding and are looking for a hairstyle that will fit right in, this is it! It's so Great Gatsby!

## 2. Flowers & Twists

![Flowers & Twists](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/2_flowers-twists_600x906.jpg)

If you are looking for a wedding updo that is super sweet, totally stylish and is oh-so-cute, this is the updo for you. It's all about the twists, the braids, the flowers and the sweetness and innocence of it. It's a beautiful updo that is perfect for a garden wedding, or a wedding on a farm or in a backyard!

## 3. Wedding Bow

![Wedding Bow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/3_wedding-bow_600x955.jpg)

Your wedding updo should be something that is a little unique and something that is totally different. That's what I thought of when I saw this updo. I think that it is so unique, so different and it's got some bling to it! It's the ideal updo for a bride that is getting married in the city and looking for something all her own!

## 4. Braided Updo

![Braided Updo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/4_braided-updo_458x600.jpg)

Braids are something that are huge this season, especially with The Hunger Games out now. If you are doing a wedding that is a little more laid back and you are looking for something that is relaxed but still complex, this is the updo for you! I love braids and I think that this could work for short or longer hair!

## 5. The Puff

![The Puff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/5_the-puff_455x583.jpg)

Oh, I think that this little updo would look ideal for any Manhattan bride that is really looking to up the teasing on their hair and really make the volume stand up. I love the puff not just in the front, but also in the back. The addition of flowers? Amazing! Give this look a try, girls!

## 6. The Bun

![The Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/6_the-bun_600x812.jpg)

I know that it can be hard to imagine a bun for your wedding day, but if done right, a bun can actually look pretty amazing. You can also add in some embellishments, such as some jewels and gems along with some flowers. Personally, I think a bun is a no hassle way to do your hair for your wedding!

## 7. Swirls

![Swirls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/7_swirls_540x813.jpg)

Finally, the last wedding updo that we're going to talk about is all about the twists and swirls! I love this look because not only is it simple, but it has some level of drama and some level of complexity as well. It's an amazing looking updo and it could go for any type of wedding!

So girls, these are just a few of the wedding hairstyles and updos that are out today and that are hitting the scenes everywhere. Do you like any of them? Share in the comments!
