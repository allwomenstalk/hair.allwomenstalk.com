# Fab Treatments to Make at Home for Dry Scalp ...

Who doesn't love soft, shiny, dandruff free hair. Alas, dry scalp is something that we all have to put up with at some point or another. Whether it's chronic or weather induced, here are a few DIY treatments that will make your dry scalp vanish!

## 1. DIY Itchy Scalp Dandruff Treatment

This one may sound weird but it actually works! All you need is yellow Listerine (yep, you read that right) in a spray bottle. Spray it on to your scalp, massage it, leave it on for 10-15 minutes and then wash it off. Repeat for a week or until you see improvement.

## 2. Mask for Dry Scalp

This mask made of banana, honey, and olive oil will ensure that your dry scalp gets all the nourishment it so badly needs.

## 3. Remedies for Dry Hair

This video gives you two remedies that are both extremely effective in treating dry scalp and issues related to it. Aloe vera is absolutely amazing for dandruff and itchy scalp. Seasame oil mixed with honey is also just as effective and it promotes hair growth.

## 4. Oils for Dry Scalp

If there's anyone you can trust blindly for hair advice, it is Keren of Beautyklove. I mean, just look at her gorgeous hair! In this video, Keren talks about five oils that you can use to tremendously improve your hair.

## 5. Coconut Oil Works Wonders!

Anyone can try this simple treatment that makes a world of difference to dry scalp. All you need is some coconut oil . Massage it on to your scalp really well, then braid your hair, throw on a shower cap and leave it on overnight. Wash and condition the next morning. Try this at least once or twice a week and you will see amazing results.

## 6. DIY Dry Scalp Lotion

This DIY lotion uses aloe vera gel, MSM poswder, horestail powder, chamomile floral water, camphor essential oil, eucalyptus essential oil, lavender essential oil, and pimento berry essential oil. Yes, that's quite a few ingredients but the end result is to die for!

## 7. Healing DIY Deep Conditioning Treatment

This three ingredient DIY treatment is all you need to get rid of scalp itchiness. Mix up some nutmeg, blended bananas, and colloidal oatmeal following the instructions in the video and voila!

## 8. Argan Oil to the Rescue

Argan oil is another great way to give your dry scalp some TLC. Just apply to your scalp, scrub it with a brush to get rid of dry flakes, and wash and condition.

## 9. Banacado Mask

Last but not least, is this moisturizing mask by Keren. Blend together two bananas, one avocado, some coconut milk and argan oil. Make sure you blend well to avoid any lumps. Apply all over your hair and scalp. Leave it on for about an hour and then wash it off.
