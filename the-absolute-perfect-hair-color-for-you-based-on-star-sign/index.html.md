# The Absolute Perfect Hair Color for You Based on Star Sign ...

How closely do you follow your zodiac sign? Do you consult your horoscope before making any major moves in your life or is it just something you do for fun? I personally find them interesting because I embody some of the more common Libra traits so it’s fun to read up on lifestyle tips for your sign. One of the fun things I found out recently is that there are hair color recommendations based on your zodiac sign. How cool is that? If you’ve been thinking about getting a different hair color lately but just can’t decide on what to do with your hair, let your zodiac sign help you decide! Keep reading to see what the stars say about what hair color you should get next.

## 1. Capricorn

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/he/oi/l0muv4oz580337e1bae18144390298_500x562.jpg)

If you’re a Capricorn, you’re often known to be disciplined, ambitious, and humorous. To match your determination and perfectionist-like nature, traditional hair colors are a good match because they allow you to easily transition from work to play. If traditional hair color is just not your style, opt for a deep tint to your hair color or a perhaps a toned-down version of pastel-colored hair.

## 2. Aquarius

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l2/lw/ia9krdr158032bb11e809766386964_500x500.jpg)

Ladies born under the Aquarius sign are known for their independent spirit, friendliness, and honesty. With an incredible set of qualities like that, ladies under the Aquarius sign would look incredible with something like rose colored hair that’s currently trending or a bolder color like blues or purples are great for fall! If you’re the type to buck trends and create your own unique hair color, that’s an even better way to show off that free-spirited side of you!

## 3. Pisces

![hair, human hair color, face, clothing, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/hr/bwhqxvlh580332f9180b0822663527_600x600.jpg)

Pisces, also symbolized and known as the fish, are the type to be compassionate, intuitive, and imaginative. Hair colors that are reminiscent of the sea such as blues or greens in a balayage style would suit your deep emotions and sensitivity. If those hues just aren’t your thing, go for [mermaid hair](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/mesmerizing-photos-of-mermaid-hair/) colors like pastel blues, greens, and violet to showcase your imaginative side.

## 4. Aries

![hair, human hair color, clothing, hairstyle, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ks/p9/nfjqynf5580334ca6efda002811526_500x495.jpg)

Aries are adventurous, confident, and bursting with energy. Energetic types often prefer and look very alluring in bold, dramatic colors. Think bright reds, copper, or any pretty color that catches your eye will work with your energy. Keep in mind that Aries can be known to be somewhat impulsive so try not to choose a color that requires tons of maintenance without thinking about upkeep first.

## 5. Taurus

![hair, face, black hair, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/uw/r7feqxxr58032c245e740340995434_600x600.jpg)

People born under the Taurus sign are often described as persistent, loving, and patient and typically prefer natural hair colors. A simple, no-fuss hair color and style often suits a Taurus due to their natural abilities to lead. However, if you’re the type who prefers to show your passion through your hair, consider dark green or even deep, dark red hue that comminutes your no-nonsense side.

## 6. Gemini

![hair, face, pink, purple, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/dm/qhp4pfkx580334ba5c661214870168_500x506.jpg)

Geminis, AKA the twins, are versatile, witty, and smart. A great set of qualities like that deserves an equally awesome hair color like sombre, the more subtle and updated version of ombre or even orange mixed into your hair. But because Geminis are so adaptable you might prefer to change your hair color more often and that’s just fine! Try temporary or semi-temporary colors and be sure you condition your tresses often so they don’t get damaged from the hair color changes!

## 7. Cancer

![hair, color, human hair color, face, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/w1/oqwi5tno58034ca202bfb215403479_600x600.jpg)

Protective, loving, and intuitive Cancers often prefer easy, breezy, feminine looks that don’t require too much maintenance. Subtle streaks of color in your hair, an eggplant or dark violet color, or a rich, red hue that’s subtle yet sexy can be a perfect match. Keep in mind that Cancers often don’t like to fuss with too much upkeep so keep that in mind before you settle on your hair color.

## 8. Leo

![hair, clothing, pink, hairstyle, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/ff/yny2ziha580332dfade0a451557702_500x500.jpg)

Leos are often described as loving, creative, and generous which works swimmingly with colors that pack a major punch like bright yellows weaved in with your hair color or pastel streaks all over your hair. Another side to Leos is that they don’t like to follow trends and would rather find a go-to signature look. If that describes you, try going a few shades lighter or darker than your natural hair to test the water first.

## 9. Virgo

![hair, face, clothing, black hair, brown,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/go/ot6rr8b25803352e46f50315105886_600x749.jpg)

Discerning, reliable, and analytical Virgos tend to prefer neat, polished looks when it comes to their appearance so it should come as no surprise that Virgos prefer a solid hair color. Just because you’re a perfectionist doesn’t mean boring, platinum blonde, silver, or even light blue are stylish yet speak to your sophisticated inclinations.

## 10. Libra

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, clothing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/g4/hcf61ejd580335a2d02a8176770426_500x624.jpg)

Libras are easygoing and charming peacemakers who also like sophisticated looks like the Virgo. If it’s been awhile since you updated your hair color, now is a good time to switch things up! Try fresh and unique green or blue-green for a change. Libras are also known to be indecisive so if you just can’t decide on a color, try some lowlights or highlights with your natural hair color.

## 11. Scorpio

![eyebrow, face, hair, black hair, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/sb/ixfg0rzi5803363cbe044240441348_600x600.jpg)

Determined and emotional Scorpios have magnetic personalities which work well with captivating hair colors like universally flattering auburn, jet black, or rich reds. Scorpios are often described as intense because of their determined nature so why not try an equally intense hair color to show off that fiery passion?

## 12. Sagittarius

![hair, face, eyebrow, nose, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n9/y2/lo1oeivk58033655d8d03773278978_600x600.jpg)

Last but not least, ladies born under the Sagittarius sign are known to be uplifting, honest, and philosophical. Mirror that positive energy through your hair color by choosing an electric purple or silver. Sags often like to aim high but that might not apply with hair care so choose a hair color that doesn’t require you to use all of your free time maintaining the color.

Change your hair color, change your life! What do you think about choosing your hair color to go with your zodiac sign is it some new age garbage or do you think it could be an undiscovered way to let your true color shine through? Let us know in the comments!
