40 Easy DIY Recipes for Hair Growth ...

By Felicia • 2 Comments

Do you want some DIY recipes for hair growth? The way your hair grows is dependent on several different factors and how they interact with each other. The following are a list of the different factors that can hurt the growth of your hair:
- PCOS
- Sudden weight loss
- Medications like blood thinners and antidepressants
- Anxiety, stress, and trauma
- Environmental factors, like sunlight exposure, pollutants, and seasonal changes
- Stress on the hair like excessive hair products and over styling
- Hormonal imbalance
- Weak immune system
- Protein, vitamin, and mineral deficiencies
- Old age

There are so many things that can affect your hair! Most of them are out of our control. This is why you need to concentrate on taking better care of your hair with some DIY recipes for hair growth, supplements, and a healthy diet. The great thing about using home remedies is that they don’t cost much.

1 Use Biotin for Hair Growth

Coconut oil or Olive oil
2-3 biotin tablets
Crush up the tablets and then mix this powder into your oil of choice. Massage the mixture into your scalp and let it sit overnight. Rinse your hair the next morning.

You should also take the biotin supplement as recommended on the bottle for your age. This hair mask should be used twice a week.

2 Vitamins for Growth

Biotin may be a vitamin that helps with hair growth, but there are several other vitamins that are able to improve the health of your hair. Vitamin E is another important vitamin. It is an antioxidant that helps to lower the oxidative stress on the scalp. This will improve your hair growth. Next up is vitamin C. This vitamin gets rid of dead cells that are built-up on your scalp and unclogs your hair follicles. You can massage vitamin C tablets into your scalp using a carrier oil. Taking both of these vitamins internally also helps.

3 Oils for Hair Growth

Coconut oil:
Warm the oil up and massage it into your scalp. Allow it sit overnight and then wash your hair the next morning. This should be done every time you wash your hair, or at least twice a week.

Vitamin E oil:
7-8 vitamin E capsules
Prick the capsules carefully and squeeze the oil out into a bowl. Massage this into your scalp and allow it to sit overnight. Wash your hair the next morning with a mild shampoo. This should be done twice a week.

Rosemary oil:
2 tbsp coconut oil
1 tsp rosemary oil
Combine the rosemary and coconut oils together and rub this into your hair and scalp. Leave this on overnight and then wash your hair the next morning. You can also mix rosemary oil into your shampoo or conditioner. This should be done twice a week.

Argan oil:
Massage the argan oil into your scalp and the ends of your hair. Let it sit for an hour and then rinse your hair out. Do this once or twice a week.

Sage oil:
2 tbsp coconut oil
1 tsp sage oil
Combine the sage oil with the coconut oil and then rub it into your hair and scalp. Allow this to sit overnight and then wash it out the next morning. This should be done twice a week.

Lavender oil:
Shower cap
2 tbsp coconut oil
3-4 drops lavender oil
Mix the lavender oil and the carrier oil together and massage it into your scalp. Wrap your hair in the shower cap and leave it for an hour. Wash and rinse your scalp thoroughly. This should be done twice a week.

Jojoba oil:
2 tbsp carrier oil
1 tsp jojoba oil
Mix your carrier oil and jojoba together and apply to your hair and scalp. Leave this on overnight and then wash your hair the next morning. This can be done twice a week.

Flaxseed oil:
2 tbsp carrier oil
1 tsp flaxseed oil
Combine the oils together and then rub into your hair and scalp. Leave this on overnight and then rinse your hair out the next morning. This can be done two times a week.

Olive oil:
Hot water
Towel
EVOO
Warm the oil up and then massage it into your scalp. Soak a towel in some hot water and then wring it out. Wrap the towel around your hair and head for 15 to 20 minutes. Rinse out your hair. This treatment can be done once every four to five days.

Castor oil:
Warm towel
Castor oil
Warm your castor oil up and run it onto your clean scalp. Wrap the hair with a warm, moist towel and let it sit for 20 minutes. This oil is sticky, so you can add a bit of lemon juice to make it easier to apply. This can be done twice a week.

Almond oil:
Massage the almond oil into your scalp. You should leave this on overnight and then rinse your hair the following morning. This can be done twice a week.

4 Folic Acid Helps Growth

Folic acid, or vitamin B9, supplements are great to help improve the growth of your hair. They work by playing a role in the function and formation of red blood cells. These are what are responsible for moving different hormones, nutrients, and enzymes to various body parts. These supplements can be taken daily for hair growth.

5 Onion Juice for Growth

Cotton ball
2 red onions
Peel your onions and chop them into small pieces. Blend up the pieces and the squeeze out the juice. Use the cotton ball to apply the juice to your scalp and let it sit for 15 minutes. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo. You can do this once a week.

6 ACV for Growth

1 liter water
75 ml ACV
Thoroughly wash your hair with shampoo. Mix the apple cider vinegar and water together and pour a cup of the solution on your hair as a final rinse. You can use this rinse every time you wash your hair.

7 Aloe Vera for Growth

Cut an aloe vera leaf open and extract the gel inside. Rub this into your hair and scalp. Allow this to sit for an hour and then rinse your hair using a mild shampoo. This can be done two to three times a week.

8 Honey for Growth

2 tbsp shampoo
1 tsp honey
Combine your shampoo and honey and wash your hair as you normally would. This can be done twice a week.

9 Teas for Hair Growth

2 cups hot water
1 green tea bag
Steep the tea bag in the water for seven to eight minutes. Pour the warm tea over your scalp and let it sit for an hour. Rinse your hair in cold water. This can be done every time you wash your hair.

10 Rice Water for Growth

2 cups water
½ cup rice
Soak the rice in the water for about 15 minutes. Strain out the rice and then pour the water over your scalp and hair. Massage this into your scalp for a few minutes and then rinse your hair. If you need to, you can wash it with a mild shampoo. This can be done twice a week.

11 Henna for Growth

½ cup yogurt
1 cup dry henna
Mix together the yogurt and henna powder. Run this through your hair from roots to tips. Leave this on until it dries completely. Wash out your hair with mild shampoo. This should only be done once a month.

12 Cayenne for Growth

2 tsp olive oil
1 tsp cayenne powder
Combine the oil and pepper together and rub this into your hair. Focus on any areas that are thinning. Leave it on for ten minutes and rinse with cold water. This can be done once or twice a month. A word of advice, make sure you don’t have any sore spots on your head because the pepper will burn it. Also, make sure that you wash your hands thoroughly after so that you don’t have any remnants of pepper on your fingers. You don’t want to rub your face or eyes.

13 Egg Mask for Growth

1 tsp honey
1 tsp olive oil
1 egg
Mix all of the ingredients together until it forms a smooth paste. Apply this to your scalp and hair and let it sit for 20 minutes. Rinse with cold water and shampoo. Do this once a week.

14 Ginger for Growth

Peel and grate your ginger and then rub this into your scalp where your hair is thinning. Allow it to sit for 30 minutes and then rinse your hair. This can be done twice a week.

15 Horsetail Extract for Growth

1 cup hot water
¼ cup horsetail
Allow the herb to steep in the water for an hour. Strain out the herb and rinse your hair in the water. This can be done each time you wash your hair.

16 Guava Leaves for Growth

Shampoo
1 liter water
Handful of guava leaves
Boil the leaves for 20 minutes and then strain out the liquid. Wash your hair with shampoo and allow it to air dry. After it is dry, apply the guava leaf water. Massage this in for a few minutes and allow it to sit for two to three hours. Rinse your hair with lukewarm water. This can be done once a week.

17 Turmeric for Growth

2 tbsp honey
1 cup milk
3-4 tbsp turmeric powder
Mix together all of the ingredients and rub into your scalp and hair. Allow this to sit for 30 minutes. Wash this off with shampoo and tepid water. This can be done once or twice a week.

18 Coffee for Growth

Warm water
Shampoo
Shower cap
2 cups water
7-8 tbsp coffee grounds
Brew fresh coffee using the coffee and water measurements given above. Allow the coffee to cool. While it cools, wash your hair with shampoo. Wring out the excess water and then pour the coffee onto your hair and scalp. Massage this in for a few minutes and cover your hair with a shower cap. Wash the hair out with warm water after 20 to 30 minutes. Allow your hair to air dry. This can be done once a week.

If you have blonde hair or light hair, you might want to skip this because coffee can stain it.

19 Curry Leaf for Growth

½ cup coconut oil
Handful curry leaves
Place the ingredients in a pot and heat until the leaves form a black residue. Be careful because the leaves like to splutter. After the residue has formed, take this off the heat and let it cool down. Strain out the oil. Rub this onto your scalp and hair, massaging it in for a couple of minutes. Leave this on for an hour and wash your hair as usual. Do this twice a week.

20 Bee Pollen for Growth

Take a bee pollen supplement as directed by the manufacturer. Make sure you pay attention to yourself once you start taking this. Some people may be allergic to bee pollen. If you experience any signs of an allergic reaction stop taking it immediately.

21 Amla Powder for Growth

2 tsp lime juice
2 tsp amla powder or juice
Mix the amla and lime juice together and rub into your scalp and allow it to completely dry. Rinse out your hair with warm water. This can be done once a month.

22 Chia Seeds for Growth

¼ cup almond milk
1 tsp chia seeds
Soak the seeds in the milk for ten minutes. Rub this onto your scalp and let it rest for ten minutes. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo. This mask can be done once every 10 to 12 days

23 Mustard Powder for Growth

¼ cup warm water
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp mustard powder
Combine all of the ingredients together and rub it into your scalp. Allow this to set for 15 minutes to an hour. Wash your hair thoroughly with a mild shampoo. This can be done once a week.

24 Yogurt for Growth

Shower cap
1 cup plain yogurt
Rub the yogurt into your scalp. You can segment your hair to make sure you get it on your entire scalp. You can also rub it into your hair strands. Tie the hair up and cover with a shower cap. Let this sit for 30 minutes. Wash your hair with shampoo. This can be done once a week.

25 Raw Shea Butter for Growth

Warm the shea butter up until it becomes a liquid. Rub this into your scalp and leave for 30 minutes. Wash your hair as usual. This can be done once a week

26 Hibiscus for Growth

2 tbsp carrier oil
2 hibiscus flowers
Wash the flowers with water. Create a paste of the flowers and a carrier oil and rub into your hair evenly. Allow it to sit for 30 minutes. Wash it out with a mild shampoo. This can be done every time you wash your hair.

27 Brewer’s Yeast for Growth

Warm water
½ tbsp olive oil
4 tbsp brewer’s yeast
Mix all of the ingredients together to form a paste. Rub this into your hair and allow it to sit for an hour. Wash your hair with shampoo. This can be done once every ten days.

28 Moringa for Growth

Massage the moringa oil into your scalp and leave it on overnight. Wash your hair as normal the next morning. This can be done once a week.

29 Wheatgrass for Growth

Rub a ½ cup of wheatgrass juice into your scalp and let it sit for 20 minutes. Wash your hair with shampoo after. This can be done once a week.

30 Potato Juice for Growth

Peel a potato and blend it up to extract the juice. Rub the juice into your scalp and let it sit for 15 minutes. Finish by washing your hair with a mild shampoo. This can be done each time you wash your hair.

31 Rose Water for Growth

1 vitamin E capsule
6-8 drops jojoba oil
1 cup rose water
Open the vitamin capsule and squeeze the oil into the rose water. Mix in the jojoba oil, making sure everything is combined. Rub this into your scalp and hair. Allow it to sit for half an hour. Finish by washing your hair. This can be done once a week.

32 Ginseng for Growth

1 cup carrier oil
1 tbsp red ginseng herb extract
Mix the oil and extract together and massage it into your scalp and hair. Leave this on for 30 minutes and then wash it out with shampoo. This can be done once a week.

33 Carrots for Growth

Grate a medium carrot and rub it into your scalp. Allow it to sit for 15 minutes and then thoroughly wash your hair. This can be done once or twice a week.

34 Coconut Milk for Growth

Rub some coconut milk into your scalp and allow it to sit overnight. Rinse your hair out with cold water the next morning. This can be done each time you wash your hair.

35 Blackstrap Molasses for Growth

Rub a quarter cup of molasses into your roots all the way to the tips. Allow it to sit for 30 minutes. Wash your hair out thoroughly.

36 Saw Palmetto for Growth

2 tbsp olive oil
6-8 drops saw palmetto oil
Combine both of the oils together and warm them slightly. Massage this into the scalp for 15 minutes and then let it sit for 30 minutes. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo. This can be done once a week before you wash your hair.

37 Baking Soda for Growth

Squeeze bottle
6 tbsp water
1 tbsp baking soda
Combine the soda and water together and pour it into the bottle. Squirt this onto your scalp and massage it into your roots. Leave it on for ten minutes. Rinse out your hair with tepid water. This can be done every 10 to 12 days.

38 Fish Oil for Growth

Fish oil is full of omega-3 fatty acids that help with lots of different skin issues. When your skin is in distress, your hair growth will be affected. When you take fish oil supplements, this will take away the stress. It will also help with the healthy development of the skin’s various layers, as well as nourishment and hydration. These supplements should be taken daily.

39 Protein Powder for Growth

You can only have healthy hair growth if your diet has enough protein. If you have a low protein intake, then a protein powder can help supply you with the nutrients and protein your body needs. Buy natural protein powder and follow the directions on the bottle for consumption.

40 Inversion Method for Growth

Pick a hair oil of choice and warm it up. Apply it to your hair and scalp and massage it in for five minutes. Fold forward so that your hair is falling over your head and your head is facing your chest. Stay this way for four to five minutes. Make sure you practice deep breathing while holding this position to help calm your mind. Then slowly bring your head back up. Make sure you don’t use sudden movements. This can be done each time you apply oil to your hair.

Proper prevention and care will help you to cope with all types of hair and scalp problems. Enhancing and restoring hair growth can be achieved easily by using any of these remedies. You don’t have to use all of them, so pick a combination of a few that you like.
