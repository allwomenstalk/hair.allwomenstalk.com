# 40 Easy DIY Recipes for Hair Growth ...

Do you want some **DIY recipes for hair growth**? The way your hair grows is dependent on several different factors and how they interact with each other. The following are a list of the different factors that can hurt the growth of your hair:
- PCOS
- Sudden weight loss
- Medications like blood thinners and antidepressants
- Anxiety, stress, and trauma
- Environmental factors, like sunlight exposure, pollutants, and seasonal changes
- Stress on the hair like excessive hair products and over styling
- Hormonal imbalance
- Weak immune system
- Protein, vitamin, and mineral deficiencies
- Old age

There are so many things that can affect your hair! Most of them are out of our control. This is why you need to concentrate on taking better care of your hair with some **DIY recipes for hair growth**, supplements, and a healthy diet. The great thing about using home remedies is that they don’t cost much.

## 1. Use Biotin for Hair Growth

Coconut oil or Olive oil
2-3 biotin tablets
Crush up the tablets and then mix this powder into your oil of choice. Massage the mixture into your scalp and let it sit overnight. Rinse your hair the next morning.

You should also take the biotin supplement as recommended on the bottle for your age. This hair mask should be used twice a week.

## 2. Vitamins for Growth

Biotin may be a vitamin that helps with hair growth, but there are several other vitamins that are able to improve the health of your hair. Vitamin E is another important vitamin. It is an antioxidant that helps to lower the oxidative stress on the scalp. This will improve your hair growth. Next up is vitamin C. This vitamin gets rid of dead cells that are built-up on your scalp and unclogs your hair follicles. You can massage vitamin C tablets into your scalp using a carrier oil. Taking both of these vitamins internally also helps.

## 3. Oils for Hair Growth

Coconut oil:
Warm the oil up and massage it into your scalp. Allow it sit overnight and then wash your hair the next morning. This should be done every time you wash your hair, or at least twice a week.

Vitamin E oil:
7-8 vitamin E capsules
Prick the capsules carefully and squeeze the oil out into a bowl. Massage this into your scalp and allow it to sit overnight. Wash your hair the next morning with a mild shampoo. This should be done twice a week.

Rosemary oil:
2 tbsp coconut oil
1 tsp rosemary oil
Combine the rosemary and coconut oils together and rub this into your hair and scalp. Leave this on overnight and then wash your hair the next morning. You can also mix rosemary oil into your shampoo or conditioner. This should be done twice a week.

Argan oil:
Massage the argan oil into your scalp and the ends of your hair. Let it sit for an hour and then rinse your hair out. Do this once or twice a week.

Sage oil:
2 tbsp coconut oil
1 tsp sage oil
Combine the sage oil with the coconut oil and then rub it into your hair and scalp. Allow this to sit overnight and then wash it out the next morning. This should be done twice a week.

Lavender oil:
Shower cap
2 tbsp coconut oil
3-4 drops lavender oil
Mix the lavender oil and the carrier oil together and massage it into your scalp. Wrap your hair in the shower cap and leave it for an hour. Wash and rinse your scalp thoroughly. This should be done twice a week.

Jojoba oil:
2 tbsp carrier oil
1 tsp jojoba oil
Mix your carrier oil and jojoba together and apply to your hair and scalp. Leave this on overnight and then wash your hair the next morning. This can be done twice a week.

Flaxseed oil:
2 tbsp carrier oil
1 tsp flaxseed oil
Combine the oils together and then rub into your hair and scalp. Leave this on overnight and then rinse your hair out the next morning. This can be done two times a week.

Olive oil:
Hot water
Towel
EVOO
Warm the oil up and then massage it into your scalp. Soak a towel in some hot water and then wring it out. Wrap the towel around your hair and head for 15 to 20 minutes. Rinse out your hair. This treatment can be done once every four to five days.

Castor oil:
Warm towel
Castor oil
Warm your castor oil up and run it onto your clean scalp. Wrap the hair with a warm, moist towel and let it sit for 20 minutes. This oil is sticky, so you can add a bit of lemon juice to make it easier to apply. This can be done twice a week.

Almond oil:
Massage the almond oil into your scalp. You should leave this on overnight and then rinse your hair the following morning. This can be done twice a week.

## 4. Folic Acid Helps Growth

Folic acid, or vitamin B9, supplements are great to help improve the growth of your hair. They work by playing a role in the function and formation of red blood cells. These are what are responsible for moving different hormones, nutrients, and enzymes to various body parts. These supplements can be taken daily for hair growth.

## 12. Cayenne for Growth

2 tsp olive oil
1 tsp cayenne powder
Combine the oil and pepper together and rub this into your hair. Focus on any areas that are thinning. Leave it on for ten minutes and rinse with cold water. This can be done once or twice a month. Also, make sure that you wash your hands thoroughly after so that you don’t have any remnants of pepper on your fingers. You don’t want to rub your face or eyes.

## 17. Turmeric for Growth

## 18. Coffee for Growth

Warm water
Shampoo
Shower cap
2 cups water
7-8 tbsp coffee grounds
Brew fresh coffee using the coffee and water measurements given above. Allow the coffee to cool. While it cools, wash your hair with shampoo. Wring out the excess water and then pour the coffee onto your hair and scalp. Massage this in for a few minutes and cover your hair with a shower cap. Wash the hair out with warm water after 20 to 30 minutes. Allow your hair to air dry. This can be done once a week.

If you have blonde hair or light hair, you might want to skip this because coffee can stain it.

## 19. Curry Leaf for Growth

Handful curry leaves

Leave this on for an hour and wash your hair as usual. Do this twice a week.

## 40. Inversion Method for Growth

Pick a hair oil of choice and warm it up. Apply it to your hair and scalp and massage it in for five minutes. Fold forward so that your hair is falling over your head and your head is facing your chest. Stay this way for four to five minutes.
