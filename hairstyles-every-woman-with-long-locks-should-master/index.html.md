# 8 Hairstyles Every Woman with Long Locks Should Master ...

I love long hair for its versatility! When it comes to styling long hair, the options are literally endless. Here are a few of those options that are both gorgeous and relatively easy to master. Happy hairstyling!

## 1. The Tousled Look

![hair, blond, hairstyle, bangs, brown,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ku/rv/e1sx4zhj578887bee1719102869133_600x600.jpg)

Tousled hair is my favorite summer go-to hairstyle! The best part is that it's such an easy look to achieve (perfect for lazy girls like me). All you have to do is wash and towel dry your hair before you sleep, apply a curl cream, split your hair into two pigtails, twist each one into a bun and secure with scrunchies. When you wake up, undo the buns, run your fingers through your hair and finish off with a sea-salt spray.

## 2. Sleek Ponytail

![clothing, hair, hairstyle, black hair, bangs,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i7/e7/nat2aqkb578887be93997809504816_600x600.jpg)

Think Kim Kardashian. Sleek ponytails are chic and perfect for both day and night. Plus they keep your hair away from your face which is a huge plus in this balmy, sweaty weather. This works best with second-day hair. Use a strong hold gel or serum at the front of your hair, then brush it into a high ponytail and secure with a bungee. Spritz on some finishing hairspray and voila!

## 3. Messy Everyday Bun

![hair, hairstyle, eyebrow, bangs, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iz/ar/goqf5yam578887bedc6d7280653451_564x564.jpg)

The messy bun is the epitome of effortless style. You can rock this look in a pinch and it's always got your back. Cute, easy going, and simple to do, the messy bun is your BFF.

## 4. Classy Chignon

![hair, hairstyle, face, long hair, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/gj/xzet5o8n59097217b6664901151111_600x600.jpg)

When it comes to formal occasions, a chignon is THE way to go! It looks way more complicated than it actually is and you can go as neat or as messy as you'd like (I personally prefer it somewhere in between). Add a pretty accessory and you're ready to dazzle!

## 5. Voluminous Curls

![hair, human hair color, face, clothing, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gf/lg/ypcl86j8590972c2f3f3c927783601_600x600.jpg)

Some days just call for big, sexy curls. To achieve this look, apply volumizing spray to your hair and blow dry with a large round brush. Section away the top of your hair into a loose bun. Now, take two inch sections of the rest of your hair, spritz with hairspray and curl using a 1 1/4 barrel curling iron. Open up the bun, separate it into two parts and curl each section away from your face. Finally, brush out the curls with your fingers and finish with serum.

## 6. Crown Braid

![hair, hairstyle, face, french braid, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/ey/odjjv3gd5909741bd2f59867284358_500x501.jpg)

I blame Game of Thrones for my obsession with braids. Crown braids are super pretty, super versatile (depending on your braiding skills) and super easy!

## 7. Fishtail Braid

![hair, person, hairstyle, photo shoot, model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/gx/lvod1hj557888836506a2085752232_593x593.jpg)

I'll admit, fishtail braids do take some practise to master but it's so worth it! There are plenty of You Tube tutorials to guide you until you become the queen of fishtail braids!

## 8. The Half up Bun

![clothing, eyewear, hair, vision care, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ac/tq/k28ub4mg5789f82d13315775902454_600x549.jpg)

If you're the kind of girl who likes funky hairstyles, try the half up bun. It's easier than saying "half up bun" and really forgiving. Seriously, you can't go wrong with this one.
