# 11 Natural Ways to Eliminate Stubborn Dandruff and Have Healthy Hair ...

There are many ways to eliminate stubborn dandruff naturally. If you have ever had to deal with dandruff you know how frustrating and embarrassing it can be. Since most dandruff is a result of an internal issue, specifically fungus overgrowth, be sure to eliminate yeast and processed sugar as much as possible from your daily diet. You will need to treat the issue internally as you test ways to eliminate stubborn dandruff externally. Don't give up until you find a treatment that works for you.

## 1. Aloe Vera Gel

![Aloe Vera Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/07/1_aloe-vera-gel_490x673.jpg)

Aloe vera gel is one of many ways to eliminate stubborn dandruff. Work pure aloe vera gel into your scalp and hair 15 to 20 minutes before you shampoo your hair, then wash your hair with your regular shampoo. Regular use of aloe vera gel can help keep dandruff in check.

**Source:** [livestrong.com](https://oureverydaylife.com/159055-alternative-treatments-to-nizoral-shampoo.html)

## 2. Olive & Almond Oil with Beet

![Olive & Almond Oil with Beet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/07/2_olive-almond-oil-with-beet_500x750.jpg)

For this method you will need to mix both almond and olive oil together in the same ratio. Then massage your scalp with this oil mixture and rest for the next five minutes. Rinse your hair well with a mild shampoo may be an herbal or ayurvedic shampoo. Beet is not only an useful vegetable, but also an effective dandruff home remedy as it can prove to be a good element for preventing the problem of dandruff. To get the exact preparation it is necessary to boil beet roots in water for a few minutes. Then use the filtrate product to message your scalp each night. White beet is the most suitable one for this purpose.

**Source:** [getridofdandruff.org](https://goo.gl/z1F6gs)

## 3. Fenugreek Paste

![Fenugreek Paste](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/07/3_fenugreek-paste_600x901.jpg)

Using the paste of fenugreek seeds is one of the useful dandruff home remedies. To make the fenugreek paste, two teaspoon of the same should be soaked from before night in water which can be made in paste consistency in the morning. Apply the paste completely on the scalp and keep it as such for a maximum of 1 hour. Then one can go ahead taking hair wash by which one can control dandruff for sure. Lime juice can be used for preventing dandruff growth as it will be more effective. It also helps to remove the gumminess of the hair.

**Source:** [getridofdandruff.org](https://goo.gl/z1F6gs)

## 4. Lemon Juice & Coconut Oil

_ Blend together 1 tbsp. of lemon juice with 5 tbsp. of coconut oil. Apply to the scalp to get rid of dandruff. Lemon juice is a natural home remedy for dandruff and will clear away flakes from your scalp. There is a myth that lemon juice can lead to hair graying. However this is not true. Leave this remedy on the scalp for 20 to 30 minutes and then wash with a shampoo.

**Source:** expertscolumn.com_

## 5. Mouth Fresheners

![Mouth Fresheners](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/07/5_mouth-fresheners_499x700.jpg)

I suggest using an organic mouth wash for this recipe. The content of antibacterial mouthwash handy enough to eliminate dandruff and itching on the scalp. Combine mouthwash with water and shampoo. Wash as usual while massaging the scalp with your fingers to increase circulation to the hair cuticle. Regular maintenance will repel bacteria and dandruff.

**Source:** [letsgohealthy.blogspot.ca](http://letsgohealthy.blogspot.com/2012/11/how-to-get-rid-of-dandruff-naturally.html)

## 6. Lemon & Garlic

_ Mix 1 tbsp. of lemon juice with 2 tbsp. of garlic. Apply paste to the scalp. Lemon juice will help you to get rid of flakes. Garlic is a natural antibiotic and is best to kill the bacteria on the scalp. Leave this anti dandruff treatment on scalp for 20 to 30 minutes and then shampoo your hair.

**Source:** expertscolumn.com_

## 7. Apple Cider Vinegar

![Apple Cider Vinegar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/07/7_apple-cider-vinegar_600x812.jpg)

Take one part apple cider vinegar and mix it with one part water. Wash your hair normally, then pour vinegar-water mixture carefully onto hair and scrub into your scalp. Take care not to let any get into your eyes. If you have psoriasis or any unhealed scratch or scab, it will burn. (If it burns, just rinse the affected area with water). Vinegar is an excellent remedy for dandruff, because the potassium and enzymes in vinegar can help eliminate dandruff and itching on the scalp.

**Source:** [wikihow.com](https://www.wikihow.com/Get-Rid-of-Dandruff-(Natural-Methods))

## 8. Tea Tree Oil Treatment

_ Tea tree oil is a natural home remedy for dandruff. Tea tree oil has natural antiseptic and antibiotic properties, which make it best to get rid of dandruff. You can apply tea tree oil directly to the scalp, or add few drops to your regular shampoo.

**Source:** expertscolumn.com_

## 9. Baking Soda

_ This may be surprising, but it's true that Baking soda has potential to clear excess flakes and clarifies the scalp from dandruff. You can add 1 tsp. of baking soda to a handful of shampoo and you will find that it can remove dandruff flakes even hiding in hair strands.

**Source:** expertscolumn.com_

## 10. Salt

![Salt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/07/10_salt_500x625.jpg)

Salt is useful for treating wounds on the scalp. When a wound doesn't heal fast it will accumulate and become dandruff. The trick is to use salt to wash hair. Sprinkle and rub the scalp with salt to taste. Let stand for a few minutes before rinsing with shampoo to clean.

**Source:** [uhealthylifes.blogspot.com](http://uhealthylifes.blogspot.com/2013/02/5-ways-to-remove-dandruff-natural-and.html)

## 11. Asprin

![Asprin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/07/11_asprin_450x650.jpg)

Crush two regular aspirin tablets. Squeeze your regular shampoo into the palm of your hand, then add the aspirin powder and mix it into the shampoo with your finger. Wash your hair, then allow the mixture to remain in your hair for two minutes. Rinse thoroughly to remove the aspirin powder.

**Source:** [livestrong.com](https://oureverydaylife.com/159055-alternative-treatments-to-nizoral-shampoo.html)
