# 11 Hairstyle Ideas for Winter Cold ...

Hairstyle ideas for winter don’t differ much than all the fabulous hairstyles you’d wear during spring, summer or autumn although some hairstyles are, of course, a bit better than the others! Some winter hairstyles are perfect if you love to wear hats to keep warm, some will help protect your hair from those extreme temperatures and some are just perfect for rainy days! Now, is that a great reason to experiment with a new hairstyle or what? And here are a few tutorials to help you come up with a great, new hairstyle idea for winter:

## 1. Protective Winter Hairstyle

Opting for a protective hairstyle in winter is definitely a great choice! After all, cold wind, rain and snow are not the only things that could damage hair – there’s that dry, overheated and not particularly healthy air we’re forced to spend our time surrounded by. Great reason to update your list of hairstyle ideas for winter with this sexy, glamorous ‘do that not only looks great, but helps you really get the most out of your protective style! Go for it, ladies, it’s a great one!

## 2. Three Easy Autumn Winter Hairstyles for a Rainy Day

Chilly rain or just a snowy day full of humidity can really mess up your mood and your looks! Luckily, there are always a few great styles to help you look glam and put together even on days such as these. Celebrity hairstylist David Dang and L’Oreal are here to help you look the part even on the worst of weather conditions with these three, humidity-friendly styles you simply must try.

## 3. Ideal Winter Hairstyle for a Windy Day

Windy, snowy or rainy – any horrible winter day is just a perfect occasion to wear a ponytail! But hey, we’re not talking about that simple “ just rolled out of bed and tied my hair with a first elastic band I found” pony! Oh, no, no, no! Your pony is going to be different, it’s going to look like you’ve tried hard and yet…you won’t be trying any harder than you usually do! Give it a go!

## 4. Twisted Bun Hairstyle

Add this trendy yet classy style to your list of hairstyle ideas for winter and say good-bye to all cold weather-related hair problems! But that’s not all – let’s not forget that this is a great variation of a classic bun you could use even in summer or spring! I love it! Hair picked up, ends tucked in – no mess, no fuss, no frizz and even great for a hat!

## 5. Classy Ponytail Variation

Cool ponytails are a great way to keep your hair nicely styled yet manageable in winter! No flyaways, no complicated stuff and yet, we’re still light years away from that too casual “gym” look. This particular pony looks really cute yet is something you can freely slip a hat or wrap a warm wool scarf over whenever the weather conditions call for it. Thumbs up!

## 6. Messy Low Bun

Slightly messy and 100% celebrity inspired – this interesting hairstyle idea for winter will help you rock a fancier beanie or any slouchy type of winter hat in a totally glamorous way! Messy finish will help hide any frizz that may occur on a snowy or rainy days as well as allow you to take your hat off if need be and without having to worry about what your hair is going to look like.

## 7. Head Scarf Hairstyle Ideas for Winter

Yes, you can rock a headscarf or a turban even in winter! In fact, a colder, rainier, windier season is when I’d choose to wear it, too! Choose a thicker one if you want to protect yourself from some serious chills or go for a thinner one if protecting your hair from the elements in a cool way is what you’re hoping to achieve. This video right here will give you some ideas on how to tie it but do experiment further, as there are plenty of other, equally cool ways to rock this amazing accessory!

## 8. Transition from Hot to Cold

You can’t wear beachy, wavy hair in Winter. Instead, you need styles that are sleek and soft. This video shows you exactly how to make your hair fit the season. It also gives you some extra tips, like the correct way to straighten your hair.

## 9. All Things Winter

Not only does this video tell you how to wear your hair in Winter, but it tells you how to dress and wear your make-up as well. The girl speaking is young, but she knows what she’s talking about.

## 10. Party Hair

Do you like keeping it simple? This video shows you how to create an adorable, easy look. All you need is a little bit of time and a headband.

## 11. SImple Sleek Hair

If you want your hair to look natural, try out this style. It looks like you didn’t put a lot of work in, so it’s perfect for a casual day with friends. This is another video that includes some extras about make-up and clothes, so you’ll be learning more than one thing!

Do you have any other interesting hairstyle ideas for winter weather? Do share! I know a winter hairstyle doesn’t differ much from all the other styles we wear during the year but still… discovering new variations of the styles that have already proved to be both winter hat and hair friendly is definitely interesting.
