# 7 Amazing Leave\-in Conditioners Your Hair Will Love ...

Leave-in conditioners are an incredibly convenient way to add shine, moisturize and condition your hair while you go about your busy life. While it might seem like hair product overkill to use a regular conditioner and a leave-in conditioner, it’s really beneficial for certain hair types. Those with dry, frizzy hair or hair that’s prone to tangles can really benefit from using a leave-in hair conditioner. Or if you have thin hair that’s often weighed down by traditional hair conditioners, using leave-in conditioners is a really excellent option.

## 1. Giovanni Direct Leave-in Conditioner

![Giovanni Direct Leave-in Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/19183216_3600_499x484.jpg)

Leave-in conditioners give us a lotta bang for the buck. Take this leave-in conditioner by Giovanni for instance. This baby helps prevents split ends, adds shine and body, improves hair health and is a must for swimmers and beachgoers. It’s also very compatible for ladies with tangle-y or coarse hair, so this hair conditioner is ideal for daily use. Available at Ulta for $7.99.

## 2. TIGI Bed Head Ego Boost Split End Mender

![TIGI Bed Head Ego Boost Split End Mender](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/19183216_6752_499x495.jpg)

Ask any hair expert and they’ll tell you that the only way to get rid of split ends is to get a trim. So while this hair care product asserts that it can mend split ends, we’ll give it a pass since it’s awesome at improving hair texture, getting rid of frizz and softening and moisturizing all sorts of hair types and textures. If you can get past the old school packaging, you'll love this product for its lightweight texture and unbelievable fragrance. Available at Drugstore.com for $14.40.

## 3. Johnson's No More Tangles Leave-in Conditioner

![Johnson's No More Tangles Leave-in Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/19183216_0361_499x469.jpg)

Johnson’s isn’t just for babies. They actually make a highly rated leave-in conditioner that both kids and adults adore. If you have curly hair, easily tangled hair, thick or coarse hair, you are going to love using this hair care product and love the results even more. Use this as a detangler or use it to smooth and moisturize your hair. Available at Walmart for $3.42

## 4. Garnier Fructis Sleek & Shine Intensely Smooth Leave-in Conditioning Cream

![Garnier Fructis Sleek & Shine Intensely Smooth Leave-in Conditioning Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/19183216_8404_499x496.jpg)

Another highly rated hair conditioner is by Garnier Fructis. Their Sleek & Shine leave-in conditioning cream is truly a fabulous find. It’s great for smoothing out the frizzies, adding softness and shine to dry, damaged hair, and making hair look and feel better overall. If you have wavy or curly hair, you’ll love the added bounce and shine. Available at Walmart for $2.96.

## 5. Design Essentials HCO Leave-in Conditioner and Blow Drying Lotion

![Design Essentials HCO Leave-in Conditioner and Blow Drying Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/27090209_4967_325x306.jpg)

This next hair care product is versatile and helps protect hair from thermal heating styling products as well as conditioning and strengthening strands. Count on this hair conditioner when you need some moisture, protection, help with detangling, reducing static and adding body. Although it says it works best on fine to medium hair textures, those with thick hair have seen great results, also. Available at Ulta for $9.99.

## 6. Pureology Essential Repair Instant Repair

![Pureology Essential Repair Instant Repair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/19183216_2021_514x463.jpg)

Pureology’s leave-in conditioner is perfect for ladies with color-treated hair. This lightweight hair care product helps prevent hair color from fading, leaves hair smooth, soft and shiny and it’s concentrated so a little dab will do ya. It also makes combing and detangling hair an absolute breeze so you can’t go wrong! Available at Target for $24.29.

## 7. Mixed Chicks Leave-in Conditioner

![Mixed Chicks Leave-in Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/19183216_0245_499x463.jpg)

Ladies with curly hair often have a difficult time finding products that work well with their gorgeous curls. Well look no further, Mixed Chicks has you covered. No matter what your ethnic background, this alcohol-free leave-in hair conditioner promises to define your curls, minimize frizz, and moisturize hair all without leaving strands feeling greasy. Available at drugstore.com for $45.

There you have it girls, 7 leave-in conditioners that’ll leave your hair feeling soft, pampered and much more manageable. No matter what your hair type, texture or concern, there’s a hair conditioner that will help make styling much easier and enjoyable. Do you use leave-in conditioners? Which one is your favorite?
