# Crave Mermaid Hair? Here's How to Get It ...

Do you have hair envy? You look at girls with mermaid-worthy hair and lust for long locks of your own? It takes patience and perseverance to grow your hair to a considerable length but there are ways you can help it along.

## 1. Don't Shampoo Too Often

![Don't Shampoo Too Often](https://img.allw.mn/content/g0/yg/w76dvve7561afcea1f3b8274460999_500x220.gif)

You don't have to shampoo your hair every time you take a shower. Excessive shampooing will rob your hair of essential natural oils that will leave your hair dry and rough. Shampoo your hair only when you want to wash away dirt and do it by only lathering up at your scalp. Just let the suds slide down your hair and then rinse it away.

## 2. Never Forget Your Conditioner

![Never Forget Your Conditioner](https://img.allw.mn/content/p8/ot/veg082v3561afe21d9bf4422773498_500x271.gif)

Every time you shampoo, be sure to condition your hair too. Heat styling and coloring will hit your strands hard, making them become thin at the bottom. Conditioner seals the cuticle after replacing the lipids and proteins inside the shaft. This will help your hair grow longer and look healthier too.

## 3. Apply a Mask

![Apply a Mask](https://img.allw.mn/content/as/x9/co0j3qp8561afe4d8191d305104071_500x261.gif)

A mask or oil treatment at least once a week will help fill up your strands with fatty acids. These fats are absorbed by the hair cuticle and leave a protective layer on your hair for when you wash your hair using your regular shampoo.

## 4. Get Regular Trims

![Get Regular Trims](https://img.allw.mn/content/ni/ek/dv053dkb561afe63e987a722661554_420x202.gif)

Trimming your hair regularly won't actually make it grow faster because only the follicles in your scalp determine how much or how fast your hair grows. Still, regular trims improve the overall look and feel of hair and reduce breakage and flyaways. Moreover, leaving split ends will cause your hair to break off high up on the strand, which will shorten your hair more than you have when you trim split ends regularly.

## 5. Try Hair-Boosting Supplements

![Try Hair-Boosting Supplements](https://img.allw.mn/content/u2/nu/tp9aje8a561afe8060c44131596103_500x281.gif)

To learn how to get really long hair, you first need to know how to provide your body with all-important hair-building nutrients, such as protein, vitamin B, vitamin K, iron, copper, magnesium, and zinc. Taking a hair-boosting supplement is one good idea, especially if you're not eating a balanced diet. Be sure to talk to your doctor before you take any supplements.

## 6. Avoid Excessive Brushing

![Avoid Excessive Brushing](https://img.allw.mn/content/np/yk/m59xu3g2561afe999c5e6675504693_499x285.gif)

Don't brush your hair too often because all that tugging will damage your hair. Be as gentle as possible and start from the bottom when trying to detangle wet hair. Remember, wet hair is more delicate and brushing can cause breakage. Use a comb instead to detangle your wet hair.

## 7. Avoid Tight Braids and Ponytails

![Avoid Tight Braids and Ponytails](https://img.allw.mn/content/mz/my/ylxvditm561afeda2c89d983533541_500x332.gif)

Pulling your hair back in a tight ponytail will eventually damage the hair follicles. Try new hairstyles and wear ponytails positioned in different areas of the scalp. Also, avoid using a rubber band to tie hair – this also causes breakage.

## 8. Say Goodbye to Your Cotton Pillowcase

![Say Goodbye to Your Cotton Pillowcase](https://img.allw.mn/content/rw/w7/xmffso2q561aff41a641d674560994_500x333.gif)

Cotton pillowcases are rough and create friction between the surface of the pillow and your hair, which will lead to hair breakage. Use satin pillowcases instead. They are smooth, so you won’t get your hair tangled up while sleeping on them.

## 9. Don't Wrap Your Hair in a Towel

No strengthening shampoo will work if you wrap your wet locks in a towel. Hair shafts will break easily when wet, so rubbing your wet tresses and pulling them into a too-tight wrap will leave you with damaged, frizzy strands.

## 10. Try a Cold-Water Rinse at the End

![Try a Cold-Water Rinse at the End](https://img.allw.mn/content/bl/f8/uz3wg1ih561b0d239d582047063079_400x225.gif)

Be sure to rinse your hair with cold water at the end of your shower. Warm water opens your cuticles and helps shampoo do its job, whereas cold water will close the cuticles and seal in moisture to help your hair grow longer and look shinier.

## 11. Be Patient

![Be Patient](https://img.allw.mn/content/fv/im/ws3of4k5561b0d3b467d1908020598_500x275.gif)

If you do all the right things to keep your hair healthy, you will eventually gain new length. Just keep in mind that even the super-healthy hair may only grow to a half an inch a month. It won't grow overnight no matter what you do. Be patient and keep doing the right things.
