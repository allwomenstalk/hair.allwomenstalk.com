## 1. Medium Length Sleek Hair with a Classic Middle Part

![Long Hair with Middle Part](https://img.allw.mn/thumbs/34/m93bpwgu49so5x32qff8l_1290x1591.jpg)

For those with **round faces**, a medium length sleek hairstyle with a classic middle part can work wonders.  **This style elongates your face**, creating a balanced and sophisticated look. The sleek middle part adds a touch of elegance and symmetry, drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Whether you’re heading to a formal event or a casual outing, this **versatile hairstyle** exudes confidence and grace. Perfect for both straight and slightly wavy hair, it’s a timeless option that always stays in vogue. To maintain the sleekness, use a good quality straightener and a shine-enhancing serum for that flawless finish.

## 2. Short Bob with Bangs

![](https://img.allw.mn/thumbs/57/nl5yknj5t40rfa46cwjty9_1290x1603.jpg)

The short bob with bangs is a chic and playful choice for those with round faces. This hairstyle frames the face beautifully, adding definition and a touch of edginess. The bangs help to break up the roundness of the face, providing a flattering contrast that draws attention to your eyes and jawline. Perfect for both casual and formal settings, the short bob with bangs is easy to maintain and style. Whether you opt for straight, blunt bangs or soft, wispy ones, this versatile cut can be tailored to suit your personal style. Keep it fresh with regular trims and a light styling product to enhance the texture and volume.
