## 4. Toni & Guy Sculpting Powder

![Toni & Guy Sculpting Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/04/2349001_104x300.jpg)

**Price:** $11.57 [boots.com](http://www.boots.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This micro-fine powder is another great product for big hair. Its weightless texture absorbs instantly into hair, giving your hair that sexy Brigitte Bardot- worthy volume and body. ## 5. Mark Hill Love Big Hair! Va Va Voom Volumising Blow Dry Mousse

![Mark Hill Love Big Hair! Va Va Voom Volumising Blow Dry Mousse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/04/2352821_92x300.jpg)

**Price:** $9.64 at [boots.com](http://www.boots.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Va Va Voom is an excellent product for adding skyscraper volume to all hair types, including fine and flat. Apply foam evenly through damp hair and use a large round brush to blow dry, drying roots in the opposite direction of which they normally fall - you’ll be amazed at how much volume this creates.

![John Frieda Luxurious Volume Anytime Volume Refresher](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/04/7_john-frieda-luxurious-volume-anytime-volume-refresher_300x300.jpg)
