# Here Are the Most Popular Hairstyles Blowing up Instagram ...

Have you guys seen the latest hair crazes on Instagram? Colors are bolder, cuts are edgier, and ladies (and gents) are getting braver with their style choices! Rock on! Check out these popular hairstyles blowing up your IG feed!

## 1. ASYMMETRICAL CUT/ GREY COLOR

Grey is in, ladies! Who would've thought that embracing those grey roots would be a fashion trend?! I sure didn't. Complementing the color with an edgy cut makes this look versatile!

## 2. SHAVED SIDES

Besides going with bolder colors, cuts are getting edgier. Loving this shaved side!

## 3. LONG HAIR, DON'T CARE

Long hair is and always will be a popular hairstyle! Let it grow, let it grow!

## 4. The BOB

For all my short haired ladies, the bob is the best blunt cut ever! (In my opinion, anyways!)

## 5. FAUX HAWK

The faux hawk is a classic look to rock!

## 6. STRAIGHT EDGE BANGS

Bangs have always been in style, it's just a matter of what style of bangs you go for!

## 7. SIDE SWEPT BANGS

Side swept bangs are my favorite. They're like faux bangs. You can sweep them to the side, or flaunt them!
