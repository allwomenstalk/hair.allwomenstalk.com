# The Struggles of Long Hair – Can You Relate?

It's great having long hair because you can wear a different style every day. But keeping it looking good takes investment. And if you have long hair, you'll certainly recognize some of these struggles.

## 1. Bed Head

![Bed Head](https://img.allw.mn/content/ns/kw/d54so79i5707b3cec0cb3201661620_650x366.gif)

Waking up with bed hair is never fun, but when your hair is nearly down to your butt it can be completely untamable!

## 2. Big Knots

![Big Knots](https://img.allw.mn/content/cs/ic/us2etf1x5707b3514d5ce195082943_500x260.gif)

Having to spend 30 minutes every morning painfully brushing out all of the knots that occurred overnight!

## 3. Bye Clumps

![Bye Clumps](https://img.allw.mn/content/ea/wu/usm1mx8v5707b174a4151118188621_500x276.gif)

That feeling of panic when for no reason you run your hands through your hair and end up with literally hundreds of strands coming out!

## 4. Washing

![Washing](https://img.allw.mn/content/ea/gt/v14myiru57079c2f6990d905177912_500x278.gif)

The literal hour it takes to wash your locks that could be spent doing much more fun things.

## 5. Shampoo

![Shampoo](https://img.allw.mn/content/pb/mh/natrgh1f5707b255af16d110149869_500x261.gif)

Having to get back in the shower because you realize you haven’t got even half of the shampoo out of your hair.

## 6. Brushing

![Brushing](https://img.allw.mn/content/pr/i8/akf6ftc35706c4367735e600566860_500x268.gif)

Having to permanently have a brush nearby to get rid of any tangles that occur on an hourly basis.

## 7. Blow Dry

![Blow Dry](https://img.allw.mn/content/ba/pg/vceym6885707ba9eecd1b937457995_400x225.gif)

The permanent ear damage that you are sure you have because you have to blow dry your hair for at least 45 minutes.

## 8. Still Wet

![Still Wet](https://img.allw.mn/content/sv/xq/u8csl82r5706c4728f06a820535057_600x400.gif)

The fact that no matter how long your dry it for, it never feels 100% done!

## 9. Styling

![Styling](https://img.allw.mn/content/fy/sa/tyr8o0hw57079d813cf52786128858_500x282.gif)

No matter what style you go for, unless it’s a simple ponytail, it’s going to take forever to achieve.

## 10. Straightening

![Straightening](https://img.allw.mn/content/cl/f2/fhq2syvp5707bb3f3136a907858722_544x306.gif)

And let’s not even talk about straightening, that’s an hour of your life every time that you will never get back!

## 11. Curling

![Curling](https://img.allw.mn/content/ke/y9/qqykf0ie5707b429e9b5b474331680_500x259.gif)

And curling? Forget about it! That’ll be at least a couple of hours!

## 12. Braiding

![Braiding](https://img.allw.mn/content/bu/pp/mr0nyxre5706bfa4707ff843768089_500x278.gif)

You hate the fact that you always give up and end up just putting your hair in to a classic braid to save time and hassle.

## 13. Messy Bun

![Messy Bun](https://img.allw.mn/content/fz/fw/w3nhf76v5707b8069d1b0818937028_320x200.gif)

Or even worse, that messy bun that you turn to whenever you feel like your hair is going to beat you once and for all!

## 14. Bobby Pins

![Bobby Pins](https://img.allw.mn/content/hr/us/d0xpz01k5707b40eec55d460787036_500x207.gif)

You are sure that there are upwards of ten bobby pins lost in your hair at any one time!

## 15. Found Them!

![Found Them!](https://img.allw.mn/content/jl/c6/b96uqfyc5707b397b42be429460909_625x353.gif)

Oh yeah, that’s right, you eventually find them when you are in the shower and they sail down the drain never to return!

## 16. Hairspray

![Hairspray](https://img.allw.mn/content/bo/h8/pq4qx3oj5707b5e0b1dc8485382643_750x450.gif)

When it comes to hairspray, it’s pretty much all or nothing. Applying just a little bit will make absolutely no difference.

## 17. Molting

![Molting](https://img.allw.mn/content/eh/xt/mkfl91gr5707b1d0a5f39764037540_400x227.gif)

You feel like a long-haired cat when you start to notice your hair on everything. Literally everything, everywhere.

## 18. Still There

![Still There](https://img.allw.mn/content/ji/hr/a42thyq557079c7b49685862217082_500x194.gif)

Even when you sweep the house and vacuum the carpets, your hair is always there!

## 19. Hair in Bed

![Hair in Bed](https://img.allw.mn/content/kw/oo/enacghz45707bb1b2d6d3361376075_460x327.gif)

Hair on your pillows, hair underneath your duvet. HAIR EVERYWHERE!

## 20. Food

![Food](https://img.allw.mn/content/ns/sg/wlp8stx25707b52eeed22073112663_500x209.gif)

We’ve all been there. You cook yourself a great meal and just before your first mouthful, you notice a familiar strand has made its way in to the mix!

## 21. Hugs

![Hugs](https://img.allw.mn/content/c4/zb/se2lmj6a5707b908ac45b988431417_500x253.gif)

Hugging your friends is a nightmare because they always end up with a mouthful of hair.

## 22. Hood Difficultly

![Hood Difficultly](https://img.allw.mn/content/ue/xi/l4p36gqx57079cd33ea2c170909305_410x236.gif)

You find it hard to put a hood up because there is literally just too much hair to fit in there.

## 23. Windows down

![Windows down](https://img.allw.mn/content/ee/y7/hxquk4075707b8d238a7d491574887_500x239.gif)

Driving with the windows down is literally impossible because you would end up crashing the car from lack of vision in the wind!
