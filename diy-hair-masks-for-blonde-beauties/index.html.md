# DIY Hair Masks for Blonde Girls Who Want Glossy Locks ...

There’s something really wonderful about blonde hair, don’t you think? If you have a blonde mane, you probably want to keep it hydrated, healthy and gorgeous every single day. Proper care of your strands goes a long way toward ensuring that it always looks and feels its best. A hair mask on a regular basis, when added to your standard hair routine, can do wonders for your blonde hair. Making your own at home is super easy and ensures that you always have one on hand when you want it.

## 1. Lemon and Chamomile Mask

Lemon is perfect for preserving the lovely blonde color of your hair and the chamomile is great for soothing, calming and restoring tired and dull strands. This mask is super easy to make and will give you the boost you’ve been looking for.

Ingredients:

Chamomile tea, brewed and cooled
Juice of 1 lemon

Directions:

Squeeze the juice of one lemon into the tea. Massage it through your hair and let it sit for several minutes before shampooing.

Source: [bustle.com](https://www.bustle.com/articles/100015-3-diy-hair-masks-for-blonde-hair-that-shines-just-like-rita-oras)

## 2. Lemon and Honey

Again, lemon is great for blonde color. Adding honey to it helps restore it and pump it full of vital antioxidants and nutrients that help it stay healthy and gorgeous. Chances are you have what it takes to make this mask so you can use it today.

Ingredients:

Lemon juice
Honey

Directions:

Add a bit of honey to lemon juice to create a thin paste. Massage it throughout your hair and let it sit for several minutes before washing.

Source: [bustle.com](https://www.bustle.com/articles/100015-3-diy-hair-masks-for-blonde-hair-that-shines-just-like-rita-oras)

## 3. Apple Cider Vinegar

Looking for a super simple mask that you don’t have to spend a ton of time preparing? Here’s one that you are going to love. The apple cider vinegar nourishes your hair and helps enhance the natural highlights in your hair.

Ingredients:

Apple cider vinegar
Water

Directions:

Dilute the vinegar with an equal amount of water and spritz it on your hair using a squirt bottle. Let it sit for 30 minutes before rinsing.

Source: [diynatural.com](https://www.diynatural.com/how-to-naturally-lighten-hair-highlight/)

## 4. Honey and Olive Oil

When you put olive oil in your hair, you hydrate and nourish it so that it’s smooth and shiny. Combined with honey, you help bring out your natural blonde color for a head of hair that will make all the other girls jealous.

Ingredients:

¼ cup honey, warmed
¼ cup olive oil

Directions:

Combine the olive oil and honey and apply all over or just where you want to bring out your highlights. Wait 30 to 60 minutes, then wash and rinse.

Source: [diynatural.com](https://www.diynatural.com/how-to-naturally-lighten-hair-highlight/)

## 5. Easy Blonde Mask

![web page, document, area, DIY:, HAIR,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/sk/c14wlfqb57712bdd1a085391398495_600x515.jpg)

This mask contains more ingredients than some of the others, but the results will make it totally worth the effort. Once you start using this blonde hair mask, you’ll never go back. It’s that great!

Ingredients:

½ cup olive oil
2 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons lemon extract
Juice and pulp of ½ lemon
2 tablespoons wheat germ

Directions:

Combine the ingredients and mix well. Apply to your hair, then put it in a bun for 30 minutes. Wash and condition as usual.

Source: [pinterest.com](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/291537775850027646/)

## 6. For Bleached Blonde Hair

If you have blonde hair that is color treated, you need a special mask to help counteract damage and restore healthy and glorious hair. This mask is perfect for doing just that and you are going to love what it can do for you.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon coconut oil
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt
1 egg

Directions:

Combine all of the ingredients and massage it throughout your hair. Let it work its magic for 15 to 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Source: [imgladiexist.blogspot.com](http://imgladiexist.blogspot.com/2013/05/diy-hair-repair-masks.html)

## 7. Marilyn Hair Treatment

Love the glorious blonde hair that Marilyn Monroe has? This mask should help you get yours looking just as beautiful. It’s super easy and you’ll see a difference with just once use.

Ingredients:

3 cups coconut oil
1 cup chamomile tea
5 tablespoons pure lemon juice
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons honey

Directions:

Stir together all of the ingredients and work the mixture all through your hair. Wait for 30 minutes or so and wash and rinse.

Source: [musely.com](https://www.musely.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
