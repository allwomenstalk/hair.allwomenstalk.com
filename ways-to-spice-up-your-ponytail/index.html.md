# 29 Ways to Spice up Your Ponytail ...

When it comes to summer hairstyles, a ponytail is my go-to. It’s simple, keeps your hair out of your face, keeps you cool and you can make it sleek, messy, curly or even braided. If you’re looking for ways to update your ponytail for the summer, I encourage you to look through these cute and creative ways to spice up your ponytail and given them a try!
