# 7 Quick & Fabulous Hairstyles That Take Less than 10 Minutes ...

It's almost summer and who has time to do their hair – not me, that's why I came up with the top 7 hairstyles that take less than 10 minutes to do! You can still combat the heat and look amazing with some of these awesome hairstyles that take less than 10 minutes! If you're heading to the beach, take a look below so that you can cut back on the time you spend on your hair and concentrate on the time you are at the beach!

## 1. Side Twist Fishtail Braid

![Side Twist Fishtail Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/1_side-twist-fishtail-braid_384x580.jpg)

One of my favorite hairstyles that take less than 10 minutes is the fishtail braid. Now, of course, you have to know exactly how to do a fishtail braid before you attempt to do this particular style in less than 10 minutes, but once you have the techniques down, you'll be able to complete this amazing [side twist](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/side-twist-hairstyles-for-girls-who-are-bored-of-braids/) fishtail braid in no time at all! It's an adorable look for the beach or even for work!

## 2. Sock Bun

![Sock Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/2_sock-bun_443x666.jpg)

This is a hugely popular look right now. I've seen so many of my friends at my office walking around with this particular look and with good reason, it's adorable! All you do is roll a sock (that is in the shape of a doughnut) down a ponytail, tuck your hair into it and you've got an amazing bun!

## 3. The Side Poof

![The Side Poof](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/3_the-side-poof_500x644.jpg)

The poof is something that I absolutely love. It's super easy to pull off and it's more like a knot along the side of your head. All you do is pull your hair to the side, make it a little knot and tuck your side pieces in. That's it! Super easy and it's oh-so-refreshing and cute!

## 4. Tousled Side Pony Tail

![Tousled Side Pony Tail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/4_tousled-side-pony-tail_452x591.jpg)

If you have long hair and are constantly looking for something to do with it, why not get some awesome beachy waves into it and then pull it into a beautiful side ponytail? This is truly one of the greatest and easiest hairstyles that you can complete in just a matter of moments!

## 5. Pin Curl Twist

![Pin Curl Twist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/5_pin-curl-twist_499x750.jpg)

If you want something that is super easy and quick, but it looks like you've spent hours on it, just pull your hair back into a messy bun, twist a few pieces in the front around to the back and pin it all together! I've done this particular one a few times and every time I do, I get a ton of compliments!

## 6. High Volume Pony Tail

![High Volume Pony Tail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/6_high-volume-pony-tail_553x702.jpg)

A ponytail is one thing, but one that has a poof in the front and is super high in volume? That's another thing! You'll need your hair dryer for this one to get the volume, but that's the only time that you'll spend on your hair! Just gather it all back and pull it into a ponytail!

## 7. Back Knot Braids

![Back Knot Braids](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/06/7_back-knot-braids_368x534.jpg)

Finally, if you want an amazing look that won't take any time at all, go with some amazing braids! This look is not only incredible on long hair, but it works for shorter hair too! I do this look a lot too and I love it! It's so easy and it beats the heat perfectly!

So girls, these are just a few of the top looks that will take you less than 10 minutes to do! What other hairstyles have you done that are quick and easy? Do tell!
