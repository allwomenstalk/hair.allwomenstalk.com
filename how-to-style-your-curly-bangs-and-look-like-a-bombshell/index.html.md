# How to Style Your Curly Bangs and Look like a Bombshell ...

If you have curly hair, it might be hard to keep your bangs in place. However, you have a few different options. According to _Allure,_ here are a few ways to rock curly bangs:

## 1. STRAIGHT BANGS, CURLY HAIR

![STRAIGHT BANGS, CURLY HAIR](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/ja/tjiwrgn456e94e1e9c619923631297_420x432.jpg)

All you have to do is grab a straightener and flatten your bangs. The rest of your hair can remain curly.

## 2. POUFY, MIDDLE-PART BANGS

![POUFY, MIDDLE-PART BANGS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r7/sp/lhv23ed356e94e1e65b51676330708_420x560.jpg)

"Separate damp bangs into one-inch sections and smooth on a light styling product while twisting hair toward your face from root to end."

## 3. BRAIDED BANGS

![BRAIDED BANGS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kr/vr/py7w636b56e94e1e9ee5d698779526_420x560.jpg)

Try making a few different braids on either side of your head. Then you should push those braids to the sides and use pins to keep them in place.

## 4. BUNNED BANGS

![BUNNED BANGS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cz/n3/w67v0v9n56e94e1eb6871538907937_420x560.jpg)

"Part hair into three or four haphazard sections from the crown backward, securing each with a small elastic. Smooth hair down with pomade or mousse before twisting each section into a loose bun."

## 5. PINEAPPLE BANGS

![PINEAPPLE BANGS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/yj/b17iv2ss56e94e1f24c98098104521_420x560.jpg)

You don't need actual bangs to pull off this look. All you have to do is put a ponytail on the top of your head. Then pin certain strands of hair in front of your forehead to create the illusion of bangs.

## 6. CHOPPY BABY BANGS

![CHOPPY BABY BANGS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/ev/wodj1uju56e94e1f35a8b228437354_420x560.jpg)

If you have the right haircut, then these bangs won't be hard to create. All you'll have to do is use the curler on your bangs every morning.
