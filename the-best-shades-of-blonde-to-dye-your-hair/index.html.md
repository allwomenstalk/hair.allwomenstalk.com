# The Best Shades of Blonde to Dye Your Hair ...

Haven't you heard that blondes have the most fun? That might just be a myth, but it doesn't change the fact that you should think about dying your hair a lighter shade. After all, it's always fun to try something new. A new hair color can change your entire appearance, so if you're looking for a new look, here are some of the best shades of blonde to dye your hair:

## 1. Strawberry Blonde

![Strawberry Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/o7/n2zx0ig355620c7b2d364427871395_592x846.jpg)

If you love red heads, but aren't ready to dye your hair such a strong color, you can start out by going strawberry blonde. It has a reddish tint to it, which is perfect for all of you ladies with rosy cheeks. It'll complement your natural beauty.

## 2. Ash Grey Blonde

![Ash Grey Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sp/hw/oit0t4s755620c9ea0c48411392239_600x903.jpg)

This is one of the colors you should try if you're a fan of the Granny trend. If you don't want to wait for your hair to go grey, then you can speed up the process by dying it ash grey blonde. Don't worry, because everyone will realize you did it on purpose and will applaud your bravery. This trend isn't one for the weak willed.

## 3. Platinum Blonde

![Platinum Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/or/eo60055z55620cb8cf8ed379764749_600x799.jpg)

You've probably thought about dying your hair this color, because the girls that try it always rock it. The best part? It's so easy to add bright colors to the tips. You can create an ombre effect with pink, blue, green, or any other color you can think of, because they'll all show up vibrantly on platinum blonde hair.

## 4. Bright Golden Blonde

![Bright Golden Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/eo/fpyvuxwk55620cd4e5339598901152_500x666.jpg)

Do you want your hair to have a golden hue that'll make you feel like a real life princess? Then dye your hair a bright golden blonde! You'll risk having your friends call you Goldilocks, but you won't care, because you'll be looking as fabulous as ever. This hair color will brighten up any woman's face, making her look sweet and approachable at any time.

## 5. Dirty Blonde

![Dirty Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/lo/ke3w3cvd55620cee93c58407328291_600x855.jpg)

Are you torn between being a blonde or a brunette? Then dirty blonde hair is the perfect option for you. It has a bit of both hues, so you won't have to choose between them. Besides, as long as your roots aren't showing, this color will always appear natural. No more annoying questions about whether your hair is "fake."

## 6. Light Blonde

![Light Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/n2/jq1q8z3d555f582230589502796148_600x900.jpg)

When you think of a blonde woman, this is probably the shade that first pops into your mind. It's a natural looking color that a lot of women look absolutely stunning with. If you want to make your hair lighter than it has ever been, then this is a pretty safe color to try.

## 7. Sandy Blonde

![Sandy Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a6/y5/m0xx5rl555620d08d3765345223313_600x744.jpg)

The name alone sounds absolutely adorable! This color is great for women who want their hair to look like it has texture. Plus, it's another shade that is super easy to add highlights or bright colored tips to. You can do pretty much anything to it and it would look fabulous.

There are so many different colors to dye your hair that you could spend your whole life inside of the hair salon. You might as well try out whichever styles you're drawn to, because you never know which one you'll want to stick with for the rest of your life. What color is your hair currently?
