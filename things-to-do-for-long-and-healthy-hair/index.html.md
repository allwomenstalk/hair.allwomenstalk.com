# 7 Things to do for Long and Healthy Hair ...

Are you looking for things to do for long and [healthy hair](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/secrets-of-healthy-hair-from-the-professionals-themselves/)? I have been in the process of growing my own hair out so I have been looking for things that help, too. These are the best things to do for long and healthy hair that I have found. I can definitely tell that these things are helping my hair grow faster and that it is healthier, too.

## 1. Get It Trimmed

![Get It Trimmed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/us/de/hhzj4ygo_600x782.jpg)

Getting a trim when you are growing out your hair can feel counter-productive, but it is actually an important step in the things to do for long and healthy hair. When you do not have your hair trimmed regularly, you start to develop split ends. They can keep right on splitting and really affect the health of your hair. They are a big contributor to hair breakage, as well.

## 2. Take a Hair and Nail Vitamin

![Take a Hair and Nail Vitamin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pv/y9/xj/n3p362t6_600x886.jpg)

A vitamin that promotes hair health can really make a big difference in how fast your hair grows and the overall health of it. Biotin is also something you can take for your hair health and growth. I take 1000 mcg of Biotin a day and I can tell that my hair has grown a lot faster since I started taking it. A lot of people have had great results by trying a supplement like this. Of course, you should never take any supplement without checking with your doctor first.

## 3. Eat Well

![Eat Well](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c3/ut/gy/v069f91d_600x902.jpg)

What you eat is going to show in your hair. It sounds crazy but it really is true. If you eat a ton of junk, expect your hair to show it. If you eat healthy, your hair is going to be healthier. It takes all kinds of vitamins and nutrients to have healthy hair. Getting enough protein is important to your hair growth and strength, too.

## 4. Massage Your Scalp

![Massage Your Scalp](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/ou/vi/ae80czs0_600x804.jpg)

Experts say that if you massage your scalp it will help your hair to grow. They say that it increases circulation and that helps to stimulate growth. I typically will do this when I go to bed at night. It is relaxing and helps me to fall asleep quicker, too. My hair is growing faster since I started using all of these little tricks.

## 5. Be Careful with Your Hair

![Be Careful with Your Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/qo/bu/sw2tcscx_600x901.jpg)

You cannot expect to have good hair if you treat it roughly. It is not going to look healthy if you jerk as you comb and put accessories in that are too tight. Treating your hair roughly does not affect the growth but it does affect the quality of your hair. If your hair gets to be in bad shape, then you have to have it cut and that certainly affects the growth of your hair. It is best to be careful with your hair in how you comb, brush and style it.

## 6. Buy the Good Stuff

![Buy the Good Stuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nd/bh/s3/roto0y8r_600x800.jpg)

If you can, buy quality products for your hair. This includes shampoo, conditioner, deep conditioner and other hair products that you use. There are a lot of decent drugstore products available if you cannot afford salon products. I have had especially good luck with Dove hair products. If possible, splurge for the salon products.

## 7. Be Patient

![Be Patient](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/08/qo/z6/tnxk02ni_600x798.jpg)

Another component of growing your hair out long and getting it healthy is to just have patience. Being patient while it grows is the hardest part of all. Your hair will probably go through some awkward stages where you will be tempted to cut it but you just have to wait it out. It takes time but these good habits will pay off. Soon you will have long, healthy, beautiful hair if you stay the course.

Growing your hair out is a process and these steps should help make that a little easier. What advice has worked for you when you were growing your hair out? I would love to hear your best tips!
