# Brilliant Recipes to Make Your Own  Natural Hair Dye ...

Girls all over the world like to color their hair. It's fun to get a whole new look from time to time. It's also great to highlight your color and make it look as great as it possibly can. If you're leery of traditional hair dye, it's easy to make your own recipes to color your hair with. Each of these tutorials will show you how to make DIY natural hair dye that will give you the color of your dreams in no time at all.

## 1. Temporary Hair Color

If you want to test out a new color before giving it a try, this easy recipe lets you do just that. The temporary color will last for a short time, allowing you to decide if you like it or not.

## 2. Cinnamon Hair Dye

Not only does cinnamon smell good, but it gives you a glorious brown color that you are going to love. This video also gives you some great tips for getting the color on just right.

## 3. Coffee Hair Color

Coffee has been used as a hair color for years. Use this recipe to turn light brown hair darker or to give your already dark hair a nice new shine.

## 4. Potato Juice Hair Dye

If you've noticed some gray hairs, this the natural dye for you. The potato juice helps mask gray and bring back the natural color you were born with. Perfect!

## 5. Use Beet Juice

If you want some fantastic red highlights, beet juice is an easy way to get just that. The color will last for several weeks and brings out the glow of brown and black hair.

## 6. Fruit Hair Dye

There are lots of fruits with juices and flesh that can naturally add a new color to your hair. Use pomegranates, like in this video, for lovely red highlights.

## 7. Homemade Hair Lightener

The trick to this homemade dye that lightens your hair is lemon juice, chamomile tea and honey. If you have always wanted blonde streaks, this is the perfect way to get them.

## 8. Get Blonde Hair

The mixture of peroxide and baking soda in this natural recipe will help you lighten your hair all over. Use caution because too much peroxide can damage your hair. Follow the directions carefully for the best results.

## 9. Henna Hair Dye

Henna is the perfect choice for giving your hair a lovely caramel brown color. It's great for an all over dye job or as a way to add natural highlights to your hair.

## 10. Blueberry Hair Dye

Would you believe that blueberries can make a great hair dye? They won't turn your hair blue, but they will give you a darker, reddish tint that you'll love.

## 11. 100% Organic

If you want an organic DIY hair dye, you can use this recipe to get what you want. It's easy to make, gives you a great new shade and helps keep hair soft and shiny. Yes!

## 12. For Natural Hair

If you have natural hair, it's probably pretty dark. That makes it a bit harder to get a new color. This DIY formula is perfect for lightening natural hair and giving you a brand new look.

## 13. Indigo Powder Paste

Green tea water, lime juice and indigo powder is used to make this hair dye. The lime juice lightens hair while the indigo powder and green tea gives it a golden glow.

## 14. Tea Hair Dye

Tea is a natural choice when you want a DIY home hair dye. All you have to do is steep the tea and then soak your hair with it. The longer you steep the tea, the more vivid your color will be.

## 15. Honey Hair Lightener

Here's another fabulous way to lighten your hair. Use this recipe for an all over color change or to add highlights to your existing color.

What color do you want to dye your hair? Which recipe will you use?
