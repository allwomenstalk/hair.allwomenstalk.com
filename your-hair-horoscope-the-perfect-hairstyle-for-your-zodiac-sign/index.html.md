# Your 2019 Hair Horoscope ...

Early in a new year it is always tempting to want to make a big change or a fresh start in some area of your life. For many people, it could be a job or a relationship, but if you’re looking for something a little smaller scale, then what about your hair!? It might not seem like a big deal, but your hair and the way that you choose to wear it can have a big impact on your personality. Here is **the perfect hairstyle for your zodiac sign** this year.

## 1. Aries

![Hair, White, Shoulder, Black, Clothing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hx/oa/g2v2px1w5c5e26a24ef48075063575_600x600.jpg)

It’s all about chic sophistication and clean lines for you this year, and nothing evokes those clean lines better than a classic slicked back ponytail.

## 2. Taurus

![Hair, Face, Hairstyle, Chin, Eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/a0/pe08yvnj5c5e26d6b391b597321224_600x750.jpg)

It’s time to stop hiding behind your hair and let your face shine, so something like a short, sleek bob cut is perfect. It’s a stylish cut but it still manages to show off your natural features.

## 3. Gemini

![Hair, Face, Hairstyle, Long hair, Black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/c1/p3kj8z3y5c5e26e94b3a4107282450_600x725.jpg)

You are the most relaxed of all the signs, so you can get away with a head of tousled waves that help to frame your face and give you that cool, casual, approachable vibe.

## 4. Cancer

![Hair, Face, Lip, Hairstyle, Beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/cz/g5y8mwe75c5e277ccaffe089743978_600x749.jpg)

Parisian bobs are the perfect style for a Cancer this year. It’s a classic look with an extra modern twist, which is the kind of combination that perfectly sums up your personality!

## 5. Leo

![Hair, Hairstyle, Ringlet, Long hair, Brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rj/zt/b26x72v15c5e27c7daebf243251755_600x600.jpg)

The more volume, the better for a Leo! Unleash that inner lion that is waiting to spring out of you by achieving a mane of your own with some amazing, bouncy curls.

## 6. Virgo

![Hair, Face, Eyebrow, Blond, Hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/j8/pwmnzzo35c5e27ee4c864394403674_600x750.jpg)

You have the kind of effortless, natural beauty that means you are able to pull of a messy top knot and still look like a million dollars. Make the most of it!

## 7. Libra

![Hair, Headpiece, Hairstyle, Hair accessory, Fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/d3/x8f4qjsv5c5e2830c80e7739698472_600x601.jpg)

You can get away with pretty much any hairstyle that you want to, but the thing that can set a Libra apart from the rest is a pretty floral accessory added to your locks.

## 8. Scorpio

![Hair, Hairstyle, Pink, Eyebrow, Beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pa/k8/axftl14n5c5e285c62886384110739_600x750.jpg)

Athleisure is a style that very much sums up your attitude to fashion, and a hairstyle that accompanies this vibe perfectly is the stand out double Dutch braid.

## 9. Sagittarius

![Hair, Blond, Hairstyle, Hair coloring, Long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/hd/roesue335c5e2877890d5935600628_600x750.jpg)

2019 is a year when you should be rocking your natural hair in whatever state it presents itself. Where curled, straight or wavy, a half up do is always a good look.

## 10. Capricorn

![Hair, Face, Black hair, Eyebrow, Hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b1/bh/ae9iis205c5e2887c1e24771737350_600x674.jpg)

Again, natural hair is very much the way you should be going this year, but the important thing for a Capricorn is volume, volume, volume!

## 11. Aquarius

![Hair, Face, Eyebrow, Hairstyle, Black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e4/os/fddzfxbu5c5e28adf0e88161984789_600x600.jpg)

You have the sort of personality where you can pull off all kinds of retro fashion, so a high ponytail is something to consider this year.

## 12. Pisces

![Hair, Hairstyle, Long hair, Face, Brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/of/lc4sj3hu5c5e28c68814c283245649_600x726.jpg)

Long mermaid waves are the perfect look for a Pisces. They are stunning and can unleash your inner sexuality and animal mangnetism!
