# 7 Hairstyles for Girls with a round Face Shape ...

Want to change something? Do you want **haircuts for round faces?** Sometimes, hair changes can be tough for women, but you're going to find all the inspiration you need right here. Check out these super modern and fabulous **hairstyles for round faces** for a brand new look you're going to love.

## 1. LONG LUSCIOUS LAYERS

![hair, human hair color, beauty, black hair, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/h9/qxfkpo18599f341e7aa15478951057_600x600.jpg)

For those lucky girls who have long and gorgeous tresses, layers are one of the **best hairstyles for round faces** and will help to open up your face and showcase your natural beauty. Just don’t forget to do a side part to show your neck as well.

## 2. A PRETTY PIXIE

![hair, purple, human hair color, hairstyle, asymmetric cut,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/y5/c79wyiwj599f35a9a8493150821694_600x628.jpg)

For girls who like big changes, and to experiment with their style, a short pixie is perfect for your round face.

## 3. VOLUMINOUS BOB

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, human hair color, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/g6/hm2hwj9b599f34d17bbbf594735084_600x750.jpg)

A long bob looks terrific, especially if you add more volume with a curling iron. Curls and waves will show your lovely round face.

## 4. SLEEK, STRAIGHT STRANDS

![human hair color, eyebrow, beauty, chin, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/in/yi0sudh6599f333a77b7d470607186_600x600.jpg)

Shoulder length or longer hair looks so amazing when it’s straight. A part in the middle of your tresses gives you a lovely proportional look.

## 5. SEXY SIDE PART

![eyewear, sunglasses, human hair color, vision care, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kc/jq/aeec8drk599f360b7b072502021994_600x577.jpg)

Such a hairstyle helps you to create an illusion of length to a rounder shaped face. Go for a side part and you'll see what I mean.

## 6. Below the BROW FRINGE

![hair, human hair color, hairstyle, eyebrow, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/s8/ijjwovh3599f370169408678055794_500x500.jpg)

Below the brow fringe looks perfect and completes a round face shape by calling attention to your eyes. It looks terrific with long and short tresses as well.

## 7. SWEET SIDE SWEPT BANGS

![hair, human hair color, hairstyle, black hair, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l1/ie/lwzooib4599f36d86b699531066223_600x600.jpg)

This provides your haircut with a structure and volume, calls attention to the cheekbones and makes you look absolutely incredible.

Stay confident and gorgeous with any of these hairstyles and you'll accentuate your beautiful round face in the best way.
