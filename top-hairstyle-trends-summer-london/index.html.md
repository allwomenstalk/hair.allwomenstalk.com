# 5 Top Hairstyle Trends for Summer 2017 in London ...

Great hair done without too much time or effort can boost your self-esteem and make a huge difference to how you feel! New hairstyles can make you feel sexy and good – all you need for a sunny day. Want to know the top London hairstyle trends for Summer 2017? Read on…

## 1. HIGH PONYTAILS

![hair, eyebrow, beauty, chin, human hair color,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p1/uf/soe8mp5c596945b48ac84335026344_600x750.jpg)

High ponytails add elegance to any occasion - slick hair all the way up or opt for a rumpled texture. Remember that the tighter you secure your ponytail the more lifted your face will look. Loose ribbons add feminine touches, and cool accessories give you endless combinations. To hold the look spray hairspray onto your brush and smooth over.

## 2. PLATINUM BLONDE

![hair, eyebrow, blond, human hair color, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/oq/d5qg2g3w5969459d82bf3655006681_600x600.jpg)

Platinum blonde is the silvery colour to opt for this season. Short styles are preferable as it’s so much easier to bleach. Crops with texture retain femininity, and good conditioning makes sure hair looks shiny, glossy, and healthy. Prepare to turn heads and break hearts with sizzling platinum blonde shades offered to you by reputable hair salons.

## 3. ROMANTIC BRAIDS

![hair, hairstyle, human hair color, blond, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/ya/eaxpca6t596945cf71a09947339658_600x750.jpg)

Romantic braids are soft and light, leaving hair undone and untidy! Hair oil leaves hair looking glossy and shiny whist the braid remains the dominant feature. You can experiment to change your look to suit your mood as pretty, soft and feminine styles are perfect for the run-up to summer. Accessorize with flowers, pearls, ribbons, and bows.

## 4. FLAT WAVES

![hair, human hair color, blond, hairstyle, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/zi/xqnt9d7o596945e647aea243087357_600x750.jpg)

Flat waves can be added to longer hair by either sleeping on braids for curls without heat damage, or by taking sections of hair and wrapping it around your curling iron to create loose s-shaped waves. It’s curl without the volume! The flat iron wave is perfect for a quick touch-up on hair at any time of the day as you can wash and air-dry your hair the night before.

## 5. EYE LENGTH FRINGE CUT HAIR

![eyewear, hair, human hair color, sunglasses, vision care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hc/zx/cahwco6y596945f75c87b169585539_600x600.jpg)

Eye length fringe provides change without having a major hair cut! It’s the ideal way to get versatile and interesting looks by creating a center fringe one day, and a side fringe or pinned back look the next. The key is finding the right one for your face shape - consult your hair stylist to get a fringe that provides a great focal point.
