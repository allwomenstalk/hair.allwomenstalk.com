# Prom Hairstyles That Wont Come Undone While Dancing ...

Everybody wants to look their best during prom. That's why you'll pay so much money on a dress and shoes, and then get your hair and makeup professionally done. Of course, you should aim to look as beautiful at the end of the night as you did in the beginning. In order for that to happen, try wearing one of these prom hairstyles that won't come undone when you dance:

## 1. Mermaid Hairstyle

This is a glamorous, elegant style that'll keep the hair out of your face. Yes, it is a little complicated, but that's okay! You don't have to do it yourself. You could always hire a professional to do it for you. Just bring in a photograph of the style that you'd like and they'll get right to work! That way, you'll have one less thing to stress over.

## 2. Party Updo

If you don't have the money or the time to get your hair done professionally, then you should try wearing an updo like this one. It only takes five minutes to complete, but it'll look like you've spent hours in front of the mirror. All you'll need are some hair ties, bobby pins, and a sparkly clip!

## 3. Braided Headband

Most girls wear their hair up for prom, but you don't have to follow that trend. You can take the advice from this tutorial and keep your hair down. Of course, you'll want to do something special to it, which is when braids will come in handy. Use that braid to create a headband out of your own hair and you'll look like a goddess!

## 4. Side Swept Cascading Curls

If you can't decide between keeping your hair down and putting your hair up, you should try this style! You'll have half of it pinned up while the other half is pushed to the side. In the end, your hair will look thick, curly, and flirty. It's the perfect look for impressing your crush!

## 5. Side Updo

Here's an updo that doesn't take all that much time. It's perfect for any formal event, which means you can use it again in the future if you ever attend a wedding or a fancy dinner party. It'll come in handy time and time again.

## 6. Heidi Klum Inspired Hair

Do you want to look like a supermodel when you step out of your limo? Then you should follow the advice in this tutorial, which was inspired by the gorgeous Heidi Klum. When you're finished, you'll end up with a braid at the front of your head and curls at the back of your head. Basically, you'll look as glamorous as Heidi!

## 7. Traditional Bun

If you want to wear a more traditional hairstyle, then you can just put your hair up in a bun. Of course, not any bun will do. This is prom! You have to go big or go home. That's why you should follow the advice in this tutorial step by step to create a high, tidy bun for long hair.

Make sure you secure your hair with as many pins and products as you need. You don't want the style coming undone while you dance! How are you planning on styling your hair for prom?
