# Pro Tips on How to Get Stronger Thicker Hair ...

We all have beautiful hair that ranges from straight to curly, but all girls need **tips for thicker and stronger hair**. It is important to make sure that we protect our hair and use the right products in order to have the best hair possible. Use these **tips for thicker and stronger hair** and you'll be good to go.

## 1. Use a Protein Treatment

Using a protein treatment is one of the best **tips for thicker and stronger hair**. Protein treatments are used once or twice a week to help keep your hair stronger. It boosts your hair with nutrients that are vital for your strands and fibrous proteins such as keratin help keep your hair strong. This treatment is also great for those who color, lighten or chemically texturize their hair. Protein treatments are also used to deep condition your hair.

## 2. Castor Oil

Castor oil is great for achieving strong and thick hair because it is high in vitamin E and fatty acids which promote hair growth. You can massage castor oil into your scalp regularly, 2 to 3 times a week. It helps to moisturize your scalp naturally without clogging your pores.

## 3. Avoid Excessive Heat

Heat damage is one of the worst things that can happen to your hair because you lose your natural curl pattern and your hair becomes weak. It is best to allow your hair to air dry after you wash it or use the cool setting on your blow dryer. This will help to keep your curls and prevent your hair from breakage. If you must use heat, try to use it at least twice a month with a heat protectant.

## 4. Moisturize Your Hair

Moisture is great for your hair because it makes your hair less prone to breakage and it helps keep the length of your hair. It is important to use a moisturizer that has water because water is great for keeping your cuticles healthy and nourished.

## 5. Protective Styles

Protective styles are great for retaining length and preventing excessive manipulation to your strands. These styles can range from braids, buns, wigs and many more. Remember, just because your hair is in a protective style, doesn't mean you can neglect it. Always be sure to wash your hair to remove build up and stick with a regime that works best for you.
