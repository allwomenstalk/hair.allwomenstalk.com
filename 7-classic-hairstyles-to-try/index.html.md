# 7 Timeless Classic Hairstyles That Never Go Out of Style

Changing your hairstyle can make you look younger and more attractive almost instantaneously. Many women are hesitant to try a new look, but if you remind yourself that your hair will ultimately grow, it won’t seem like such a major risk. It is important to consider your face shape and hair texture before choosing a new hairstyle, as you don’t want to be left with a haircut that is unflattering or difficult to style. These timeless looks offer versatility and elegance for any occasion. Here are 7 classic hairstyles to try.

## 1. The Versatile Bob

The great thing about the bob is that it is a versatile haircut. You can wear it super short around your ears or longer, falling on your shoulders. You can have it leveled equally all over or graduated from back to front or vice versa. Also, the cool thing about a bob is that when it grows out, it doesn’t look untidy and unkempt. It is easy to transform the bob into another look as and when you choose. If you're looking for inspiration on how to maintain this style, check out our guide on [short hair styling](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-a-bob/).

## 2. The Long and Strong Tresses

Wearing your hair long and strong can have a dazzling effect on your overall appearance. Whether you choose to keep it straight or wavy, long hair has always been a favorite with women. It offers you a range of styling options from elaborate up-dos, chic [ponytails](#4), braids, and more. To keep your hair healthy, follow these [essential hair care tips](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/hair-care-tips/). With some clever highlights, you can really rock this look.

Why choose a classic hairstyle?

- They never go out of style.

- They are versatile for different occasions.

- Most are easy to maintain and grow out gracefully.

- They complement various face shapes and hair textures.

## 3. The Pixie Look

This look became all the rage in the early 60s when it was donned by the iconic supermodel [Twiggy](https://www.vogue.co.uk/article/twiggy-biography). Suddenly, women everywhere were converting their long tresses into smart pixie cuts, and although decades have passed, its popularity hasn’t withered. The pixie look doesn’t suit everyone, so check your face shape and listen to your stylist before proceeding. It's a bold choice that highlights facial features beautifully.

## 4. The High Ponytail

The high ponytail is a classic that never fails to look both sporty and extremely chic. It's a versatile style that works for the gym or a red-carpet event. I think the high ponytail works best with straight long hair, so use a flat iron to straighten your tresses and then tie it up in a slick, polished finish. For those with shorter hair, [stunning updos](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/stunning-updos-for-long-hair/) can offer a similar sophisticated vibe.

## 5. The Wavy Voluminous Look

Right from the days of the original Charlie’s Angels, the wavy, voluminous look has been a staple of Hollywood glamour. While it has evolved from the heavily fringed style of the '70s to a more understated and elegant look, it is still incredibly popular. Most women can carry it off to perfection with the right [volumizing products](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/best-products-for-wavy-hair/).

| Hairstyle | Best Face Shape | Hair Texture |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| The Bob | All shapes | Straight or Wavy |
| Pixie Cut | Oval or Heart | Fine to Medium |
| High Ponytail | Round or Square | Long and Straight |
| Ballerina Bun | Oval | Long and Smooth |

## 6. The Messy Curly Statement

A messy curly head of hair can look incredibly effortless and stylish on the right face shape. Applying a styling gel or mousse can add extra gloss and definition to your curls, making the look even more appealing. Tying curly hair up with a few loose curls falling around the face is also a great touch for a romantic feel. It's about embracing natural texture.

## 7. The Ballerina Bun

[Audrey Hepburn](https://www.biography.com/actor/audrey-hepburn) first brought this look to the forefront in the iconic film _Breakfast at Tiffany’s_, and ever since then, women everywhere have been trying to replicate its elegance and glamour. If you have an oval face and [longish hair](#2), you too can try it out. You’re sure to feel like a film star. It's the ultimate "polished" look for any formal event.

Classic hairstyles become timeless because they survive the greatest test of all: time. All the looks mentioned above have made it through the decades and remain the most sought-after styles for women today. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance bob or a glamorous ballerina bun, these classics never disappoint.
