# Locks of Love \- How to Make Your Hair Smell Amazing All Day Long ...

We pay so much attention to the looks of our locks that we sometimes forget about the smell of them. After all, most people don't come close to us to smell our hair, anyway. However, you never know when a cutie will lean in to whisper to you. That means the more delicious your hair smells, the better. If you've never really thought about the odor of your hair, here are a few ways to make it smell amazing all day long:

## 1. Brush Your Hair to Get Rid of Dirt

Brushing your hair does more than keep it smooth and tangle-free. It also makes sure that there's no dust or dead skin sticking to it. So the more you brush it, the better your chances are that it will smell great all day long.

## 2. Put Lavender Oil or Jasmine Oil on Your Hairbrush

There are specific types of oils that are healthy for your hair and will make it smell absolutely amazing. The best types to use are lavender oil or jasmine oil, which have delicious scents. All you have to do is put a little on your hairbrush and then brush your hair with it. If you're worried about ruining your brush, then just use your hands instead.

## 3. Invest in a Scented Hairspray

If you hate the smell of your regular hairspray, head to the store to buy a new brand. There are different types that are actually scented. Find a smell that appeals to you, so that when you spray your hair into place, you'll also be making yourself smell amazing. It kills two birds with one stone.

## 4. Buy Shampoo with a Delicious Scent

You have to wash your hair, no matter what, so you might as well use a great smelling shampoo and conditioner. If you do, then there will be a noticeable difference. Haven't you ever stepped out of the shower and had a friend or boyfriend comment on how amazing you smell? That's all because of your shampoo!

## 5. Use Lemon Juice in Your Hair

Lemon juice is healthy for your hair, so if you like the smell of it, you can add it to your hair. That way, you'll have the scent on you all day long. How do you go about this? Just grab a lemon or two, juice it, and apply it to your gorgeous hair.

## 6. Soak Your Hair in Rose Water

Rose water smells delicious, which is why you might want to soak your hair in it. It'll assure that your locks get saturated with a specific scent. You won't even have to put on perfume after using rose water because you'll already smell amazing.

## 7. Stay Away from Heat to Avoid Having Your Scalp Sweat

Try not to spend too much time outdoors when it's burning hot out. If you do, then you're going to sweat. Even though you'll probably be more concerned with your underarms, it's possible for your scalp to sweat as well, and you don't want that to happen.

You don't need to spray your hair with sticky perfume in order to have it smell nice. All you need to do is follow one or two of these tips. Do you have any additional advice for keeping your hair fresh?
