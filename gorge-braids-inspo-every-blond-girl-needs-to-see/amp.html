This beautiful blonde-haired girl in the picture is wearing a classic French braid, which is a timeless hairstyle that has been around for centuries. This particular braid is created by starting with three sections of hair and then weaving them together in an over-under pattern. The braid is then secured with a hair tie or elastic band at the end.

French braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For a more formal look, you can add accessories like clips, ribbons, and bows. For a more casual look, you can leave the braid loose and let it hang down your back.

The French braid is also versatile and can be used to create a variety of different hairstyles. You can create a half-up half-down style by leaving the top portion of your hair loose and braiding the bottom. You can also create a halo braid by wrapping the braid around your head and securing it with bobby pins.
