# How to Make Dry Shampoo Your Hair's BFF ...

We have to face the fact that dry shampoo is a genius product. Whatever your views on how often you should wash your hair, dry shampoo can not only prove to be your savior but it’s clever enough to among your regular hair care products for general styling. Coming in aerosol, mousse and powder form, dry shampoo is a real 21st century product for the 21st century woman. It’s more for than when you skip the suds and here’s why and how …

## 1. Use Dry Shampoo to Go Longer between Washes

Of course this is the obvious use – this is what dry shampoo was invented for. So you don't want to shampoo your hair every day but are worried about the natural oils in your scalp that make your hair look greasy. Try a wash free day and use dry shampoo instead. You might need to experiment with a few to find the one that works best for your hair. Try travel sizes to avoid expense and waste.

## 2. You Can Make Bombshell Waves Using Dry Shampoo

Look like a glam goddess using your dry shampoo. You will need a 1-inch clampless curling wand to create waves in 2-inch sections. Next up, spray some dry shampoo on your roots. Now, flip your head over and start running your fingers gently through your hair. You can use a hairspray to set it and maintain volume.

## 3. Dry Shampoo Can Protect Your Hair from Humidity

Irrespective of your hair texture, you can use your dry shampoo to tame your mane even in humid weather. Simply apply dry shampoo to your roots and style as usual. This will keep you from dealing with limp locks even if it is humid out there. You may want to use a mousse-based dry shampoo if you have curly or wavy hair – this will help prevent frizz.

## 4. Dry Shampoo is Great when You Don’t Have Time to Shower after the Gym

One of many things you can do with dry shampoo is to avoid sweat-matted, frizzy hair after a workout. If your workout session doesn't start at the end of the day, the chances are you have some post-gym plans for the day as well. Your dry shampoo will help you get a fresh look without even getting your hair wet. Simply apply it directly to your hairline as well as the under layers of your hair for a perfect look.

## 5. You Can Use Dry Shampoo Overnight Too

You cannot use your regular shampoo before going to bed and notice its effects when you wake up the next morning – your hair will look untidy and frizzy. Dry shampoo is definitely an exception because you can use it at night and save yourself from shampooing in the morning. Just spray some of it on your roots and tie your hair, preferably in a top knot. The shampoo will work to absorb dirt and oils for a fresh, clean mane.

## 6. Dry Shampoo Can Give You Bigger, Better Hair

You can rely on dry shampoo for longer hair with a better hair texture. It's suitable even for hair that's thin. It revitalizes the roots and adds volume to your hair. The good thing is that unlike many other products, the volume it adds to your thin hair will stay for the rest of the day. You can even find dry shampoos especially designed to give you a volume boost.

## 7. Dry Shampoo is Family Friendly

Your guy can use dry shampoo too – either to go for longer between washes, after the gym, or even as a pre-date “freshener-upper” when going out straight from work. And guess what – there are even dry shampoos formulated for dogs, so you can jeep your pampered pooch as beautiful as you.

Dry shampoo is trendy and cool and that’s great because we love products that make our life easier – right? Do you use dry shampoo?
