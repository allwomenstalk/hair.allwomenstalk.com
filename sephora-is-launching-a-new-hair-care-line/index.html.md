# Sephora is Launching a New Hair Care Line ...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zu/in/nv5zkktv56d44d3516a52614370900_600x285.jpg)

Makeup isn't the only thing worth buying at Sephora. Soon, the popular store is going to start carrying the Madam C.J. Walker Beauty Culture hair collection. It's "a 25-piece line that caters to women of color, though anyone can use it. If you have curly, wavy, or straight/heat-styled hair, there's something for you." According to [Seventeen,](https://www.seventeen.com/beauty/hair/news/a38453/sephoras-newest-haircare-line-is-a-total-game-changer/) it's a real game changer.

The new hair care line was created by [Sundial Brands](https://www.sheamoisture.com/) and will be available at Sephora in Spring. That means you should save up your money, so you can buy something special once the products hit the shelves. Are you looking forward to buying their items, like coconut oil and [deep conditioning treatment](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/steps-for-the-perfect-deep-conditioning-treatment-for-your-hair/)?
