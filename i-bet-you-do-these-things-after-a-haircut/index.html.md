# I Bet You do These Things after a Haircut ...

A visit to the salon is always such a treat. From the nice head massage you get from the shampooing process to the new cut or style that makes you look and feel good, it's a joy. It's no wonder then that most of us leave the salon practically walking on air. And we can't but help indulge in our experience - outwardly. Here's how the love of a new haircut manifests itself. I bet you can totally relate to this.

## 1. Delayed Decision

![Delayed Decision](https://img.allw.mn/content/ia/wp/lmoz8thm56f31e7ecbaae099699974_500x206.gif)

You just have to wonder why you left it so long to change the color / go short/ get a bob. 'Cos, dammit girl you look good,

## 2. Reflections

![Reflections](https://img.allw.mn/content/v2/kc/dxijgc5i56f316c0d8512598875917_500x271.gif)

You look in every mirror and reflective surface you pass just to admire your new style.

## 3. Fingers

![Fingers](https://img.allw.mn/content/zt/cu/qoq4hhos56f31687a147a358746549_500x240.gif)

You can't but help running your fingers through your hair to feel how soft and silky and bouncy it feels

## 4. #hair

![#hair](https://img.allw.mn/content/sx/qy/xeokz14z56f3175a9142f477504314_570x320.gif)

#snipsnipnewhair becomes your selfies' tag for the day. And of course you have to post your new style from various angles.

## 5. Social Media

![Social Media](https://img.allw.mn/content/bh/vz/wjpbujve56f31973846e9061524832_500x305.gif)

And of course, you'll update your profile pic and avatar on every social media platform and website.

## 6. Catwalk Strut

![Catwalk Strut](https://img.allw.mn/content/ec/pf/ne1tkxxj56f317c86ba4b632800592_498x280.gif)

No matter your destination, you walk like a Victoria's Secret Angel, flipping your hair as you go.

## 7. The Weather

![The Weather](https://img.allw.mn/content/u4/iv/t83ptbzj56f320d4f1720752142043_640x360.gif)

Heaven forbid that it dare rain when you leave the salon. You can't run in these heels.

## 8. Consuming

![Consuming](https://img.allw.mn/content/e7/fi/j4ie1xwr56f31ce470c58352755426_500x281.gif)

You can't hold a conversation for 5 minutes before mentioning your hair.

## 9. The Flick

![The Flick](https://img.allw.mn/content/ei/r2/agisemg556f31d25d6218658738631_500x250.gif)

Every 30 seconds or so, you have to flick your hair. You just can't help yourself.

## 10. Recommendation

![Recommendation](https://img.allw.mn/content/r0/sg/x8j6znla56f31ee89b053395567592_490x276.gif)

Just in case no one has noticed your new do, you tell them of a great stylist/salon you can recommend.

## 11. Persistence

![Persistence](https://img.allw.mn/content/z7/g4/qc1f9xqi56f31f883582c565274280_500x269.gif)

And just in case they still don't notice, you puncture conversations with phrases like "my stylist told me today ..."

## 12. BFF

![BFF](https://img.allw.mn/content/eb/sq/ub64bipq56f3213698da5847729138_500x269.gif)

No matter what, you know your BFF is going to gush about how fabulous you look.

## 13. No Poo

![No Poo](https://img.allw.mn/content/tz/v0/i8fknbj456f3204f9cddf479720760_500x282.gif)

You can't bear the thought of washing your hair because you just know you won't be able to style it as well as the salon.
