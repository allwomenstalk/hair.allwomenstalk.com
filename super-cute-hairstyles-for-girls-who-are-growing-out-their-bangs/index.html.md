# 17 Super Cute Hairstyles for Girls Who Are Growing out Their Bangs ...

Bangs are difficult to keep up with, once you go bang, it's so hard to go back! If you are at that awkward in-between phase of growing out your bangs, don't worry, I've got you covered. Don't let your bangs win, below is some hair inspo to help you style your hair beautifully!

## 1. Whispy and Shaggy

Own it! If your bangs are a bit longer, get a little shaggy and just own 'em!

## 2. Braided up Perfectly!

The great things about bangs is that you can hide them in a braid – which is exactly what I do most of the time.

## 3. Rope Braids Are the Best

There isn't just one type of braid either, this rope braid is perfect to tuck your bangs into.

## 4. Parted down the Middle

You don't have to have your bangs covering your forehead all the time – instead, part them right in the middle!

## 5. Headband It up!

Oh yes, she got a little creative with her headband – love it!

## 6. Pulling It Back

Pulling back and tucking your bangs is a great way to keep your hairstyle chic and effortless.

## 7. Beachy Waves

This hairstyle is so popular right now! How adorable do those waves look?

## 8. Cute Bang Braid

This is Heather's favorite style with her little baby bangs. So adorable!

## 9. Throw on a Hat

Not great at braiding? That's okay! Just throw on a hat.

## 10. Long Wisps in Front

When your bangs are grown out enough, just have a few wisps in the front, they look so cute!

## 11. Side-swept It up

Sweep those shorties to the side for a different look!

## 12. Parted to the Side

Or, you could actually part them to the side. God, I love this look!

## 13. Slicked Back Bangs

![Slicked Back Bangs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ar/ft/o9qmhmzk574e5aafb11f3297930326_600x600.jpg)

Don't let your bangs get in your way at all, instead, slick them back.

## 14. Held by a Bobby Pin

![Held by a Bobby Pin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xu/h9/tgvsjg56574e5ab05278b264395480_480x705.jpg)

Bobby pins are so your friends when you have bangs.

## 15. Back and in a Headband

![Back and in a Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ap/y3/d9uwq4rx574e5ab0e7eb1547917002_600x900.jpg)

My favorite look for sure!

## 16. Fishtail and Bobby

![Fishtail and Bobby](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r4/ps/je143qbx574e5ab1a3c59140626201_570x776.jpg)

Even if your bangs are really long, bobby pin them back with a braid!

## 17. Two Braids

![Two Braids](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/et/ay/tp9ios91574e5ab570693550417563_600x906.jpg)

I've been trying to get Heather to do this look, it's so cute!
