# 11 Best Natural Conditioners Your Hair Will Thank You for ...

Natural hair care products don’t often get the recognition they deserve. We put a lot of focus on using natural products on our face, but our hair is just as important! I don’t think anyone would choose to use hair care products that are filled with harmful chemicals. There are tons of reasons to use natural hair care products and for me, having more manageable hair is reason enough. If you’re looking to try some natural ingredients on your hair, check these highly rated natural conditioners out. I think you’ll fall in love!

## 1. Alterna Caviar anti-Aging Replenishing Moisture Conditioner

![Alterna Caviar anti-Aging Replenishing Moisture Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044152_6711_249x247.jpg)

Dry, brittle hair doesn’t stand a chance against Alterna’s Caviar Anti-Aging Replenishing Moisture Conditioner. You can soften, strengthen and shield your hair against color fading and signs of aging the natural way. Help your dry hair maintain moisture, protect your hair from environmental stresses and more in just a few minutes!

## 2. Avalon Organics Thickening Conditioner

![Avalon Organics Thickening Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044152_6507_221x343.jpg)

Have you started seeing more strands of hair in your shower drain? Avalon’s thickening conditioner is here to help! Use this conditioner to build body, nourish and encourage growth in thinning hair. Biotin, saw palmetto, wheat protein and vitamin E join forces to strengthen your strands and add volume.

## 3. Aesop Nurturing Conditioner

![Aesop Nurturing Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044155_6623_599x550.jpg)

See what all the hair snobs are raving about with Aesop’s natural conditioner. This chic little bottle houses tons of botanicals, proteins, nourishing oils and vitamins to soothe and smooth dry, over-processed hair. The nourishing formula will thoroughly nourish and hydrate each strand of hair so you’re left with soft, glossy locks.

## 4. Carol’s Daughter Monoi Repair Conditioner

![Carol’s Daughter Monoi Repair Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044152_4491_424x391.jpg)

The Carol’s Daughter brand has a huge celebrity following and women everywhere are discovering why. Using only natural ingredients like Monoi oil, bamboo water, B-vitamins, Tahitian Tiare Gardenia Flower and Coprah Coconut oil, Carol’s Daughter’s Monoi Conditioner will soften and strengthen your hair like never before.

## 5. John Masters Organics Citrus and Neroli Detangler

![John Masters Organics Citrus and Neroli Detangler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044153_1470_115x431.jpg)

Considered part basement alchemist and part organic innovator, John Masters has been creating high quality organic hair care products for over 20 years. If you have dry hair that’s prone to tangles, this detangler will be your hair savior. It contains 17 certified organic ingredients to strengthen hair, help relieve dry scalp and help make tresses easier to comb through.

## 6. WEN Sweet Almond Mint Cleansing Conditioner

![WEN Sweet Almond Mint Cleansing Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044152_8256_249x243.jpg)

WEN’s cleansing conditioner is the perfect hair care product for ladies who are looking to make their hair care routine more efficient. This conditioner is a five-in-one product so it’s formulated to improve your hair’s health, add shine, tame frizz, moisturize and keep your hair color bright. It’s also meant to replace your regular shampoo and conditioner, deep conditioner and hair detangler so you have more time for more important things, like stalking celebs on Instagram!

## 7. Rahua Conditioner

![Rahua Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044153_3430_316x412.jpg)

Rahua’s all-natural conditioner consistently gets high marks for fortifying weak, damaged hair and reviving lackluster locks. This double-duty product can also be used as a styling cream in addition to using it as a conditioner. Quinoa, aloe, green tea leaf extract and Palo Santo make this natural conditioner a delight for all the senses.

## 8. Davines Nounou Nourishing Conditioner

![Davines Nounou Nourishing Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044153_8284_299x259.jpg)

Davines’ natural conditioner might look more like a tub of hummus than a hair conditioner, but it works brilliantly regardless of its appearance. Chemically-treated and color-treated hair will soak up all of the nourishing tomato extract, vitamin F and sweet almond butter to leave it feeling softer, healthier and full of body.

## 9. Giovanni Direct Leave-in Weightless Moisture Conditioner

![Giovanni Direct Leave-in Weightless Moisture Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044154_4060_599x583.jpg)

Leave-in conditioners can be tricky to use sometimes since it’s easy to apply just a little too much and your hair instantly feels like a ball of grease. Giovanni’s leave-in conditioner promises not to leave your hair feeling greasy, gummy or sticky. In fact, they think theirs is the best detangler on the shelves! If you have coarse or curly hair, this leave-in conditioner works wonders on softening and detangling your hair at a nice price.

## 10. DevaCurl One Conditioner

![DevaCurl One Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044153_9002_249x247.jpg)

Calling all curly and wavy-haired gals! DevaCurl has a line of fantastic products for curly and wavy hair and their One Conditioner is one of their absolute must-haves. No matter how tight or dry your curls or waves are, One Conditioner is up for the job. Use this in conjunction with their LowPoo or NoPoo products for the best results.

## 11. Ojon Rare Blend Deep Conditioner

![Ojon Rare Blend Deep Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06044153_7875_249x247.jpg)

In just two minutes, you can get softer, shinier hair with Ojon’s Rare Blend Deep Conditioner. Made with exotic natural ingredients such as African shea, Brazilian muru muru and Indian kokum butters, this intense deep conditioner will restore and revive even the driest hair to a healthier, more manageable state.

All of these conditioners have gotten rave reviews from hair mavens for moisturizing, restoring and protecting hair just as well as, if not better than, their traditional counterparts.
