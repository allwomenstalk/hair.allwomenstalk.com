# How Women with Long Hair Can Fake a Lob ...

What's a lob? It's just a long version of a bob. Of course, you don't have to grab a pair of scissors in order to pull off the look. You can always fake it!

All you're going to need is a curling iron, texture spray, a teasing brush, and a few bobby pins. Use the materials to tuck your long hair behind your neck, and you're all finished!
