# Beach Beauty 101 \- Hairstyles That Are Perfect for Wearing in the Water ...

When you head to the beach, you don't want to wear your hair in a style that will get messed up easily. You don't want to wear anything that'll end up tangled, either. That's why you need to get in front of the mirror and figure out what's convenient, but will still make you look good. If you're looking for ideas, here are a few hairstyles that are perfect for wearing to the beach:

## 1. Top Knot Messy Bun

This video shows you how to do a messy bun, side bun, and easy braided side pony. These hairstyles are perfect for wet or dry hair, which makes them great for the beach. Even if all your hard work ends up coming out when you hit the water, you can end up doing it again while your hair is drenched with ocean water.

## 2. Scarf Updo

Don't be afraid to accessorize at the beach. As long as you wear a scarf that you won't cry about losing, then you don't have to worry. All you need for this style is a scarf, some hairspray, and a few bobby pins. It's about as easy as it gets.

## 3. No Heat Beach Waves

Around this time of year, everyone wants their hair to look wavy, like they've just stepped out of the ocean. Well you don't have to wait until you actually set foot in the water to achieve that hairdo. This tutorial shows you exactly what you should do if you want beautiful beach waves. You don't even need a hairdryer or curler. All you need to do is wash your hair and then spray it with some coconut milk and water. Once you do it once, you'll be able to do it again and again.

## 4. Beach Waves for Short Hair

If you have short hair, it can be difficult to get the beach waves that everyone loves. But this tutorial will show you exactly what you need to do to achieve the look. That means no more stress for all of you ladies with short hair!

## 5. 3 Random Quick and Easy Hairstyles

This video shows you three different hairstyles, the first of which is the easiest. Of course, the rest of them aren't all that hard either. They just take a little practice. While there's nothing wrong with wearing your hair in a simple ponytail or all the way down at the beach, these styles are great alternatives when you're looking to impress someone.

## 6. Casual Beach Braid

Braids look amazing at the beach. They'll keep your hair out of your face while helping you look stylish. As long as you secure them tightly, then you won't have to worry about them coming undone in the water, either! Nothing could go wrong.

## 7. 3 More Hairstyles

This tutorial will show you how to create a side lace braid, a
fishtail with a twist, and a some-up some-down do. They're so easy to style that the girl in the tutorial actually does her hair while on the beach. That proves that you can do it at any time, anywhere.

Your body isn't the only thing you should show off at the beach. You should be flaunting your locks too! How do you usually wear your hair when you head to the beach?
