# 7 Excellent Products for Frizzy Hair ...

**Products For Frizzy Hair** are specially designed to control and tame frizz. I’m sure I’m not the only one who dreams of having smooth, glossy locks. Frizz and flyaways can be a result of dryness or breakage so to tame frizz you can target the problem from various stages. **Products for frizzy hair** can range from moisturising shampoos and conditioners to anti-frizz styling serums. Below are just a few excellent **products for frizzy hair**.

## 1. John Frieda Frizz Ease Hair Serum

![John Frieda Frizz Ease Hair Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/01/1_john-frieda-frizz-ease-hair-serum_127x276.jpg)

**Price:** $8.99 at [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)
This hair serum is one of the most popular **products for frizzy hair**. One of the key active ingredients is silk protein, which helps moderate frizz. Apply this hair serum on wet, towel-dryed hair after shampooing and conditioning. Rub a small amount onto your palms and distribute evenly throughout the length of your hair.

## 2. Josie Maran Organic Argan Oil

![Josie Maran Organic Argan Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/01/2_josie-maran-organic-argan-oil_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $14.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Argan oil is a popular active ingredient in **products for frizzy hair**. This particular product is 100% pure argan oil. It’s rich in vitamin E and essential fatty acids which help give hair a smooth appearance. This product is also has various other uses and can be applied to skin and nails as well as hair.

## 3. Bumble and Bumble Crème De Coco Shampoo

![Bumble and Bumble Crème De Coco Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/01/3_bumble-and-bumble-cr-egrave-me-de-coco-shampoo_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** from $7.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Start combating frizz from the very beginning with this Bumble and Bumble shampoo. It’s formulated to help restore moisture and minimise frizz. This shampoo is also compatible with colour-treated hair. It also has a lush coconut scent.

## 4. Kerastase Nutritive Oleo Relax Smoothing Masque

![Kerastase Nutritive Oleo Relax Smoothing Masque](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/01/4_kerastase-nutritive-oleo-relax-smoothing-masque_257x212.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)
A deep conditioning treatment is a great idea for undoing damage caused to hair from the elements. While this particular smoothing mask is a splurge, it’s well worth it. This product is specially designed for those with thick, curly hair.

## 5. Sephora Collection Boar Detangling Brush

![Sephora Collection Boar Detangling Brush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/01/5_sephora-collection-boar-detangling-brush_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Just brushing through your hair can cause breakages, so a good quality brush is a must. This brush is made with boar bristles and is designed for short to medium length hair. It is designed to overcome frizz and static, ultimately giving your hair a smooth and glossy finish.

## 6. Living Proof Straight Spray

![Living Proof Straight Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/01/6_living-proof-straight-spray_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** from $17 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This styling spray helps extend the life of straight hairstyles. It can be applied to wet or dry hair and kept in for days. **Products for frizzy hair** often contain oils or silicones but this one is formulated without any such things, helping prevent build-up and keeping your hair feeling lightweight.

## 7. Garnier Fructis Sleek and Shine anti-Frizz Serum

![Garnier Fructis Sleek and Shine anti-Frizz Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/01/7_garnier-fructis-sleek-and-shine-anti-frizz-serum_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $5.99 at [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)
When looking at **products for frizzy hair**, keep an eye out for those containing argan oil. This product contains both argan oil and apricot. When using on dry hair, apply a small amount of serum between your palms and skim lightly over the surface of your hair. If you’re too heavy handed you’ll just end up flattening your hairstyle.

There’s no doubt that the majority of us want smooth, luscious locks. **Products for frizzy hair** are big business.
