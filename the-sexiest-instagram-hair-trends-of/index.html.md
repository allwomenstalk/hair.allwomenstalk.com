# The Sexiest Instagram Hair Trends of 2015 ...

Hair is all the rage, from color to cut and style! Each gives off its own unique benefit to the person! 2015 has been all the rage for the newest, edgiest hairstyles! Check out the sexiest hair trends!

## 1. BOLD COLOR

Colors have been a popular trend in 2015! Colors from red, green, and grey to pastels!

## 2. The BLONDE

Blonde is always a popular color. This year I saw it more frequently all over IG!

## 3. SHORT HAIR, DON'T CARE

I noticed short hair on almost all of my friends on my newsfeed! insane! I love it!

## 4. MAN BUN

The oh so fantastic man bun definitely had its popular run on IG!

## 5. LONG HAIR, DON'T CARE

The ever so classic "long hair, don't care" look is still one the top trends of 2015!

## 6. CURLY HAIR/NATURAL HAIR

I noticed a lot of people embracing their natural hair, and friends who got perms to get the curly hair feel! LOVE IT!

## 7. BEACH WAVES

The look of tossed curls, the beach waves ... so they say- the only thing missing is that ocean!

## 8. CARAMEL HIGHLIGHTS

Caramel highlights/lowlights have been all the rage for as long as I can remember. I think they'll always be a popular trend!

## 9. GREY HAIR

Grey hair was def a popular color in the salon! And, I'm guilty as charged for having grey colored hair!

## 10. PIXIE CUT

The pixie cut, especially with shaved sides and the line, is also a popular trend on IG!
