# Hair Tutorials for Girls Who Want a Change of Style ...

Changing your hairstyle is one of the easiest ways to elevate your look or just spice things up a bit in your everyday routine. For those of us who are looking for some simple ways to take a break from your go-to hairstyles, we found some rad Instagram hair tutorials that'll show you some super helpful tricks to amp up your look.

## 1. Curls with a Flat Iron

This isn't your average video with curling your hair with a flat iron. This tutorial shows you how to create super tight and loose curls using chopsticks!

## 2. No Heat Curls with Straws

If you want to avoid heat damage and who doesn't try creating bouncy curls with straws as seen here in the video. What a fun and easy way to totally change your hair!

## 3. Front to Back Braid

I love the loose look of this braid once it's brought to the back! This might be easier if you ask a friend to do it for you but it's a quick and easy way to create a unique braid.

## 4. Double Ponytail

If you want your ponytail to have lots of length and volume, a double ponytail is the answer. Not only does this style trick give you extra length it gives the appearance of fuller hair!

## 5. Fishtail Bun

Combine two fishtail braids to wrap your bun and you've got yourself and epic bun! This is a stylish alternative to your regular old bun and you can wear it just about anywhere.

## 6. Elegant Bun

This next video shows you how to create a chic bun that looks like a rose! This bun takes a little more time but the end result is so unique and sophisticated it's worth it!

## 7. Easy Updo

If you need to create a graceful updo for a special event and don't want to wear a traditional bun, opt for this easy do! This "curled" hairdo is totally heatless and would be easy for someone without any hairstyling skills like me!

## 8. Loop-through Braid

Try this easy loop-through braided style when you want to do a little something different than just your average braid. This look is cool, casual and ideal for weekends and hanging out with friends.

## 9. Voluminous Braid

And here's a video tutorial for a super full braid for ladies with medium hair. This is a unique look that really takes a regular, three-strand braid to the next level.

## 10. Romantic Waves

Create beautiful, soft waves with a flat iron! This is just another way to get more bang for your buck with one heat tool. Perfect for anytime you want some romantic waves for a special night out.

## 11. Braided Ponytail

This is a cool ponytail with a twist! Adding a couple of braids to the sides of your ponytail really adds a stylish touch to an everyday look.

Which video did you like best, ladies? I find it hard to pick just one. Would you ever try out any of these hairstyles?
