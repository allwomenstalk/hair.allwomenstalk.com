# 7 Sexy, Old Fashioned Hairstyles to Bring Back ...

Don't underestimate the importance of gorgeous hair, because it can make or break your appearance. That's why you need to pick a style that fits the look you're going for that day. The right hairdo is capable of making you look even more fabulous than you normally do, so set your alarm for an early time and have fun with the mirror. If you're looking to branch out, here are a few old fashioned hairstyles to bring back:

## 1. Victory Rolls

Here's a great hairdo that will look just as fabulous now as it did back in the day. Don't worry if you're not a pro when it comes to styling your hair, because this tutorial will explain all you need to know. It shows you how to do Victory Rolls in six different styles, so you can choose whichever one appeals to you. You're bound to find at least one that you fall in love with.

## 2. Beehive

The beehive doesn't belong in the past. If you style it the right way, it can look absolutely adorable. Plus, you'll look unique compared to your peers, because no one really tries to capture this style anymore. Who knows, you might end up bringing the trend back.

## 3. Faux Bob

Have you always wanted a bob, but were too nervous to chop your hair off? Well, then this is the style for you! This tutorial will show you how to make your long locks look like they're cut short, into a bob. It's a great way to test out whether or not you really want to get that haircut.

## 4. Marcel Waves

If you're tired of wearing your hair straight, it's time to make it wavy for a change. This tutorial is done by a professional, but you can easily do it on your own. All you need is a curling iron and you're good to go, girl!

## 5. Gibson Tuck

This hairstyle is amazing, because it can work at an elegant event or at a normal day of school. This look will get the hair out of your face, so that everyone can see just how beautiful you are. That's why you should put on your best make-up whenever you decide to go for this style.

## 6. Rosie the Riveter

There's no better role model out there! If you want to show the world that you're an independent woman that shouldn't be messed with, start by wearing your hair like Rosie the Riveter. This tutorial is eight minutes long, but that doesn't mean it's hard to follow. It's actually a pretty easy look to capture, so don't stress out.

## 7. Audrey Hepburn

Audrey Hepburn was a beauty, so why wouldn't you want to look like her? You don't have to capture her image with your outfit like the woman in this tutorial, because copying her hairstyle is enough. It'll make you look like the most fabulous girl in the room, no matter where you go.

You don't have to wear your hair the same every single day when there are so many different styles to choose from. Most of them don't even take up all that much of your time, so you don't have to worry about waking up early to get ready. What other old fashioned hairstyles do you love wearing?
