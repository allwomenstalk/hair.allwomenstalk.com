# 7 Chignon Hairstyles Fit for Fancy Parties ...

Chignons are typically worn during fancy occasions, like a wedding or an anniversary. Of course, the styles that [Seventeen](https://www.seventeen.com/beauty/hair/g836/chignon-hairstyles/?slide=6) has shown us would work well for a day at the office as well. Don't believe it? See for yourself:

## 1. Side Swoop

![Side Swoop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o8/n8/bltjycqv560404950aa57735131571_480x640.jpg)

If you keep this bun in place with a sparkly clip, it'll draw even more attention to your lovely locks.

## 2. Crimped Knot

![Crimped Knot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hn/v6/kdshxjcj56040495f4056351358754_480x640.jpg)

The only difference between this bun and any other bun is that you need to crimp your hair before putting it up.

## 3. Quick Roll

All you have to do is place a headband on your head and tuck pieces of your hair over the elastic.

## 4. Bow Bun

![Bow Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/x0/lpnm3i4z560404983f104367714704_480x640.jpg)

Create the lowest bun you can, and then secure it with a fun bow!

## 5. Side Knot

![Side Knot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/bd/m7ox2tw8560404992971f708781961_480x620.jpg)

Separate your hair into two sections, twist those sections together, and then put your hair up into this fun bun.

## 6. Side Chignon

![Side Chignon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/r0/hx17kz8b56040499c91b2726758935_480x640.jpg)

Do what you'd normally do, except on the side of your head instead of the back.

## 7. Braided Twist

![Braided Twist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bj/n1/ddkudizb56040499d780e647299816_480x640.jpg)

Create a messy bun and pair it with an adorable braided headband.

Now you have a few new ways to wear your beautiful hair! Which one of these styles do you think is the prettiest?
