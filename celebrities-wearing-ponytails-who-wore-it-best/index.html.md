# Stylish Celebrities Wearing Ponytails \- Who Wore It Best?

I love seeing celebrities wearing ponytails because they are so versatile. A ponytail can look casual or chic – or both simultaneously. Ponytails look just as good on the runway as they do while running errands. While these 9 celebrities wearing ponytails all rock them differently, the question is, who wore it best?

## 1. Beyoncé

![Beyoncé](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/m6/v5npt1ih_600x800.jpg)

It should be no surprise that Beyoncé is one of the celebrities wearing ponytails on this list. Queen B has rocked several different hairstyles and the ponytail is no exception. Bey wore a sleek ponytail to the Grammy Awards with her hair wrapped around the band. This elegant updo accentuated Beyoncé’s amazing bone structure and really made her eyes shine.

## 2. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/sc/ae3y6n38_600x901.jpg)

There is so much to love about Vanessa Hudgens' high, curly ponytail. I love the lines she created with her winged liner, her lipstick, and her high ponytail. This was the perfect hairstyle to show off the gorgeous detailing on her high neckline.

## 3. Lady Gaga

![Lady Gaga](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w8/ye/p74sviy8_419x594.jpg)

One of my favorite things about Lady Gaga is her ability to take something ordinary and turn it into something extraordinary. I think her long rainbow ponytail is the perfect example of this. Her ponytail started on one side of her head and twisted itself around to the other, making this the most unique ponytail on the list and one of my personal favorites.

## 4. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/z4/xs1b8wim_600x988.jpg)

Reese Witherspoon looked like a '60s Barbie doll in a black and white dress and high blonde ponytail at the 83rd Academy Awards. This classic look of hers is one of my all-time favorites from the star. Years later, I am still trying to copy this flirty and fun hairstyle.

## 5. Kim Kardashian West

![Kim Kardashian West](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wr/fi/zxucaf90_600x900.jpg)

Kim Kardashian West is known for her sleek ponytails. While I love her usual tight ponytail, I have to admit, I’m a big fan of this looser style on her. I love the added gold chains in this look, which coordinate with her gold hoops and gold detailing on her top.

## 6. Bar Refaeli

![Bar Refaeli](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h8/jr/zzyotuzo_600x853.jpg)

Bar Refaeli looked like a goddess in her midnight blue open back dress, accentuated with silver detailing and her blonde ponytail. Her high pony showcased her stunning diamond earrings, blue eyes, and pearly white smile.

## 7. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qe/bg/zgjk2ivq_600x749.jpg)

Kerry Washington took the basic ponytail to a new level by adding curls and opting for bobby pins instead of a band as a fastener. Kerry Washington is a trendsetter in real life just as she is on her hit show Scandal.

## 8. Olivia Wilde

![Olivia Wilde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bc/bc/nvkvs9vy_600x823.jpg)

Olivia Wilde is another star who added a twist to the basic ponytail. Her long locks were tied in a high ponytail with bands tied a few inches apart down the length of her hair, creating a wavy and original look. This stylish ponytail is cute and easy enough to replicate that anyone could attempt it at home.

## 9. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/m5/cek029bq_600x780.jpg)

The always-elegant Taylor Swift is no stranger to the ponytail. The hairstyle has become a red carpet staple for her. I adore the side ponytail she wore with her own hair wrapped around the band. The side ponytail showed off the gorgeous embellishments on her gown.

Now that you have seen the contenders for the best celebrity ponytail, it's time to sound off - who wore it best? Sound off in the comments!
