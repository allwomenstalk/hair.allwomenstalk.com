# 7 Haircut Ideas for Your Next Beauty Appointment ...

You don’t want to head to the beauty parlor without some new haircut ideas. Sometimes, all you need is a little change. At other times, you’ll only be satisfied with a drastic cut. Whether you want to go big or small, here are some new haircut ideas to consider the next time you’re in need of a new ‘do.

## 1. Pixie Cut

![Pixie Cut](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18144710_6345_467x571.jpg)

This is for all of the brave women out there. If you’re tired of taking care of long locks that need tons of shampoo and conditioner, why not cut it all off? Pixie cuts have become popular; there are dozens of celebrities sporting the look. This is one of the new haircut ideas you should only get if you’re absolutely sure about it. But then again, your hair will grow back. A [bad haircut](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/neat-tricks-for-dealing-with-a-bad-haircut/) might feel like the end of the world, but it’s not even close.

## 2. Layers

![Layers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18144742_4943_584x801.jpg)

If you want to leave your hair long, get it styled into layers. It's your choice how long they are, but no matter what, they’ll frame your face, and keep your hair from looking dull. It’s not that big of a change, but it can make you look entirely different. Remember that you’re in charge of your own body. Tell your stylist exactly what you want and she won’t refuse.

## 3. Dye

![Dye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18144931_5565_492x750.jpg)

A new style doesn’t have to involve scissors. Instead of getting a cut, you could dye your hair whatever color you choose. Pick a natural shade or go bold with pink or blue. If you want an unnatural color, but don’t want to be too risky, get highlights. You could even get one strand dyed or get a colored extension put in.

## 4. Bangs

![Bangs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18145004_8476_500x750.jpg)

Bangs are small, but they can make a big impact on your appearance. Even if you’re already sporting bangs, you can style them in a new way. You can get side-swept bangs or have them cut straight across. You can get them styled so that they look choppy or blunt. There are plenty of choices, it's all up to your tastes.

## 5. Bob

![Bob](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18145040_3449_600x783.jpg)

Not sure if you prefer short or long hair? Try getting it cut to a medium length. Bobs don’t work for everyone, but you never know until you try. Look at Jennifer Aniston during her Friends days. She created the Rachel cut, but you can keep it alive.

## 6. Shaved

![Shaved](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15201012_2370_479x718.jpg)

If you want to be unique, you have to take risks. Shave one or both sides of your head. It’s a drastic move that will grab everyone’s attention. It sounds crazy, but you might love it. It only works with some haircuts, so make sure that your style would match the decision.

## 7. Part

![Part](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18145133_6148_500x750.jpg)

This isn’t exactly a haircut, but it’s an easy way to change up your look. Simply change the way your hair is parted. You can move it to the middle or have it arching to one side. You don’t even have to leave the house to change it. You have the power in your own hands.

Don’t go to the hair salon without having some sort of idea of what you want. It's always helpful to be able to tell the stylist exactly what you're looking for. What hairstyle are you currently rocking? Do you love it or are you looking for a change?
