# Selfie\-Ready Party Hairstyles for New Year's Eve ...

New Year's Eve is one of the best opportunities of the year for memorable selfies. The festive spirit puts huge smiles on everyone's faces and the propensity for photo bombing is enormous. Make sure you're selfie-ready with gorgeous makeup and hair. Try one of these styles and look your best.

![Fishtail Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/jm/wd63t66e5676acbcbdab1589919909_422x594.jpg)

## 4. Primped up Pixie

![Primped up Pixie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/qg/at5tgycn5676ad0d2a25d846738508_600x624.jpg)
