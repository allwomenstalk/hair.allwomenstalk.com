# 7 Cute Pom Pom Accessories to Brighten up Your Hairdo ...

Hair accessories come in all manner of designs these days, but lately we’ve noticed a trend for pom pom accessories. Yep, that’s right. Fashion has gotten all crafty on us as pom poms of all sizes and colours are adorning everything from headbands to hair clips. Right now there seems to be a pom pom accessory to suit all of your hair styling needs. The following are just a few pom pom accessories that will no doubt brighten up your day.

## 1. Mixed Pom Headband

![Mixed Pom Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/mb/irj1nx83_600x428.jpg)

Pom pom accessories like this ASOS headband would make a fun addition to any outfit. This headband features an array of colourful pom pom details set on a slim contrast band. Just slip it on over any hairdo to instantly brighten up your look.

## 2. Two-Tone Pom Pom Headband

![Two-Tone Pom Pom Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/ek/u1nw0dab_600x725.jpg)

Really make a statement with this pom pom topped headband by Piers Atkinson. It features two oversized pom pom details on top of a metallic leather headband. It’s a playful way to work this season’s must-have pastel trend into your look. This particular headband is pretty pricey and will set you back a couple of hundred dollars, so your best bet would be to shop around for similar styles at a fraction of the cost.

## 3. Pom Pom Bun Holder

![Pom Pom Bun Holder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l0/vh/pvidwc9i_600x276.jpg)

Give your normal sock bun or ballerina bun a makeover with this cute pom pom bun holder from ASOS. It features several pom pom details set snugly onto an elastic band. Just pop your hair into a bun or ponytail and slide the bun holder over the top. It’s an easy and effective way to add some pom pom touches to an everyday outfit.

## 4. XL Pom Headband

![XL Pom Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rt/ip/nu70sm97_600x617.jpg)

If you like your pom pom accessories oversized then this headband from ASOS is for you. It features a large statement pom pom design on a slim, ribbon covered headband. The bright peach colour is eye-catching and would definitely stand out against most hair colours. Wear it with anything from a t-shirt and jeans to a cute party dress.

## 5. Pom Pom Flower Hair Clips

![Pom Pom Flower Hair Clips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/l0/cah79u1x_600x269.jpg)

Brighten up your look with these pom pom flower hair clips from Boohoo.com. They come in an oversized design and in a bright pink hue. The beauty of hair clips is that you can place them wherever you like. Wear them atop your head for a Minnie Mouse-inspired look or wear a single one next to a low chignon for a more elegant affair.

## 6. Pom Pom Hair Band

![Pom Pom Hair Band](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h0/qi/k6rr6no1_600x240.jpg)

This hair band from ASOS features flower-inspired pom pom designs. It features yellow, pink and orange pom poms set on an elastic slim-cut band. Wear it over your locks for a festival-friendly flower crown feel, or slip it on under a messy bun for a quirky take on a relaxed updo.

## 7. Pom Pom Ears Headband

![Pom Pom Ears Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/r4/tfe1bh8r_600x472.jpg)

Add a bit of whimsy to your look with this pretty pink pom pom headband from ASOS. The pom poms are made from delicate mesh fabric, giving the headband a feminine and flirty feel. Pair it with a white lace dress for a summery, garden party kind of outfit.

If you consider your craft skills to be up to scratch, and have some time spare, you could always try making your own unique pom pom hair accessories. Are you a fan of the pom pom look? Which of these pom pom accessories would you dare to wear?
