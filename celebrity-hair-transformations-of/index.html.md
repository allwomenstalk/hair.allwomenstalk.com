# 15 Celebrity Hair Transformations of 2014 ...

It seems like 2014 has been the year of drastic hair transformations. Whether it be for a new movie role or just switching things up for the new season, celebs have been in and out of the salon! Here are some of the most recent hair transformations:

## 1. Kristen Cavallari

![Kristen Cavallari](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/k5/hu0y3k10_600x400.jpg)

Short hair, don't care? Kristin Cavallari teased her fans on Instagram by posting a photo of her chopped off ponytail but after a while, she finally posted a photo revealing her adorable new do'.

## 2. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/xn/q2ykcn8y_600x400.jpg)

Vanessa's always switching things up when it comes to her hair. Her latest look: a fiery red ombre.

## 3. Shailene Woodley

![Shailene Woodley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/pr/sdoqjbfx_600x399.jpg)

Shai is no stranger to experimentation. She chopped off her long locks for The Fault In Our Stars and while still rocking the short cut, she's lightened it up with some blond highlights! She debuted her new look at Miu Miu's show during Paris Fashion Week.

## 4. Camilla Belle

![Camilla Belle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/kj/gy6gag9c_600x400.jpg)

Camilla's proof that you don't have to go darker for the fall! She's stepped out with some lightened locks and looks fabulous.

## 5. Elle Fanning

![Elle Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z6/iq/qa4yu4g6_600x400.jpg)

At the premiere of The Boxtrolls, Elle debuted a new cut and a warm brown color!

## 6. Dakota Johnson

![Dakota Johnson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ta/sh/wnptz7gx_600x400.jpg)

Dakota's constantly going back forth between being a blonde and being a brunette. Hey, I think she looks great either way!

## 7. Gabrielle Union

![Gabrielle Union](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/lq/vmxsvpnt_600x400.jpg)

Gabrielle Union's honey highlights and choppy bangs give her an awesome edginess.

## 8. Drew Barrymore

![Drew Barrymore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lm/kh/dzfm03sf_600x400.jpg)

Drew Barrymore's never been the type to shy away from hair experimentation. She's gone from short to long, blonde to red, and so much more. At Stella McCartney's Green Carpet Collection, she debuted a new chestnut hue.

## 9. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xp/yn/ohyqs6qu_600x400.jpg)

Selena reminded us all of how cute bangs are when she debuted her new look on Instagram.

## 10. Meagan Fox

![Meagan Fox](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/t6/n3s4cm5k_600x400.jpg)

I was a huge fan of Meagan's long wavy hair but her darker and shorter do' is even more flattering. We all know Meagan Fox can rock any color/cut!

## 11. Keke Palmer

![Keke Palmer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/g3/aiqg7zia_600x400.jpg)

Broadway's newest Cinderella chopped off her long locks in favor for a pixie cut before her debut in the show.

## 12. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z1/wf/n5yn4xxp_600x399.jpg)

Is there any look that Emma can't pull off? She showed off her new auburn bob at the Venice Film Festival.

## 13. Lena Dunham

![Lena Dunham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/e6/y03a1fgh_600x400.jpg)

Lena Dunham changed things up drastically when she debuted a new platinum blonde bowl haircut on Instagram! She captioned the photo “change is good,” and it looks like she might've started a trend! Her Girls co-star, Zosia Mamet, recently debuted a new platinum 'do too.

## 14. Malin Akerman

![Malin Akerman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/qp/xo5gtvo6_600x400.jpg)

Malin debuted a dramatic pixie cut at the 2014 Emmys and I have to say, she pulls this cut off like no other! She tweeted, “Yup! #changeisgood #Emmys2014.”

## 15. Anna Paquin

![Anna Paquin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s6/yr/che2xw8h_600x400.jpg)

True Blood's Anna Paquin tweeted her hubby Stephen Moyer asking if he was into her mermaid-esque locks and he quickly responded with "Wow wife... You look amazing." AW.

Who had your favorite hair transformation?
