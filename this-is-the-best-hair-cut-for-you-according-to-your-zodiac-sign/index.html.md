# This is the Best Hair Cut for You According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Have you ever wondered what your zodiac sign says about your appearance? I remember a few years ago a friend of mine mentioned that she can always tell when someone is a Leo by their hair. According to her, Leos typically have voluminous hair, similar to a lion’s mane, and they pay close attention their appearance. I don’t know what sort of hair Libras have but if I’m an example then it must consist of lots of dry shampoo and tying it back as much as possible, not glam at all! If you’re curious to see what your zodiac sign says about your hair and the best cut for you, keep on reading to find out!

## 1. Aries

Aries are known to be strong-willed, passionate, and like to take risks. This year, let that risk-taking spirit guide you to an edgy, cool cut that allows you to get down to business without a lot of fuss. Consider a no-nonsense cut like a short bowl cut with soft edges or a blunt, shoulder-length look. Both styles offer you the convenience of not too much upkeep while staying stylish and chic.

## 2. Taurus

The Taurus woman is mentally and physically strong and loves the finer things in life. A long, layered cut or even a bob with choppy layers would make a perfect hairstyle for you. Feminine but also big and expressive is where it’s at for the Taurus sign. Neither haircut requires too much styling and upkeep making it ideal for the busy bee.

## 3. Gemini

Geminis are another super feminine sign who is also easily bored and likes change. Take advantage of your versatility and try out some new haircuts and styles. A long, blunt bob or an edgy undercut can satisfy that need for change while keeping things fresh and on trend. In case you get bored, both styles allow you to easily change again so you’re never bored with your hair.

## 4. Cancer

Family-oriented, patient, and emotional Cancers are said to be going through an experimental phase this year and a cool way to experiment with your hair is to opt for a short haircut. Get a pixie cut or if you’re not ready to go take it that short, try out some wispy bangs that’ll really change your overall look without a drastic change.

## 5. Leo

Leos are courageous, daring, and love to be the center of attention so your haircut can’t be boring and bland. Although most Leos have a lot of long hair, there’s no need to hide behind that hair with that dazzling personality. Consider cutting it short and slicking it back or get a shoulder-length cut with layers which are both classic but fresh for the season.

## 6. Virgo

Virgos are known to be perfectionists who are analytical and successful. Allow that intuitive and independent nature to guide you to your new perfect haircuts such as a shoulder-length bob or distressed, chin-length bob. Both styles are easy to maintain so they won’t get in the way of your quest to success!

## 7. Libra

Libra ladies are all about balance so it should come as no surprise that they crave a feminine hairstyle that allows for flexibility. Keep the length long with some layers or try a blunt, medium-length cut if you’re not quite ready for a lob. It’s all about showing off your romantic and sophisticated side!

## 8. Scorpio

Scorpios like things big, bold, and are often passionate lovers. Your hair should reflect that fiery passion and your penchant for all things dramatic. Opt for a sexy style like an A-line bob with blunt bangs or even keep it long and free-flowing with some waves or curls to show off your mysterious side. Both haircuts are sure to turn some heads!

## 9. Sagittarius

Women born under the Sagittarius sign are feisty and versatile with a wild streak. Opt for a balance between fun and sophisticated with a classic blunt bob or if you’re feeling funky, go a la Miley Cyrus by keeping your locks long on top and shaving the sides of your hair which will convey your adventurous and fun-loving ways.

## 10. Capricorn

Capricorns are powerful, organized, and love to solve problems. They’re also not afraid to experiment and what better way to be experimental than with your hair? Capricorns like cool but uncomplicated so a shoulder-length cut with short bangs and layers framing the face or an asymmetrical pixie. Both styles are crazy cool but don’t require too much time to do.

## 11. Aquarius

Creative, funky, and loves her freedom, Aquariuses need a haircut that allows them to change their hairstyle whenever they get the creative urge while still being able to show that funky side. Why not try a lob with long or wispy layers? The length and cut allow you to curl or create waves and you get tons of movement with this style.

## 12. Pisces

People born under the Pisces sign are often described as mysterious, selfless, and emotional. A symmetrical A-line cut to your length of choice or long hair with an undercut would suit your personality well. Pisces are supposed to have patience and symmetry this year so a cut that requires maintenance is not a problem!

Are you ready to change your hairstyle to go with your zodiac sign? We’d love to hear your thoughts below!
