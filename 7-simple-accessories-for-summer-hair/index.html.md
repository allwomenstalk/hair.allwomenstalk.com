# 7 Simple Accessories for Summer Hair ...

During the hot summer months, it is easy to get busy, throw your hair into a ponytail, and forget it. The heat can make your hair frizz and hard to manage, but you don’t have to settle for a ponytail every day. Instead, try these 7 simple accessories for summer hair to update your look with effortless style.

Keep summer hair out of your eyes with this darling headband. The black satin band goes with anything, from formal dresses to casual denim. The pretty pink rose adds a delicate feminine touch that is perfect for summer.

## 2. Green Pearl Satin Flower Hair Clip

![Green Pearl Satin Flower Hair Clip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q1/p7/qymkkn6n54c8f7a472267637936359_500x440.jpg)

**Price:** $6.95 at [etsy.com](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Leave your hair long and flowing, but pin back just a few strands on the side with this pretty little clip. The soft pastel green will accent any of summer’s bright colors and the tiny rhinestones in the middle add a touch of class to your every day style. Again, try this accessory with casual outfits or more formal ensembles, and feel free to use it as a pop of pretty color.
