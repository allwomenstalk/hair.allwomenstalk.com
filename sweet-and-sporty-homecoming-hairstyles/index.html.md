# Homecoming Hairstyles That Are Sweet and Sporty ...

Whether it's a high school game or rooting from the couch watching your favorite pro team, these football worthy **homecoming hairstyles **will have you looking ultra chic and sporty. Here are a few cute ideas:

## 1. Blue Braids

Or pair them with the colors of your favorite team to show your spirit. Just braid some ribbon in and you're good to go.

## 2. Whimsical Waves

Messy yet classy. This **homecoming hairstyle** is sure to have all eyes on you and not the game! It's easy to glam up or dress down.

## 3. Half up, Half down Hair

This tried and true cheerleader hairstyle will never go out of style, and for good reason! It's the epitome of a football game and is sure to wow the fans.

## 4. High Ponytail

Another classic cheerleader look but suits women of all ages. The high pony is one of the perfect **homecoming hairstyles** to rock on game day.

## 5. Loose Knot

Easy? Check. Cute? Check. Classic? Check. You've got all your boxes ticked off with this cute and simple **homecoming hairstyle.**

## 6. Bold HeadBand

Like the braids mentioned in #1, wear a bold colored headband to represent the team your rooting for. It'll look extra cute with that matching jersey I'm sure you're planning to wear.

## 7. Cute Curls

Looking to glam up your **homecoming hairstyles**? Look no further than some gorgeous curls. These will give you the ultimate look of classy and bad assy at your next sporting event.

There you have it! Seven completely different **homecoming hairstyles** that are perfect for wearing to a football game. Which one of these do you like the best?
