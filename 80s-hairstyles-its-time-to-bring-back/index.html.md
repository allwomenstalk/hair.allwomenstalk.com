# 80s Hairstyles It's Time to Bring Back 🤘 ...

80s fashion wasn't as bad as everyone thinks. In fact, they had some pretty awesome ideas, from [weave ponytail hairstyles](https://www.juliahair.com/weave-ponytail.html) to Rachel Green’s layered cut. That's why we should bring back these hairstyles that _Allure_ has kindly reminded us existed:

## 1. The Side High Tail

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d4/fn/phiwp_hair_face_hairstyle_clothing_nose_420x560.jpg)

If you're sick of regular ponytails, make yours on the side.

## 2. The Whale Spout

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x1/am/cbzkk_hair_facial-expression_face_nose_hairstyle_420x560.jpg)

This can give your hair the volume you've been dreaming about.

## 3. Half up

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n9/jk/bczzh_hair_eyebrow_image_person_nose_420x560.jpg)

This style is pretty similar to what we currently wear, so you might as well try it.

## 4. The Wrap up

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/fv/xmyrw_hair_face_woman_red_clothing_420x560.jpg)

Don't you have a bandana lying around that you've been waiting to use?

## 5. Feathered

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jw/jf/fuwg6_hair_face_nose_cheek_hairstyle_420x560.jpg)

There's no reason to keep this hairstyle in the past when it's so fashionable.

## 6. Pulled Back with Ribbon Barrettes

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/ax/mget4_hair_face_black-and-white_hairstyle_monochrome-photography_420x560.jpg)

This will make you look super sweet and innocent.

## 7. Curls

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hx/i5/x8nns_hair_face_hairstyle_afro_blond_420x560.jpg)

Stop straightening your hair, because you can't go wrong with curls.

## 8. The Super High Pony

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uv/aw/su0kb_hair_face_eyebrow_hairstyle_blond_600x900.jpg)

Who doesn't love the ponytail that shows off all your length?!

## 9. French Braid

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a0/v9/ghps2_hair_hairstyle_braid_long-hair_french-braid_600x873.jpg)

It's cute, fun, and functional! Can't argue with that.

## 10. Crimping

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yu/v4/flw2z_hair_clothing_hairstyle_blond_girl_600x924.jpg)

## 11. Mega Volume

## 12. MoHawk
