# 7 Half\-up Hairstyles for Fall ...

Fall is the perfect season for putting half of your hair up and the other half down. After all, it'll make you look casual and cute at the same time, and isn't that what autumn is all about? If you agree, check out these adorable hairstyles from [Good Housekeeping](https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/beauty/hair/g2800/half-up-half-down-hairstyles/?slide=13):

## 1. Curly Half-Updo

![Curly Half-Updo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/x2/yv0o5f5m560c12bf14423290077490_480x652.jpg)

Pull the majority of your hair back, but leave a few sexy strands out in the front.

## 2. Smooth Volume

![Smooth Volume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/z1/uc9endzn560c12bf87db8301411987_480x671.jpg)

There's no one classier than Kate, so why not steal her style?

## 3. Womanly Waves

![Womanly Waves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rk/a8/vrgunpr7560c12bfdaec4292697007_480x730.jpg)

Curl your beautiful hair and then part it in the center.

## 4. The Hun

![The Hun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u2/sq/ljenui7w560c12bfe8d6e785923079_480x599.jpg)

## 5. The Miniature Hun

## 6. Half Ponytail

![Half Ponytail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/sg/gi6ozhsv560c12c079c88966618984_480x721.jpg)

This is a simple way to spice up a classic look.

## 7. Barely up Hairdo

![Barely up Hairdo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/wd/qq7q0n3p560c12c11eb0c549589813_480x789.jpg)
