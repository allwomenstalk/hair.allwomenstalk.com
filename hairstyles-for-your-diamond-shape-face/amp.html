8 Cute Short Cut

Via hairstyle88.com
Diamond shaped faces usually have great cheekbones. Show them to their advantage.

***

Diamond shaped faces are characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, with prominent cheekbones. This face shape is especially flattering for those with high cheekbones, as it can help accentuate them.

When choosing a hairstyle for a diamond shaped face, it is important to keep the face shape in mind. Short cuts work well for diamond shaped faces, as they draw attention to the cheekbones and away from the narrow forehead and chin. A cute short cut can add texture and volume to the face, making the cheekbones appear more prominent.

Bobs, pixies, and shags are all great short hairstyle options for diamond shaped faces. When styling a bob, it is important to keep the sides and back shorter than the front. This will help create the illusion of a wider forehead and chin. For those with thin hair, adding layers to a pixie cut can help create the appearance of more volume. Shags are great for those with thicker hair, as they can help create texture and body.

When styling short hair for a diamond shaped face, it is important to remember that bangs should be kept away from the forehead. Instead, opt for side swept bangs or a longer fringe that will help add balance to the face.
