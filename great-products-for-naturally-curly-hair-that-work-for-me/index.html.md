# 7 Great Products for Naturally Curly Hair That Work for Me ...

Have you been looking for some great products for naturally curly hair? If, so you’re not alone. I am always on the lookout for products that will work well with my natural curls. Over the years, I have tried tons of products for naturally curly hair and have been very disappointed with about 90% of them. I wanted to share with you some of the products that work best for me. I hope some of them work well for you, too.

## 1. Tresemme FLAWLESS CURLS CONDITIONER

![Tresemme FLAWLESS CURLS CONDITIONER](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/b3/hukbl5e0_154x384.jpg)

One of my new favorite products for naturally curly hair is TRESemme Flawless Curls Conditioner. Before I started using this conditioner, my hair was very dry and this has made a huge difference. My hair seems well hydrated and my curls are bouncer. Typically, I use a leave-in conditioner as well, but I don’t need it with this product. It is available in a 32 ounce bottle and can be purchased at most drugstores for around $5.

## 2. Herbal Essences Curl Boosting Mousse Totally Twisted

![Herbal Essences Curl Boosting Mousse Totally Twisted](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0u/fe/0izmzqfu_56x296.jpg)

On days that I’m going to be home and decide not to blow dry my hair, this is one of the products that I use. Unlike some mousses, you don’t have to use a lot of this one to get the look you want. If your hair is a bit flat, it is a great volume booster. I must also mention that this product smells wonderful, too. You can pick this mousse up at most drugstores for under $5.

## 3. Infusium 23 Repair & Renew Leave-in Treatment Spray

![Infusium 23 Repair & Renew Leave-in Treatment Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c7/rc/d2gf8gvb_355x355.jpg)

I know that this product is not specifically for curly hair, but it’s works great with my natural curls. It is a leave-in treatment that is designed to repair damaged hair, but it is also the best product that I've found to fight frizz. My stepmom and sister both have naturally curly hair and love this product, too. You can purchase this product in several different sizes, but the spray bottle seems to work best for me.

## 4. Catwalk Curlesque Curls Rock Amplifier

![Catwalk Curlesque Curls Rock Amplifier](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zm/qe/ifg2lfyc_79x300.jpg)

If you need a little bit of help defining your curls, this is a great product to try. I love that it defines my curls without making them feel stiff like many other products do. When I first started using this product, I had to order it online. But lately I’ve been seeing it at Walmart. It does cost around $15, which is more expensive than most of the products on my list, but it does last a long time.

## 5. Kinky-Curly Knot Today Leave-in Detangler

![Kinky-Curly Knot Today Leave-in Detangler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tm/bk/ktplk22d_600x901.jpg)

I have recently started using Kinky-Curly products and so far I really like them. My daughter has naturally curly hair and I always apply a detagling product to it after it is washed. Unfortunately, most detanglers don't work very well on her hair because of the texture of it, but this one does a great job. It works well on dry hair, as well. You can get this leave-in detangler at your local drugstore for around $12.

## 6. L'oreal EverCurl Leave-in Cream

![L'oreal EverCurl Leave-in Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/io/jdiztvuc_600x981.jpg)

This is a great multi-use product. It boost curls and fights frizz at the same time. It does seem a bit heavier than other leave in creams that I've used, so I apply it sparingly. I also try to avoid applying this product to the roots of my hair because it can make them look greasy if you apply too much. You can purchase this leave-in cream for around $8 at your local drugstore.

## 7. Big Sexy Root Pump plus

![Big Sexy Root Pump plus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/x4/rxeow64l_300x298.jpg)

I know that this is not a product designed for curly hair, but I love it. My hairdresser used in on my hair about 6 months ago and since then its an item that I keep on hand. My hair is both long and thick and it tends to be a bit flat on top at times, so I use this to fluff it up a bit.

I love hair products and buying them is definitely a weakness of mine. Do you like hair products as much as I do? Are there any that you would like to recommend to me? I can’t wait to try out some of your recommendations.
