# Praiseworthy Tutorials for Styling Curly Hair ...

If you have curly hair like me, you probably spend quite a bit of time wondering how to style your curls. It can be hard to get them to cooperate with you and more often than not, I end up with frizz or poofs where I definitely don't want them. Well, no more! There are some really great tutorials here that will help you get the best looking curly hair in town. Don't forget that taking good care of your hair by using the right products (those formulated for curly hair) will really help you have good hair days every day.

## 1. Easy Hairstyles for Curly Hair

If you want easy hair, you will find it here. There are three really simple styles that you can create from start to finish with ease. No more hours spent trying to make wacky curls!

## 2. Curly Hair Routine

This fantastic video will give some really easy styling steps that you can build into your routine. That means effortless curls any day of the week. Even when you're in a hurry to get out the door in the morning.

## 3. No Heat Style

You know that heat styling can really do a number on your hair, right? Follow the steps in this video to tame your curls into submission without high temps that can damage your strands.

## 4. 5 Minute Style

When you're short on time, but still want glorious curls, this is the video for you. It will show you a simple, but gorgeous, style that you can get done from start to finish in just 5 minutes. Perfect!

## 5. Big Curly Hair

This tutorial will be a lifesaver if you have natural hair. With these steps, you'll have the most outstanding curls you've ever had. Yay! the best part? It's so easy to follow along with this tutorial.

## 6. Curly Pixie Cut

If you have short curls, it can be even harder to get them to do what you want. This video helps you go from poofy to tamed in mere minutes. You'll never swear you want to grow your hair out again because you are going to get so many compliments with this look.

## 7. Naturally Curly Hair

Natural curls are something that every girl with straight hair wants and something that girls with curls hate. If you have curls, this video will take you to the other side because you'll love how fab your curls look. Get ready to turn some heads everywhere you go.

## 8. Second Day Styles

Haven't washed your hair today? Perfect - that means you can get any of these curly styles with no problem at all. When you let a bit of hair oil distrubute through your strands, you'll find that it's much easier to get your curls to stay put once you've styled them.

## 9. Create Ringlets

If you want to transform your hair into perfect ringlets, just watch this video. It's packed with easy steps to help you get the look you've been craving all along. You'll have perfectly bouncy curls all day long and what girl doesn't want that?
