# 7 Virtual Makeover Websites to See How Youd Look with Different Haircuts ...

Ready for a new haircut, but scared about the possibility of it looking hideous once it's over with? There's no need to worry anymore, because you can take precautions to assure that it'll look fabulous at the end of the day. All you have to do is head to one of the best virtual makeover websites out there. Here are a few that'll allow you to see how a new haircut would look on you:

## 1. Virtual Haircut and Makeover

As long as you have a snapshot of yourself facing the camera without your hair in your face, you're all ready for [goodhousekeeping.com](https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/beauty/makeup/). After uploading a picture, you can apply different foundation, eye makeup, lipstick, and blush to your face. Then, of course, you can move on to the hairstyle option where you can test what you'd look like in all different types of styles.

## 2. TAAZ Virtual Makeover

On _taaz.com_, you can change your makeup, hair, and even add accessories to your own photograph. There are plenty of different celebrity hairstyles for you to choose from, and once you do, you can change the color of the hair to fit your needs. It's a great way to test the waters before heading to a hair salon for a real makeover.

## 3. Daily Makeover

If you don't have a picture to upload, [dailymakeover.com](https://stylecaster.com/virtual-makeover/) allows you to pick a model that looks similar to you and style her instead. In addition to the hair and makeup options, you can also give her contacts or put a picture frame around her head.

## 4. Virtual Makeover

[Marykay.com](https://www.marykay.com/en-us/tips-and-trends/mobile-vmo-app) is obviously on the Mary Kay website, which means that all of the makeup you try on is all from their brand. That makes it easy to find in real life if you stumble across something you love. On this site, you can even do your nails! What more could you possibly ask for in a virtual salon?

## 5. Look Good, Feel Better

Are you just as worried about your eyebrows as you are about the hair on your head? Well, _lookgoodfeelbetter.org_ allows you to test out different eyebrow shapes and colors. When it comes time to pick out a hairstyle, it'll ask you if you want to take a quiz, which will ask you about your face shape in order to help you find the style that's truly right for you. This site is about as helpful as it gets.

## 6. Makeover by Sally Beauty

Whether you want short hair, medium hair, long hair, or an updo, [sallybeauty.com](https://www.sallybeauty.com/gift-cards/) will show you what you'd look like with it. The tools on this site also allow you to adjust how long or thick the hair is, which means that you'll get a realistic portrayal of what you'd look like. There's no better way to see yourself in a new style.

## 7. Hollywood Hair Virtual Makeover

Here's one last site to help you out on your hairstyle journey. _Instyle.com_ allows you to choose whether you have straight hair, natural curls, or wavy hair and then gives you styles that would work best for you.

Your hair can make or break your look, which is why you want to make sure your positive about a haircut before you head to the salon. Is there a hairstyle that you've been dying to try out, but haven't done so yet?
