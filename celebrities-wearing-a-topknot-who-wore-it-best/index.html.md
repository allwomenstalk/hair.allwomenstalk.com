# 7 Celebrities Wearing a Topknot \- Who Wore It Best?

I love seeing celebrities wearing a topknot bun. The look can be casual and toned down or sleek and sophisticated; in other words, topknot buns are extremely versatile and can look great worn anyway. This is one of the many reasons I love this hair trend. Some celebrities have been sporting the look for many years, while others are just now jumping on the topknot bandwagon. No matter when or how they chose to wear it, one thing is for certain, each of the celebrities on this list wears the hairstyle well! Now we want to hear from you, which of these celebrities wearing a topknot pulls it off best?

## 1. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/f3/ykroksr7_560x841.jpg)

J-Lo is one of the celebrities wearing a topknot. Jennifer Lopez is no stranger to the hairstyle and has been seen wearing one on several occasions. I’m a huge fan of when she wore the bun to the 2013 Grammy Awards. Her bun along with her bronzy makeup and sexy black gown gave her one of the best looks of the night.

## 2. Kourtney Kardashian

![Kourtney Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/e7/kymf0pcl_395x594.jpg)

For the opening of the Kardashian Sisters' New York City store, Dash, the eldest sibling rocked a topknot. Kourtney wore an all black ensemble with gold accents to the opening. Her slicked-back bun really showed off her gorgeous bone structure!

## 3. Amandla Stenberg

![Amandla Stenberg](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ve/s7/h40k6oht_395x594.jpg)

How cute is the Hunger Games star, Amandla Stenberg, in her adorable topknot? Amandla wore the bun to the 2012 San Diego Comic-Con. She wore a patterned skirt, black top, white blazer, a colorful necklace, and the most adorable topknot the world has ever seen.

## 4. Kelly Osbourne

![Kelly Osbourne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dv/df/04oi9wfv_600x796.jpg)

The ever fashion-forward Kelly Osbourne wore a topknot to the 2013 Make-A-Wish Gala. The star didn’t sport just a normal topknot, she rocked a braided topknot. I love this twist on the classic bun. Although you can’t see it in this photo, the star also added gold spikes to the back of her head, just above her neck. Kelly wore the topknot with a nude lip, winged liner, and a black dress.

## 5. Beyoncé

![Beyoncé](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qy/dl/zqcfdfeu_600x900.jpg)

Beyoncé also wore a topknot differently than the norm. Bey wore the bun with fringe, giving the style a new dimension. Queen B wore this look out with her husband wearing a green and gold dress. Her honey-colored hair really showed off her radiant skin.

## 6. Gwen Stefani

![Gwen Stefani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/jh/v90368qq_460x615.jpg)

Gwen Stefani sported her classic red lips to the Museum of Contemporary Art Gala in 2011. She also donned a topknot bun. The singer is no stranger to the hairstyle, but there was something extra special about the way she wore it that night. Perhaps it was the gorgeous black Carven dress she wore to the event that made her topknot really stand out.

## 7. Demi Lovato

![Demi Lovato](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ux/jv/j8yyh3op_600x893.jpg)

When Demi Lovato attended the X Factor Finalists Party, she sported a red mini dress, a spikey leather jacket, and a chic topknot bun. The hairstyle was the perfect choice for the outfit as it showed off the studs and spikes on her jacket. The singer matched her lips to her dress for a glam yet edgy look.

I love seeing celebrities rocking the topknot. It is such a great hairstyle. I love each of the looks on this list so now I want to hear from you – who wore it best? Tell us who you think pulls off the look best in the comments section!
