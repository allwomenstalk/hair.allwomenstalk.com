# 9 Celebrities with Red Hair \- Who Wore It Best?

Whether it’s bright, natural, highlights, or dyed, there are many celebrities with red hair. There is something naturally glamorous about red locks and these celebrities pull off the shade like pros. Although each of the stars on this list look great, we want to know which of these celebrities with red hair wore it best?

## 1. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gs/vp/ahrqzqw6_531x726.jpg)

One of my favorite celebrities with red hair is Rihanna. Rihanna is a fashion maven who is constantly setting red carpet trends. Who could forget the bright red braid she wore to the 2011 Met Gala? Rihanna looks great in every hair color, but there is something extra special about her red locks.

## 2. Jessica Chastain

![Jessica Chastain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qv/xx/ii8rnqa8_600x895.jpg)

Jessica Chastain, star of the 2012 thriller Zero Dark Thirty, is a talented actress with a gorgeous head of red hair. Her natural red hair makes her stand out among the crowd and her appearance at the 2013 Oscar’s was no exception.

## 3. Gerard Way

![Gerard Way](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/vb/vzm0ee93_500x750.jpg)

Front man of the now disbanded My Chemical Romance, Gerard Way, has sported many hair colors throughout his 14 years as a musician, including vibrant red. Way first debuted his red mane in 2010 for My Chemical Romance’s fourth and final studio album, Danger Days: The True Lives of the Fabulous Killjoys and is sporting the look again for his first solo album Hesitant Alien.

## 4. Deborah Ann Woll

![Deborah Ann Woll](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bp/gq/cme6zfkn_600x842.jpg)

Deborah Ann Woll, perhaps better known as Jessica Hamby from True Blood, makes me wish I were born with natural red locks. Deborah looked absolutely radiant walking the red carpet at the season 7 première of True Blood. Her smoky eyes and the gold detailing on her dress really made her red hair pop.

## 5. Hayley Williams

![Hayley Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wp/h0/rgzimdq4_600x805.jpg)

Hayley Williams, front woman of Paramore, is known for her vibrant hair. Hayley is almost always rocking some shade of orange or red. Hayley is known as much for her ever-changing hair as she is for her standout vocals. While I’m always a big fan of her hair, I love the red she adorned for the 2011 Elle Women in Music event. Her black and white striped dress looked gorgeous against her fiery red locks.

## 6. Alyson Hannigan

![Alyson Hannigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nb/a4/o1iq5n05_600x842.jpg)

Whether you know her as Willow Rosenberg or Lily Aldrin, there’s no denying that Alyson Hannigan is TV’s favorite redhead. One of my favorite looks of hers was at the Make Equality Reality event in Los Angeles. Alyson wore a tight black dress with matching nails, shoes, and bracelet. The all black ensemble really made her straight red locks stand out.

## 7. David Bowie

![David Bowie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/rp/dvjpna9x_600x870.jpg)

Style icon David Bowie is known for pushing boundaries with his appearance. Out of all his iconic looks, his red hair will forever remain my favorite. Bowie has sported red hair as himself and as his alter ego, Ziggy Stardust.

## 8. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dk/qm/avgzf1g4_415x601.jpg)

There is so much to love about Emma Stone, including her red hair. Although Emma has experimented with other colors such as blonde, I will always love her red hair best. My favorite look was the one Emma wore to Vanity Fair’s 2010 Oscar party. Emma’s curly red locks looked radiant against her red lips, green earrings, and black gown.

## 9. Nicola Roberts

![Nicola Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/m3/e33sebk1_425x594.jpg)

English recording artist, Nicola Roberts, gives me major hair envy. I first noticed her when she walked the red carpet for Cosmopolitan’s Ultimate Women Awards in 2010. Her natural red hair was styled perfectly in loose curls and complimented her green leopard print dress perfectly.

Now it’s time for you to sound off in the comments – who wore their red locks best?
