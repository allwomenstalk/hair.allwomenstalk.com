# 7 Amazing Natural Remedies for Hair Loss All Girls Need ...

Are you looking for **natural remedies for hair loss?** You are in the right place.

Women naturally like to wear their hair long. It is the center of beauty, and nicely done hair can make your look. On the other hand, thin, dry-looking hair is sure to attract looks of dismay from your girlfriends (and guys, as well).

Your hair says a lot about you. It says whether you’re healthy or not, or whether you’ve been taking care of your hair with the right beauty products. It can even tell us what you’ve been eating (with lab analysis, of course).

So why do you experience hair loss and what are some **natural remedies for hair loss**?

Often, hair loss can be as a result of a faulty diet or improper care of your hair. Take note of relaxers and other chemicals you use on your hair, and avoid those you think are contributing to your hair loss. You might want to consider using herbal-based hair care products rather than the synthetic brands.

Then make sure you eat a healthy balanced diet and pay more attention to your hair. You know when it’s due for a makeover and when you need to wash and reset it. All of these things will enhance your hair growth.

However, it’s quite normal to lose hair every now and then. Your hair is always growing. As a hair grows from your follicles, it does so in three key stages.

The first stage (the anagen) involves the growth of hair over an eight-year period. The second stage (the catagen) is the shedding of hair, which happens within a four-week window, and the third stage (the telagen) is the time in which hew hairs starts growing again.

So, yes, hair loss is normal. However, when this hair loss is progressive and rapid, it presents a problem. The cause of this type of hair loss varies, from lack of certain essential minerals and iron in the body, to hormonal imbalance and disease. Certain infections can also cause you to lose hair quickly.

Also, psychological and emotional disorders can trigger reactions in your system that can result in hair loss.

There are a few other causes of hair loss, but let’s move on to the most important thing for you right now - home remedies to prevent hair loss.

## 1. Onions

One of the best **natural remedies for hair loss** is onions. This rich food ingredient is a powerful source of sulfur, which can improve hair growth by stimulating the production of collagen. Using a fine blend of onion on your scalp over a period of time can help to prevent hair loss and also grow new hairs at the same time.

To use: chop some onions into bits and squeeze out the juice. Massage the juice into your scalp and leave for up to 15 minutes before rinsing out with shampoo and warm water. Note that you have to repeat this process two or three times each week for the best results.

## 2. Garlic

Not many know what good garlic can do for your hair. Like onions, garlic also contains sulfur and is a great natural remedy for hair loss. Garlic has been used over the years in traditional medicine for re-growth of thinning hair.

To use garlic the remedy: blend some cloves of garlic into a fine paste and add some coconut oil to it. Then boil this mixture for a few minutes over moderate heat. Allow the mixture to cool down before applying directly onto your scalp. Wash off with shampoo and warm water after about 10 minutes.

## 3. Aloe Vera

This is another rich natural hair care product you need to try out. Aloe vera contains enzymes that act to promote the growth of healthy hair. Applying aloe juice to your scalp can help to stimulate hair growth and also prevent hair loss.

## 4. Coconut

Like garlic and onions, coconut also contains active ingredients that can improve your hair texture and prevent hair loss.

Coconut milk and coconut oil are rich in proteins, essential fats and minerals like potassium and iron, which can reduce hair breakage and strengthen hair texture. They also help to improve the overall quality of your hair.

Using coconut oil as a sort of hair lotion is good for you. Alternatively, you can swift from your regular hair care product to coconut or herbal-based hair care cream for your hair.

## 5. Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a beautiful flowering plant that contains rejuvenating properties that can help to prevent hair loss to a great degree. It can also help to nourish the hair and counteract premature graying. Also, using hibiscus oil or extract on your scalp will help cure dandruff and skin inflammation.

## 6. Egg

Eggs are viable sources of protein, sulfur and other important nutrients like selenium, iodine, iron and zinc. These ingredients are essential for promoting hair growth and prevention of hair loss.

## 7. Certain Hair Styles

Definitely, there are some types of hair that can cause loss. When you style your hair tightly, or you use harsh chemical products, you’re bound to lose some hairs along the way. I know you want to look good, and you’re going to pay well for that nice hairdo. But try to select styles that won't have to pull on your hair. it’s better if you let your hair hang freely.

Finally, for those considering the option, some medications can boost hair growth and prevent hair loss. But you have to also consider the side effects of such drugs before making your decision.
