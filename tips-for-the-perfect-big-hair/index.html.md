# 10 Tips for the Perfect Big Hair ...

Grab your teasing comb and buckle up, because we’re diving into the secret world of luscious locks.

The first thing you need to know is that **volume** is king-or queen! Whether you're aiming for _retro curls_ or that modern, effortless kind of **volume**, it's all in the technique. One of my favorite tips you’ll find in this guide is to start with a strong foundation: you need the right _products_. Focus on shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed to add _body_ to your hair. Trust me, it makes all the difference. ## 1. Blow Dry Better

One of the first and foremost tips for big hair is to learn how to blow dry better. For the best volume boosting results, dry hair in sections, lifting them upwards and directing the hot air at your scalp. Avoid flipping your head upside down to blow dry, as this often results in hair getting big outwards instead of upwards.

## 2. Roll It up

Add more volume to your hair by setting it with rollers before you style it. Even if you’re not after curls or ringlets, this will help give boost and body. Roughly dry hair and roll using medium sized curlers from the ends to the roots, then allow hair to fully dry. Take out the rollers, loosen out any unwanted ringlets, and get styling!

## 3. Prioritise Products

When it comes to tips for big hair, choose your products wisely. Too often you’ll find that certain products weigh down and flatten hair. Instead, opt for products like volumizing shampoos, conditioners and root lifters that are designed for the sole purpose of making your hair bigger and better. Different products also target different types of hair (fine, wavy, frizzy) so also take this into account when choosing one for you.

## 4. Back Brush, Not Comb

I’m sure I’m not the only one who has had bad experiences with backcombing hair. While teasing hair this way is guaranteed to build up extra height, it can also lead to nasty knots. Go easy on your hair by teasing it with a flat back brush instead of a comb.

## 5. Spritz the Spray

Hairspray is no-brainer for keeping hair intact and in place. Use it to your advantage when polishing off your big hair look. A tip for big hair is to be light handed in the application. Too much hairspray concentrated in one area can flatten the hair so lightly spritz the stuff on your ‘do instead.

## 6. Stay Dirty

Hair seems to keep volume better if it’s a little bit dirty. You’ll often that that freshly washed hair tends to be slippery and unable to keep shape as well as hair that’s a few days old of a wash. Plan ahead so that you style your big hairdo a day or two after you last washed your hair.

## 7. Polish if off

All that teasing and blow drying can lead to fly aways. Keep your hair in place by once again utilising the magic of hairspray or another smoothing product. Apply said product to your hand and then lightly wipe it over your ‘do, making sure not to flatten it in the process.

## 8. No Part-y

Parting your hair will only help it flatten out quicker so if you want your hair to have extra body, say buh-bye to the part altogether. Instead, brush it back for a glamorous sixties inspired look. If getting rid of your part is out of the question, simply flip it to the other side for a cheeky volume boost.

## 9. Try Dry Shampoo

Spraying dry shampoo onto your hair is just another one of those ingenious tips for big hair. When it comes to this tip for big hair, apply it at the roots instead of over the top of your hair to achieve that extra volume.

## 10. Allow for Movement

Big hair often looks the best when it appears to have some movement. If you’re wearing your hair out, a few waves can help add the appearance of bounce. If you’re wearing your hair up, try a big hairstyle that’s not too restrictive.

Next time you’re thinking of adding extra volume to your locks, try out these tips for big hair. When it comes to achieving extra volume, what’s your best tip for big hair?
