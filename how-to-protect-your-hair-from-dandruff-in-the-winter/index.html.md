# How to Protect Your Hair from Dandruff in the Winter ...

Dandruff is a pain, right? It can instantly ruin a look or hairstyle or even just your outfit! If you feel like you've tried everything and can't find the solution, try some of the different hacks on this list. You may just the answer to all of your dandruff problems!

## 1. Coconut Bun

![Coconut Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/oy/vnkhk73e5655c162bb237580364603_600x875.jpg)

Source: [The Beetique: Coconut Oil Hair](https://www.thebeetiqueblog.com/2014/11/coconut-oil-hair-treatment.html?spref=pi)
If your ends are seriously lacking in moisture, try sleeping with coconut oil in a bun to rejuvenate your entire scalp and cut down on dandruff!

## 2. Brown Sugar and Olive Oil

![Brown Sugar and Olive Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g4/nh/r6a2ii825655c162edff5724212856_600x768.jpg)

It sounds like the weirdest combination, but this mixture will fix your scalp completely!

## 3. Mint Spray

![Mint Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/tb/blgu2dyb5655c163070a4900680185_600x840.jpg)

Source: [3 Homemade Treatments for Dandruff](https://helloglow.co/3-homemade-treatments-for-dandruff-thinning-hair/)

## 4. Watered-down Listerine

![Watered-down Listerine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xx/fx/lcad399h5655c162313c7458759961_540x613.jpg)

Source: [12 Homemade Dandruff Treatments &](http://everydayroots.com/dandruff-remedies)
Listerine was originally used for dandruff decades ago. Water down a clear version (you don't want your hair to turn blue or green!) with two parts of water for every one part of Listerine for the answer to your dandruff prayers!

## 5. Aspirin in Shampoo

![Aspirin in Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/dl/l47eq2jk5655c1624537e289955519_213x315.jpg)
