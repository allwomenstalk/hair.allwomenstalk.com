# 20 Ideas for Long Sexy Hair ...

I am always looking for long sexy hair ideas. We all know how gorgeous long hair can be, but sometimes we all need some new ideas for long sexy hair. If you have long hair, you are not going to want to miss this.

## 1. Wavy Hair, Straight Bangs

My first long sexy hair idea is inspired by none other than Reese Witherspoon. She always looks so glamorous with her perfectly wavy locks and simple straight bangs. The look is simple yet chic.

## 2. Pulled Back

Of course there is always something sexy about long hair that is pulled back and pinned up. I love the way it exposes the neck and looks so elegant. It’s perfect for the red carpet, to office, or a lounge day at home.

## 3. Slicked Back

Kim Kardashian has been sporting a new look with her long hair. What I love about this this long sexy hair idea is this simplicity. This look is pulled off by using a flat iron to get perfectly straight hair and then applying a little hair gel through the crown of your head to slick it all back.

## 4. Braids

Braids seem to be all the rage right now. Everywhere I look I am seeing a new braid trend. It’s a good style to try with long hair. You can start with a simple look, pulling all your hair to side with a braid.

## 5. Straight and Sleek

There is nothing new about this long sexy hair idea, but it is still a favorite. I love the look of long straight hair.

## 6. Blunt Bangs

Take a hint from Zooey Deschanel. She looks so beautiful with her blunt cut bangs. This long sexy hair idea is an easy way to update your current look without giving up any length.

## 7. Swept to the Side

This is probably the look I wear most often. I have long hair that is somewhere between wavy and curly. I like to pull all my hair to one side and let the waves cascade down my shoulder.

## 8. Pony

Ponytails are always a great long sexy hair idea. For a more sophisticated look tie the ponytail at the base of your neck.

## 9. Ombre

Khloe Kardashian has this long sexy hair idea down. She has soft caramel highlights around her face and a few chunks on the ends of her hair. The added color really helps make her [loose curls](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/summer-hair-loose-waves-and-curls-are-causing-a-stir/) look amazing.

## 10. Lots of Layers

Nothing is more sexy than perfectly laid out layers in long hair. Layers add movement, texture, and height long hair.

## 11. Shine

Want a simple long sexy hair idea? Why not add a little shine? You can get beautiful shiny hair by adding a little shine serum when you dry your hair. Add it to the length of your hair, not your roots for perfect shine without looking oily.

## 12. Add Some Bump

There is something very appealing about long hair that has a little height at the crown. Teasing isn’t just for the girls in Jersey.

## 13. Beach Waves

My next long sexy hair idea comes from Mary-Kate Olsen. Her golden waves look like perfect beach day hair. You can get the same look by using a salt-infused styling product in damp hair and then scrunching the hair from the ends to the roots.

## 14. Accessories

Whether it’s a stylish headband or a fun clip you can always create new long sexy hair ideas by adding in accessories. Try out a few things for yourself.

## 15. Big Loose Curls

I love this long sexy hair idea. In fact it’s one of my regular looks. My hair is already wavy so getting big curls is easy for me. You can get the same look with hot rollers or a large curling iron.

## 16. Wild Curls

Another great look, not many curly haired girls have super long hair. If they do, they wear it straight. There is just something about a long mane of curls that makes me drool.

## 17. Faux Bob

There have been a few recent photographs of Selena Gomez sporting this long sexy hair idea. To create this look, you tuck your long locks under using pins to create a shoulder or chin lengths bob.

## 18. Bun

Don’t you think buns are just the cutest? I sure do. I love a big perfect bun right on the top of the head. It’s great to wear to work or an elegant night out. What am I saying, buns are perfect any time!

## 19. Side Pony Tail

This sexy long hair idea is similar to the side sweep. It’s slightly different because instead of just placing your long hair over your shoulder you tie it low to the side. Unless you are really bold, don’t tie a high side pony tail.

## 20. Half up

You can also wear your hair half up. This works to keep your hair out of your face. You can either let the hair cascade down or twist it up. Either way is totally sexy.

With these 20 sexy long hair ideas you are sure to find one or two that work for you. What are some of your favorite sexy long hair ideas?
