# These Pixie Cut Tutorials Are Perfect for Short Haired Girls ...

Pixie cuts are so adorable! I could never, ever do it because I don't believe I'd look good at all, but that's probably why I love them so much on other women. It's also the perfect time to go in for a crop if you've been thinking about taking the plunge because it is HOT outside. I'd love to chop off all my hair, I just wish it could automatically grow back in the fall. Since that's impossible, I'll continue admiring girls with cropped hair – and looking for amazing ways to style those cute pixie cuts, like these! If you already have a pixie or if you're going in for a chop, here's some style inspo for you!

## 1. Curly Faux Hawk

Faux hawks are so cute! I especially love them with curly hair. Seriously, how pretty is this? It's sexy, it's sassy, and it's just all around gorgeous. Follow Lorissa Turner's tips and you'll see how easy it is to pull off this style, too. Make your curls stand out and turn your short pixie into something super dynamic!

## 2. Curl & Style

A little curling, a little styling – that's really all you need to look fierce and fabulous. It's easy, it doesn't take long, and if you're rocking a short 'do, this may soon work its way into your weekly hairstyle rotation. Pay attention to the products, though – they're definitely helpful!

## 3. Aisha Ebony Style

Wait until you see what Aisha Ebony does to Daneille's hair! You'll definitely want to pay attention to the styling process and, again, the products are super essential. Fortunately, everything's really easy to follow along with – you can probably style your own hair as you watch. The result? A stunning, sleek pixie that you'll want to wear every single day!

## 4. Those Products

Speaking of products, I really can't overemphasize how important the right products are. You want your pixie style to stay in place, right? These products can also keep your hair healthy, shiny, soft, silky, and super manageable, so pay close attention!

## 5. Curly & Wavy

Cropped cuts look so amazing when they're curly and wavy. Curls and waves add so much texture and depth, plus you can do so much! Take a look at what the amazing Jazzy Rachel does to get her hair absolutely perfect. Am I the only one who just loves to watch her work?

## 6. Messy Curls

Messy curls are so sexy! Besides, who doesn't love the opportunity to leave their hair looking all tousled? This is another 'do that depends heavily on the products you use. The key is to make your hair manageable while also ensuring that it stays in place. You don't want to end up losing all those curls by the middle of the day, right?

## 7. Little Flips

Here's another tousled look. I am absolutely in love with this 'do! You'll need a flat iron – probably a small one that's better suited to short hair – along with a brush, a rat tail comb, and various other products, but it's really not hard to achieve these tousled little flips. Besides, the end result is definitely worth all the work!

How do you style your short hair? Give up some tips in the comments!
