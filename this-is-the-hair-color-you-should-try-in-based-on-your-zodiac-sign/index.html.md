# This is the Hair Color You Should Try in 2019 Based on Your Zodiac Sign ...

If you are anything like me, then the start of a new year always brings with it an itch to make a big change or adjustment in your life. Some people like to start a new job, some people like to start a new relationship, but what if you wanted to start a little bit smaller scale than that? Something that you can do quickly and efficiently is change your hair! Here is **the hair colour that you should try in 2019 according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

Fiery orange is going to be a big thing this year, and it matches an Aries perfectly because you are the fireiest and most passionate of all the signs!

## 2. Taurus

![Hair, Hairstyle, Hair coloring, Blond, Brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nx/oi/r8dl7frd5c686b44738ad535492873_600x696.jpg)

Metallics are going to be a big trend in 2019, and for a Taurus, rose gold is the tone that you should be aiming for. It's the perfect blend of subtle but stunning, which is the line that you always follow in your fashions.

## 3. Gemini

![Hair, Hair coloring, Hairstyle, Red, Layered hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hb/o6/x98herpr5c686bcd095ac853372629_600x683.jpg)

You are very much a dual personality, so why not express that in your hairstyle with a bold bi-colour look? Bring some of that zany Harley Quinn style energy!

## 4. Cancer

![Hair, Eyebrow, Face, Hair coloring, Pink,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/dh/lv3mm5es5c686bed35a14289397906_600x751.jpg)

Rose pink is the perfect hair colour for a Cancer. It exudes that soft, warm energy that you naturally have.

## 5. Leo

![Hair, Face, Blond, Hairstyle, Beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nf/ew/manh88mn5c686cc4b2afb344770746_600x750.jpg)

You need to show your lion pride with a full main of beautiful gold hair! It’s the queen of metallic colours, and you will certainly feel like royalty with the way people react to the look!

## 6. Virgo

![Hair, Blond, Hairstyle, Chin, Layered hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sz/so/sqfym76k5c686cf7727ce097020892_600x751.jpg)

A white-gold balayage is something that would look great on a Virgo. It has the perfect balance of harmony that embodies you own very chill lifestyle and attitude.

## 7. Libra

![Hair, Face, Eyebrow, Hairstyle, Blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fq/sf/ohl5hfkw5c686d2543480778578048_600x600.jpg)

If you have never gone brunette before, then this is the year to do it. It can be a drastic change from blonde, but you never know until you try!

## 8. Scorpio

![Hair, Red, Hairstyle, Hair coloring, Pink,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zg/ms/tgu7eg5u5c686d49720b2838147999_600x750.jpg)

You need a hairstyle that exemplifies your fiery and adventurous spirit, and a beautiful mulled wine hue is the exact thing for it.

## 9. Sagittarius

![Hair, Face, Blond, Eyebrow, Hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/ln/pm2x2jrf5c68855a246c9969444629_600x600.jpg)

Platinum bleach! It might seem scary to imagine, but trust me, you won’t want to go back once you see how amazing it looks on you.

## 10. Capricorn

![Hair, Blond, Hairstyle, Hair coloring, Long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pf/gu/da98dq4f5c6883f8ea1e7586144456_600x627.jpg)

Champagne tones are the way to go for you this year; something luxurious and opulent to match your sophisticated personality!

## 11. Aquarius

![Hair, Hairstyle, Face, Blond, Chin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ut/xu/fb2w31nc5c686db59aff6029183803_600x736.jpg)

## 12. Pisces
