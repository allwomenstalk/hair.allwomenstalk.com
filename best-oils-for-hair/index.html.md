# 6 Natural Oils That Will Rescue Your Hair from Distress ...

Every girl loves her hair and will do everything she can to make it look better, feel stronger, and grow faster. That's why you need to consider trying the best natural oils for hair that are increasingly available. It’s not uncommon to see tons of different bottles of shampoos, conditioners, and masks on the bathroom shelves of almost every single girl. However, sometimes we all forget that the answer to strong and healthy hair is much easier than you would expect. The best oils for hair can treat anything from dryness to dull color to flat strands. Here is a list of 6 natural oils for hair that will makes your locks luscious and lovely.

## 1. Castor Oil

This is one of the best existing oils to treat your hair. It prevents hair loss and helps with the regrowth process. Castor oil has the antiviral, antibacterial, and antifungal properties which make it an effective treatment for scalp disorders whether it’s dandruff or other problems. Also, this oil is rich in protein which stimulates hair growth and boosts the general health of your hair. You can also apply it to your eyelashes and eyebrows. Make sure to buy organic and hexane-free castor oil for the best results.

## 2. Burdock Root Oil

This oil deeply nourishes and supports your scalp and hair follicles, allowing your hair to grow thicker and faster. Burdock oil has vitamin A and essential fatty acids that are helpful in treating hair loss and scalp dryness. These nutrients are required for maintaining a healthy scalp and promoting natural hair growth. You can add it to your everyday shampoo or apply it as a mask three times a week.

## 3. Chamomile Oil

This oil has soothing and calming properties which are excellent for the anti-inflammatory treatments. Rubbing this oil on your scalp relieves itchiness that may be caused by dandruff, psoriasis, or other conditions. Also, chamomile oil can be used to protect your hair from various types of environmental damage. However, be aware that it might slightly lighten your hair color and give it a light golden shine.

## 4. Lavender Oil

The same as chamomile oil, lavender oil also has soothing properties and can be used for any type of hair. It has great healing properties for mild injuries on your scalp. If you massage lavender oil into your scalp it will improve blood flow and therefore will promote hair growth. Lavender oil also has antiseptic and antimicrobial properties that are important in fighting dandruff and other fungal disorders. Lavender oil deeply nourishes your skin and hair and prevents dryness and hair loss.

## 5. Tea Tree Oil

This oil will be your ultimate treatment for any skin or hair problem. It’s known to be a number one treatment for dandruff. Tea tree oil cleans out your hair follicles and promotes an unobstructed flow of natural skin oils, thus helping your hair to grow faster and healthier. It can be applied, by itself or in combination with other essential oils, two to three times per week.

## 6. Rosemary Oil

This oil is best for preventing premature graying and thinning of the hair, as well as for improving blood circulation. Though it can be used by people with all types of hair, it is best suited for those with oily hair. Similar to other oils mentioned above it helps with fighting dandruff and other scalp disorders that are caused by skin dryness. Even though it’s meant to reduce oiliness of hair, it still perfectly nourishes and strengthens your hair.
