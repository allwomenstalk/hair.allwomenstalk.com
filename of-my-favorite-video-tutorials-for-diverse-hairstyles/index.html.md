# 7 of My Favorite Video Tutorials for Diverse Hairstyles ...

I am eternally grateful for the creation of YouTube; having access to amazing tutorials for diverse hairstyles has helped save me from bad hair days! I've always seen myself as a bit of a visual learner. It's easier to check for progress if you have the video in front of you as opposed to reading directions without any images of the results. With these simple tutorials for diverse hairstyles, your wallet will be thanking you later for not having to run to the nearest (and expensive) beauty salon!

## 1. Pump It up Pin up- Natural Hair Tutorial

I love how attainable this retro-inspired look is, making this video one of my favorite tutorials for diverse hairstyles. Natural Resources Salon is a great source to turn to for incredible ways to style thicker hair. I love that there is a YouTube channel catered to black women! All of the tutorials featured on this site are incredibly diverse. The best part is that each one can be easily achieved with little need for outside products.

## 2. Five-Strand Side Braid

Braid guru LuxyHair has once again managed to bring complex braided hairstyles to the homes of viewers everywhere. If this type of braid isn't your niche, LuxyHair has plenty of other style for you to covet! I am in love the uniqueness of this hairdo and the fact that it looks difficult but isn't.

## 3. Vintage up-do

My friend is obsessed with this YouTube tutorial for perfecting this vintage half-up hairdo. The best part about it is that all it involves are simple rollers and a well-determined twist. This is one of the most devastatingly gorgeous and intricate hairstyles that I've seen in a long time. I admit that I am a total sucker for anything and everything vintage!

## 4. Beach Waves

This summery look is perfect for those days when you want to look like you just came from surfing the magnificent waves. Maintaining this look isn't as easy as it seems, which is what makes this quick tutorial all the more helpful. Get ready to finally rock this fun look with little effort!

## 5. French Roll

Following these simple instructions was conducive to me obtaining this beautiful hairdo! Watching this video made me feel more confident at the prospect of trying different hairstyles for my hair type. I like knowing that I can afford to break out of just wearing my hair down in a curly do. It also doesn't hurt that it is reminiscent of the late Lucile Ball. This just may be my go-to hairstyle!

## 6. Trim Your Own Bangs

Ladies, it's time to say goodbye to inconvenient trips to the beauty salon! This video is helpful in learning how to maintain your bold and beautiful bangs at home. Now you can achieve this look made even more famous by the brilliant Zooey Deschanel.

## 7. French Braid Fringe

Get ready to embrace your inner model with this final marvelous look. Out of all the tutorials featured above, this is one of my favorites. I love how versatile this style is; when I tried it on my hair, the results were astonishing! It will compliment both a daytime and nighttime look.

The amount of money that you can save from watching these videos that help you style your hair on your own is endless! What are some of your favorite styles featured here? What are your favorite hair tutorials to follow?
