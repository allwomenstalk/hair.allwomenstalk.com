# 9 Heavenly Hairsprays for Your Best Hair Day Yet ...

Love it or hate it, we all have hairstyles that require some extra hold at times and that’s when you’ll want to use one of these heavenly hairsprays to keep your style intact. I had a mild obsession with hairspray back in the day. I couldn’t fathom the idea of leaving my house without dousing my entire head with hairspray to make sure my hair didn’t budge. These days, I’m not so rigid when it comes to my hair and actually appreciate some movement in it. If you’ve fallen out of love with hairspray, take one of these heavenly hairsprays for a test drive and reignite the flame.

## 1. TRESemme Tres Two Extra Hold Hair Spray

![TRESemme Tres Two Extra Hold Hair Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/um/z27fe1q3_500x499.jpg)

Wondering exactly what it takes to be considered one of the heavenly hairsprays on this list? Well, it needs to be good quality, easy-to-use, hopefully has a nice fragrance and has a decent price tag. TRESemme made the list because it provides lots of hold without being sticky or stiff and keeps hair in place in various climates. Plus, you can’t beat the price. You can find it at many drugstores for under $5.

## 2. Bumble and Bumble Cityswept Finish

![Bumble and Bumble Cityswept Finish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/ae/ammvi229_450x448.jpg)

For ladies who don’t mind paying a little extra for their hair spray, Bumble and Bumble makes a top rated finishing spray that works with all hair types. Described as a misting balm, this spray is formulated to create a well-defined look that gives that piece-y effect that we all want while taming flyaways and providing lightweight hold.

## 3. Garnier Fructis Style Sleek and Shine anti-Humidity Hair Spray

![Garnier Fructis Style Sleek and Shine anti-Humidity Hair Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/qz/heglbzgd_500x500.jpg)

If you live in a humid area, this is another beauty bargain you won’t want to pass up. Garnier Fructis’ Anti-Humidity Hair Spray allows you to rock sleek, shiny hair that keeps its shape no matter how hot and bothered your hair might get. Perfect for the upcoming summer season, this will definitely be one of your hair go-tos when the weather gets warmer and your hair wants to misbehave.

## 4. Alterna Haircare Caviar anti-Aging Working Hair Spray

![Alterna Haircare Caviar anti-Aging Working Hair Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a8/t5/l6cvz9yd_450x448.jpg)

I’m still not sold on the whole anti-aging hair product thing, but I’m more than willing to try them out. If you’re looking for a hair care product that offers progressive, flexible control that you can brush through, resistance to humidity while adding fullness and shine-pick this up ASAP! You’ll never have to worry about your hair feeling hard and sticky again.

## 5. L’Oreal Elnett Satin Hair Spray

![L’Oreal Elnett Satin Hair Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ap/si/0itxn2zj_300x384.jpg)

Elnett hairspray has legions of fans around the world, but I also know that there are some people who don’t get the hype surrounding this product. I’m somewhere in the middle. I like the product, it provides reasonable hold without any stiffness or stickiness, but I hated the fragrance and wanted a little more hold. There is an unscented version of the product, but it still has a slight fragrance. So, while I’m not a hardcore fan of this product, I know the superfine mist and flexible hold make this a holy grail product for many that is certainly worth trying out.

## 6. Big Sexy Hair Spray and Play Volumizing Hairspray

![Big Sexy Hair Spray and Play Volumizing Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rr/p1/md2w922c_500x500.jpg)

Although Sexy Hair’s Spray and Play hairspray calls itself a volumizing spray, it does that and whole lot more. This hair spray provides movable hold, amazing lift, shine and it protects your tresses from damaging UV rays. Any hair type can enjoy this all-in-one hair care product and as a bonus, it holds up well against humidity, too!

## 7. Aveda Control Force Firm Hold Hair Spray

![Aveda Control Force Firm Hold Hair Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kc/qz/mjd4kb6i_300x399.jpg)

Aveda’s Control Force is a long lasting, firm hold hairspray that stands up well against heat, wind and humidity without leaving any flakes behind. The fast-drying formula is made with antioxidants and UV filters so hair stays healthy and in place all day. It also contains the organic essential oils Bulgarian lavender, bergamot, vetiver and palmarosa for a calming scent.

## 8. CHI Enviro 54 Hairspray Firm Hold

![CHI Enviro 54 Hairspray Firm Hold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0h/xy/rbwz85iw_416x401.jpg)

Using thermal styling tools daily can sure take a toll on your hair, but CHI’s Enviro 54 hairspray can help minimize that damage while providing lasting hold. Just a little spritz goes a long way to tame flyaways and frizzies, and it won’t weigh your hair down one bit. Using positively charged silk molecules, your hair will be strengthened and regenerated and you’ll be able to create various textures and styles that will last all day.

## 9. Herbal Essences Tousle Me Softly Tousling Hairpsray

![Herbal Essences Tousle Me Softly Tousling Hairpsray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/ni/p9xrcfux_500x499.jpg)

This is another drugstore hairspray that gets a lot of love from users. Herbal Essences found a way to give us the soft, tousled look without any stiffness or stickiness. Use this hairspray when you want your hair to be touchably soft and look effortlessly sexy. Fight frizz and humidity while your hair stays soft and shiny.

Every girl needs a good hairspray in their hair care arsenal. I hope this list of highly rated hairsprays will help you find your perfect match. Do you use hairspray? If so, what’s your favorite?
