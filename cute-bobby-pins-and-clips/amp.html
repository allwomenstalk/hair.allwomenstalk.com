10 Cute Bobby Pins and Clips ...

Tasha “Fancy” Hair Pins • Ukiyo5 Star Hairclips • Floral Rosette Claw Clip • Tasha “Meltamazing” Hair Pin • Fab Glitter Bow Pins • More ...

By Lyndsie

I really love bobby pins. They're pretty much my favorite kind of hair accessories, because they're so easy to use. It doesn't matter whether you have long hair or short hair, you can still use pins and clips. They hold hair back, up, or just make a really cute statement. In researching this, I found a ton of cute bobby pins and clips, and really wanted to share them with you!

1. Tasha “Fancy” Hair Pins

Price: $28.00 at shop.nordstrom.com
A lot of cute bobby pins and clips have rhinestones these days. It probably comes as no surprise that I like these hairclips because they are so pretty and pink. However, they also come in a really chic black. Either way, these clips would make a wonderful accessory for an updo, a down 'do, or even a simple ponytail. They provide just the kind of glamour you need to really dress up your hairstyle.

2. Ukiyo5 Star Hairclips

Price: $10.00 at shanalogic.com
If you're looking for something funky and really sassy, the stars will do the trick. They are PVC, for a really punk look -- which I, for one, always love. Unlike other hairclips, they have metal spirals, so they're really easy to twist into your hair.

3. Floral Rosette Claw Clip

Price: $3.80 at forever21.com
Many cute bobby pins and clips are made out of fabric. That gives them a really feminine look, which is great for summer. This particular clip would look really pretty with the right kind of dress. You just have to be careful of what you wear it with, since it does have a floral motif.

4. Tasha “Meltamazing” Hair Pin

Price: $20.00 at shop.nordstrom.com
I absolutely love this pin. It, too, comes in many different colors, but I love this metallic choice the best. This stone in the middle gives the pin a lot of glitz. However, it's the filigree surrounding the stone that I really love, and I think it looks better in this pewter kind of shade -- even though there's a pink option!

5. Fab Glitter Bow Pins

Price: $1.50 at forever21.com
Bows are really popular hair accessories right now. This pair is great, because they are alive with glitz and glitter. They can add just the right touch of glamour to an otherwise humdrum outfit. I also love the fact that they are blue, because although they come in different colors, you just don't see a lot of blue these days, especially with hair accessories.

6. Lovely Butterfly Hair Clip

Price: $7.00 at shanalogic.com
I love butterfly clips. Back when I was in high school, these were all the rage, and it's nice to see them coming back. The detail work on this butterflies gorgeous. It almost seems like it could fly straight off your head of nectar. It's a simple bobby pin, so it willlook great with any kind of hairstyle. It, too, comes in different colors, but I like the purple best because it seems little bit whimsical.

7. Animal Instinct Hair Clips

Price: $2.50 at forever21.com
There's no denying the enduring popularity of animal prints. I have to admit, I don't usually like animal prints myself, but these are really cute. They keep up with the bow trend as well, and would make a really cute addition to your hair, whether you're wearing it up or down.

8. Clip Us up, Scottie

Price: $7.99 at modcloth.com
A lot of cute bobby pins and clips these days have a kind of vintage feel, and these epitomize that. These little Scottie dogs are so adorable! If you're really into a whimsical look, you don't have to look any farther than this. What could be cuter?

9. Vintage Candy Pops Hair Clips

Price: $10.00 at shanalogic.com
Don't these look absolutely delicious? They're totally lollipop-cute, and for that reason I simply couldn't resist including them. They're absolutely retro, which is another enduring trend right now. For a little bit of candy sweetness in your look, these bobby pins are all you need!

10. Lace Chiffon Floral Bobby Pin

Price: $1.50 at forever21.com
I absolutely adore chiffon, so it's no surprise that I fell for this. It has a blue option as well, but I love the black and cream best. I think it gives the pin a really vintage appeal. Besides that, it would be the perfect addition to a little black dress.

I hope you think these are cute bobby pins and clips, too. Of course, everyone has different tastes, especially when it comes to the accessories you put in your hair. What kind of hair accessories do you like the best?
