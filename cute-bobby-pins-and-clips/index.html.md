# 10 Cute Bobby Pins and Clips ...

I really love bobby pins. They're pretty much my favorite kind of hair accessories, because they're so easy to use. It doesn't matter whether you have long hair or short hair, you can still use pins and clips. They hold hair back, up, or just make a really cute statement. In researching this, I found a ton of cute bobby pins and clips, and really wanted to share them with you!

**Price:** $28.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A lot of cute bobby pins and clips have rhinestones these days. It probably comes as no surprise that I like these hairclips because they are so pretty and pink. However, they also come in a really chic black. Either way, these clips would make a wonderful accessory for an updo, a down 'do, or even a simple ponytail. They provide just the kind of glamour you need to really dress up your hairstyle.
