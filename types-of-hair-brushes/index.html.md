# The Ultimate Hairbrush Guide to Tame Those Locks ...

There are so many **types of hair brushes**. A hairbrush guide? No, we aren't kidding you. And this could definitely be a step up in the hair game for you, girl! Because obviously, hair is as important as makeup and your outfit, right? And what's scarier than a bad hair day. So, tame your hair with the right brushes and trust us when we say this - it will make it a whole lot easier. Well yeah, you can thank us later! Here are some **types of hair brushes** to choose from.

## 1. PADDLE BRUSH

When in a hurry, the paddle brush won't ever let you down. Get one of that fits right into your bag and brush that mane on the go. It will detangle the hair easily and would help in with scalp stimulation. Use a natural bristle paddle brush if you have thin hair as it would evenly distribute the hair oil from the roots to the tips while being gentle on your strands. If you have dry hair, go for the synthetic ones as they cause least static.

## 2. SMALL BRISTLE BRUSH

If those bangs are out of place, use a small bristle brush. Usually used for bangs and short hair, it easily combs through and will definitely help to keep the bangs in place.

## 3. VENTED METAL round BRUSH

Volume is the key. Thick voluminous locks are what every girl prays for. But no more praying because you just got lucky. Get a vented brush and blow dry those locks speedily into a voluminous hairstyle. Curl as you reach the tips and voila! It's relaxed yet looks beautiful. Plus, it's also anti-static.

## 4. EXTENSION BRUSH

The only difference between a paddle brush and an extension brush is that the bristles on the extension brush are looped so as to not get caught in your extensions. If you wear extensions, we recommend using an extension brush instead of a regular paddle brush to keep those extensions nice and smooth throughout the day.

## 5. Teasing Comb

A teasing comb is the ultimate hair styling tool for the very dressy girl in you. There is no way we could've gotten the required volume in the hair updos. You could also use it to part your hair evenly. Create an amazing hairstyle with this for any special occasion. It always has your back. Just tease and smooth!

## 6. WIDE TOOTH COMB

Used for detangling wet hair, it glides through and provides minimum hair breakage when combing through wet locks. If you have curly hair, then feel happy for yourself. Use the wide tooth comb in the shower when you use a conditioner to evenly distribute the conditioner in your hair.

## 7. MIXED BRISTLE BRUSH

Mixed bristles help to distribute hair oils evenly from the roots to the tips, which polishes hair. The best part about them is that they suit all hair types, no matter what. They are popular among stylists and surely make you feel like one when you use them. Invest in a good one and watch the magic.

## 8. Round Brush

Need those amazing natural looking curls, pick up your dryer and a round brush. The thinner the brush, the tighter your curls. Work your way through the hair and create romantic curls or a sexy bombshell look.
