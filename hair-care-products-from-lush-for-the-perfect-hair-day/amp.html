9 Hair Care Products from LUSH for the Perfect Hair Day ...

No Drought • Soak and Float • Shine so Bright • Curly Wurly • Happy Happy Joy Joy • More ...

By Lyndsie

Hair care products from LUSH will make sure you always have a great hair day. It doesn't matter whether your scalp is itchy or oily, whether your hair is thick or fine, you'll find something that suits your hair type. I've tried to showcase a versatile mix of hair care products from LUSH, but this is just a sampling of the fab products they offer. Don't be afraid to share your favorites, and feel free to browse for something tailored specifically to your hair type!

1. No Drought

I use a lot of hair care products from LUSH myself and can personally vouch for this one. I hate washing my hair every day but also have no desire to go back to my '90s grunge roots by rocking an oily scalp. LUSH's No Drought dry shampoo gets rid of all that oil and gives you serious volume, all while giving your hair gorgeous texture and a wonderful matte finish. The scent is also tops, an intoxicating mix of lime and grapefruit that will leave you sniffing your locks all day. Depending on the size you buy, prices range between $6.95 and $13.95.

2. Soak and Float

Are you still using shampoo out of a bottle? No way, it's 2014! Help yourself to a shampoo bar – especially one that, to quote LUSH, “smells like campfires.” It's also ideal for any irritation, so if you have a dry scalp or a problem with flakes and dandruff, this is your answer. It uses cade oil to banish the microbes that lead to itchy scalps and dandruff, along with lavender oil and marigold petals.

3. Shine so Bright

This is a fantastic hair balm that can prevent your ends from splitting while giving you marvelous shine. Thanks to olive oil, free range eggs, extra virgin coconut oil, and shea butter, your hair will be shiny and strong. You can also use it as a finishing balm to tame the frizz and fly-aways. Thanks to scents like rosewood, grapefruit and orange flowers, and neroli, your tresses will also smell scrumptious! It's yours for just $9.95.

4. Curly Wurly

Make no mistake, this isn't just for curly hair, although it will definitely do wonders for those curls. This coconut shampoo is ideal for anyone with frizz, but it's also amazing for dry hair. If your locks are a little unruly, using this will get you lots of bounce, shine, and volume, without any of those frizzy fly-aways. At $25.95, it's a little more expensive, but you get nearly 8 ounces and you only need a bit. Thanks to the addition of avocado butter and eggs, your hair will also be much stronger and more moisturized.

5. Happy Happy Joy Joy

The name might take you back to Ren and Stimpy, but the actual product will put your hair in heaven. Available in small, medium, and large at prices ranging between $12.95 and $39.95, this conditioner is entirely vegan. It uses orange blossom water, rose water, and nourishing almond milk to leave your hair soft and conditioned. It's especially great for processed, colored, and bleached hair.

6. Sea Spray

LUSH doesn't just have shampoo and conditioner, of course. If you're looking for more natural products, look no further. Sea Spray is an amazing hair mist that promises not just volume, but those enviable, beach-ready waves – even if your hair is pin straight. It uses seaweed as well as sea salt, and will also give you shine while helping your hair hold its shape. Besides, at $12.95, it's the same price as comparable products filled with chemicals rather than refreshing, natural ingredients.

7. Retread

Here's another conditioner, specifically made to fight the frizz. No matter your hair type, Retread will smooth your hair and make it shiny, while providing plenty of bounce. It will nourish and moisturize, owing to a delicious combination of yogurt, cantaloupe, seaweed, soya milk, avocado, jojoba, and olive oils, and lanolin. That's seriously power packed, and it's yours for $29.95.

8. The Hair Doctor

You don't need a prescription for this hair treatment, which is perfect for processed hair that needs a little help. If you've got an irritated scalp or a processing problem, using this deep cleanser will get your hair back in shape. With some peppermint oil, sweet almond oil, and coconut oil, your hair will be soft, hydrated, and full of volume. It's $15.95 in stores.

9. Dirty

This hair cream is another product you simply can't live without. The best part is that it's great for girls and guys! It's a firmly holding cream but it leaves your hair looking natural, not sticky and stiff. It's part of the Dirty line as well, so you know it smells divine. Get three and a half ounces for $15.95 and you'll never style your hair with anything else!

LUSH has something for every imaginable hair style, whether your hair is dry or oily, lackluster or frizzy. If these products don't do it for you, I promise you can still find what you need. In the meantime, do you have any other hair care recommendations?

If you're on the curly side like me, you’ve got to try Curly Wurly from LUSH. It's moisturizing and leaves curls so bouncy and happy. Plus, the coconut scent—yum!

Flyaways can be a pain, right? LUSH has some great moisturizing shampoos and conditioners that tame those pesky hairs. Trust me, giving them a go makes all the difference!
Is there a LUSH product recommended for hair and scalp health?
Oh yes, Hair Doctor from LUSH is a must-try. It's like a head spa in a jar that revitalizes both your hair and scalp. You'll feel so refreshed afterwards!
