Frequently Asked Questions

What is Northern Lights hair color?
It's a vibrant hair dye style inspired by the colorful auroras, featuring shades like greens, purples, and blues.
Can I try Northern Lights hair on dark hair?
Yes! Dark hair can be lightened first to make the colors pop beautifully.
How long does Northern Lights hair color last?
With proper care, the vivid colors typically last 4-6 weeks before fading.
Is Northern Lights hair damaging to my hair?
Lightening hair can cause some damage, but using deep conditioners helps maintain hair health.
How do I maintain Northern Lights hair color?
Use color-safe, sulfate-free shampoos and avoid excessive heat styling to keep colors bright.
Can I do Northern Lights hair at home?
It's best done by a professional stylist to get the perfect blend and avoid damage.
Will Northern Lights hair suit my skin tone?
These colors are versatile and can be customized to complement your unique skin tone.
How often should I touch up Northern Lights hair?
Touch-ups every 4-6 weeks help maintain the vibrancy of the colors.
What styles work best with Northern Lights hair?
Layered cuts or waves enhance the multi-dimensional color effect beautifully.
Is Northern Lights hair suitable for professional settings?
Yes, with the right color intensity, it can look stylish yet appropriate for many workplaces.
