# 10 Got to Have Hairstyles for Girls with Long Hair ...

ATTENTION ALL LADIES WITH LONG LOCKS! We all know how frustrating it can be having long hair, (at times). The tedious side work, long mornings spent in the bathroom, when you just can't seem to tame the beast head of hair, and we can't forget the whole in your wallet after shopping for hair products. With the warmer weather right around the corner, it's not unusual to want to just cut all your hair off. We picked out some the latest and greatest hairstyles for the warm months to come. They're easy to do and will leave all ladies with long hair feeling refreshed and sexy.

## 1. Get Beachy

![Get Beachy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x1/mi/dhkr6zho550cc4b382b19951650239_465x700.jpg)

These beach curls are perfect for the Spring and Summer. They look so natural and are very easy to do.

## 2. Natural Blonde

![Natural Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/se/a5/h357h2i4550cc4b3a5094579236371_305x608.jpg)

These light shades of blonde seem to be the latest and greatest for the warmer weather. The [loose curls](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/summer-hair-loose-waves-and-curls-are-causing-a-stir/) are adorable!

## 3. Colorful Headband

![Colorful Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iy/j7/a7ab848b550cc4b40e1bc207273196_400x504.jpg)

Headbands are the perfect accessory for any outfit in the Summer, whether you're wearing blue jeans or a sun dress. I love the colors and patten in this one.

## 4. Messy Bun

![Messy Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k6/sq/zey8xsl1550cc4b42d4b7582317158_500x750.jpg)

You can never go wrong with the messy bun. It's a great way to show off your eyes and keep you feeling cool, while still looking your best.

## 5. Cute Braid + Pony Tail

![Cute Braid + Pony Tail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ls/ai/faejysqx550cc4b4c6708443027363_600x600.jpg)

This hairstyle is so cute. The high pony with the side braid is perfect for the upcoming months. She rocks it with those shades and gold jewelry!

## 6. ) Low Pony and Braid

![) Low Pony and Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/vr/awm6e7ub550cc4b4edc2d803841395_441x608.jpg)

Braids are IN this Spring/Summer! They look so classy and elegant. You can use braids to dress up or dress down any outfit.

## 7. ) Simple and Straight

![) Simple and Straight](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/ce/vicro0pl550cc4b4c94a9079387477_443x699.jpg)

There is something so becoming about long, straight hair. It's simple and very easy to do! The half up/half down is adorable too.

## 8. Volume and Curls

![Volume and Curls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/du/zp1l2hm2550cc4b550de5211976161_500x750.jpg)

I am obsessed with this hairstyle. The loose curls with the bangs pulled back into a poof is super chic and attractive.

## 9. Ombre

![Ombre](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/yw/d6i3cw7i550cc4b63435f363245680_600x912.jpg)

Light hair colors are always a must for the warm weather. I love the Ombre look. It's so stylish and fun.

## 10. Messy Ponytail

![Messy Ponytail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vz/u3/a51fjkn7550cc4b5b2900088441159_600x600.jpg)

If done right, the messy ponytail is perfect for any occasion in the Spring and Summer. It can make your hair look so much more thicker and fuller.

Now, after seeing these adorable hairstyle trends go and play around with these ideas and let us know which style you like the best in the comments! Happy Spring!
