# 38 Gorgeous Braids You've Got to Learn Now ...

It's summer, and to my delight, gorgeous braids and braided hairstyles are still totally on-trend. So whether you're more Elsa or Katniss, or someone in between, here are a few gorgeous braided hairstyles to kick your summer style up a notch (or three).

## 1. Braided Headband

![hair,hairstyle,long hair,hair coloring,brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/dt/hem05_hair_hairstyle_long-hair_hair-coloring_brown-hair_479x638.jpg)

Via [Hairstyles for Long Hair](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/242842604881386743/)
This is my favorite braided hairstyle, and it's actually pretty simple to do! Tiny braids from each side of your hair, pulled across to make a band, and fastened with a boddy pin or clip behind each ear. It's so pretty! Maybe not the best for humid summer days, but ideal for indoors (air conditioning) or cooler nights.

## 2. Braided Bangs

![hair,human hair color,red,blond,face,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/z2/rb9aa_hair_human-hair-color_red_blond_face_236x471.jpg)

Via [Quick & Easy Hair Ideas ...](https://www.hercampus.com/school/bryant/quick-easy-hair-ideas-class/)
This one's easy, too! Start at one side, French braid across, then once you get past your temple, just braid the end and clip back.

## 5. Braid and Bun

![hair,face,black and white,hairstyle,monochrome photography,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/zs/wpbvt_hair_face_black-and-white_hairstyle_monochrome-photography_325x425.jpg)

## 6. Fishtail+

![hair,hairstyle,blond,hair coloring,long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/e0/r94hl_hair_hairstyle_blond_hair-coloring_long-hair_500x496.jpg)

## 7. Braided Band

![hair,blond,hairstyle,clothing,long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v6/kj/tl37g_hair_blond_hairstyle_clothing_long-hair_552x736.jpg)

Via [15 Cute Hairstyles with Braids ...](https://pophaircuts.com/cute-hairstyles-with-braids)

## 8. Multiple French Braids

![hair,hairstyle,face,braid,french braid,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ig/z6/ht2br_hair_hairstyle_face_braid_french-braid_236x295.jpg)

Via [Thick white girl cornrows. Section ...](https://goo.gl/SWKRDt)
This one looks complicated, but it's not. Flip your head upside down, and do three of four French braids, securing each with a clip til you're done with all of them. Then gather all of them into a high ponytail, fasten with a hair elastic, and you're done!
