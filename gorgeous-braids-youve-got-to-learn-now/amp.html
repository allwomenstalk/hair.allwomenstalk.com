38 Gorgeous Braids Youve Got to Learn Now ...

By Jennifer

It's summer, and to my delight, gorgeous braids and braided hairstyles are still totally on-trend. So whether you're more Elsa or Katniss, or someone in between, here are a few gorgeous braided hairstyles to kick your summer style up a notch (or three).

1. Braided Headband

Via Hairstyles for Long Hair
This is my favorite braided hairstyle, and it's actually pretty simple to do! Tiny braids from each side of your hair, pulled across to make a band, and fastened with a boddy pin or clip behind each ear. It's so pretty! Maybe not the best for humid summer days, but ideal for indoors (air conditioning) or cooler nights.

2. Braided Bangs

Via Quick & Easy Hair Ideas ...
This one's easy, too! Start at one side, French braid across, then once you get past your temple, just braid the end and clip back.

3. Braided Butterfly

Via Butterfly Hairstyle - AllDayChic
I don't have a clue how to do this one - YouTube, anyone? - but wow, is it pretty!

4. Waterfall Braid

Via Pictures of Waterfall Braid For ...
There are a million YouTube tutorials for this one, but I'm sure your stylist already knows how to do it. It's so pretty!

5. Braid and Bun

Via Hair, Makeup, Skin & Nails
This one's simple, too! Do a loose French braid for the top of your hair, then fasten with a hair elastic at the top, and make a messy bun.

6. Fishtail+

Via Beauty
Braid a few strands of hair, then gather all of your hair over one shoulder and make a fishtail braid. So Boho!

7. Braided Band

Via 15 Cute Hairstyles with Braids ...
Tease the back of your hair for the "bump," then braid the long strands at your temples and pin them back under the "bump." So pretty!

8. Multiple French Braids

Via Thick white girl cornrows. Section ...
This one looks complicated, but it's not. Flip your head upside down, and do three of four French braids, securing each with a clip til you're done with all of them. Then gather all of them into a high ponytail, fasten with a hair elastic, and you're done!

9. Bridal Braids

Via Swoonworthy Braided Wedding Hairstyles
How romantic and sweet! Wouldn't this be gorgeous for a summer bride?

10. French to Fishtail

Via 14 Braided Hairstyles for 2014 ...
Start at one side, with a French braid, then for the end, finish with a fishtail.

11. Trio

Via beautyhigh.com
Love her ombre color, love her trio of braids.

12. Boho Milkmaid

Via Friday Favorites
Make two braids, wind them round your head, fasten with bobby pins, done!

I have no idea how to do this one - there's so much going on here! - but it sure is pretty!

14. French Braid + Bun

Via Lovely Loxs!: Wedding
French braid each side, and wrap around a low bun - pretty for bridesmaids!

15. The Flower Braid

Again, no idea how this is done, but wow is it pretty!

17. Punk

If Barbie had braids, this might be what she'd look like - French braids on the sides, with a high pony.

18. Braids + Pony

Via A Red-Hot Braided Hairstyle With ...

19. Hair Inspo

A few small crown braids, tucked into a low ponytail - effortless chic!

20. French Bangs
