# These Essential Oils Are a Must for Girls Wanting Healthier , Longer Hair ...

Essential oils are amazing and there are several **essential oils for longer, healthier hair**! There are so many ways to use them that can be beneficial for so many aspects of your life. One of the various ways that essential oils can help your life is by helping make your hair not only healthier, but also longer. As an added perk, they can even make your hair feel silkier and smell fabulous! It is important to remember that essential oils should always be mixed with a carrier oil before using them on your skin. Some of the common carrier oils include coconut oil, almond oil, jojoba oil, sunflower oil, and olive oil. Here are some of the best **essential oils for longer, healthier hair**.

## 1. Lavender Essential Oil

Lavender essential oil has so many amazing uses, which is why it tops my list of **essential oils for longer, healthier hair**. When used as part of a hair treatment, it can help prevent hair loss, promote hair growth, and increase blood circulation. It’s a fantastic moisturizer, so it is also great for those that suffer from dandruff. Due to it’s calming properties, it can even help you fall asleep easier if used at night.

Homemade Remedy:
8-10 drops of lavender essential oil
4 tsp of a carrier oil

Massage into your scalp and leave in overnight if possible. Wash out in the morning.

## 2. Rosemary Essential Oil

Rosemary essential oil is a wonderful, earthy oil that has amazing beneficial properties. For your hair, it can increase cellular metabolism, which will stimulate hair growth and healing. Aside from helping to promote hair growth, rosemary essential oil can help prevent baldness, slow the appearance of gray hairs, treat dandruff, and clear up clogged pores that cause acne and an itchy scalp.

Homemade Remedy:
5-8 drops of rosemary essential oil
4 tsp of a carrier oil

Massage into your scalp and leave in for 3-4 hours. Wash as normal afterward.

## 3. Roman Chamomile Essential Oil

Although either type of chamomile essential oil is fantastic, Roman chamomile essential oil seems to work better for promoting hair growth. This essential oil is known for being soothing and having anti-inflammatory properties, so it works great for helping to cure a dry, itchy scalp and dandruff. As a nice added bonus, chamomile essential oil is a natural conditioner, so it will leave your hair soft and shiny.

Homemade Remedy:
4 drops of Roman chamomile essential oil
2 tsp of a carrier oil

Massage into your scalp, then rinse it out.

Homemade Remedy 2:
5 drops of Roman chamomile essential oil
1 tbsp of sea salt
1/3 cup of baking soda
Warm water

Mix to make a paste. Massage paste into your scalp and at the base of your hair. Leave in for 30 minutes, then rinse it out.

## 4. Cedarwood Essential Oil

Cedarwood essential oil has a wonderful woodsy aroma and is fantastic for hair growth. Because it balances the oil producing glands of the scalp and significantly improves blood circulation, it helps promote vigorous hair growth. It’s also known to help with fungal problems and dandruff buildup.

Homemade Remedy:
3 drops of cedarwood essential oil
2 tbsp of a carrier oil

Massage into your scalp and smooth it through your hair. Leave it in for 1 hour, then rinse it out.

## 5. Clary Sage Essential Oil

Clary sage essential oil is a super oil that has a sweet, yet earthy aroma. It is known for its healing properties, and when used for a hair treatment it can promote hair growth, prevent premature balding, normalize oil production, and control dandruff. As well, it can even help manage frizzy and curly hair.

Homemade Remedy:
10 drops of clary sage essential oil
2 tsp of a carrier oil

Massage into your scalp. Leave it in for 30 minutes, then wash it out.

While there are other essential oils that can be beneficial for your hair, the five listed above are the best out there. If you’ve tried essential oils as a hair treatment, let us know about it in the comments!
