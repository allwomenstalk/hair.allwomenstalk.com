# 7 Trendiest Hairstyles for Teens That You Should Try Today ...

There are tons of adorable, trendy hairstyles for teens that you should try! If you haven't already caught onto the latest trends in teen hairstyles, it's time that you did. These hairstyles for teens would look fabulous on anyone, but seem to be tailor made for a youthful face like yours. So next time you're at a loss for hairstyles, try out one of these stylish 'dos!

## 1. Fishtail

![Fishtail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ex/ov/ijb70wx6_600x903.jpg)

Braids are becoming increasingly popular, especially as hairstyles for teens! Fishtail braids look tricky, but once you get the hang of them, they are easy as 1, 2, 3! Yet despite their relatively simple structure, fishtails are stylish beyond belief. They're a great go-to style for when you want a laid back look, but still want to be a trendy teen!

## 2. Pixie Cut

![Pixie Cut](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/nl/jearzgh2_600x904.jpg)

This style is not for the faint of heart, as it may require that you make some drastic changes! After all, to quote the fabulous Coco Chanel, "A woman who cuts her hair is about to change her life." Your teenage years are all about making bold choices and testing your own limits. Everything you do now helps shape who you become later, and if you want your hair to be a part of that you should consider a pixie cut! We know that this hairstyle is increasingly popular and forever fashionable, as nearly every celebrity has tried it out at least once! So if you're courageous enough, give this trendy hairstyle a shot!

## 3. Pastel Colors

![Pastel Colors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f9/sm/xzwpu8ia_600x902.jpg)

Another bold move in the world of hairstyles is pastel-dyed hair. I don't think anyone really anticipated this trend, but it's here and seriously trendy! Not everyone can rock pastel colors equally, but if you think you can, why not give it a shot? Once you start experimenting with lilacs and lavenders on your lovely locks, you may get hooked on this fun and daring trend!

## 4. Long and Layered

![Long and Layered](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/b6/jn2bnlcr_600x820.jpg)

Okay, so maybe scissors and hair dyes aren't your thing. No worries, there are still plenty of trendy hairstyles for girls sporting long, "virgin hair"! A great example is the long and layered look! We all have Jennifer Aniston's beautiful layered hair to thank for this immortal trend! But young stars, such as Selena Gomez, rock this look and so can you! It doesn't take much time to visit your hairdresser and achieve this look. All you need to do is keep up with it regularly and you'll have one of the trendiest hairstyles for teens!

## 5. Messy Bun

![Messy Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/a9/iqta2qo3_426x640.jpg)

At first, I didn't understand this trend at all! I thought girls were just too lazy to actually do their hair in the morning. But then I found out that girls were actually trying to achieve the messy bun look, watching tutorials on YouTube and spending time in the morning perfecting their look each day! So if you haven't already, today is the day to experiment with this carefree, trendy hairstyle!

## 6. Frozen-inspired Side Braid

![Frozen-inspired Side Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bn/gb/pyyzocrb_600x800.jpg)

If you're looking for a truly trendy hairstyle, you can't go wrong with a Frozen-inspired side braid! This Disney movie touched the hearts of every teen who saw it, and inspired many of them to model their hair after Queen Elsa. Side braids were already trending before Frozen was released, but their popularity surged after Elsa and Anna took the world by storm! That's why one of the trendiest looks a teen can achieve is a Frozen-inspired side braid!

## 7. Beach Waves

![Beach Waves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/ge/jkw0s94w_600x1065.jpg)

Come spring and summer, beach waves hair will become one of the trendiest hairstyles around! Countless young celebs have sported this look, and you can too! Nothing screams summer and youth like beach waves. Plus, beach waves look amazing on anyone and everyone!

There you have it! Seven fabulous and trendy hairstyles for teens! If you can, I encourage you to try all of them at some point. But maybe you'll choose a favorite and make it your signature look! Which of these hairstyles are you eager to try?
