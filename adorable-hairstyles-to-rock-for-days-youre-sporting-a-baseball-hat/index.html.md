# Adorable Hairstyles to Rock for Days Youre Sporting a Baseball Hat ...

Wearing a baseball hat shouldn't be reserved for bad hair days. You can totally rock the hat anytime you want a cute casual look. The trick is figuring out what to do with your hair so it looks awesome too. Need some inspiration? You have come to the right place. Here are some really easy tutorials that will help you style your hair in the most perfect way for those days when you throw on a baseball hat and go. You'll never look cuter!

## 1. Secrets of a Stylist

When it comes to taking advice, a stylist is always someone you can trust. In this easy video, you'll learn how to wear a baseball hat without sacrificing your adorable style. It's as easy as creating some loose waves and then topping it with your favorite hat. Awesome!

## 2. Hair and Makeup

This fantastic tutorial will show you how to do your hair for a baseball hat and how to do your makeup to go with it. The end result is a super fun, but casual look that's great for weekends or evenings out with friends. Once you see how awesome you look, you'll want to wear a baseball hat everywhere you go.

## 3. Perfect for Outdoors

Sometimes you're wearing a baseball hat because you're playing sports or going for a run. That doesn't mean you have to give up having cute hair though. In this video, you'll learn how to make your hair look cute, even if you're wearing a hat out of necessity. Whether you're on the baseball field, at the beach or playing Frisbee at the park, you'll be the best looking girl there.

## 4. Three Fantastic Styles

In this video, you get three really cute hairstyles that work perfectly with a baseball hat. One is loose and flowing, one is braids and the other is a low bun with some pieces flowing out. Whichever one you choose, you'll be the prettiest girl everywhere you go. And they're so easy!

## 5. For Medium Short Hair

If your hair is short or medium in length, this is the video for you. It's loaded with three simple looks that let you wear a baseball hat without looking like a slob. Whether you go with the cute braids or the other looks, you will be totally happy with how you look.

## 6. Perfect for Summer

In this great tutorial, you'll learn two styles that are great for warmer weather. Since you probably wear your baseball hat more often in the summertime, it's a great idea to have a couple of go to looks that let you rock whatever outfit you're wearing with your hat. You're going to love these, I promise!

## 7. Basball Cap Styles

If you want something trendy and super cute, this is the video for you. The adorable girl in it will teach you how to do a down your back braid with a ponytail that's super cute. It's easy and gives you a style that you can bet won't be something all the other girls are wearing.

Which of these is your favorite? How often to you wear hats?
