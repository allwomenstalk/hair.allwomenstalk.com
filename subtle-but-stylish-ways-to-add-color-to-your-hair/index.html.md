# 17 Subtle but Stylish Ways to Add Color to Your Hair ...

I always say that your hair is the one thing you can really play with, but all the same, adding a bright, unexpected, or uncommon color to your hair is kind of daunting. Bold shades are just that – bold. You might not be ready for a standout change, but that doesn't mean you have to completely avoid adding vivid hues to your hair. You can go for a subtle, stylish approach at first – after all, you can always add more, but you can't take away the color.

## 1. Pale Pastel Tips

![Pale Pastel Tips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zr/zz/u5inxlg956f353b9bcdbf481156440_467x700.jpg)

Pastel shades like this mix surprisingly well with light hair.

## 2. Just the Tips

![Just the Tips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jd/zv/bj7wvgit56f353b93964e373909129_600x900.jpg)

Add color to just the tips of your hair and, if you need to, you can easily hide it.

## 3. In the Bangs

![In the Bangs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/h9/r3f0o04j56f353b925319321845400_506x640.jpg)

This is so dated, but I remember all the cool, brainy college girls rocking this look back in the late '90s and early '00s.

## 4. Dark at the Roots

![Dark at the Roots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xy/tk/dgg4qdra56f353b976c3a719658590_459x705.jpg)

I just love this color, sorry, but transitioning from dark roots to your hair color of choice is a great way to add a pop to your look.

## 5. Face Framing

![Face Framing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/az/pn/yudrlvg256f353bb3d84e986474517_600x861.jpg)

A few wisps of color around the face – perfection! Also, I die for Rachel in this picture.

## 6. Subtle but Stunning Highlights

![Subtle but Stunning Highlights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mt/rd/lkueaj5556f353ba70284887085308_600x600.jpg)

I love everything about this. Every single thing. This girl slays her whole look! And silvery highlights in dark hair, that is magnificent.

## 7. Go with Grey

![Go with Grey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l5/sn/qt4egsmt56f353ba5a3d3283925812_600x900.jpg)

I confess, I love the grey trend. It's a natural color, after all. Tone it with blue, green, and a little lavender, though, and the results are truly gorgeous.

## 8. The Girl with Her Hair on Fire

![The Girl with Her Hair on Fire](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pz/s2/lc8sebwe56f353bbce545437776696_588x747.jpg)

Yes. Yes to all of this. Absolutely on fire.

## 9. Start in the Middle

![Start in the Middle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pa/yv/j7bzlwrx56f353baa7b65128221234_600x780.jpg)

You have to admit, this is so pretty. Look at the subtle strands of pink hiding up top!

## 10. Blended from the Back

![Blended from the Back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/pk/zmu20a9256f353bc57b11138214260_520x781.jpg)

Blend color from the back and near the bottom of your hair to keep it a teensy bit of a secret.

## 11. Sprinkle in Little Wisps of Color

![Sprinkle in Little Wisps of Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/al/dl6vey8h56f353bb724cf155468260_600x600.jpg)

This style is absolutely iridescent.

## 12. Pick a Dark Shade

![Pick a Dark Shade](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kp/w7/n1aw7dyo56f353bbba5ad091255366_500x694.jpg)

Remember, shades of blue and purple can be so dark, they look almost black. That look is dramatic, yes, but it's still technically kind of subtle.

## 13. Layer Your Shades

![Layer Your Shades](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l8/d3/niwu0aw956f353bc15832927956947_600x611.jpg)

Your natural hair blends in with the brightly colored bits. I think this looks particularly lovely with longer hair.

## 14. Put in a Few Pastels

![Put in a Few Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j7/xf/b7958y9n56f354181d7bd762174140_500x827.jpg)

You might choose to wash out the color a bit more, though.

## 15. Blend with Blonde

![Blend with Blonde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/k1/j7n3jvfc56f353bc54c93584021374_600x600.jpg)

Told you this worked.

## 16. Sorry, I've Got a Thing for Purple

![Sorry, I've Got a Thing for Purple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dz/kr/hkmrpb6h56f353bc68559312205191_480x698.jpg)

I'm not really sorry

## 17. Choose Extreme Contrasts

![Choose Extreme Contrasts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/o0/fqyizisj56f353bdf134d661562262_600x800.jpg)

Like blonde and copper. Blonde and anything, really. Copper and anything, also.

The best part is that you can now use impermanent and semi-permanent color products, plus you don't have to rely on dye. Think you might make a bold hair move now?
