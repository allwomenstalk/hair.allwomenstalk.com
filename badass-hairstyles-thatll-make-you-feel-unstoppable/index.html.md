# 7 Badass Hairstyles Thatll Make You Feel Unstoppable ...

You don't need to shave half of your head or dye your hair pink to make a statement. There are plenty of edgy hairstyles that'll change up your entire look without forcing you to make a permanent change. That way, you can look sweet and innocent one day and badass the next. If you're ready to feel unstoppable, you need to test out these edgy hairstyles:

## 1. The Vikings Inspired Hairstyle

It doesn't matter if you've ever seen The Vikings, because you can appreciate this hairstyle. It looks a little messy, but that's in nowadays, so you won't feel out of place! You should start by leaving your hair in a braid overnight, so that it looks crimped in the morning. Then you can start styling it!

## 2. Edgy Waves

You don't have to have long hair for it to look badass. Girls with short hair can look just as sexy. This tutorial will show you how to create big, edgy hair when you don't have all that much to work with. After a little help from hairspray and a flat iron, you'll have hair that everyone will compliment you on.

## 3. French Braided Ponytail

This is a style inspired by Nicole Scherzinger from The Pussycat Dolls. It requires you to divide your bangs into three sections, so you can braid them. Of course, you should be incorporating hair from other places of your head to create the crazy style.

## 4. Faux Shaved Sides

This style is perfect for fall. It's a little tricky to create this look at first, but it shouldn't take you all that long to get the hang of it. If need be, ask your roommate or your mother for a little bit of help. It'll give you two some bonding time! When you're finished, it'll almost look like the sides of your head have been shaved.

## 5. 2NE1 Inspired Hair

If you like KPop, you've seen this hairstyle before. It's inspired by CL from 2NE1. All you have to do is grab a thin section of your hair at the crown and secure it for the time being. Then you should pull back the hair in front of your ears and tie them together with elastics. Then you can release the top section of your hair, so that it falls over the rest of it.

## 6. Fauxhawk

You don't need wild colored hair to make this style look amazing. It looks great with blonde, brunette, or red hair, as well! After you curl your hair, you can start styling it by pulling the sides back, so that all of the attention is directed toward the front.

## 7. Grunge Hairstyles

If you're looking for a few more hairstyles, you're in luck! This tutorial shows you how to create five different grunge styles. A few of the styles involve pigtails, which can be hard to pull off, so make sure you pay close attention to how she does them. You should also make sure you have a beanie nearby, because it can really help you pull your look together.

When you wear your hair in one of these styles, you're bound to feel badass! Then you'll have more confidence than ever before. Which one of these hairstyles would you wear around town?
