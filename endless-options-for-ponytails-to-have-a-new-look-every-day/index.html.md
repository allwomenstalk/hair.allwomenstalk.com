# Endless Options ⏳ for Ponytails to Have a New Look Every Day ...

I wear my hair in a ponytail probably five times a week but it’s a work ponytail: simply pulled back and stuck in a band. But, I do love to see other ponytail styles, especially dressy ones that are great for going out. A ponytail can be so much more than a simple scrape back and tie. Check out these fabulous ponies and follow the video tutorials to change up your style.

## 1. Classic Ponytail

Let’s start with the age old favourite, the classic, simple ponytail. This tutorial in particular is great for helping to achieve a [fluffy ponytail](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/puffy-ponytails-to-pep-up-your-style/) even if your hair is on the thinner side.

## 2. High Ponytail

The high ponytail is a style that you can rock in a professional setting that elevates your look and also keeps you cool and your hair out of your way. It’s funny how a simple positioning adjustment can make such a difference!

## 3. Low Ponytail

Whenever there is a high option, there is always a low option too! A low ponytail is quite an underused and underappreciated style, but paired with the right outfit it can look great!

## 4. Messy Ponytail

A messy ponytail is the perfect kind of updo to wear if you are going out for the evening but still want your hair to be mostly out of your face. It looks great and feels great too!

## 5. Ariana Grande Ponytail

It’s fair to say that Ariana Grande has pretty much cornered the modern market when it comes to be recognized for a ponytail! You can replicate her thick, luscious locks by following this great tutorial.

## 6. Big Volume Ponytail

If you want to rock a ponytail but don’t just want to scrape your hair back and forget about it, then this big volume style is all party in the back!

## 7. Dutch Braid Ponytail

This style takes the difficulty up a little bit with the incorporated Dutch braid, but once you get the hang of it, it will definitely become your favorite, go-to hairstyle for both casual and smart casual occasions.

## 8. Retro Ponytail

The swinging 60s was all about the glorious beehive, and this cool ponytail idea incorporates the functionality of a ponytail with the stylish volume of a slight bouffant effect on the top. Channel your inner Amy Winehouse!

## 9. Figure 8 Ponytail

Once you have the hang of this nifty figure of 8 pattern, you can transform a boring classic ponytail in to something much more fun and inventive. Make sure to teach all your friends too!

## 10. Pull through Braid Ponytail

This is a classic example of something really simple looking like it has been done by a professional. This pull through braid ponytail uses lots of different skills to produce a great looking style.

## 11. Heart Braided Ponytail

Once you have your braiding skills down, then why not try this inventive, amazing looking heart shaped style that give you both a practical day time style and a talking point with your friends, family and colleagues!

## 12. Twisted Ponytail

If braiding isn’t your favorite thing in the world, then this twisted ponytail is much easier to achieve and looks equally as good when the final stages are perfected.

## 13. Anime Ponytail

If you are a fan of anime, then it’s pretty likely that you will be involved in cosplay at least once in your life! This kind of ponytail is instantly recognizable as typical anime style.

## 14. Woven Ponytail

Who says ponytails are just simple looking options for your hair? This woven creation is really intricate but when broken down in to easy stages in this tutorial, it can be achieved by anyone!

## 15. Knotted Ponytail

This is another one that looks incredibly intricate and complicated but is actually very simple when you know all of the proper steps. This knotted style is something that you don’t see very often; very interesting and unique.

## 16. Barbie Ponytail

It would be fair to say that many girl’s first fashion icon is her Barbie! You can carry on this on in to your adult life by replicating her iconic ponytail, all with the help of this tutorial!

## 17. 1950s Ponytail

Have you noticed that a lot of 1950s and 1960s fashions are coming back in trend? Make sure you are ahead of the curve by mastering this simple but effective 1950's style ponytail. It’s the basis for some great retro and vintage outfits.
