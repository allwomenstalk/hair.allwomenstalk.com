# Hot Summer Hair Colors of the Year ...

Looking for **hot summer hair colours**? Every year when summer rolls around, lots of people feel like they want to show a completely different side to themselves, and that can include mixing it up with different clothes, different makeup and importantly for this particular article, different hair! If you are planning on making a trip to the salon to have a game-changing summer haircut, then why not go all the way and change the colour too? Of course, if you are going to do something like this, then you need to make sure that your choice is in keeping with the latest and hottest looks right now. To save you some time and help you make a decision, here are some of this year’s **hot summer hair colours**.

## 1. Pink

![hair, human hair color, blond, hairstyle, purple,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/qq/f27341eb5b1065c0d1717689173127_600x682.jpg)

Soft pink is a hair shade that comes in and out of fashion and 2018 looks set to be another in year. Kylie Jenner is the perfect example of somebody who has rocked pink hair this year and looked amazing doing it. The good news is that pink hair can go with pretty much any skin tone under the sun! It's one of the best **hot summer hair colours** to choose this year.

## 2. ICE White

![hair, blond, human hair color, hairstyle, wig,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/hz/le0slepr5b1065e43c613574822339_600x600.jpg)

Many of the catwalks have seen so much white on white for this season so what better colour to match the outfits than ice blond hair? You’re sure to turn heads wherever you go if you can pull off this colour.

## 3. Rose Gold

![hair, human hair color, pink, blond, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m2/pb/rtfhkqox5b1065fd50cee946759992_600x600.jpg)

Rose gold has made a huge impact on the gadget and accessories world, so it was only a matter of time before it made its way to hair too! It provides a perfect combination with a sun-kissed skin complexion and has most recently been rocked by none other than Haley Baldwin at the 2018 Met Gala.

## 4. Hidden Rainbow Hair

![hair, human hair color, hairstyle, blond, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/ko/d8kb83p55b10664c4adb1318625524_600x600.jpg)

Last year was the year for full-blown rainbow hair, and this summer provides a little twist in the style with a more subtle, hidden look. The trend in 2018 is to hide the rainbow colours under the layers toward the back of your head. It might take some getting used to, but you can achieve some sensational ponytail looks with hidden rainbow hair, and ponytails are definitely a go-to style in the summer when the humidity is getting to you!

## 5. Warm Chocolate

![hair, human hair color, fashion model, beauty, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bw/uh/i1v5lhtw5b1066fd9668e646066649_600x600.jpg)

Who says blondes have more fun? Well, nobody anymore! Follow in the footsteps of stars like Selena Gomez who has recently swapped her dark black locks for warm chocolate ones. It is a neutral colour, but it still manages to make a statement.

## 6. Silver Ombre

![hair, human hair color, hairstyle, long hair, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iz/qx/t0pn7p9n5b106727a2665477308706_600x751.jpg)

We spend our lives worrying about the emergence of grey hairs, but that isn’t the case with the new silver ombre trend! You can go pale or you can go full blown metallic, whichever end of the spectrum feels more comfortable to you.

## 7. Baby Blue

![hair, eyewear, human hair color, hairstyle, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pr/qg/s45fi5s85b10674a42a4b390639588_600x702.jpg)

Okay, so this one is definitely a bit more out there, but baby blue hair is a fantastic trend this summer for anyone who is brave enough to try it! Take a look at recent pictures of Kesha, she looks stunning rocking the light blue hair, and you can too! It’s the perfect shade for the summer because it evokes both the glorious sky and the irresistible ocean!
