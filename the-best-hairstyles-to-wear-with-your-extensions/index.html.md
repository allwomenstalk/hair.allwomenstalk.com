# The Best Hairstyles to Wear with Your Extensions ...

Not all of us have long locks of hair. Of course, that doesn't matter, because if we can't grow it, we can buy it. Hair extensions are more commonly used than you'd think, so you shouldn't be embarrassed to buy them. Most of the celebrities that you see are wearing them, even though you'd never realize it. If you decide to buy them for yourself, here are some of the best hairstyles to wear with your extensions:

## 1. Half-up Half-down

You can wear this style with curly hair or straight hair. it doesn't matter what the texture is, because the styling tips are the same. All you have to do is clip in your extensions in a particular way. As long as you listen closely to the woman in this video, you shouldn't have a difficult time following along.

## 2. High Ponytail

The more hair you have, the bouncier your ponytail will look. That's why it's a great idea to put in extensions before you push your hair back into a ponytail. This video even gives you little tips and tricks, like that the more layers you have, the easier it'll be for you to blend in your extensions. It'll also give you in depth instructions on how to use extensions if you've never done so before.

## 3. Old Hollywood Hair

This hairstyle is inspired by Lana Del Rey. All you have to do is put in your hair extensions and then tease your hair into a side part. Once you're finished, you can curl your hair to create smooth waves.

## 4. High Bun

This video will show you how to create a high bun using clip in extensions. You should start out by placing those extensions in your hair, and then you should move on to actually styling your look. To do that, you should pay close attention to the woman in this video, because she doesn't actually speak. You need to watch her to see what she does next.

## 5. Waves and Fishtail Braids

This video will show you to create different hairstyles that you can wear with your extensions. You can straighten your hair, push it back into a pony, give yourself waves, or give yourself a fishtail braid. Whatever style you choose, you know you're going to look fabulous.

## 6. Ponytail and Braids

This video will show you how to create three more hairstyles that work well with clip-on hair extensions. The first look involves a ponytail, the second involves braids, and the third involves a side ponytail. Each of the looks are equally stylish, so you have a hard choice to make when you step in front of that mirror.

## 7. 8 More Hairstyles

If you still haven't found a hairstyle that you like, then this video will change that. There's eight different hairstyles included in it. The woman in the video will show you how to create a simple pin back, an easy side braid, double fishtail braids, milkmaid braids, a basic ponytail, a big messy bun, tied down braids, and big curls.

If you're going to add extensions into your hair, you need to know how to rock the look. That's why these style swill come in handy. What's your favorite hairstyle to wear with your extensions?
