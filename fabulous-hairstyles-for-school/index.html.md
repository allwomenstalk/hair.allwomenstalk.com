# 7 Fabulous Hairstyles for School ...

With the new school year in full swing for most high school students, the number one question is back on the table-what hairstyles for school will you rock this year? Of course, you attend school to learn and grow as an intellectual, but where does it say you can’t do that and look fabulous? And a big part of looking fabulous is wearing the right hairstyles for school!

## 1. Waterfall Braid

![Waterfall Braid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/j4/d9vpu9a1_600x1040.jpg)

Hairstyles for school aren’t meant to be too showy or glamorous, but you may still want to look elegant and show off your hairstyling expertise! If that’s the case, then try out a look like the waterfall braid. It’s simple enough that it doesn't distract others, but unique and beautiful enough to draw some well-deserved attention!

## 2. Bow Bun

![Bow Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/dx/jyb5zbr5_600x817.jpg)

Another scarcely-seen look is the bow bun! This look is probably rare primarily because it appears difficult to master, but with the right amount of practice you can perfect the bow bun look! You may get so good at it that you’ll tie your hair into a bow regularly and gain a signature look! It’s certainly a look to be proud of and have tons of fun with!

## 3. Side Swept Curls

![Side Swept Curls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/up/h0rbruo3_600x894.jpg)

Side swept curls are great for someone who might not have the skills it takes to accomplish complex hairstyles. But even though this look is relatively easy to achieve, you’ll receive compliments for it all day long! This ‘do is simple enough to pull off with a pair of jeans and a T-shirt, but also classy enough to turn heads!

## 4. Vintage

![Vintage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/mt/gejzemk4_600x900.jpg)

Vintage looks are timeless and fabulous! You don’t have to emulate Marilyn Monroe every day, but once in a while it’s fun to do so! With the right accessories, and by completing the look with some thrift store treasures and the right makeup, you will have a totally vintage, totally adorable look for any day this school year!

## 5. High Ponytail

![High Ponytail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/xx/qrf6gkp9_600x915.jpg)

Nothing says high school quite like the classic high ponytail! But you don’t have to be a cheerleader to pull off this look! You can rock the high ponytail, a peppy, easy-to-master look, anytime, any place! And what better place to wear it than your school?

## 6. Messy Bun

![Messy Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/o1/l32zzk2x_600x902.jpg)

Lucky for you, grunge is the new glamorous! A disheveled, carefree hairstyle may be the perfect style to master this school year. During rushed mornings when you’re possibly dealing with unwashed hair, grab your favorite scrunchy and go! You’ll get more compliments than criticisms, just try it and see!

## 7. Braided Headband

![Braided Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j2/bm/cazmz4ah_600x781.jpg)

Who needs hair accessories when you’ve got the longest locks in school? If you have long enough hair, you can certainly own the braided headband look! People won’t expect it, and will be amazed by your braiding prowess! Not only that, but you’ll feel like you look good too and that’s what matters!

Deciding how to style your hair is one of the most common struggles girls face on a daily basis. It seems simple enough but really, hair can make or break an outfit! And in high school, it often seems like what you look like is all that matters. Even though this isn’t the case, there’s no harm in looking good! How will you style your hair differently this year?
