# Dust Away Your Dead Skin  9 Different Remedies for Dandruff ...

Don't you hate when you wear your favorite black dress and the shoulders get littered with flakes? Well, you won't have to worry about that anymore! There's no reason for you to have dandruff when there are so many different remedies that you can try. If you haven't found something that's worked yet, here's what you can do:

## 1. Wash Your Hair with Dandruff Shampoo

This one is the most obvious, and it's the first thing that you should try. Don't worry about tossing out your favorite moisturizing shampoo with a fruity scent, because there are plenty of dandruff shampoos that will keep your hair smelling fresh. You just have to keep an eye out for them.

## 2. Use Apple Cider Vinegar after Shampooing

Apple cider vinegar does wonders for your hair. Not only will it get rid of dandruff, but it works as a conditioner and can get rid of tangles. It can even reduce frizz and protect you from split ends! There's nothing it can't do, so make sure that you buy some during your next trip to the store.

## 3. Crush ASPIRIN and Add It to Your Shampoo Bottle

Aspirin does more than relieve your cramps during that time of the month. You can take two aspirins, crush them up, and slip them into your shampoo bottle. After you shake it up, it's ready to be put on your hair.

## 4. Massage Your Scalp with Olive Oil

Olive oil can come in handy, so you should always keep some on your shelves. You don't have to add this product to your shampoo because it's safe for you to just rub it right into your scalp. It sounds gross but it does the trick!

## 5. Massage Your Scalp with Aloe Vera

Grab the extra aloe vera that was left over from the last time you got a sunburn. Now, put it in your hair, massage your scalp, and then wash it right out. It's that easy.

## 6. Start Eating Healthier

Your diet impacts your skin and hair. That means that you might be able to get rid of your dandruff just by eating healthier. Plus, it'll help keep your nails strong and your stomach toned. Change your diet and your whole life will change.

## 7. Drink More Water Every Single Day

While we're on the topic of eating healthy, you should drink healthy as well. Stop gulping down caffeine and drink as much water as you can every single day. It'll help you in more ways than one.

## 8. Exfoliate Your Scalp

Have you ever heard of this before? Most people assume that their face is the only place that needs to be exfoliated. However, you can buy special scrubs to exfoliate your scalp. That way, you'll get rid of all that dead skin faster.

## 9. Wash Your Hair More Often

Sometimes, women get dandruff when they don't wash their hair enough. So if you usually go too long without washing, then you should change your routine ASAP. It's easy to do, and it might be all you need to get rid of all those gross flakes.

Everyone gets dandruff, but that doesn't make it any less embarrassing when it gets all over your clothes and hair. How have you gotten rid of dandruff in the past?
