# 17 Mind\-blowing Expert Tips to Thicken Your Hair ...

I love thick hair. I have wicked thin hair and I'm always looking for ways to make it thicker. Are you in the same boat? Try some of these awesome ways to thicken up your hair! Seriously, they work!

## 1. Change up Your Hair Washing Habits

![Change up Your Hair Washing Habits](https://img.allw.mn/content/wu/cd/a4kjof595737a2dd526d0871893819_500x281.gif)

I know, this one will be hard. Shampoo about twice a week so you can cleanse your scalp and hair without stripping it of all of that moisture.

## 2. Use Lukewarm Water

![Use Lukewarm Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/z3/ur/rvbv63555737a2debad9a501641317_500x200.gif)

Not hot! Keep that in mind.

## 3. Avoid Combing Your Wet Hair

![Avoid Combing Your Wet Hair](https://img.allw.mn/content/wc/m2/lwuawjxi5737a2ddc1b6b327754760_500x281.gif)

It can cause all kinds of hair damage. Instead, let it air dry and then detangle it with a wide-toothed comb.

## 4. Manage Your Stress

![hair, face, nose, beauty, hairstyle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ye/cg/mcy4z3s758db4c29a85b4729421515_500x260.gif)

Your hair can actually fall out when you have excess stress!

## 5. Aloe Vera Deep Treatment

![Aloe Vera Deep Treatment](https://img.allw.mn/content/m3/a3/xu59hb5c5737a2de1913d501702952_529x352.gif)

You just extract the gel, rub it onto your scalp, leave it on your hair for a half an hour and wash it away. It'll leave you hair looking beautiful.

## 6. Biotin

![Biotin](https://img.allw.mn/content/v8/ko/jc2x6jzx5737a2de27b6c578189066_500x261.gif)

It works! Trust me.

## 7. Choose the Right Brush

![Choose the Right Brush](https://img.allw.mn/content/vv/bb/d5lvqxr85737a2de9e085725658883_500x314.gif)

Boar bristle is the brush that you totally should use for the ultimate thick hair.

## 8. Choose the Right Hairstyle

![Choose the Right Hairstyle](https://img.allw.mn/content/lk/jm/dr37oor45737a2df9fa78732675021_500x277.gif)

Chin or shoulder length hair is perfect for the girls with thinner hair.

## 9. Change How You Style It

![Change How You Style It](https://img.allw.mn/content/j6/hb/vust0ylz5737a2df76a47947651569_500x281.gif)

Remember, a bit of volume can make you look like you have really thick hair.

## 10. Linseed Oil is Huge!

![Linseed Oil is Huge!](https://img.allw.mn/content/dc/n8/p58qax1m5737a2df6e7ff656609640_500x356.gif)

This will stimulate hair growth and will look so thick.

## 11. Don't Stress out

![Don't Stress out](https://img.allw.mn/content/j4/tf/iqd5lra75737a2e2d0c03016050816_590x332.gif)

Seriously, you'll lose more hair which won't look thicker at all.

## 12. Look for Vitamin-rich Shampoos

![Look for Vitamin-rich Shampoos](https://img.allw.mn/content/xd/oy/g5p57em45737a2e032b21936226643_500x279.gif)

Shampoos with vitamin E and B5 are huge.

## 13. Use a Paddle Brush

![Use a Paddle Brush](https://img.allw.mn/content/hp/to/iws27f0n5737a2e0866d1272480226_500x302.gif)

Ditch the round brush and instead, use a paddle brush, it'll help your ends lie flat.

## 14. Opt for a Blunt-style Cut

![Opt for a Blunt-style Cut](https://img.allw.mn/content/gu/hs/iew3vkr45737a2e0656d7627593772_500x281.gif)

This will help keep your ends crisp.

## 15. Long Layers

![Long Layers](https://img.allw.mn/content/v1/ez/zuhownvj5737a2e1e962e242228055_490x275.gif)

Trust me, they will make your hair look so, so thick!

## 16. Pantene Expert Hair Regrowth Treatment

![Pantene Expert Hair Regrowth Treatment](https://img.allw.mn/content/as/o1/zsba9dc15737a2e153bef695243288_500x281.gif)

OMG – this treatment? Amazing. It preserves your hair and is amazing. Try it!

## 17. A Mix of Light and Dark Pieces

![A Mix of Light and Dark Pieces](https://img.allw.mn/content/f3/x7/ldh16mhy5737a2e40415e610306249_500x272.gif)

For your color, do a mix!
