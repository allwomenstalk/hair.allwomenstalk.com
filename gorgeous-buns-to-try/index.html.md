# 8 Gorgeous Buns to Try ...

Which are your favourites?

## 1. Looped Bun

![Looped Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d2/nk/wuh62b4p5701cb9c6bab5036192350_600x800.jpg)

Loop your pony tail into a low bun for a tidy, simple look.

## 2. Wrapped Bun

![Wrapped Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wg/s4/xj3gatp75701cba59a917011310681_600x837.jpg)

Twist and clip your hair and wrap the underside around for a subtle, sophisticated look.

## 3. Crown Braid Bun

![Crown Braid Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/x6/nejz3r4o5701cbad9b7d9391470381_587x602.jpg)

Braid your hair along the crown and make a neat bun at the back for a feminine look.

## 4. High Bun

![High Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/dl/ow3hjn525701cbb42ac43878484337_500x488.jpg)

Use a hair doughnut to secure your hair near the top of your head for a classic look.

## 5. Side Bun

![Side Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gu/eq/ix90vrmr5701cbbae5c29374764559_500x333.jpg)

Braid your hair on either sides and bring it together in a side bun for an elegant look.

## 6. Low Braid Bun

![Low Braid Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/iv/x3cfpk4y5701cbc253e94613163498_600x903.jpg)

Braid one side of your hair and secure it, making a small bun to sit directly underneath for an original look.

## 7. Loose Bun

![Loose Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/fe/icfx6m175701cbc8e489a306587801_500x333.jpg)

Create a high bun near the top of your head with a relaxed hand to loosen to base of your hair for an effortless look.

## 8. Messy Bun

![Messy Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o3/lm/l7csuaec5701cbd0a0593703299123_500x593.jpg)

Create a braid from the front of your hair and pull it backwards into a high bun, with a few tendrils framing your hair for an stylish look.
