# Lovely Hairstyles for Girls Who Rock  Their Curly Hair ...

I have curly hair so I can commiserate with you when it comes to doing a great hairstyle without a ton of frustration. I could tell you how to style your hair, but it’s much easier to try something new if you can watch someone else do it first. Then all you have to do is follow the steps and you should have the look achieved in no time at all. If you’re a fellow curly headed beauty and want to try something new, any of these tutorials will help you do just that. Have fun!

## 1. Eight Bun Styles

This video will show you not one, not two, but eight different ways to wear a bun if you have curly hair. Maybe you want a braided bun or you want two adorable buns on either side of your head. No matter what your desire, you should be able to learn how to tame those curls into a cute bun in a matter of minutes with this tutorial.

## 2. Quick and Simple

If you’re anything like me, you want things fast and easy when you style your curls. If you’re tired of the same old style, you can learn how to do something new that’s both quick and simple with this video. There are six different curly hair styles in this tutorial and each one is going to make every day a good hair day. Yay!

## 3. 15 Ways to Style Curly Hair

Whether you want an up do or want to leave your hair down and flowing, there’s something for you in this video. Each of the styles in this video are super easy to do and will give you something new to try for more than two weeks. From something simple like a deep side part to a cute half pulled back style, you’ll find so many choices on this video.

## 4. Perfect for Everyday

Every day hairstyles for curls are ideal because they give you an option for going to work or for the casual things you like to do on your days off. This video shows you five adorable curly hair looks that are perfect for anything you’re doing during the day. Dealing with mega curls isn’t always easy, but this tutorial should help you get them under control.

## 5. For Short Curls

If you have short hair that is still super curly, you will need some different styles than girls with long curls. Shorter hair allows your curls to tighten up because they aren’t so weighed down by the length. However, they can be hard to wrangle into submission. Watch this tutorial and you’ll get some great ideas for styling your short, curly hair.

## 6. 10 Styles in 2 Minutes

Only have a few minutes to style your curls? You are not alone. I like to find fast ways to make my curls look great because I’m in usually in a rush to get going in the morning. This video saves the day by showing you 10 adorable curly hair styles, each of which takes only two minutes or less. Perfect!

## 7. Totally Cute and Easy

This video is packed with cute and easy styles that are made for curls. How many, you ask? There are a whopping 21 different looks here so you’ll never be at a loss for what to do with your hair. They’re easy, they’re fast and they’re perfect for any day of the week or any event you’re attending.
