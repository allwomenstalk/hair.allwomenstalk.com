# 7 Signs Bangs Will Look Amazing on You ...

Are you toying with the idea of bangs? Bangs are something that’s becoming trendy to have right now. You may have noticed them on celebrities or models recently. There are several different styles of bangs that you can choose from, according to what suits you best. These are 7 signs bangs will look amazing on you.

## 1. You Have a High Forehead

High foreheads scream for bangs. If you’re one of those unlucky girls whose forehead stretches on forever, then there’s a good chance that bangs would look good on you. How do I know? I have a high forehead and have learned that bangs are my friend. I need at least a side bang to help me out.

## 2. You’ve Got Frown Lines

There’s another advantage to bangs. They cover all those unattractive frown lines and other wrinkles on your forehead. If you’re starting to notice you’ve got frown lines and that your wrinkle cream doesn’t seem to be working, bangs may be the answer for you. Does it fix the problem? Nope. But it does cover it up, which is a good alternative.

## 3. You Want to Look Younger

Several different studies have shown that bangs make you look younger. Part of this is because they help hide some of the early aging signs your forehead may be showing. They’re also just viewed as a more youthful hair style. But this isn’t set in stone. It depends on the individual.

## 4. You Have Beautiful Eyes

If you’re always getting compliments on your eyes then that’s a good sign bangs would work well for you. Bangs let your eyes take center stage. In fact, it calls a lot of attention to them. If your eyes are one of your best features than bangs would probably be a good choice for you.

## 5. You Enjoy Doing Your Hair

If you like styling your hair then bangs might be a good choice. Bangs do require styling so if you enjoy styling your hair this is right up your alley. There are many different ways you can style your bangs. Some people can style them using only a hair dryer while others may need to use a flat iron or a curling iron. Considering the time that bangs take to style is an important consideration to make before having them cut.

## 6. You’re Not Happy with Your Current Hairstyle

If you aren’t happy with your current hairstyle then bangs may be just what you need. It’s a way to change things up without being too drastic. It’s a change that isn’t as drastic as a full on haircut or a color change. If you don’t like your bangs then you can always grow them back out. All women crave a change in their hairstyle from time to time.

## 7. You’ve Done a Trial Run and like the Results

If you had bangs before and liked how they looked then that’s a good sign you’d like them now. You may also have tried to pull some hair over to see how they might look on you. If you like that image of yourself then that’s a good sign bangs are a good choice. It’s good to at least try to visualize what they’d look like. This helps you to know if it’s for sure what you want to do before you take the step of having them cut.

These are 7 signs bangs will look amazing on you. So what are your thoughts? Are you going to go for it?
