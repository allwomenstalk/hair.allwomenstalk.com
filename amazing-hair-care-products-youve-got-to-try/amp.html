10 Amazing Hair Care Products You've Got to Try ...

It’s a 10 Miracle Leave-in • L'Oreal Advanced Haircare Total Repair 5 Damage Erasing Balm • Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil • Aussie 3 Minute Miracle Moist Deeeeep Conditioner • Alterna Bamboo Style Cleanse Extend Translucent Dry Shampoo • More ...

By Lisa

I don’t know about you, but I’m always on the lookout for my new favorite, gotta-have-it hair care products. Aside from beauty products, I think hair care products are right up there when it comes to looking and feeling my best. Finding a good leave-in conditioner, hair spray or multi-tasking product that can make my life easier just makes my day! So, if you’re interested in discovering some incredible hair care products, keep reading for more!

1. It’s a 10 Miracle Leave-in

This is one of the hair care products that is considered to be the holy grail product for many women! The name of the product refers to the number of things it can do: repairs dry, damaged hair, adds shine, smooths and controls frizz, seals and protects hair color, protects from heat, detangles, stops hair breakage, creates silkiness, enhances natural body and prevents split ends! This line has a large variety of hair care products so there’s something for everyone!

2. L'Oreal Advanced Haircare Total Repair 5 Damage Erasing Balm

If you have dry, damaged and over-processed hair, you’re going to want a good deep conditioner to restore moisture. L’Oreal’s Damage Erasing Balm claims to repair one year of damage in one use and while that’s a tall order to fill, it certainly does a great job of hydrating, softening and strengthening damaged hair!

3. Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil

Using a hair oil might sound a little intimidating because it might weigh down your hair or cause your hair to appear greasy, but fear not! Bumble and Bumble’s Hairdresser’s Invisible Oil is a lightweight oil for your hair that adds shine, tames flyaways, strengthens and detangles your hair. Not only will this hair care product make your hair look amazing, it also contains UVA/UVB protection to shield your hair from the sun!

4. Aussie 3 Minute Miracle Moist Deeeeep Conditioner

Finding a good quality hair care product that doesn’t break the bank can be challenging but Aussie’s Deeeeep Conditioner makes it fast and easy to do just that! We all know that using a deep conditioner takes time: the longer you leave it in, the better it works. But for those of us who just don’t have the time or the patience, this product will give us great results in as little as three minutes!

5. Alterna Bamboo Style Cleanse Extend Translucent Dry Shampoo

Another must have when it comes to hair care is a dry shampoo. Dry shampoos come in super handy if you’re unable to wash your hair, want to refresh your hair and scalp or just want some added volume. The only downside to many of the drugstore dry shampoos are that they can leave a white cast on dark hair. Alterna’s dry shampoo is translucent so it leaves no residue behind!

6. TRESemme Thermal Creations Heat Tamer Protective Spray

Using a heat protectant in your hair is essential because it creates a barrier between the heat tool and your hair and protects your hair from heat damage. If you frequently use heat tools like a blow dryer, flat iron, curling iron or all of the above, make sure you take a few extra moments to spritz a heat tamer to protect your tresses and add shine!

7. Ouidad Mediterranean Bay Leaf Exfoliating Hair & Scalp Treatment

We all know that exfoliating is one of the most important steps in skin care and now our scalp can also reap the benefits! If you’re looking for a hair and scalp treatment to help with dry scalp, promote healthy hair growth and get rid of any product build-up, this can do it all in one step. If your roots feel oily and you regularly use a lot of hair care products, give this baby a try!

8. TIGI Catwalk Curls Rock

I’ve always envied girls with naturally curly hair. Having straight hair has its advantages but I’ve always dreamt of waking up with a head of ringlets in my hair. For those lucky ladies with curls, TIGI’s Curls Rock helps separate and amplify your curls while adding shine and volume. Apply after washing your hair for frizz-free, natural looking curls!

9. Living Proof Satin Hair Serum

Do you have long hair that takes forever to dry? Does your hair easily tangle? Would you like your hair to be more manageable? Living Proof’s Satin Hair Serum can help with all those hair concerns and more! This hair serum asserts to eliminate frizz, helps make hair easier to style and provides natural movement, all without silicone or oil!

10. L'Oreal Elnett Satin Hairspray

Elnett is a cult beauty product in the hair care world for good reason. This hair spray offers long-lasting hold without leaving your hair sticky or crunchy. What you get is a fine mist of strong hold with movement and soft, shiny hair. The original formula has somewhat of a “old” fragrance, so opt for the unscented type for the hold without the scent!

It seems like no matter what our hair care concern, there’s a product out there to help us achieve the look we want. There are plenty of affordable and higher end products that are effective and multi-task to make hair styling easier and more enjoyable. What’s your holy grail hair care product?
