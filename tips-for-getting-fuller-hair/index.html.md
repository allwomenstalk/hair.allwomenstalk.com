# 13 Tips for Getting Fuller Hair ...

If you're like me, you've been dying to find all of the top tips for fuller hair that is going to stand up and stay up the entire day! Whether you are in need of a correct conditioning treatment or you really need to start using root lifter, I've got all of the top tips for fuller hair that will keep your tresses looking volumized and beautiful all day! Give them a try and let us know how they work for you!

## 1. Condition Correctly

![Condition Correctly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07141816_5836_500x666.jpg)

Full hair isn't something that is going to come super easy or right off the bat, it does take a little work and knowing all of the right tips for fuller hair that really, actually work! Conditioning correctly and knowing the right conditioner for you is honestly the greatest way to keep your hair full! You don't want something too heavy and you don't want to over-condition. You should also leave the conditioner in for about 10 minutes and rinse it out in cool or cold water.

## 2. Castor Oil

![Castor Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07143307_2500_353x493.jpg)

You probably don't think that something like castor oil can actually help not just the thickness of your hair, but also make sure that it's totally volumized, right? Well, just a few drops of this oil in your hair before you blow dry can actually change your hair completely and make it super thick and super full!

## 3. Dry Shampoo

![Dry Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07141918_3736_600x784.jpg)

While you might think that constantly washing your hair every single day is ideal, skipping a few days is so much better for you hair! Luckily, dry shampoo can allow you to do that! Just a few sprays of this awesome powdery liquid is a great way to soak up any oil that you have along your scalp and it can leave your hair smelling amazing!

## 4. No Hairspray

![No Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07141842_6438_600x1026.jpg)

Did you know that hairspray can actually totally weigh your hair down in the worst way? Ditch the hairspray and instead, opt for something lighter, such as a light spritzing of hair gel. You want to keep it light and airy, not something that is going to make your hair stick together.

## 5. Upside-down Blow Drying

![Upside-down Blow Drying](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07142021_9552_500x750.jpg)

Now, I actually use this tip every single morning – I blow dry all of my hair upside down and use a bit of root lifter (which we'll get into later!) and I swear, my hair has all kinds of volume and is super full, all day! It's an awesome trick and if you are already blow drying your hair – it is just one more step!

## 6. Coconut Oil

![Coconut Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07142314_9798_500x686.jpg)

Coconut oil (yep! The one you find in your grocery store!) is a great way to really thicken up your hair and even a great way for you to get a full head of hair. All you need to do is melt it down, wait until it cools, apply it to your hair and after about twenty minutes, wash it out! It'll leave your hair super soft and so shiny!

## 7. Root Lifter

![Root Lifter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07142945_3442_348x500.jpg)

Remember when I talked about upside down blow drying? Well, a little root lifter added onto that can go a really long way! I use Pantene's root lifter spray gel and it's incredible! Not only does it not leave my hair feeling weighed down, but it's a product that I've been using for years!

## 8. Massage

![Massage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07142840_6810_375x475.jpg)

Massage your scalp for a few minutes every night before you go to bed. Doing this helps to activate your hair follicles and promotes growth. When your hair grows more it will likely grow thicker as well. You can even throw in some natural oils to this massage if you feel like the massage isn't enough.

## 9. Vitamin E

![Vitamin E](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07144406_1033_449x675.jpg)

Vitamin E is good for a lot of different health reasons, but I bet you didn't know it's good for your hair! Vitamin E promotes hair growth as well as help to prevent hair loss. The less hair you lose the more hair you have, so it's always good to take preventative measures.

## 10. Hair Masks

![Hair Masks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07144824_2870_433x650.jpg)

You can buy hair masks in the store, but you can also make natural hair masks at home.They can promote healthy and fuller hair growth.They can be made from things like mayo, avocado, honey and other things that could be found in your kitchen. They don't take too long to make so just look for some and get started!

## 11. Drink Water

![Drink Water](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07144011_6436_533x746.jpg)

Water is vital to hair growth and good for your health in general. Water helps transport nutrients to your hair and it also helps to take toxins away from your hair.If you ever needed another excuse to drink more water, here it is!

## 12. Zinc

![Zinc](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07144727_1213_500x750.jpg)

Not having enough zinc in your diet can cause you to lose your hair. Try to pump up the zinc in your diet to combat hair loss which will help your hair become fuller. You can do this by eating more nuts like pecans, almonds, and cashews.

## 13. No Smoking

![No Smoking](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07144637_1814_364x546.jpg)

Finally, if you can, ditch the smoking habit! This is another thing that can break down your hair and can actually cause it to fall flat. I know it's addictive, but if you can ditch the cigarettes, so much the better for your health and your hair!

So, now that you have all of the top tips for full hair, what other tips do you have? Anything else that you use that works for you to create volume?
