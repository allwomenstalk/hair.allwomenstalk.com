2 Sweet and Simple Updo on Naturally Curly Hair

Source: dg53.mycdn.me
Got natural curls or waves? Lucky you! In 5 simple steps you can achieve this romantic updo.

***

This sweet and simple updo is perfect for girls with naturally curly or wavy hair who are looking for a stunning prom hairstyle. With just five simple steps, you can create a romantic updo that will look amazing in photos and last the entire night.

Start by brushing out your curls to create a smooth base. Then use a hair tie to secure your hair into a low ponytail. Take a section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and add a few more pins to secure any loose hair.

To add a little extra glamour to the look, you can add a few hair accessories such as a flower clip, ribbon, or a sparkly headband. If you have shorter hair, you can add a few extensions to the bun to give it more volume.

To finish the look, spritz your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep the style in place. With a few simple steps, you can easily create a romantic updo that will make you look and feel like a princess on your special night.

17 Loose Low Updo

Source: 20 Quick and Easy Short
A loose, low updo with some wispies to frame your face looks super sophisticated,

***

This loose, low updo is a great option for girls with short hair who are looking for a sophisticated hairstyle for prom. This style is achieved by gathering the hair at the nape of the neck and securing it with bobby pins. To add a bit of flair to the look, some of the wispies around the face can be left out to frame the face. This look is perfect for those who want to look polished and stylish without spending a lot of time styling their hair.

To achieve this look, start by washing your hair and blow drying it. Once dry, use a curling wand to create loose curls throughout the hair. Then, part the hair on either side of the head and gather the hair at the nape of the neck. Secure the hair with bobby pins and leave some of the wispies around the face out. Finally, spray the hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep the updo in place.

24 Finger Waves

Source: Demi Lovato Hair - StyleBistro
If your hair is too short for an updo, why not try glam vintage finger waves like Demi Lovato?

***

Finger waves are a classic hairstyle that has been around since the 1920s. They are characterized by their wavy, soft, and romantic look. The style is created by using a curling iron to create small, tight waves all over the hair. It is a look that is perfect for special occasions, such as proms.

Demi Lovato is just one of the many celebrities who have rocked this vintage style. She has been seen wearing finger waves with a deep side part and bangs. This look is great for girls with short hair, as it adds a bit of glamour and sophistication to any look.

Finger waves are a great way to add texture and volume to short hair. To achieve the look, start by using a curling iron to create the waves. Make sure to use a heat protectant to protect your hair from any damage. When you’re done curling, use a strong-hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.
