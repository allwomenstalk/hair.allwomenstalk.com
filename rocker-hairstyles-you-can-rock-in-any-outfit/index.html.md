# 7 Rocker Hairstyles You Can Rock in Any Outfit ...

The rocker look is all the rage these days among those who want to look and feel unique. When it comes to edgy style, you can show off your rocker style in many ways, from the clothes and makeup you wear down to accessories, such as [biker wallet chains](https://www.bikerringshop.com/collections/wallet-chains) and studded belts. You can also show off your rockstar style through your hairstyle.  You're a sweetie, but that doesn't mean you can't look tough. Whether you're going to a concert or are just headed to the mall with a few friends, there are rocker hairstyles fit for the occasion. Some of them are a bit hard to handle, so make sure you give yourself a bit of time to prepare yourself before you leave the house. Get ready to rock and roll, because here are a few hairstyles that'll make you look like a rockstar:

## 1. Layered Ponytail

If you want to spice up your regular ponytail, you can use the tips in this tutorial. It shows you how to create a layered ponytail and how to pin up your bangs to give your hair more volume. This one isn't a hard look to capture, so it's worth learning.

## 2. Edgy Side Pullback

This style takes a little bit of work, but you'll love the end result. As long as you have some hairspray, hair bands, and time on your hands, you'll be able to create a look that all your friends will ask about. Just be prepared to wash your hair when you get home, because you're going to have a lot of spray stuck in it.

## 3. Twisted Waterfall Braid

As awesome as this style looks on pink hair, it'll look equally fabulous on whatever shade your locks are. It may appear incredibly difficult to do, but it's actually pretty straightforward. Once you get the hang of it, you shouldn't have a problem completing the look. If you do, you could always ask a friend to give you a hand.

## 4. Side Swept Updo

Do you love side swept hair? Then you'll adore this look with a faux hawk effect in the back and a poof in the front. If you end up becoming a fan of this YouTuber, she has a separate video you can watch that explains how she did her gorgeous makeup. That way, you can steal her entire look.

## 5. BackCombed 80s Hair

You've probably heard of the adorable Zoella before, because she's pretty popular on YouTube. In this video, she explains how to capture the 80s rocker look by making her hair as big and poofy as possible. Even though we're already in 2015, it doesn't mean that you can't borrow this style from your parent's youth. They might even be able to help you out!

## 6. Teased Hair

You need rocker makeup to go with your rocker hair, don't you? This video will show you how to put up your hair, apply your eye makeup, and even design your nails. So if you want to make sure that every part of your appearance is on point, all you have to do is follow the advice in this video.

## 7. Unisex Style

Women aren't the only ones who look fabulous with rocker hairstyles. If you have a boyfriend who doesn't mind having his hair played with, you can use the tips from this video to style his hair for him. If you have short hair, then you could even try out the look on yourself. There's nothing wrong with unisex styles.

With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to rock every single one of these fabulous styles and feel good doing it. Do you know of any other rocker hairstyles that we should try?
