# 21 Stunning Side Pony Hairstyle Ideas Inspired by Your Favorite Celebs

Is there any hairstyle that's as stylish and easy to master as the side ponytail? While it's true that a misstep can leave you looking like a bad 80s pop video, when done right, a loose side ponytail is the epitome of super-chic sophistication. If you've been wondering [how to do a side ponytail](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/easy-hairstyles-for-long-hair/) that looks modern and effortless, you're in the right place. Get a little inspiration from these iconic celebrity looks, and you'll be rocking this versatile side pony hairstyle with confidence in no time.
