# The Easiest Way to Begin Trying New Hairstyles: 16 Looks to Inspire You

A new day, a new hairstyle! It's easy to get stuck in a hair rut if you're not careful. Here are some of our favorite Instagram hairstyles for today to inspire you to change things up! Before you browse this fabulous hair inspiration, here's our special hair tip of the day:

Suffering from dry ends? When curling your hair, try to keep them out of the clamp or left out from being wrapped around the iron. You don’t want to dry them out even more. If you're looking to build a better foundation, check out our guide on [daily hair care routines](/daily-hair-care-routine/).

To recreate these looks, having the right tools on hand makes a huge difference:

- A high-quality curling iron or wand

- Heat protectant spray

- Styling clips for sectioning

Before you begin, consider adjusting your heat settings based on your hair type. Over-processing can lead to unnecessary damage.

| Hair Type | Recommended Heat Setting | Prep Product |
|-----------|--------------------------|--------------|
| Fine | Low (250°F - 300°F) | Volumizing spray |
| Medium | Medium (300°F - 350°F) | Light serum |
| Thick/Coarse | High (350°F - 400°F) | Smoothing cream |
