# 26 Updos for EveryDay Hair ...

Updos for everyday hair aren’t easy to come by. The idea of an updo suggests a formal event, such as a wedding or banquet. However, sometimes you just need an updo for everyday hair when you want to get your hair out of your face! Here are some ideas for achieving that cool, casual look.

## 1. Twisted

![Twisted](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e7/jl/n1lqv9wk_355x457.jpg)

Via [Best Medium Hairstyle Casual Updos ...](https://goo.gl/BKOXyZ)
Even though this look is fashionable and stunning, the “messy factor” ensures that it will be an updo for everyday hair.

## 2. Messy Low Bun

_ Via I love this hairdo_
Messy buns are all the rage in the world of casual daytime updos! If you haven’t sported a messy bun yet, it’s time that you do!

## 3. Braided Side Bun

![Braided Side Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u0/y8/qoqu0ged_319x464.jpg)

Via [mediumhairstylesz.com](https://goo.gl/VUOVf0)
This side bun is an adorable look that will be fun to try out. Add a spunky braid into the mix and you’ve got yourself a flawless updo!

## 4. Fishtail

_ Via I N S T A ..._
Fishtails are great because they look complex, but are relatively simple to do once you get the hang of them. They are cute and fashionable, definitely a look worth trying out!

## 5. Vintage Curls

![Vintage Curls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w6/bv/uz5eqxkg_334x500.jpg)

Via [The One Hair Product You ...](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.latest-hairstyles.com/prom/hair-product-must-have-purse-prom.html)
If your hair holds a curl well, you should definitely consider a look featuring vintage curls like this stylish updo!

## 6. Coachella Buns

_ Via coachella the beauty rules_
This look is completely adorable and increasingly popular! It fits in well at music festivals, such as Coachella, but also looks great as an everyday updo.

## 7. Braided Low Bun

![Braided Low Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/jy/u4s54esc_360x480.jpg)

Via [fox and gypsy](http://foxandgypsy.blogspot.com/)
You can definitely take this look and dress it up, wearing a nice dress and accessories, or dress it down, with some jeans and a T-shirt.

## 8. Bangs

![Bangs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s6/ty/my51zfeb_481x673.jpg)

Via [Casual Updos Hairstyles of Celebrities ...](https://goo.gl/eWpjSF)
If you have bangs, let them loose! Put your longer hair in a casual bun, but leave some hair free so that you achieve an overall laid back look.

## 9. Sock Bun

![Sock Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xv/vt/lpnjw2js_350x508.jpg)

Via [Profile](https://www.brandforce.com/domain/bloom.com/)
Who doesn’t love a sock bun? This look is easy to achieve, but looks flawless!

## 10. Messy High Bun

![Messy High Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/g4/jol727ba_500x700.jpg)

Via [Paul Mitchell Schools](https://paulmitchell.edu/)
Another popular style is the messy high bun, which Ashley Benson rocks!

## 11. Braided Headband

![Braided Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ms/c4/ra3y104d_434x650.jpg)

Via [emotional chaos on imgfave](https://goo.gl/WzN1BG)
Braids really are a girl’s best friend! You can take your casual updo to the next level by braiding yourself a headband.

## 12. French Twist

![French Twist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/mf/jrmm49jt_600x900.jpg)

Via [Pretty Simple :: French Twist](https://camillestyles.com/beauty/pretty-simple-french-twist/)
The French twist manages to be both elegant and casual! You could wear it to the grocery store as well as to an important business meeting.

## 13. Flip Ponytail

![Flip Ponytail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/sd/eibs5wlo_506x842.jpg)

Via [Top 6 easy casual updos ...](https://hair-fashion-online.blogspot.com/2013/06/easy-casual-updos-for-long-hair.html)
This look appears complex, but can be worn casually and look amazing!

## 14. Volume!

![Volume!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ai/hn/lbjx9a25_346x400.jpg)

Via [Should You Try a Casual ...](https://www.liveabout.com/kids-and-teens-4145444)
Adding volume to your hairstyle can make it more fun and relaxed.

## 15. Twisted Bun

![Twisted Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b6/b4/u99pep4c_600x600.jpg)

Via [HAIR](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/480688960201291670/)

## 16. Bow Hair

_ Via squidoo.com_
Personally, I adore this look! If you have enough hair to accomplish this hairdo, by all means go for it!

## 17. Tucked Away

![Tucked Away](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mg/wt/wrw6puel_333x500.jpg)

Via [Wedding Ideas- How To Plan, ...](https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/weddings)
This conservative, unique look deserves considerable attention! If you have the skill, you should definitely try out this hairstyle.

## 18. Carefree

_ Via hair-styles-4_
This is about as messy as a messy bun can get! But it communicates a fun, carefree vibe.

## 19. Tucked in Bun

![Tucked in Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gw/tn/znd4m4rx_500x750.jpg)

Via [Apostolic Hairdos](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/422775483741434550/)
Combine the best of both worlds by tucking your loose bun into a braid, and leaving a few tresses loose in the front.

## 20. Pin-up

![Pin-up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iq/q4/ral1ld6w_313x362.jpg)

Via [Vintage Life](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/543950461213603924/)
With the right accessories, you can achieve any look! For example, with a spunky bandana, you can create this adorable pin-up look.

## 21. Nice and Neat

_ Via squidoo.com_
Unlike many casual updos, this look is nice and neat! Even though it’s relatively tame, it still works well for an everyday look.

## 22. Flower Power

![Flower Power](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/rw/kempt2me_360x300.jpg)

Via [Fashion and Style | via ...](https://weheartit.com/entry/79664437)
Accessories are everything when it comes to spicing up your every day hairstyle! Flowers around a neat sock bun create a sweet and effortless look.

## 23. Bow and Braided Bun

_ Via Makeup.com_
This braided bun would look fabulous all on its own, but the striped yellow bow adds interest to the look!

## 24. Half up

![Half up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/qt/mrby2o4q_350x453.jpg)

Via [Hair](https://www.pinterest.com/pin/517210338428672667/)
You can always leave some of your hair down, and go for a half-up, half-down style that is totally stunning!

## 25. Twisted Low Bun

![Twisted Low Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/gc/cj176nbp_370x368.jpg)

Via [Be Inspired - Hair](https://www.brandforce.com/domain/bloom.com/)
A plain, low bun can be sweet and fun, but wrapping it up neatly in a twist takes it to new heights!

## 26. Low Side Bun

![Low Side Bun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zj/bm/kdtritfd_580x762.jpg)

Via [Most Popular Prom Hairstyles](https://megpics.wordpress.com/2012/06/06/prom-hairstyles/)
With side swept bangs, a low side bun, and a few tendrils of hair hanging down, you can create a flawless everyday look!

When it’s hot outside, you want to get your hair off of your shoulders and out of your face. But sometimes with updos, you run the risk of looking too formal. Luckily, there are plenty of casual updos you can rock. Which updo are you eager to try?
