# 9 Headwraps to Tame Your Summer Beach Hair ...

Headwraps are a quick fix for taming unruly summer hair. Simply braid or tie your hair back and pop on a headwrap to keep your locks off your face. I love the idea of headwraps worn with sun-drenched, salt-addled beach hair. The look is casual, cool, and effortless. Check out the following fashionable headwraps for some style inspiration.

## 1. Through the Wire Headband

_ **Price:** $13.99 at modcloth.com_

Polka dots are so in right now, and you can incorporate the trend into your summer beach look with this bright yellow headwrap. This polka dot satin headband can be molded and shaped thanks to a bendable wire that runs from one end to the other. Simply wrap the band around your head and twist the ends together.

## 2. Abstract Geo Headwrap

_ **Price:** $4.80 at forever21.com_

Headwraps are an easy way to work some colour or a print into your outfit. This multicoloured headwrap features a geometric print and has a knotted front. Pull your hair up into a loose, high bun and slide on this headwrap for a cool summer look.

## 3. C’mon Let’s Twist Headband

![C’mon Let’s Twist Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/05/3_cmon-lets-twist-headband_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $19.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This headwrap is made from a stretchy melon-coloured fabric and features a twisted knot detail at the top. Experiment wearing it with your hair up or down, as each look can be equally stylish.

## 4. Poodle Print Bow Headband

![Poodle Print Bow Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/05/4_poodle-print-bow-headband_560x524.jpg)

**Price:** $14.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/6FDvjs)

The poodle print on this headwrap is just too cute! This pale pink headwrap would be perfect for working a retro inspired look at the beach. Team it with a vintage-style swimsuit or bikini and finish the look of with bold cat eye sunglasses.

## 5. Floral Self Tie Headwrap

_ **Price:** $8.90 at forever21.com_

Go for a gypsy inspired look with headwraps like this floral number. This satin headwrap features floral pattern and has a self tie back. Ideally, wear it with your hair out or loosely braided to one side.

## 6. Baroque Bow Headband

![Baroque Bow Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/05/6_baroque-bow-headband_560x360.jpg)

**Price:** $16.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/XxvYG8)

This headwrap features an ornate gold pattern on it. It has a fairly wide band with a large bow detail to the front and an elastic back. Team it with beach basics and oversized sunglasses for a sense of mystery and glamour.

## 7. Right Hair, Right Now Headband

![Right Hair, Right Now Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/05/7_right-hair-right-now-headband_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $19.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This knotted headwrap features a Missoni-inspired zig-zag pattern. It’s made from a stretchy fabric and features a knotted detail to the front. Style one of these headwraps at the beach by teaming it with a mustard coloured shirt and denim shorts.

## 8. Gingham Print Headwrap

_ **Price:** $3.80 at forever21.com_

Add a vintage twist to your outfit with this gingham headwrap. It features a cute bow detail and an elastic bottom. Keep it preppy by teaming it with a classic outfit like a sleeveless oxford shirt and tailored shorts.

## 9. Eugenia Kim Turban Headband

![Eugenia Kim Turban Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/05/9_eugenia-kim-turban-headband_460x448.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/bCBro1)

For the most part headwraps are fairly affordable but if you want to splash out then this designer option is worth a look. It’s made from aqua silk-charmeuse and features a knot detail at the front. Keep it glamorous by teaming this headband with a luxe kaftan and plenty of jewellery.

The trick to pulling off headwraps is to choose a style and design that complements your hair best. Thick hair looks great with a wider headwrap while thinner hair can manage with slimmer styles. What do you think of the headwrap and will you be donning one this season?
