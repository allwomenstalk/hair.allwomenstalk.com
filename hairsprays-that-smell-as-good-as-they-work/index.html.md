# 7 Hairsprays That Smell as Good as They Work ...

Hairsprays that smell good are a hair product that every girl should have on hand. Let’s be honest, there are few things worse than a bad smelling hairspray. It’s a misconception that the good hairsprays have that strong, alcohol smell to them. It is possible to find hairsprays that smell as good as they work! So the next time you find yourself hairspray shopping, consider these hairsprays that smell good and work well to get the best of both worlds!

## 1. Kenra – Hairpsrays

![Kenra – Hairpsrays](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/av/wy/ue/dzeritht_160x600.jpg)

One of my favorite brands of hairsprays that smell good is Kenra. No matter if you choose their light, medium, firm, or super hold hairspray, it is guaranteed to smell wonderful. The hairspray has a light vanilla scent to it and the scent itself isn’t overpowering. Kenra’s line of hairsprays are gentle on the hair, yet keep your hair in place. This is a great choice if you use hairspray to style your hair as opposed to just setting it because it is more workable than most sprays! Kenra is only sold in salons, so grab a bottle the next time you visit your stylist.

## 2. TRESemmé – Climate Control Hairspray

![TRESemmé – Climate Control Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n2/ve/vo/bvzlj6ln_282x383.jpg)

One of the best drugstore hairspray buys is TRESemmé’s Climate Control Finishing Spray. This light hairspray will leave your hair smelling good while controlling frizz and flyaways. This hairspray is formulated to protect your hair from changing climates! It will leave your hair looking nice without getting stiff or hard. You can find this good smelling hairspray at any drugstore.

## 3. Paul Mitchell – Freeze and Shine Super Spray

![Paul Mitchell – Freeze and Shine Super Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/a3/zj/xpx38f1a_499x488.jpg)

Paul Mitchell’s Freeze and Shine Super Spray contains algae, aloe, jojoba, henna, and rosemary to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Another benefit is that it protects your hair from sun damage. This great smelling hairspray has even won the Reader’s Choice award at [totalbeauty.com](https://www.totalbeauty.com/reviews/product/6127921/paul-mitchell-freeze-and-shine-super-spray). You can get this hairspray at your local salon.

## 4. Bumble and Bumble – Classic Hairspray

![Bumble and Bumble – Classic Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ao/kr/lg/ckywsal2_449x447.jpg)

This hairspray has the best of both worlds – flexibility and hold. Best of all, this product has been formulated to not flake. This means your scalp will stay looking pretty no matter how much hairspray you douse your locks with. This hairspray smells amazing and comes in a cute bottle. There is so much to love about this great spray! You can get yours at any Sephora store or at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 5. Alterna – Caviar anti-Aging Working Hair Spray

![Alterna – Caviar anti-Aging Working Hair Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/03/zn/ak2tlzzc_449x447.jpg)

When it comes to good smelling hairsprays that also work, it doesn’t get much better than Alterna’s Caviar Anti-Aging Working Hair Spray. This spray contains no parabens, sulfates, synthetic dyes, phthalates, triclosan, or GMOs. It goes on dry so you can run a brush through your hair after applying. If fights humidity and protects your hair and scalp from the sun. So even if you aren’t worrying about protecting yourself from aging right now, you should still try out this spray! You can get it at your local Sephora store or at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 6. Lea Journo – Voilà Finishing Spray

![Lea Journo – Voilà Finishing Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rh/xd/ry/lha7117l_449x440.jpg)

Voilà Finishing Spray by Lea Journo is a medium-hold hairspray that leaves your hair flexible and shiny. This product is free of parabens and sulfates, and, best of all, is cruelty-free! This spray smells amazing and won’t leave your hair feeling sticky after you spray it on. Visit [leajournoshop.com](https://goo.gl/tkvMZW) to pick up a can!

## 7. Big Sexy Hair – Hairspray

![Big Sexy Hair – Hairspray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/yp/j7/d6m4s9bn_230x547.jpg)

Of course Big Sexy Hair hairsprays had to make this list! There are two kinds, Spray and Play (a more flexible hold) and Spray and Play Harder (a stronger hold). Both of these hairsprays smell fantastic and will protect your hair against UV rays. This hairspray is guaranteed to hold up in humidity as well. You can shop Big Sexy Hair at [sexyhair.com](https://www.sexyhair.com/).

If you are picky about the way your hairspray smells, consider giving one of these 7 hairsprays that smell as good as they work a try! What’s your favorite good-smelling hairspray? Share it with us in the comments.
