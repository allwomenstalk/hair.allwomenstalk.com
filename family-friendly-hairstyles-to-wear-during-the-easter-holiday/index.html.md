# 7 Family Friendly Hairstyles to Wear during the Easter Holiday ...

When you visit your family for Easter, you should try to look your best. That way, everyone will realize just how stylish you really are. In order to do that, here are a few family friendly hairstyles to wear during the Easter holiday:

## 1. Retro Bouffant

You're going to start by separating your hair one inch from the front hairline. Move the lower half of your hair out of the way, and then tease the top of your head, moving back in one inch sections. Then you should gather all of your hair together in the back, twist your hair and tuck it under to form your shape. Pin it in place and you're all finished!

## 2. Spring Half-do

This is a girly hairdo that all of your aunts are going to compliment you on! You're going to gather the top of your hair into a high ponytail. Then you're going to braid a section of your hair on each side and secure it together. Then you should make another braid on each side and tuck it underneath the loop that you've already made. If you'd like, you can even curl the rest of your hair!

## 3. Braided Flower Hair

This style is a bit fancy, but if your family makes a big deal out of Easter, that might be the type of look you're trying to go for. In order to create it, you're going to need a bunch of bobby pins. You should also know how to create a rope braid, because it's a huge part of the style. If you have those necessities, then you shouldn't have a problem with this updo.

## 4. Greek Goddess

This style will make you look fabulous, but it won't look like you're trying too hard. That's why it's perfect for a casual day with your family. You're basically going to create two tiny braids that go over the top of your head, and curl the rest of the hair that falls around your face.

## 5. Chic Updo Style

This is a natural hairstyle tutorial. After you wash and dry your hair, you should create a ponytail. Then you should turn that ponytail into a bun by using a foam doughnut. All you have to do is wrap your hair around it and secure it with bobby pins. Then you can curl the rest of your hair with curlers.

## 6. Fishtail Braid

If you don't know how to create a fishtail braid yet, then it's time for you to learn. All you need is a brush and a few elastics. You're going to start by brushing down your hair to get rid of all the tangles. Then you're going to create two braids, one on each side of your head and securing them together. Then you can start creating the actual fishtail braid.

## 7. Messy Updo

This updo is perfect for any woman with short hair. You can start by using a straightener to create texture in your hair. Then you can start twisting and pinning your hair in order to keep your hair up in the air.

Your family will find you adorable in any of these hairstyles, so give them a try over the holiday! How do you plan on wearing your hair this Easter?
