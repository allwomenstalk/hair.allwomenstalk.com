## 1. Coconut Oil and Honey Hair Mask 🍯

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, long hair, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hb/yl/cu7dsboc59120d9287089727068106_600x648.jpg)

This mask is great because the coconut oil helps hydrate dry hair and the honey infuses each strand with antioxidants for good health and repair.

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon coconut oil
1 tablespoon raw honey

Directions:
Combine the two ingredients, then lightly heat them in a saucepan. Massage the mixture into dry or wet hair from roots to tips. Cover with a shower cap and allow the mask to soak in for 30 to 40 minutes. Wash and style as usual.
Source: [theeverygirl.com](https://theeverygirl.com/coconut-oil-honey-hair-mask/)

## 2. Banana, Avocado and Coconut Hair Mask 🍌🥑

![hair, human hair color, face, black hair, clothing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p9/f7/ax0rnwo859120da6d9109939891792_600x599.jpg)

Bananas and avocados are both great for nourishing dry hair and keeping it soft and beautiful. Combine them with coconut oil and you have a pretty great hair mask.

Ingredients:
1 banana
1 avocado
1 tablespoon coconut oil

Directions:
Mash together the banana and avocado and stir in the coconut oil. Massage the mask through your hair, let it sit for about 30 minutes, then rinse.
Source: [thefashionspot.com](https://www.thefashionspot.com/beauty/645717-diy-coconut-oil-hair-masks/)

## 3. Strawberry and Coconut Oil Mask 🍓

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, long hair, brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/ln/a4ss0o2x59120db9b17ce875700633_600x600.jpg)

Strawberries probably aren't the first fruit you think of when you decide to make a hair mask, but they are full of vitamin C, which promotes lush, healthy hair. And this one smells really great too!

Ingredients:
1 handful strawberries
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon coconut oil

Directions:
Mash the strawberries, discarding the stems. Add the honey and coconut oil and mix well. Apply the mask to your hair, let it soak in for about half and hour, then wash and rinse.
Source: [thefashionspot.com](https://www.thefashionspot.com/beauty/645717-diy-coconut-oil-hair-masks/)

## 4. Ultimate Hair Mask 🍳

![hair, human hair color, face, clothing, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ai/yp/az1z0f4859120e41ab9ac203076302_600x717.jpg)

The name of this one makes is a clear winner when you need a hydrating hair mask to combat dryness and make your hair shiny and soft.

Ingredients:
1 raw egg
1 tablespoon coconut oil
1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
Warm the coconut oil for about 15 seconds. Cool slightly, then add the egg and olive oil. Whisk until smooth. Massage the mask into your hair, cover and let it soak in for an hour. Wash and rinse.
Source: [bellafitnessblog.wordpress.com](https://bellafitnessblog.wordpress.com/2015/06/17/the-ultimate-hair-mask/)

## 5. Coconut Lavender Hair Mask 🌿

![hair, black hair, hairstyle, long hair, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/zn/ut5puy3v59120e68be457318504880_600x692.jpg)

Not only does this mask smell really soothing, but it calms dry hair and adds moisture back into each strand. You're going to love it!

Ingredients:
5 drops lavender oil
3 to 4 tablespoons coconut oil

Directions:
Mix the coconut oil and lavender. Apply the mixture throughout your hair and let it sit for 15 minutes. Rinse.
Source: [adelightfulhome.com](https://nofussnatural.com/diy-coconut-lavender-hair-mask/)

## 6. Coconut, Milk and Honey Mask 💧🍯

![hair, human hair color, blond, hairstyle, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pg/ed/tr3rvelm59120eb476aca135282373_600x599.jpg)

The combination of milk, honey and coconut is one you can always count on to hydrate and nourish dry hair, bringing it back to its glory days. This one uses coconut milk but I though it was valuable to add since it's super hydrating.

Ingredients:
1 can coconut milk
2 squirts honey

Directions:
Combine the two ingredients well. Place in a pump bottle. Apply a generous amount to your strands, let it soak in for 30 minutes, then rinse.
Source: [gloryboonblog.com](https://gloryboonblog.com/2013/01/31/coconut-milk-honey-for-hair-loss/)

## 7. Lemon and Coconut Mask 🍋

![hair, hairstyle, braid, long hair, french braid,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/xr/baoa5rfx59120e9437de0374853570_600x701.jpg)

With this mask you add moisture back into dry strands, but you also add vitamin C for strength and health.

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon coconut oil
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine the two ingredients and massage into your hair. Wait about 30 minutes before rinsing.
Source: [beautymunsta.com](https://beautymunsta.com/coconut-oil-hair-mask-for-luscious-hair/)
