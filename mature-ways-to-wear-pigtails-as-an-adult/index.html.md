# Mature Ways to Wear Pigtails as an Adult ...

You don't have to be a toddler in order to pull off pigtails. You can wear them as an adult to a party, to class, or to a date. As long as you dress in an outfit that shows you're a grown woman, and not a little girl, then you shouldn't have a problem. Here are a few of the most fashionable ways to wear pigtails as an adult:

## 1. Dutch Pigtails

Dutch pigtails are adorable, no matter what age group you're in. This tutorial will not only show you how to braid your hair to create the sweet style, but it'll also show you how to put in extensions. If you're bored of your short hair and don't feel like waiting to grow it out, then this tutorial will be a lifesaver for you.

## 2. Fluffy Pigtails

Fluffy pig tails are great to wear on special occasions, like when you're going to a party or a concert. All you have to do is style your hair in high pigtails, like you'd normally do, and then use your comb to tease your strands. The last step is to take some bobby pins in order to get your hair in exactly the right position. It'll take a little bit of time to get it right, but the end result will be well worth it.

## 3. Fishtail Pigtails

As you already know, fishtails are great to wear to the beach, because they won't get ruined when you step into the water. That's why this hairstyle is perfect for summer. If you don't want to braid your hair into one boring ponytail, then you can just create these pretty pigtails.

## 4. Natural Pigtail Buns

If you don't want to harm your natural hair, then you should follow this tutorial to create pigtail buns. They're super adorable, and they don't require you to add any heat to your locks. That means that you won't end up burning your naturally beautiful hair.

## 5. High Pigtails

Even though the girl in this video looks rather young, the style she shows you how to create will look fabulous on a woman of any age. All you need to create it is a comb and some hairbands. You don't even need to use hairspray, unless you're super worried about the style coming undone throughout the day.

## 6. Anime Pigtails

Whether you're a fan of anime or have never watched an episode in your life, you have to admit that the girls in it are quite cute. For this hairdo, you need a curling iron, so make sure that your hair is completely dry before you start to style it. You don't want to end up hurting your beautiful hair.

## 7. 10 More Types of Pigtails

If you haven't enjoyed any of the pigtails on this list, then you'll definitely find something you love in this video. Why? Because there are 10 entirely different styles that are all super easy to create. This video alone will give you ideas for every single day of the week, so you're bound to look fashionable in front of all of your friends.
