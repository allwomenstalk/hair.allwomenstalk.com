# 49 Best Easy Natural Hairstyles ...

Ever get frustrated with your hair? We all know that hairstyles can be time-consuming and costly, but don't worry! We're here to explain some easy natural hairstyles for you.

Some easy hairstyles that take little to no time are as simple as braiding or bun. Braids can be accomplished with a single braid or by braiding several smaller braids together. If you're not sure how you should make your hair look, try styling it with a bun. This is a quick and easy way to have the appearance of hair that is pulled back and held in place by elastic bands, making it look more sophisticated than messy fly-aways. Buns come in many different styles, but the most popular one is called an inverted bobbed bun which includes two plaits hanging down (like clip-on bangs), and one tucked underneath (the rest of the hair).

## 1. Wash-and-Go Hairstyle

Haircuts and hairstyles are very much a personal choice, so you can choose to style your hair however you please. But there’s one styling technique you must be careful with: the Wash-and-Go. It is essentially a low maintenance way to style your hair with just water.

## 2. Jumbo Topknot Hairstyle

In college, some people like to experiment with their hair. A few of my fellow students sported jumbo topknots at one point or another, as did I. Though these hairstyles can be fun, they take a lot of time and effort to maintain during a busy week.

## 3. Ponytail Hairstyle

Ponytail hairstyle is a controversial topic, with people either loving them, or loathing them. Whatever the case may be, one thing is for sure: There are numerous styles that can be achieved from the simple ponytail. In this post we will go over some of the many great ways to create your own unique ponytail style and make it your own.

## 4. Bantu Knots Hairstyle

Bantu knots are a style of hair that enhances the natural beauty of a woman's head by weaving in large sections with different colors. It can be both woven into small braids, or single-strand plaits. A braid can take just as long or even longer to complete than the hairstyle itself but it is well worth the time spent getting it right. The hairstyle originates from South Africa and is popular worldwide due to its unique appearance, which combines African and European elements.

## 5. Twist-Out Hairstyle

A twist-out hairstyle is one of the old-school ways of rockin’ natural hair. It’s an easy way to transition between wash and go styles without having to put products in (especially if you use a water-based moisturizer).

To do a twist-out, after washing, drying, and detangling your hair, take sections of your hair in dime dime sized sections at the root. Then braid each section until you reach the end. Once it's braided all the way down, unravel or undo it into larger twists that are about 2 inches in length. Secure with an elastic band before twisting again into smaller sections that are about ¾ inch in length.

## 6. Braid-Out Hairstyle

The braid-out hairstyle is a very common, easy, and versatile way to rock your natural hair. Once you learn how to braid your hair, you can do a braid out for any occasion--from formal to casual. To do a braid-out, after washing and drying your hair, detangle it and divide it into four sections. Take 1/3 of the amount of hair in the leftmost section and add it to the rightmost section until each section has equal amounts of hair. Braid each section with cornrows from one side of your head to the other. Then undo each braid into smaller braids that are about 2 inches in length.

## 7. Curl Formers Hairstyle

A curl-former is a very unique and versatile hairstyle that is meant to give you bigger curls and more volume. Once you learn how to properly curl hair, your hair will respond so well and will be so bouncy that you’ll wonder why you haven’t done this in the past!

To do a curl formers, after washing and detangling your natural hair, divide it into four sections. Take three of these sections in the front when working with each section. Apply coconut oil or an oil (only if it won't make your hair greasy) to each section, gently massage into each section while separating with your hands.

## 8. Water Wave Magic Curlers Hairstyle

Although this is not the conventional type of roller that you can use for the water wave hairstyle, this is a variation of the water wave that comes out so well for some people. To do this you need to have fine, or thin hair to get good results from them.

You need to have flexible micro beads, these are sold at any beauty supply store. You will also need some setting lotion (you can make your own setting lotion). Put a tiny amount in your hands and rub between both hands, apply only the tiniest amount of water to each bead, roll the bead onto each section of hair (about ½ inch away from scalp) while applying pressure with your fingers at first and then let go.

## 9. Perm Rods Hairstyle

A perm rod is used to set permanent hair, or in this case, natural hair. It helps create separation between the texture of your hair and make it smoother and straighter.

To do a perm rods you need to have very soft, or fine natural hair. After washing and detangling your hair, secure a section of your hair. Curl it with a curling iron and then let it down. Separate the curl by combing it out in the back of your head with a large pick comb or using another type of brush that has a wide barrel (about 12 inches). Then apply the lotion to each section and push into it (only if needed).

## 10. Roller Set Hairstyle

Although this is a very common, easy and simple way to do your hair, it’s a good alternative for you to try if you’re tired of using heat on your hair. If you have thin or fine hair and you want more volume, this is the best kind of roller set to do.

To do a roller set, after washing, detangling and blow-drying your hair with a blow dryer with diffuser attachment, section your hair into four sections (two in the front and two in the back). Place the roller towards the ends of each section while making sure that you don’t put too many rollers on your head.

## 11. Pigtail Cornrows Hairstyle

The pigtail cornrows hairstyle is a cute, creative, and easy way to rock your natural hair. It’s a wonderful hairstyle for school or work.

To do this hairstyle, you first have to do medium-sized cornrows in the front and back of your hair. Then you take the remaining hair from the front and back of your head and braid it into one section. Take small pieces from both the left and right side of this braided section, add them to the middle of the braid making pigtails with at least 8 small braids on each side that are about 2 inches in length.

## 12. Flat Twists Hairstyle

Flat twists are two strand twists that are flat rather than round. These twists expand the hair by exposing more of the hair shaft and create a looser and bulkier look. This hairstyle can also be considered a Faux-Locs starter because it’s a great way to expand your natural hair by lengthening it without going through the processes of locking it.

To do this hairstyle, you wash, dry, detangle and then section your hair into two sections (the left section is about 4 inches big and the right section is about 3 inches big).

## 13. Messy Bun Hairstyle

In this hairstyle, you basically do your hair in a bun and then back tie it with an elastic band. To do this hairstyle, you wash, dry and detangle your hair. Then spray some light-weight texturing product on your hands and scrunch in your hair. Next, use a rat tail comb to separate the hair into two sections on the left side of your head then bring it over to the right side of your head and secure it with a rubber band or elastic band. Next take the remaining hair that’s on the right side of your neck and secure it with another elastic band. Finally back-tie all of this hair into a ponytail or any type of bun if you know how to do that.

## 14. Phony Pony Hairstyle

The Phony Pony is a hairstyle created by Janet Collection. It’s a very cute, small and easy style to do as you only need to add hair to one ponytail. You can change the look of this hairstyle by adding or taking away hair in the ponytail or use an elastic band that matches your hair color.

For the main ponytail, wash, detangle and blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment. Then section off about 4 inches of hair from both sides of your head (the left section should be about 2 inches big and the right section should be about 3 inches big).

## 15. Curly Clip-Ins Hairstyle

This hairstyle is done by taking four to five small pieces of hair, clip-ins, and then sew them together. (To do this, you should first do a regular clip-in. Then you should start with the shortest piece of hair on top and sew it by folding over).

After sewing together your clip-in hair, section off a ¼ inch or half inch strand from both sides of the middle piece. Next, gather ½ inch strands of your hair from both sides of the top section and secure them to the middle section with the help of an elastic band (make sure that you don’t take too many strands in one section because it may appear to be thinner than it originally is).

## 16. Double Braids Hairstyle

This hairstyle is the easier and smaller version of the cornrows hairstyle. To do this you first have to do a regular cornrows. Then you take all your braids and sew them together with a clothes pin, or something similar to that. In this hairstyle, you don’t use any hair accessories and this is why it appears to be thin.

To do this hairstyle, after detangling your hair and blow-drying it with a diffuser attachment, section off around 4 inches of hair and braid each section in four separate parts (two on each side).

## 17. XL Box Braids Hairstyle

To do an XL Box Braids you first have to wash, detangle and then section off around 8 inches of hair on both sides of the head. After that you take four to five small pieces of hair and sew them together in the front or sides of your head. (The moment you begin doing this, make sure that you space each piece of hair at least three quarters in between each other so it doesn’t look as if someone has cut your hair).

After sewing these six sections, divide them into two rows.

## 18. Pineapple Hairstyle

For the pineapple hairstyle you first have to do a regular braid. Then after doing that, you take the two ends of your braid and weave them together with an elastic band. This hairstyle is unique because it looks like you actually put hair into your braids but no one else has done this before.

To do this hairstyle, after detangling and blow-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment, section off around 8 inches of hair from both sides of the head (the left section should be about five inches big and the right section should be about four inches big).

## 19. Afro Puffs Hairstyle

Afro puffs are cute, bouncy, and easy to do. They’re great for school or work because they make your hair look neat and organized. This hairstyle can be done by taking five to six ponytails of hair on each side of the head.

## 20. Bohemian Braids Hairstyle

This hairstyle allows your natural curls to shine through. You can create this hairstyle by taking your hair in four separate sections (right side: top section, middle section, and bottom section). After you take a section, secure it with an elastic band (make sure that you don’t take too many strands in one section because it may appear to be thinner than it originally is).

## 21. Brushed Out Hairstyle

This is a hairstyle that has been done in the past by famous celebrities. To do this, you section off small amounts of hair on the top and sides of your head. Then you begin brushing out the hair into natural curls.

## 22. Fishnet Braids Hairstyle

This hairstyle can be done by taking small pieces of hair (1/4 inch to 1/2 inch) and sewing them together with an elastic band. After doing this, divide them into two rows and sew each row together at the bottom (do not let your hair fall off on both sides). After sewing all four rows together, you can take some bobby pins or clips and secure your hair with those to make sure that it stays in place.

## 23. Faux Hawk Hairstyle

This hairstyle can be done by taking small pieces of hair and weaving them into the hair of your choice. When you do this make sure that you space the pieces at least three quarters in between each other so it doesn’t look as if someone has cut your hair.

## 24. French Braids Hairstyle

This basic hairstyle can be achieved by taking your hair and sectioning it off into two rows or sections on each side of your head. After that, take three sections of your bangs (front) and one section from the back and braid them together to create one long braid.

## 25. Braided Faux Hawk Hairstyle

For this hairstyle you’re going to take three to four small pieces of hair and sew them together. (To do this, you should first do a regular braid. Then you should start with the shortest piece of hair on top and sew it by folding over it). After sewing together your braid add more hair if needed for the length.

## 26. Ponytail Puffs Hairstyle

This is a girls hairstyle that has been done in the past by famous celebrities. To do this, you first have to wash, detangle and then section off around 6 inches of hair on both sides of the head.

## 27. Half-Up, Half-Down Hairstyle

This hairstyle can be done by taking small pieces of hair (1/4 inch to 1/2 inch) and sewing them together with an elastic band.

## 28. Sideswept Braids Hairstyle

This is a hairstyle that will make your hair look more neat and clean. This is achieved by taking small sections of your hair on one side and then sectioning off another section on the other side of your head. After you do this, braid both sections together around two to three times each. Or, if you are not sure how much hair you need, start with the bottom section first, then add the second (middle) section and then put on another braid around two or three times.

## 29. Headband Hairstyle

This trendy hairstyle is done by taking your hair and sectioning it off into small pieces of hair (1/4 inch to 1/2 inch). Then before you sew it, you should space each piece at least three quarters in between each other so it doesn’t look as if someone has cut your hair. After doing this, sew all the pieces together with a soft elastic band.

## 30. Corn Stalk Braids Hairstyle

For this hairstyle you first have to wash, detangle and then section off around 2 inches of hair on both sides of the head. After that, you sew both ends together by about two inches deep by about four inches wide on both sides of your head.

## 31. Banding Hairstyle

The best thing about this hairstyle is that you can do it on your own. This is achieved by taking small pieces of hair (about 1/4 inch to 1/2 inch) and sewing them together with an elastic band. After doing this, you should divide the bands into two to four sections and sew those sections together. Then, you should wrap the elastic band around the center of your head a couple of times so that everything looks connected.

## 32. French Braid Hairstyle

This hairstyle will make your hair look nice and neat. This is done by taking small sections of your hair on one side and sectioning off another section on the other side of your head.

## 33. Sideswept Statement Hairstyle

This is a hairstyle that doesn’t require too much maintenance. This can be achieved by taking small sections of your hair on one side and sectioning off another section on the other side of your head.

## 34. Updo Hairstyle

This hairstyle can be done by taking small sections of your hair on one side and sectioning off another section on the other side of your head. After you do this, braid both sections together around two to three times each or, if you are not sure how much hair you need, start with the bottom section first, then add the second (middle) section and then put on another braid around two or three times.

## 35. Head Wrap Hairstyle

This hairstyle can be achieved by taking small sections of your hair and braiding them together in a circular motion.

## 36. Ponytail Hairstyle

To do this hairstyle, you have to wash, detangle and then section off around 6 inches of hair on both sides of the head. After that, you should sew both ends together by about three quarters deep to make a ponytail effect. After taking out the elastic band (out of the ponytail) try smooth the hair over to conceal the elastic band. Then take small pieces of hair (1/4 inch to 1/2 inch) and sew them together with an elastic band.

## 37. Hair Paint Wax Hairstyle

For this hairstyle you should first prepare the hair to be dyed by washing, detangling and then drying the hair. After that, you should dye your hair a color of your choosing. Keep in mind that this hairstyle only works if your natural hair color is blonde because it is the only one that will make the dye pop out more.

## 38. Elongated Braid Hairstyle

This hairstyle can be achieved by taking small sections of your hair on one side and sectioning off another section on the other side of your head.

## 39. Fishtail Braid Hairstyle

For this hairstyle you first have to wash, detangle and then section off around 6 inches of hair on both sides of the head. After that, you should sew the ends together with a small elastic band by about three quarters deep to make a fishtail effect.

## 40. Braid Wave Hairstyle

You should first wash and detangle your hair to get rid of any knots that may be in your hair. Then take small sections of your hair from both sides and braid them together around two to three times each (making sure not to overdo it).

## 41. Baby Hairs Focus Hairstyle

To do this hairstyle, you first have to take an elastic band and braid your hair. Then you should section off a small piece of hair by around 1/4 inch each and then braid that piece of hair. After braiding that one, repeat the process again with another section of hair (if you want to include more than one) until you have braided all the pieces of hair that you want. The next step is to place either a small or large blob of wax onto a candle then heat it up so it melts. When it’s melted, take the wax and put it on your hairspray bottle (or any bottle containing hairspray) so that it will be easier to spray on your hair.

## 42. Statement Clips Hairstyle

For this hairstyle you should first mess up your hair and then section off small pieces of hair by around 1/4 inch to 1/2 inch. Then you should braid each section of hair and then take out the elastic band that is holding your braids together. After taking out the elastic band, you should clip on large statement clips on both sides of your head where there is a small section of hair that was left out at either side (either a front or back).

## 43. Visible Scrunchie Hairstyle

For this hairstyle you should first wash, detangle and then section off around 2 inches of hair on both sides of the head. After that sew the ends together by about half deep to make it a scrunchy effect.

## 44. Scrunchy (Braided) Hair Hairstyle

## 46. Halo Braid Hairstyle
