# How Stylists Create Sand Art Hair ...

If you can't decide on a color to dye your hair, why not use every color you can think of? If you ask your stylist for sand art hair, then you'll have an entire rainbow on your head. According to [Harper's Bazaar,](https://www.harpersbazaar.com/beauty/hair/g5953/rainbow-hair/) here's how the process is completed:

## 1. Section the Hair

![Section the Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lc/pa/uajq242856c47828a6803104846482_480x720.jpg)

First, the hair is separated into triangular sections.

## 2. Apply the Dye

![Apply the Dye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ba/pz/gpxmyl0356c4782875fde503336188_480x720.jpg)

Next, they apply the colors. They "started with six main colors-pink, blue, yellow-green, a bluish-purple, violet, and a pastel purple-gray."

## 3. Let the Color Set in

![Let the Color Set in](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pr/od/m3g7tmsr56c47828d46b9331883241_480x720.jpg)

After the color is in, you'll need to sit for 30 minutes.

## 4. Blow out the Hair

![Blow out the Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/j7/ejeuo6gm56c47828ec932014593437_480x720.jpg)

Now, it's time to finally let your hair dry so you can see the results.

## 5. Create Curls

![Create Curls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mb/ix/irrvqvcj56c47829184c3071588493_480x720.jpg)

If you like your look, you can amp it up by creating Hollywood styled curls.
