# Home Hair Treatments for Split Ends That Actually Work ...

A couple of great reasons why DIY hair treatments are worth trying is they're super inexpensive, take a short amount of time and chances are you have a lot of useful ingredients lying around the kitchen that you can use in a fab hair mask!

Here are our top picks for DIY hair masks / hair treatments that you can make and test out at home!

## 1. Honey, Egg and Olive Oil Mask

Looking to try a DIY mask that will strengthen and soften your hair as well as reduce split ends? The egg mask is well worth trying! For this all you need is honey, an egg and olive oil, and for best results repeat this mask once a week!

## 2. Mixed Oils Mask

For this mask, you'll need argan oil, coconut oil, castor oil, avocado oil and honey. Many of these oils are great for repairing dry, dead and damaged hair which makes them a great option for removing split ends and repairing your hair! P.S mixed oils mask starts at 2.47 if you want to skip to this!

## 3. Coconut Oil, Honey and Yoghurt Mask

For this mask, start by warming your ingredients and then mix 2 tablespoons of coconut oil with 2 tablespoons of honey. Now add 2 tablespoons of yoghurt and give it a good mix! Leave the mixture on your hair for 30 minutes and rinse.

## 4. Castor Oil Hair Mask

For this you will need castor oil, vitamin E capsules and extra virgin olive oil. Once you have these ingredients you can get started on this super moisturising and softening hair mask that's also great for eliminating split ends!

## 5. Banana, Grapeseed Oil and Apple Cider Vinegar Mask

This is a super fab organic hair mask you can do at home to say goodbye to your split ends! Start by mushing up a banana in a bowl, then add one tablespoon of grapeseed oil followed by 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar.

## 6. Coconut Oil Mask

Coconut oil comes in different forms so it's best to opt for the type that's going to work best for you! This video explains how to use coconut oil in the form of a spray, but there are many other hair masks out there you can watch if you have coconut oil in a jar or another form.

## 7. Shea Butter, Sunflower Oil and Olive Oil Mask

This video will show you how to make a super effective hair mask using Shea butter! Other ingredients you will need is sunflower oil and olive oil. Apply the mask on wet hair, use sparingly, leave on for about an hour before washing out!
