# Perfect Hairstyles for a Gorgeous Look at Coachella 2018 ...

You can look fabulous this year with these gorgeous **Coachella hairstyles**. Coachella is one of the best music festivals around. It's in California so you know the weather is likely to be great too. If you're planning to go this year, you need the perfect hairstyle for the big event. Every style on this list is perfect for a summery day and will have you looking your best. Here's what to consider for **hairstyles for Coachella 2018**.

## 1. Half up Bun

This trendy hairstyle is super easy to do. It's a little but casual, but totally polished and put together. You can't go wrong at a music festival with this look.

## 2. Curls with a Flower Headband

Wearing a flowered headband is the perfect way to celebrate Coachella. This easy video will show you how to make your very own.

## 3. Mohawk Braid

You are going to love this braid! It's easy to do and will give you a stylish and adorable look that is ideal for spring and summer. Including your trip to Coachella.

## 4. Coachella Inspired Boho Look

Since Coachella is a boho style festival, this hairstyle is perfect. You have a little bit of braiding for visual appeal and it holds your hair out of your face while you sing and dance.

## 5. Glitter Roots

Going to a music festival is all about standing out and sparkling in a good way. Use this video to help you get fabulous glitter roots that will turn all the heads.

## 6. Messy Braid

This side braid is a little bit mussed, which gives it that casual look you want for Coachella. It's super easy to do and will keep you cool as you dance to the music.

## 7. Crown Braid

This is one of your best looks for Coachella. It's a little but boho and keeps your hair out of your face and off your neck. Plus, it's super cute!

## 8. Coachella Hippie Hair

This adorable look involves making several braids that mix in with your long, loose locks. It's perfect for a summer day!

## 9. Arrowhead Half up Braid

If you're looking for something totally unique, this is it. This looks complicated, but it's actually pretty easy. And you can bet no one else is going to have the same hairstyle.

## 10. Boho Braided Headband

You've never seen anything like this before. It's a totally cool new way to wear braids that you are going to adore.

## 11. Twisted Updo

If ever there was a perfect hairstyle for day outside listening to music, this is the one. It's cute and easy and will gt you loads of compliments.

## 12. Easy Boho Bun

There's so much to love about this look. The pieces hanging down in the front frame you beautiful face, but the back is off your neck and looking trendy. Perfect!

## 13. Criss Cross Braids

This style is great because you get to make a few braids and then cross them in the back. It's a great idea for keeping your hair out of your face, but still down around your shoulders.

## 14. Boho Curls

If you want to wear your hair down to Coachella this year, this easy curly hairstyle is your best bet. It looks awesome!

## 15. Easy Flower Hairdo

You'll be the most beautiful flower child at Coachella this year with this style. It's essentially a slightly messy bun with flowers in it.

## 16. Bubble Bun

This is a trendy new hairstyle you've probably seen before. It's perfect for Coachella because it's easy and looks perfect. Add a flower if you want to.

## 17. Perfect High Ponytail

You can never go wrong with a high ponytail. It's the perfect look for a fun day at Coachella.

Are you going to Coachella this year? How will you wear your hair?
