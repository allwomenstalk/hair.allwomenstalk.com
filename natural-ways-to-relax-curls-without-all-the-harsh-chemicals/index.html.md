# Natural Ways to Relax Curls without All the Harsh Chemicals ...

If you have curly hair, you know what I'm talking about when I say that sometimes you just don't want to deal with them. There are plenty of ways to relax curls, but many of them involve using harsh chemicals. If you prefer to go a more natural route, you are in luck. There are some great ways to relax your curls without chemicals and these videos will help you get perfect hair any day of the week.

## 1. Coconut Cream Natural Relaxer

The blend of coconut oil, yogurt, avocado oil and coconut milk are ideal for giving your hair a natural relaxation that will leave it straighter, shinier and smoother. When you want to get rid of curls for a while, this is the video for you.

## 2. Natural Relaxer Hair Mask

This mask is super easy to whip up and leaves your curls relaxed. It also fights frizz so your hair is smooth and shiny all day long. Use purreed bananas to get the look you're going for. It's super easy and totally effective. You're going to love the way you look!

## 3. Natural Relaxer with Okra

The combination of coconut milk, vinegar and okra in this formula work to relax natural hair so it's not frizzy and damaged. This treatment is super easy to make and really easy to use and you will love how it infuses each strand and helps relax them. Perfect!

## 4. Homemade Hair Straightener

If you want straight hair, you don't need to look any further than your own kitchen to get what you want. This recipe uses things you already have lying around and is affordable and easy. The best part is that it's totally natural. Ingredients include coconut milk, lemon juice, olive oil and cornstarch.

## 5. Loosen Curls with Baking Soda

If you want to go from kinky curls to more relaxed waves, all you need is some baking soda. As a bonus, the baking soda also helps remove built up junk in your hair so that it looks and feels healthy and beautiful all day long.

## 6. All Natural Hair Relaxer

This handy video will take you step by step through a super easy way to naturally relax your hair. Start by washing and blow drying it, then follow the directions and when the short tutorial is over, you should have the perfectly relaxed hair you're after. Hooray!

## 7. Loosen Tight Curly Hair

There are days you love your tight curls, but there are probably days when you wish you could get rid of them for awhile. If that describes you, follow the easy instructions in this video that teach you how to loosen them up and turn them into waves you love. This recipe uses corn flour, lemon juice, olive oil and yogurt to help your hair relax. The natural ingredients are also healthy.
