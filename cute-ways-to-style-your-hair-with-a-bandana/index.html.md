# Cute Ways to Style Your Hair with a Bandana ...

Haley shows how a girl can wear her hair 8 **different ways with a bandana**! Totally adorable! The best part? You can buy bandanas at the dollar store in about any color! Short hair lengths that usually have limited options may even use bandanas.

Watch her flirt with the camera!

Haley's Corner
Published on May 13, 2018

Nice as a refresher course, huh? I usually wear bandanas like a scarf. Now I am going to use her techniques. I am tired of wearing my hair in a simple ponytail, also. I want something trendy and different!

She says this is "in" this summer 2018. Why not carry the style into the winter season?

Another advantage of this style is that you can dress it up or dress it down! Wear with a mini-skirt. Wear with leggings. Wear with a blouse. Wear with a t-shirt. There are no rules!

So, happy bandana-ing!
