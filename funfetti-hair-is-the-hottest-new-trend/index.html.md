# Funfetti Hair is the Hottest New Trend ...

You don't have to have straight hair in order to pull off the hottest trends of the year. If you have an afro, there's a new style that's meant just for you. I's called Funfetti hair and it looks as gorgeous as it sounds.

According to [Seventeen,](https://www.seventeen.com/beauty/hair/news/a39040/funfetti-hair-is-the-amazing-new-rainbow-hair-color-you-have-to-try/) Funfetti hair is when there are "jewel tones throughout [the] hair and it [is] very bold and attention-grabbing." It's a great way to stand out from the crowd.

Now all of you ladies with gorgeous afros have a fun new way of dying your hair! Would you wear your hair like this?
