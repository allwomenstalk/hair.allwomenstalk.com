# 11 Knotted Ponytails to Be Stylish and Cool All Summer Long ...

Summertime is for simplified hairstyles that keeps your hair out of your face without too much work. Beach days call for lazy hair looks that appear as if you put major effort in, but are oh so simple to achieve! Upgrade your basic ponytail with 11 of these knotted ponytails below to keep you looking stylish and cool all summer long!

## 1. Ponytail Twist

![clothing, dress, woman, flower, bride,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/ua/ywwo6plk59301f99382a4641303681_600x900.jpg)

This nontraditional pony features a makeshift knot made out of… itself. Sweep your long hair back and out of the way, then wrap outer strands around the base of your pony and pin everything into place. The end result is oh-so chic.

## 2. Sleek Knotted Low Pony

![hair, hairstyle, blond, beauty, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/yt/yrxqcsda59301f99daad9008051455_600x812.jpg)

This stick-straight, slicked-back pony is totally on trend thanks to its minimalist appeal and subtle detailing. Crisscross your strands before securing them in a pigtail at the nape of your neck, then wrap your hair around the base of your pony, covering the hair tie.

## 3. Low Pony Knotted Twist

![hair, blond, hairstyle, hair coloring, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o2/uf/e0ylwyom5937f5ee7ed53018693435_600x600.jpg)

This look begins like a regular low pony but then takes a distinctly fashion-forward detour. Choosing two strands from the underside of your hair, wrap them around the base of your ponytail to form a simple, braid-like twist. It’s a super easy detail that will have you looking instantly polished.

## 4. Messy Single-Knotted Low Pony

![hair, blond, hairstyle, hair coloring, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/be/cm/v3h6flyl59301f99b5aad429279409_600x799.jpg)

Since messy hair is the order of the day (and is a total cool-girl staple to boot), switch up smoothed-down strands for this lived-in look. The low-key, high-impact style is so chic, you can expect to see us rockin’ it on the reg.

## 5. Dpouble Knot Ponytail

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, beauty, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/nu/zhwb4nvt59301f99e470d184034380_600x725.jpg)

You don’t have to be at the beach to rock this creative double knotted ponytail - it’ll slay from the boardroom to the boardwalk. Practice makes perfect, so spend a few minutes learning this easy DIY and rock it to every upcoming event on your calendar.

## 6. Knotted Ombre Pony

![hair, hairstyle, face, hair coloring, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ch/hc/zgkzv4vt59301f99f15e1618930712_600x810.jpg)

It’s worth mentioning that the knotted pony looks dope AF on a ‘do that’s dyed ombre. The pop of color begins right at the base of the pony and makes for a two-toned hairstyle perfect for any trendsetter.

## 7. High Knotted Pony

![hair, hairstyle, face, forehead, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e3/h3/hgfjz80v59301f9a4fdad947747007_600x699.jpg)

A high ponytail with a trendy knot is a fun and flirty variation of this season’s ultra-hot hairstyle. This original twist is a welcome distraction from all the low ponies we’ve been seeing (and loving).

## 8. Two-minute Pony

![hair, hairstyle, face, long hair, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r4/ul/ptsti15259301f9c91e34783113778_600x900.jpg)

The side ponytail is a tried-and-true favorite of all our Instababes with enviable hair game. With a sophisticated knot and curled ends, this fashion gal has pulled off a polished, knotted side pony FTW.

## 9. MEssy Knotted Ponytail

![hair, hairstyle, long hair, blond, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/ax/foqi2g5q59301f9d243a1158626128_600x798.jpg)

In our wildest bohemian dreams, we never even imagined the OTT-chic look of this long, wrapped and triple-knotted ponytail. Use two sections of your hair to delicately wind down the length of your tresses to create this romantic style that’s decidedly fairy-tale-approved.

## 10. Festival-ready Knotted Ponytail

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, dress, costume,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/kc/bnybs5vo59301f9d87a9e625413926_600x753.jpg)

It takes super-long hair to pull off this ornate look, but we’re dying to see a multi-knot twist go down IRL. The intricate plaits and twisty-turns play up the glam factor in this pony that would make Rapunzel jealous.

## 11. Ponybun

![hair, hairstyle, long hair, brown hair, hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/px/yx/f3nwt80z5937f606512c3544918203_600x600.jpg)

Celebrate your style in ultimate lazy-girl fashion. This super easy look is so simple, you can be out of bed and on your way to your nine-to-five in a few minutes flat.
