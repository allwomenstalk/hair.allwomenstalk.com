# Super Quick  and Easy Hairstyles for Those Lazy Days Every Girl Has ...

Every girl has those super lazy mornings when you don't have the time or can't be bothered doing a hugely intricate hairstyle...but still need to do something with it! Keep reading for a bit of inspo for quick and easy hairstyles to do on these lazy days and don't forget to comment your faves! 😍 😘 ❤️ 💖

## 1. Top Knot

![hair, face, clothing, blond, photography,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/ph/ng03zx7w5822aab4db4ac317550238_564x564.jpg)

For this style, all you have to do is roughly separate half of your hair using your hands and push it up until it's in the desired height where you want the bun - whether that's directly on top of your head or somewhere lower at the back. Then grab a hair tie and make a small bun and keep looping it over until your bun becomes nice and tight!

## 2. High Pony

![hair, hairstyle, face, long hair, forehead,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/qt/zs0uz4925822aac284e15387234807_500x549.jpg)

Simply tip your head upside down and brush your hair out. While you're doing this, grab a hair tie and with your free hand, gather all your hair into a pony tail. Flip your hair back, still holding your hair in place, and secure it with a hair tie! Tip for an invisible hair tie: take a bit of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around so it fully covers the hair tie, before securing it on the underside of the base with bobby pins.

## 3. Doughnut Bun

![hair, eyebrow, face, hairstyle, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j0/sh/k4xguqia5822aacc4ca52203753312_500x506.jpg)

Start off by using your hands to create an easy pony tail and bring it up to the desired height, then use a hair tie to secure it into a normal ponytail. Grab a hair doughnut and thread it through your pony so that it's sitting at the base. Fan your hair around the doughnut so that your it's completely covered and not visible. Grab a second hair tie and loop it over the top of the doughnut to secure it. You'll now have lots of loose ends around the base, so pull all of these round either way and pin them down with a few bobby pins and clips. This might sound time-consuming or confusing but I assure you it can be done within a couple of minutes at the most!

## 4. Half Pony

![hair, human hair color, blond, hairstyle, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lp/kp/u20n0do65822aad6eb3a1190055360_546x569.jpg)

For this style, all you'll need to start off by brushing your hair and make sure it's all behind your shoulders. Then take a hair tie and, with your free hand, grab a small area of hair (preferably the front pieces) and keep looping your hair tie around until it's a cute half pony, with the rest of your hair left loose underneath!

## 5. Messy Bun

![facial expression, human action, face, hair, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/r3/raelo0kh5822aae24051b768424253_500x491.jpg)

For a great high messy bun, all you have to do is start off by tipping your head forward and basically make your bun while upside down. This makes it a lot easier to get your bun up higher on your head. Otherwise, simply pull your hair back to the desired height and secure it with a hair tie. Lastly, gently pull the sides out so that it looks looser and achieves the messy bun look!

## 6. Side Ponytail

![hair, face, clothing, black hair, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jy/by/rmgowp3r5822aeb31915b386854847_550x590.jpg)

For this style, simply brush all your hair to either side so that it sits in front of your shoulders. Then, grab a hair tie and secure it into a ponytail on the desired side, making sure you keep your hand relaxed for a loose side pony, or be quick and firm if you want it to be tighter. If this isn't enough for you, feel free to jazz it up by adding a cute ribbon on top!

## 7. Side Bun

![hair, face, hairstyle, hair coloring, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/cq/hkuy86a45822aebda25a9275631682_600x558.jpg)

A great side bun is super easy to do for those lazy days! All you have to do is find the desired height for your bun and head towards a pony tail by looping the hair tie round. On the last one, though, only loop it round half way to turn it into a bun. Lastly, pull the loose strands round either way and use bobby pins to clip it down for a neat, tidy side bun!
