# 7 Pinup Girl Hairstyles You'll Look Hot Wearing ...

You don't have to be a model in order to wear your hair like a pinup girl. While some of the hairdos may make you look a bit old fashioned, there's nothing wrong with stealing styles from the past. It'll make you look unique, which is guaranteed to turn some heads! Here are a few pinup girl hairstyles all of you would look hot wearing:

## 1. 1940 Pinup Hair Tutorial

This tutorial shows you more than how to create a pinup girl hairstyle. It also shows you how to do your makeup, so you can make sure that your entire look fits together perfectly. All you have to do is section your hair, curl it, and pin it. After you do that a few times, your look is complete! It's not nearly as hard as it looks, so don't be afraid to give it a try.

## 2. Curly Hair Pinup Tutorial

This hairstyle is meant to be messy, so it's hard for you to mess it up. First off, you need to separate the top portion of your hair in order to create bangs. Then you need to curl your hair, starting at the bottom, and pin it up once you're finished. Once you do that over and over until all of your hair is curled, you should take the pins out and tease your hair. Then you're pretty much done! Of course, if you'd like to add an accessory, a scarf will look great with the style.

## 3. Modern Pinup Tutorial

Here's another tutorial that will show you how to do your makeup in addition to your hair. The best thing about this look is that it puts a modern twist on the traditional pinup style. That means that you won't get any weird glances when you wear it out in public. You'll look just like anyone else in this century--except a bit more fashionable.

## 4. Rosie the Riveter Tutorial

For this look, you're going to need some hairspray, bobby pins, a round brush, and a bandana. However, you might even need a curling iron if you want to make sure that your bangs curl in the right direction. Once you have all of those items together, press play on this tutorial, and take advice from the Rosie the Riveter master.

## 5. Easy Pinup Tutorial

This is about the easiest [pinup hairstyle](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/fabulous-pinup-hair-tutorials/) to capture. It shouldn't take you any longer than five minutes to complete, because all you have to do is part your hair on the side and backcomb your hair a bit. Once you do that, you can use hairspray and pin your hair back, twisting it a bit. That's all it takes!

## 6. Bumper Bang Tutorial

The most important aspect of this hairstyle is the bangs, so make sure you pay close attention to how the woman in this tutorial styles them. However, you should be aware that you need a special hair wrap in order to make this hairstyle look its best, so make sure you buy it before you try to recreate this look.

## 7. Traditional Pin up Tutorial

Here's one last pinup inspired hairdo that isn't hard to create. The best part? It doesn't look all that old fashioned, so you won't look out of place wearing it in public. Besides, it doesn't get any easier to look as fashionable as a model.

Pinup styles are incredibly attractive, so don't hesitate to try them all out! Are there any pinup girl hairstyles that you've tried wearing lately?
