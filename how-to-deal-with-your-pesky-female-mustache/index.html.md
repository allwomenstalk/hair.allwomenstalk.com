# How to Deal with Your Pesky Female Mustache ...

Unfortunately, men aren't the only ones capable of growing mustaches. Some women are stuck dealing with hair in the most uncomfortable and embarrassing places. Of course, removing hair from under your nose is a little bit different than removing it from your legs and bikini area. If you're cursed with a pesky female mustache, here are a few ways you can deal with it:

## 1. Apply Makeup in the Direction Your Hair is Growing

![Apply Makeup in the Direction Your Hair is Growing](https://img.allw.mn/content/f3/cp/te1csycj56576343a0007552672573_500x206.gif)

You don't actually have to remove your hair. You can just cover it with makeup. If you always apply foundation, then you won't even have an extra step in your makeup routine. Just make sure that you apply the makeup in the direction that your hair is growing. Otherwise, it can make your makeup look clumpy, and your hairs will stick out more.

## 2. Bleach Your Mustache

[https://media.giphy.com/media/Wigpv4KU5t4oU/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/Wigpv4KU5t4oU/giphy.gif)
Most women who have mustaches just bleach them. However, you can't buy any type of bleach and rub it on your skin. There are special products at the store that are meant to be placed underneath your nose. Buy one of them, and then follow the instructions on the box carefully, so you don't end up irritating your skin.

## 3. Wax Your Mustache

[https://media.giphy.com/media/r4nseWoVWdiM0/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/r4nseWoVWdiM0/giphy.gif)
You probably don't want to put a razor to your face, even though that's what your boyfriend does. It's better if you wax your mustache. Of course, just like bleaching it, you should make sure that you buy products that are specifically meant to be placed on your face. Otherwise, you could end up getting red and blotchy skin.

## 4. Tweeze Your Mustache

![Tweeze Your Mustache](https://img.allw.mn/content/bi/kq/euj0zc9e565763434c21c439288347_500x244.gif)

If your mustache is really only a strand or two of hair, then all you have to do is tweeze those hairs. It'll only take two seconds, and it'll be relatively painless. Besides, if you get hairs on your chin (which is more common than you think), you can get rid of them at the same time.

## 5. Use Depilatory Cream on Your Mustache

![Use Depilatory Cream on Your Mustache](https://img.allw.mn/content/cs/c6/enfw239p56576343e5296075684008_500x282.gif)

Depilatory cream is hair removal cream. It's a product that you can rub on your skin and then wipe away, along with your mustache. However, some women don't respond well to these products, which is why you need to be careful. Make sure you buy the type meant for your face, and meant for delicate skin. Then the chemicals won't be as harsh.

## 6. Thread Your Mustache

![Thread Your Mustache](https://img.allw.mn/content/b1/pe/vvexhz4f56576343cd3f6771969708_492x258.gif)

Did you know that your eyebrows aren't the only place that can be threaded? You can actually have your mustache threaded as well. Of course, you'll have to go to a salon and ask a professional to do it for you. You don't want to end up bleeding.

## 7. Get a Laser Hair Removal Treatment Done

![Get a Laser Hair Removal Treatment Done](https://img.allw.mn/content/uk/or/h4wih8zq56576343be13d743187580_500x281.gif)

If you can't stand the routine of getting rid of your facial hair, then you could always get a laser treatment. Yes, it'll be expensive, and yes, it's a little extreme. However, it will take care of your problem for good. Then you won't have to worry about those pesky hairs for any longer.

You should't be embarrassed about your hair. It's natural, so no one should judge you over it. Of course, if you're more comfortable getting rid of it, just follow this advice. What other tips do you have for ladies with female mustaches?
