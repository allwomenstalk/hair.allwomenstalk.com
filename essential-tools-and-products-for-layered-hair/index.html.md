# 7 Essential Tools and Products for Layered Hair ...

If you’re a new convert to layers, you need the scoop on the best products for [layered hair](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/reasons-why-you-shouldnt-get-layers-in-your-hair/). Even the best hair stylist can’t work miracles. Despite a great hair cut, you need to follow up with high quality tools and products at home to keep those layers looking lovely. Without these 7 must-have products for layered hair, your style could be falling flat.

## 1. Redken Layer Lift

![Redken Layer Lift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/1_redken-layer-lift_277x263.jpg)

The Redken line offers several quality products for layered hair styles. But Redken Layer Lift is recommended because it provides a gentle mist of gel that will control frizz and give even the thinnest hair some much needed volume. Just spray a bit into damp hair, starting at the roots and blow dry. See your local salon for this product and others in the Redken brand, or visit Ulta.com to purchase online.

## 2. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Full Form Mousse

![Bumble and Bumble Thickening Full Form Mousse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/2_bumble-and-bumble-thickening-full-form-mousse_152x358.jpg)

If you’re more of a mousse gal when it comes to hair products, Bumble and Bumble makes the perfect product that will give those layers a lift. One of the biggest problems with layered looks occurs when your hair goes flat. The layers can look choppy and misshapen, but with this Thickening Full Form Mousse, you’ll have serious volume. Apply to damp hair in sections and blow dry with an air concentrator for voluminous layers with movement. Check out BumbleandBumble.com for more details on how to get this miracle mousse delivered to your home.

## 3. Revlon Fold and Go Hair Dryer with Air Concentrator

![Revlon Fold and Go Hair Dryer with Air Concentrator](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/3_revlon-fold-and-go-hair-dryer-with-air-concentrator_599x476.jpg)

I bet you’re wondering what an air concentrator is. You know that little plastic attachment that came with your old hair dryer and ended up in the trash? Well that’s an air concentrator. And for ladies rocking a layered look, the air concentrator can be the most important part of the hair drying process. Attaching this piece allows the air flow from the hair dryer direct access to individual sections of hair, which is exactly what layered hair needs! The Revlon Fold and Go Hair Dryer is perfect for its convenient retractable cord, folding handle, multi-heat and speed switches and, of course, the air concentrator attachment. Your local Walmart likely has one in stock. But Walmart.com has a whole line of Revlon dryers for you to pick from.

## 4. Bedhead High Roller Ionic Porcupine round Brush

![Bedhead High Roller Ionic Porcupine round Brush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/4_bedhead-high-roller-ionic-porcupine-round-brush_236x600.jpg)

A round brush is a must-have tool for layered hairstyling. This ionic porcupine round brush by Bedhead has an easy grip handle and tangle-free bristles that will boost shine, fight frizz and keep those layers defined. Just curl your hair under the brush from the root and gently work it through to the ends while blowdrying with your handy dandy air concentrator hair dryer. To get your hand on one of these bristled beauties, click on over to Ulta.com

## 5. Hot Clips for Sectioning

![Hot Clips for Sectioning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/5_hot-clips-for-sectioning_200x200.jpg)

If you’d like to take the time to give those layers an extra boost, clipping back individual sections of the hair during the drying and styling process is the way to do it. These 4” clips from Hot Tools Professional are slip-resistant clips and will keep your hair sectioned while you’re styling away. Use while your hair is still damp, so you can focus on one layered section of hair at a time. Look for them on Ulta.com or at your local beauty supply store.

## 6. Tool Structure Tortoise Backcomber

![Tool Structure Tortoise Backcomber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/6_tool-structure-tortoise-backcomber_224x223.jpg)

This cheap and easy tool is an essential resource for layered locks. This tortoise colored comb by Tool Structure makes backcombing for added volume and definition easy and snag-free. It’s durable, portable and perfect for pushing up those roots and making those layers pop. It’s so affordable, in fact, maybe you should get one to keep in your purse and one for your bathroom. Click over to SallyBeautySupply.com to order yourself a few.

## 7. Forceful 23 Super Strength Finishing Spray

![Forceful 23 Super Strength Finishing Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/7_forceful-23-super-strength-finishing-spray_277x251.jpg)

Yup. It’s another Redken product. What can I say? Redken knows what they’re doing when it comes to layered hair. This flexible finishing spray doesn’t flake or get crispy like hair spray, but it does provide a durable hold that can be styled and re-styled with a glossy and frizz-free shine. Durability with movement? That’s the dream result for layered hair. Get yourself some Forceful 23 Super Strength Finishing Spray from Redken on Ulta.com

When it comes to layered hair, some finesse is involved, but the tools, products and patience required will pay off in the end. When your hair has the definition and bounce that it deserves, you’ll know it was worth the extra time and money. So, how do you keep your layered hair in line?
