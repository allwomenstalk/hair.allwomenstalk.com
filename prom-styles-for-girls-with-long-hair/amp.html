1 Half up

Source: 7 Gorge Natural Hairstyles That
Simple but such a pretty look!

***

Prom night is an important night for any young girl. It's a night to go all out and look your best, from your dress to your hairstyle. Girls with long hair have a lot of options when it comes to prom hairstyles. Half up hairstyles are a great option for prom night, as they look elegant and are easy to do.

To achieve a half up hairstyle, start by brushing your hair and then sectioning it off. Take the top section of your hair and start to make a high ponytail. Secure the ponytail with an elastic band. Then, take the bottom half of your hair and brush it to make it smooth. Take the top half of your hair and wrap it around the elastic band. Secure the half up look with bobby pins.

You can also add some curls or waves to the bottom half of your hair to give it some texture. To do this, use a curling iron and curl the ends of your hair. You can also use a flat iron to create soft waves. If you want to add some color to your look, you can use a temporary hair dye or highlights.

2 Curly Side Bun Fishtail Braid

Source: 50 Cute and Trendy Updos
This style has a little bit of everything.I like it!

***

The Curly Side Bun Fishtail Braid is the perfect blend of elegance and boho-chic. To achieve this look, start by curling your locks to create soft waves. Then, sweep your hair to one side and begin to form a loose fishtail braid, working your way down. Once the braid is complete, twist it into a bun at the nape of your neck, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands to frame your face for that effortlessly romantic touch. This hairstyle is ideal for prom as it stays put all night while adding a fairytale flair to your ensemble.

6 Loose Braided Hair Tutorial

Source: 14 DIY Hairstyles For Long
A romantic updo that's feminine and charming.

***

This hairstyle adds a touch of sophistication and grace to any prom ensemble. Begin by parting your hair on the side and braid loosely, allowing strands to fall softly and frame your face. Secure the braid at the nape with a clear elastic before tucking it under to create an elegant low bun. Fasten with bobby pins, and to add a bit of glamour, adorn with sparkling hair accessories or intertwine with delicate floral accents. The result is an effortlessly chic look that's sure to turn heads.

The big curls hairstyle adds a touch of romantic whimsy to any ensemble, making it perfect for that dreamy prom night. 12 Twisted Bun

Source: DIY Braided Chignon
Get twisted with this intricate updo.A great way to show off a backless dress!

***

This elegant hairstyle fits perfectly for the girl who wants to exude sophistication and charm. To achieve this look, begin by sectionally twisting your hair from the sides and gather everything into a low ponytail. From there, twist the length of your ponytail until it coils upon itself, forming a chic bun. Secure with bobby pins, making sure to tuck in the ends for a polished finish. Spritz with a bit of hairspray to keep it all in place throughout the night. It’s a style that’s both classic and contemporary, sure to turn heads and leave a lasting impression.

14 Faux Bob

Source: 25 Prettiest Long Hairstyles for
Want to rock short hair for prom? You won't have to commit to cutting your hair with this chic look.

***

The faux bob is an elegant solution for those longing to experiment with a shorter hairstyle without the permanence of a cut. It's a clever technique involving pinning your long locks under to create the illusion of a bob. This style adds a touch of sophistication to your prom ensemble and works beautifully with various hair textures. Add some gentle waves before pinning to give your faux bob extra volume and movement. Finish with a light spritz of hairspray, ensuring your 'short' do stays put as you dance the night away.

16 One Minute Knotted Half up

Source: Barefoot Blonde by Amber Fillerup
This is another quick and easy look just in case you're running low on time!

***

The One Minute Knotted Half Up is a brilliant option when you need a chic style, fast. By simply taking two sections of hair from each side and tying them into a small knot at the back of your head, you create an effortlessly elegant look that's perfect for prom. Secure with a couple of bobby pins, and you're good to go. This hairstyle not only saves time but also keeps you looking sophisticated throughout the night. Plus, it allows you to showcase both your beautiful locks and your dress's neckline!

17 Side Braided Bun

Source: 50 Cute and Trendy Updos
A super cute style for your special night out.

***

The Side Braided Bun is more than just your average updo; it's a woven masterpiece that adds an elegant twist to your overall ensemble. To create this look, sweep your hair to one side and weave it into a tight, sleek braid. As your braid reaches the nape of your neck, twist and pin it up into a low bun that sits just behind your ear. This hairstyle is particularly suited for gowns with asymmetrical necklines or detailed shoulders, giving an air of romantic sophistication that will turn heads throughout the night. Add a spritz of hairspray to ensure your style holds from dusk till dawn.

19 Elegant Long Hair Twist

Source: A Showcase of Amazing Prom
A simple side braid with lots of curls.

***

The Elegant Long Hair Twist is a captivating choice for those desiring a blend of classic and romantic. To achieve this look, begin by curling your hair into loose waves, adding texture and volume. Next, take a section of hair from one side and start braiding it towards the back of your head. Intertwine the braid with a strand from the opposite side to create a gentle twist. Secure the design with bobby pins, allowing a few tendrils to escape for a whimsical touch. This hairstyle exudes a timeless grace that is perfect for the magical prom night ambiance.
