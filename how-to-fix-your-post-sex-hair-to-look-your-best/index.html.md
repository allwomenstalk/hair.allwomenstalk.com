# How to Fix Your Post\-Sex Hair to Look Your Best ...

You'll spend hours doing your hair to make it look good for your boyfriend. However, as soon as you see him and have sex with him, all of your hard work will be ruined. If you're spending the day in bed with him, it isn't a big deal. However, if you're planning on leaving the house, it can be a big hassle. But there's no need to worry, because here are a few tips for fixing your post-sex hair to look your best:

## 1. Keep the Essentials in Your Purse

![Keep the Essentials in Your Purse](https://img.allw.mn/content/y3/r4/lp8bvdab5697ba36245d8244659105_500x268.gif)

Make sure you always have a brush and some bobby pins somewhere inside of your purse. Or, if you're in a serious relationship, then you can just leave those items in his bathroom cabinet. That way, you won't have to worry about lugging them back and fourth between your house and his house.

## 2. Put Your Hair into a Braid

[https://media.giphy.com/media/itUUwVy9jNjBS/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/itUUwVy9jNjBS/giphy.gif)
If your hair looks like a mess after sex, then all you need to do is create a new hairstyle. No, you don't have to spend hours in front of the mirror trying to create an elaborate updo. All you have to do is twist your hair into a basic braid. That way, no one will be able to see how unruly it looks.

## 3. Get on Top of Him

![Get on Top of Him](https://img.allw.mn/content/nd/d6/y1ipetnq5697ba362ad46488116582_500x260.gif)

The way you behave during sex will determine how messy your hair looks when you're finished. If you're worried about ruining your perfect hairdo, then make sure to get on top of your partner. That way, your head won't be pressed against the sheets. You need to do whatever you can to keep yourself off of your back, because that's the easiest way to ruin your look.

## 4. Bring a Hat

![Bring a Hat](https://img.allw.mn/content/uh/e7/l7wm7hk65697ba369647c528148846_480x270.gif)

Never leave your hats at home. If your hair really gets out of hand, then all you have to do is place a hat on your head. It's a simple fix. As long as you pick a fashionable hat, no one will even know that you're trying to cover up a mess. They'll just assume you added a cute accessory to your look.

## 5. Put Your Hair up during Sex

![Put Your Hair up during Sex](https://img.allw.mn/content/re/vj/owv4fgpi5697ba3734ad9739069968_500x206.gif)

If you're worried about your hair getting everywhere while you're making love, then you should put your hair into a ponytail. It doesn't have to be perfect, since it'll probably end up getting messed up, anyway. Just get it out of your face. That way, you won't get it greasy by pushing it back with your hands again and again.

## 6. Keep His Hands Occupied so They Don't Touch Your Hair

![Keep His Hands Occupied so They Don't Touch Your Hair](https://img.allw.mn/content/lf/il/tisb88nl5697ba385596c331231370_500x253.gif)

As long as you remind your man to keep his hands out of your hair, then there shouldn't be a problem. Of course, if you're too embarrassed to tell him that you're worried about your hair, you can grab his hands and place them on another area of your body.

## 7. Buy Satin Pillows and Blankets

![Buy Satin Pillows and Blankets](https://img.allw.mn/content/hy/d6/btwwymcu5697ba380aac8930444842_500x281.gif)

Some blankets will hurt your hair more than others. That's why using satin sheets are your best bet. If you use anything else, it could cause static electricity, and you know how horrible that could make your hair look.

You don't have to turn down sex out of fear of ruining your hair. As long as you're careful, everything will stay in place. What other tips do you have for fixing post-sex hair?
