# How to Create the Best Curls in Your Hair ...

[Redbook](https://goo.gl/YHLdmR) recently sat down with actress, Debra Messing, in order to get all of her curly hair secrets. It doesn't matter if your hair is naturally curly or if you use an iron on it to get the style you want, because these tips will work for anyone.

## 1. Put Your Hair in a Bun

![hair, face, hairstyle, eyebrow, forehead,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c0/as/onimjtbu5803e6ce97619518434875_600x732.jpg)

If you have to rush out of the house in the morning, you should curl your hair before you go to bed. Since you don't want to ruin your hard work while you sleep, put all of your hair up into a bun. When you take out your scrunchie in the morning, you should end up with the cutest curls ever.

## 2. Buy Hair Masks

_ It only takes ten minutes for you to use a deep conditioning mask on your hair after you shower. Any brand will protect your hair from the elements, but Messing suggests using Kerastase Chronologiste Creme de Regeneration, which you can buy at Kerastase_.

## 3. Non-Silicone Spray

![hair, clothing, face, hairstyle, wig,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kj/de/yhkcq1km5803e6802ad8f096597164_600x749.jpg)

If it's raining or incredibly humid out, then you should use a non-silicone spray in your hair. That way, your hair will stay the way you styled it before you left the house. The weather won't ruin your beautiful curls.

## 4. Heat Protectors

![hair, human positions, blond, hairstyle, sitting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h8/yp/igtwt3te5803e71f56d81124984390_600x600.jpg)

If you use blowdryers and curling irons often, then you need to buy products that will protect your hair from damage. Messing suggests Bumble and Bumble hairsprays, which are her absolute favorite. You can buy them [Bumble and Bumble](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 5. Leave in Conditioner

![hair, face, eyebrow, hairstyle, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mm/id/qh3btg1a5803e74787fae675589840_600x600.jpg)

Leave in conditioner can also prevent your hair from becoming too damaged. All you have to do is place it on your wet hair and leave it on until it dries. It'll make your curls shine.

If your hair is naturally curly, it's time to embrace the look you were born with! What other curly hair tips do you have?
