# 7 Great Smelling Hair Products That Actually Work ...

We all appreciate hair products that work well, and it’s a bonus if they are great smelling hair products as well! I have worked in the salon industry for quite some time, so throughout the years I have experimented with tons of hair products. Have you ever bought a hair product that smelled terrible? If so, you will definitely appreciate this list of great smelling hair products that actually work!

## 1. Clary Z-11 Oil

To add shine to your hair while using great smelling hair products, use Clary Z-11 Oil. Clary Z-11 Oil is made up of an Amazon oil blend of 11 key ingredients including Passion Fruit, Brazil Nut, Black Cacao, and Buriti. These unique ingredients and lightweight oils add shine and leave hair with a lustrously smooth finish. Z-11 oil has fragrance notes of water hyacinth, Amazon lemon, Amazon orchid, Acai berry, and Brazilian rosewood.

## 2. S-Factor Smoothing Lusterizer by Tigi

Smoothing Lusterizer from Tigi’s S-Factor line of hair care products is one of my favorite great smelling hair products! It smells deliciously sweet like candy! In addition, it silkifies, softens, and fights humidity. You can apply it to wet or dry hair for a silky, smooth sheen.

## 3. Kevin Murphy Hair Resort Spray

Kevin Murphy Hair Resort Spray is perfect for getting hair that looks like you spent a day at the beach. This volumizing beach hair spray smells like honey, citrus, and tangerine…it’s amazing! You just spray it onto damp or dry hair and scrunch for the perfect beach day waves.

## 4. Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray

It can be really difficult to find a hairspray that works well and smells great, so I was excited when Moroccanoil released their Luminous Hairspray! This lightweight hairspray is infused with argan oil and has Moroccanoil’s signature scent. It’s a long-lasting, workable hairspray that won’t leave a sticky residue. It’s perfect for fighting frizz and humidity.

## 5. Shu Uemura Moisture Velvet Nourishing Shampoo

Shu Uemura’s Moisture Velvet Nourishing Shampoo opens with the scent of crisp grapefruit and green almond before transforming into notes of camellia petals and lily. It smells amazing and leaves your hair feeling velvety soft. It rebalances the moisture of dry hair for the ultimate softness.

## 6. Carol’s Daughter Monoi Hair Repairing Mask

I love tropical scents, so I love the way Carol’s Daughter Monoi Hair Repairing Mask smells! It is loaded with the fragrance of warm coconut and gardenia flowers giving it a sugary and tropical scent. Use hair masks such as this one periodically to nourish and rejuvenate your hair! You’ll look like you just left the salon with smooth, shiny hair after using this delicious smelling hair mask!

## 7. Kenra Platinum Texturizing Taffy

If you want to add texture and separation to your hair while maintaining control you will love Kenra’s Platinum Texturizing Taffy! It works so well with its pliable formula and has an amazing scent! It has a matte finish, which is a unique feature since so many products boast shine. This product is ideal for shorter hairstyles, but can add texture to longer hairstyles as well.

Great smelling hair products that actually work will make your hair smell and look amazing all day long! Your hair is an important part of your appearance, so it’s important to use fantastic hair products! Do you have a favorite great smelling hair product?
