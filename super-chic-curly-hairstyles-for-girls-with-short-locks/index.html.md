# Super Chic Curly Hairstyles for Girls with Short Locks ...

Whatever your length of hair may be, having curly hair can take any hairstyle to the next level! Most think that you have to have long locks to rock curls. This sooo isn't true. If you're thinking about chopping off your hair, consider these gorgeous short hairstyle ideas!

## 1. Wavy Lob

Follow Carly Walters from Mane Addicts as she shows how to create beachy waves on short hair or lobs. It'll look like you just got back from vacay.

## 2. WATERFALL BRAID for Short Hair

This style of braid is perfect for a second day look that's still pretty and put together. The dirtier the hair, the better! No need to wash for this look.

## 3. Short CURLY BOB

This lasts for days and is a very natural curly look. For the next day rocking this look, simply add hairspray and you're good to go!

## 4. Messy Beach Waves

Look like a beach babe in no time with this messy beach waves hair tutorial. Start with unwashed hair, use dry shampoo and spray on your roots to add volume, and create waves with a flat iron. No curling iron needed!

## 5. BOUNCY CURLS

These carefree curls are created using RUSK miracurl and Macadamia healing oil treatment. It shows how easy it is to create bouncy curls with this curling iron.

## 6. MESSY Textured WAVES

This textured style is so fast and simple. The bottom layer of your hair should start out completely straight. Curl the rest of your hair, using larger and smaller sections to create a messy look.

## 7. Messy Ringlets

After tightly curling your hair, take a boar bristle brush through your hair to create the messy ringlet look. Finish with argon oil and hair spray to keep your look lasting all day long.

## 8. EDGY Wavys

To rock this edgy lob, you'll need hair oil, finishing creme, and texturing hair spray. Use the curling iron to alternate the waves for a more natural look. You'll have touchably soft, undefined, edgy waves!

## 9. ASYMMETRICAL WAVES

This asymmetric bob is full of rough texture on one side and smooth on the other. This natural look is full of volume to keep this look feeling fresh and on trend.

## 10. SHOULDER LENGTH WAVES

Use the biggest curling iron you can find for this shoulder length look. There's no need to spend too much time trying to blow dry your hair after washing because this bigger curling iron with smooth your hair for a quick, cute style.

## 11. Wavy Pixie

With this wavy pixie look having long hair on the top and short on the sides, you'll quickly curl, spray, and go. It'll really show off your facial features, too. So edgy and spunky!

## 12. Curls and Bangs

Keep your bangs straight with some wave for a soft, lady like look full of texture. This look is very natural and perfectly frames your face.

## 13. Bedhead Waves

Use a straightening iron or curling iron to create this look. After washing your hair, towel dry, add mouse to give your hair lift, applying at the roots. This is to make sure it's very through. Add a heat protector and blow dry for extra volume.

## 14. Natural, Shiny Curls

For natural curls, this process shows how to deep condition hair with a protein treatment, muddy organic hair mask, olive oil hair lotion, and argon oil.

## 15. Spiral Curls

Get these super tight curls without any heat!

If you don't have short hair now, I bet you're thinking about chopping it all off to create these short wavy hair styles! What's your favorite look? Comment below!
