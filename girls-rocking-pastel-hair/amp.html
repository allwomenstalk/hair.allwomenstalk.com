Frequently Asked Questions

What is lavender hair and why is it popular?
Lavender hair is a soft pastel purple shade that offers a fresh, trendy look, perfect for those wanting a subtle yet striking change.
How do I maintain pastel hair colors like lavender?
Use color-safe shampoos, wash with cool water, and avoid excessive heat styling to keep pastel colors vibrant longer.
Can I bleach my hair to achieve pastel colors?
Yes, bleaching is usually necessary to lighten your hair before applying pastel shades for the colors to show up vividly.
How often will I need to touch up pastel hair colors?
Pastel colors tend to fade quickly, so touch-ups may be needed every 4-6 weeks to maintain brightness.
Are pastel hair colors suitable for all skin tones?
Pastels can complement many skin tones; lavender works well with cool undertones but can be adapted with the right shade.
Can I do pastel hair colors at home or should I visit a salon?
While DIY kits exist, visiting a salon ensures the best application and color result, especially when bleaching is involved.
What are some other popular pastel hair colors besides lavender?
Mint green, baby blue, peach, and soft pink are trendy pastel colors to consider.
Will pastel hair colors damage my hair?
Pastel hair coloring often involves bleaching, which can cause dryness, but proper care and moisturizing treatments minimize damage.
How do I transition from pastel hair back to my natural color?
Gradual color fading and regular trims can help, and consulting a colorist for a professional dye removal is best.
Can pastel colors work on short hair styles?
Absolutely! Pastel colors look fantastic on all hair lengths, adding a unique flair to short hairstyles.
