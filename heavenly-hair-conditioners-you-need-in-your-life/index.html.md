# 9 Heavenly Hair Conditioners You Need in Your Life ...

Heavenly hair conditioners aren’t always easy to find. There are lots of conditioners for hair that promise us the softest, silkiest and glossiest locks around but don’t always deliver! I know we all have different hair types and needs so there’s rarely one product that everyone loves. But, I know of nine absolutely heavenly hair conditioners that I know you ladies will love! Keep on reading for all the details!

## 1. Pantene Pro-V Aqua Light Weightless Conditioning Shake

![Pantene Pro-V Aqua Light Weightless Conditioning Shake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/1_pantene-pro-v-aqua-light-weightless-conditioning-shake_499x496.jpg)

Pantene’s Aqua Light Weightless Conditioning Shake gets my vote for heavenly hair conditioners! I love the idea of a dry conditioner that also works as a heat protectant. Another great feature of using a dry hair conditioner is that it won’t weight your hair down so you can enjoy luscious locks easily and quickly! Find it at Walmart for $6.27.

## 2. Rahua Conditioner

![Rahua Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/2_rahua-conditioner_499x450.jpg)

If you have strict requirements for your hair conditioner, Rahua’s might fill the bill! Rahua conditioner is totally natural and helps heal brittle ends, make hair more manageable and increase moisture retention. It’s 70% certified organic, 95% USDA organic, vegan and can be used to prevent fizz and tame flyaways while you’re styling your hair! Available at Dermstore.com for $34.

## 3. L'Oreal EverStrong Reconstruct Conditioner

![L'Oreal EverStrong Reconstruct Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/3_loreal-everstrong-reconstruct-conditioner_499x495.jpg)

L’Oreal makes some of the better sulfate-free hair products and conditioners for hair. This conditioner is also vegan and it helps strengthen your hair so it resists breakage and reconstructs hair fiber after one use. It also has a rosemary and mint fragrance that smells amazing and really helps wakes up the senses! You can find it at Target for $5.99.

## 4. Klorane Conditioner with Oat Milk

![Klorane Conditioner with Oat Milk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/4_klorane-conditioner-with-oat-milk_499x448.jpg)

For ladies who are seeking a hair conditioner that soothes an itchy scalp, Klorane’s got one that’ll soothe and restore the natural beauty of your hair. This dermatologist-tested conditioner is rich in oat milk, lipids and vitamins, and made without parabens so you can feel good about using it! This conditioner also got high marks for detangling hair and being gentle on all hair types! Find it at Drugstore.com for $14.

## 5. Shu Uemura SHUSU SLEEK SMOOTHING CONDITIONER for COARSE and UNRULY HAIR

![Shu Uemura SHUSU SLEEK SMOOTHING CONDITIONER for COARSE and UNRULY HAIR](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/5_shu-uemura-shusu-sleek-smoothing-conditioner-for-coarse-and-unruly-hair_599x536.jpg)

If you’re a fan of Shu Uemura’s beauty products I think you’re going to love his collection of hair products, namely this hair conditioner! Coarse and unruly tresses have met their match with the Shusu Sleek Smoothing Conditioner that purports to deeply nourish and discipline unruly hair with black cumin oil to soften even the coarsest or driest hair like the Japanese Shusu fluid weaving style. Get it at [shuuemuraartofhair-usa.com](http://www.shuuemuraartofhair-usa.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $58.

## 6. Pantene Pro-V Beautiful Lengths Strengthening Conditioner

![Pantene Pro-V Beautiful Lengths Strengthening Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/6_pantene-pro-v-beautiful-lengths-strengthening-conditioner_449x419.jpg)

If you’re like me and can’t imagine spending over $50 on a conditioner for hair no matter how much you’d like to, Pantene makes a great budget alternative! Ladies with long hair that can use some moisture, strengthening and help against breakage and damage, check this guy out at Walgreens for $3.99.

## 7. L’oréal Professionnel Vitamino Color Conditioner

![L’oréal Professionnel Vitamino Color Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/7_lor-al-professionnel-vitamino-color-conditioner_599x597.jpg)

Color-treated hair needs a conditioner that will help preserve your color while moisturizing your hair. It’s a little pricier than typical L’Oreal hair products you see in retailers, but it gets high marks for its fab fragrance and its hydro-resist technology, which creates a protective barrier that repels water on the surface of your hair. Get it at Amazon for $15.68.

## 8. Organix Nourishing Coconut Milk Conditioner

![Organix Nourishing Coconut Milk Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/8_organix-nourishing-coconut-milk-conditioner_499x446.jpg)

We’ve all heard of the wonders of coconut oil, but what about coconut milk? Turns out it actually makes an incredible conditioner for hair, also! This nourishing coconut milk conditioner is safe for color-treated hair and works to hydrate, balance and add strength and elasticity to all hair types. You’ll also be glad to know that it’s paraben and sulfate-free! Get it at drugstore.com for $5.59.

## 9. Clear Conditioner Volumizing Root Boost

![Clear Conditioner Volumizing Root Boost](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2013/08/9_clear-conditioner-volumizing-root-boost_409x387.jpg)

While most hair conditioners focus on dry hair, we know that not all ladies have parched hair. If your hair has plenty of moisture and you’re looking for a little boost in the volume department, check out Clear’s root boosting conditioner. This conditioner has lots of nutrients and botanicals to nourish all hair types! Get yours at Target at $4.99.

Conditioners can do so much for our hair besides just moisturizing our hair. We can count on conditioners to detangle, tame frizz, soften, nourish and give our hair a healthy shine! Now that you’ve seen some of my picks for amazing hair conditioners, you have to tell me yours!
