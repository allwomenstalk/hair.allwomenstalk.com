# Wear These Hot Hairstyles with Side Bangs ...

Side bangs are always in style. It doesn't matter how much time passes, because they're always going to look cute. If you currently have side bangs, there are certain hairstyles that'll look amazing on you. That's why you should watch the tutorials down below, which will show you a few hot hairstyles you can wear with your side bangs:

## 1. Everyday Fancy Loop Ponytail

This hairstyle doesn't take all that long, which is why it's perfect for all of you ladies who hate spending more than ten minutes in front of the mirror each morning. Don't you worry, even though the woman in the video has long hair, it also works with medium length hair. Of course, it looks best if you have side bangs, because it'll make your flirty ponytail look even cuter than ever!

## 2. Retro Hairstyle

This is a hairstyle inspired by an adorable Bollywood look. As long as you're able to bump up your hair in the back to make it look like it has tons of volume, you shouldn't have a problem recreating this look. Even though your side bangs will be pushed back, it'll still be obvious that you have them. After all, they help complete the look.

## 3. Knot Bun

This hairstyle looks complicated, but it's actually pretty easy. You're basically going to loop different sections of your hair together. All you'll need are a few bobby pins to keep everything in place, and of course, some gorgeous side bangs. The little pieces of hair falling in front of your face is what makes this look so cute.

## 4. Perfect Bun Updo

You've created buns before, but have you ever created one that looks this sleek and elegant? If that's the look you're going for, you're going to start by putting your (second day) hair up in a high ponytail. Then you're going to grab a bun maker and pull that ponytail through it. Use as many bobby pins as you need to keep everything in place.

## 5. Five Strand Braid

You don't actually need side bangs in order to pull off this look, but it helps add some extra cuteness to your look. Of course, if you don't know how to make a simple braid, you should probably learn how to do that before you attempt this five strand braid, or even the four strand braid.

## 6. Baby Doll Hair

If you want to look as sweet and innocent as a doll, then you can try to create this hairstyle. All you really have to do is curl the bottom of your hair while keeping your bangs straight and smooth. You can even add some cute bobby pins to your bangs to draw attention to them.

## 7. Side Flat Twists

If you want to look badass for a certain concert or party, then you should try to recreate this hairstyle. It'll look like you've shaved your head on one side when really, everything is still in place.

If you have side bangs, then you should try all of these cute hairstyles! If you don't have side bangs, you should still feel free to try out these styles, because they'll look cute on anyone. Do you currently have side bangs?
