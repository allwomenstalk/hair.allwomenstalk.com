# 7 Ways to Create Fabulous Volume in Your Hair ...

Learning how to create volume in hair is so hard! I know when I first started to learn how to create volume in hair, I used to get frustrated because my hair wouldn't hold volume. Well ladies, I've learned all of the tips, tricks and lessons on how to create volume in hair and I am more than willing to share! If you've been trying to create volume in your hair, take a look below at some of the ways that have worked for me!

## 1. Change the Part in Your Hair

![Change the Part in Your Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/1_change-the-part-in-your-hair_554x831.jpg)

I used to part my hair in the middle, for years, but when my hairstylist asked me to part it on the side, I was amazed as how much volume there was! If you haven't changed the part in your hair in a while, why not switch it up to the other side! This is absolutely one of the greatest ways on how to create volume in hair and to really change up your hairstyle!

## 2. The Right Haircut

![The Right Haircut](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/2_the-right-haircut_500x682.jpg)

Did you know that having the wrong haircut can actually make your hair fall flat? If you've been trying to learn how to create volume in your hair, you've got to choose the right haircut! Lots of layers can actually stop the volume in your hair. Try a straight-across, blunt cut. Your hair will look voluminous and thick!

## 3. Blow-Drying

![Blow-Drying](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/3_blow-drying_600x568.jpg)

I know it's hard to blow dry your own hair, especially trying to blow dry it straight. If you take a round brush and just use it at the crown of your head, pulling at the roots, I promise you'll get all of the volume that you need! It'll also keep your hair looking beautiful and shiny all of the time!

## 4. Volumizing Shampoo/Conditioner

![Volumizing Shampoo/Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/4_volumizing-shampoo-conditioner_500x742.jpg)

I love, love, love John Frieda's Volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Ladies, if you aren't using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, try it! This is absolutely one of the best ways on how to create volume in your hair!

## 5. Teasing

![Teasing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/5_teasing_553x692.jpg)

You definitely don't have to tease your hair to death, but a little teasing at the crown is a great way to cause all sorts of volume! For me, I use the teasing right at the back and it really pushes the volume up and looks great! Try it ladies!

## 6. Scrunch Your Hair

![Scrunch Your Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/6_scrunch-your-hair_554x831.jpg)

If you have curly hair and you are looking for ways on how to create volume in hair, don't worry! When you are blow drying, just scrunch your hair up. It'll make your curls so much more pronounced and it will truly make your curls that much more voluminous!

## 7. Root Lifter

![Root Lifter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hair/2012/06/7_root-lifter_500x667.jpg)

Finally, you want to make sure that you find a root lifter that really works. Paul Mitchell is mine and just a few sprays at the root of your hair along with a bit of blow drying with a round brush will really make your hair look voluminous! What's your favorite root lifter ladies?

There you have it! All of the ways on how to create volume in hair. So ladies, what's your favorite way on how to create volume in hair? Do you try anything different?
