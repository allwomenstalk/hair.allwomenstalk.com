# 7 Hair Tutorials for Women Who Wish They Had Shorter Hair ...

You don't want to cut off all of your hair and then end up regretting the decision. There's a way for you to keep your long hair while trying out a short style to see how much you like it. All you need is a few minutes in front of the mirror and plenty of bobby pins to hold everything in place. Interested in the idea? Here are a few hair tutorials for women who wish they had shorter hair:

## 1. Faux Bob

You don't want your bob to look like you're trying to hide your hair. To fix that, you should make sure to create shapes with your bob and add texture, so that you look more stylish. First, you're going to start out by making a base. Next, you're going to curl the back of your hair. After that, you'll pin the back, pin the front, and call it a day!

## 2. Fake Short Hair

Here's another video that'll show you how to create a faux bob. You're going to start out by curling the ends of your hair instead of curling all the way up to the top. Once you're finished with that step, you should divide your hair from ear to ear and clip it out of the way. Tease the hair that's still down and then start pinning it up!

## 3. 50s Makeup and Faux Bob

This video will show you more than how to create a super curly bob. It'll also show you how to do your makeup to fit the hairstyle. Everything you see was inspired by Taylor Swift, so you know that you're going to end up looking fabulous.

## 4. Great Gatsby Faux Bob

If you loved this book, or at least loved the movie, then you should try following the advice in this tutorial. It was inspired by the lovely ladies of the 1920s. The woman in this tutorial suggests that you start by parting your hair to the side, because it'll help you look your best. Of course, you could always switch things up and try doing your own take on the look.

## 5. Faux Bob

This video will show you how to do a lob, which is basically a [long bob](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/sick-of-having-long-hair-check-out-these-long-bob-inspos-now/). If you've been thinking about cutting your hair, but don't have your heart set on the idea yet, then you should really follow the advice in this video. All you're going to need is a large elastic hair tie that you can buy at the ninety nine cent store. However, the style works best if you already have layers in your hair.

## 6. 1920s Faux Bob

This video will show you how to do a finger waved updo and how to create a faux bob. If you're dressing up as a flapper next Halloween, these looks will come in handy. Of course, you could always wear this look during any other season and look sexy, as well.

## 7. Messy Faux Bob

You don't have to make your bob perfect. In fact, it'll actually look better if it appears messy. So don't worry if things don't come out the way you wanted. You're still going to look pretty.

Now you can experience what it would be like to have short hair without actually grabbing your scissors. How long is your hair?
