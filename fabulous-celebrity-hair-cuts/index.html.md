# 20 Fabulous Celebrity Hair Cuts ...

Celebrity Hair Cuts are known for setting style trends, and with good reason. Through the help of skilled stylists and hair experts, celebs often have the trendiest cuts imaginable. In fact, they are the trendsetters – just look how long Jennifer Aniston's “Rachel Cut” stayed popular! You'd be surprised at how easy it is for regular folks like you and me to pull off the most glamorous celebrity hair cuts, too. It's just a matter of making sure your hair type is right for a particular style. If you're dying to try the looks of some of your favorite stars, start out by taking a look at these fabulous celebrity hair cuts!

## 1. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/rj/m74lkqhf54c930eae1f8d307641757_500x400.jpg)

Of all the gorgeous celebrity hair cuts on this list, Emma's is definitely my favorite. Her short cut made waves, for a very good reason. It suits her beautifully, giving her a definition edge, and the style complements her bone structure to perfection. Emma is definitely one of my biggest girl crushes!

## 2. Victoria Beckham

![Victoria Beckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/dv/bl3fbi7h54c930ebeb834075659340_500x347.jpg)

Victoria's hairstyles haven't changed a whole lot within the last few years. She know how good that sleek, angled bob looks on her. Recently, however, she edged it out even more, going shorter in back and longer in front, with the layers and angles more extreme. This suits those cheekbones beautifully, and it's especially cute when she adds some curly texture!

## 3. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kf/il/ajq6nat554c930ed4c75f798952470_500x380.jpg)

Reese has managed to get a fabulous cut by letting her hair grow, actually. Cute as she looks with her flippy short 'dos, her current cut is lovely, too. While her hair is long and luscious, she's taken to wearing long bangs that frame her face and highlight her eyes. Gorgeous!

## 4. Halle Berry

![Halle Berry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/sb/x6hmjua254c930ee6e437339356187_500x375.jpg)

Halle Berry is unquestionably stylish, especially when it comes to her hair. This year, she's been switching it up a bit. Sometimes it's her signature cropped pixie, which is to die for. Then she'll let it grow a little, so it's all flips and curls and tousles. Whichever way she decides to cut and style it, however, she will always have a place on this list!

## 5. Angelina Jolie

![Angelina Jolie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k5/l4/s91pgv4y54c930ef7321f737789518_500x349.jpg)

Do you all remember when Angie used to be all about long, black hair and fitted, gothic dresses? No more! Her style has really evolved into something glamorous, and her current cut emphasizes that. It's long, lush, and much softer, but not wild. She may not be guilty of the wildest celebrity hair cuts, but she's definitely got one of the most stylish ones!

## 6. Demi Moore

![Demi Moore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/li/l51oljxh54c930f085c3e108261664_500x361.jpg)

Demi's another woman who manages to have one of the top cuts on this list by not actually cutting her hair at all. Those long, shiny locks have become her signature, and they suit her beautifully. Man, I wish I had the patience to let my hair get that long...

## 7. Amber Rose

![Amber Rose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y6/gy/hdnwzv5254c930f193591670636039_500x333.jpg)

If you want to talk about bold cuts, you have to mention Amber Rose. It takes a lot of courage to buzz off all your hair, and she went a step further by making it platinum. You have to admit, though, it highlights her gorgeous bone structure and the color looks gorgeous against her skin tone.

## 8. Carey Mulligan

![Carey Mulligan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nh/z8/ia9732k254c930f28be3c506923197_500x419.jpg)

Carey Mulligan is just adorable, but I love this new cut even more than her usual pixie. It's a little longer than usual, a little spiked, a little tousled, a little curly – it's a little bit of everything, and all of it gorgeous. In fact, I like it so much I kind of want to try it myself!

## 9. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hw/kb/uu7iolmp54c930f3e8ad0144503829_500x375.jpg)

The world got used to Jessica Alba's long, luscious, sexy locks, so it came as a surprise when she chopped her hair. I think it was a gorgeous surprise, though. Her edgy, messy bob makes her look even younger than she already does, and really shows off her sloe eyes and lush lips.

## 10. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/a8/f01w3c3d54c930f520d91473320126_500x325.jpg)

Beyonce is responsible for some of the best celebrity hair cuts ever, and the look she showed off at the VMAs is no exception. Maybe it's that bump making her glow, but right now, Beyonce can do no wrong. And neither can her hair!

## 11. Keira Knightley

![Keira Knightley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wd/xp/spn51ii854c930f631c6e551202533_500x350.jpg)

Bobs are getting really popular, huh? Keira Knightley's even decided to give the look a try, and well done, because it really suits her. It gives an edge to her usual, romantic look, and calls even more attention to her strong, regal features. Man, this girl is gorgeous!

## 12. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zr/ex/t536q9uv54c930f77bda3433090259_500x333.jpg)

Okay, okay, maybe Emma hasn't really gotten a cut, exactly, but she's definitely changed her hair. Why is she here, then? Because she's my biggest girl crush ever! Although I love her as a redhead and hope she goes back to it, I'm loving this blond more than I thought I would.

## 13. Katie Holmes

![Katie Holmes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qr/jq/fixafwz154c930f8cf168307016610_500x377.jpg)

Katie has gone through a lot of haircuts, and some of them have been very, very bad. Like, very bad. Her new sleek bob, however, is quite sophisticated and it suits her face very well. Let's just hope she doesn't crop it all off again!

## 14. Scarlett Johansson

![Scarlett Johansson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/lq/s7qwtyyv54c930f9d2e2e288759107_500x333.jpg)

Even ScarJo's gotten into the bob trend – and, again, it really works for her! Scarlett looks like a sex kitten when her hair's long and wavy, and while that's no bad thing, this kind of cut takes her luscious lips, lovely eyes, and curved jaw, and makes them look rather high fashion. Rawr, kitten, rawr!

## 15. Evan Rachel Wood

![Evan Rachel Wood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/lk/ozw7gs5j54c930fad4e1b335127676_500x333.jpg)

Evan Rachel Wood is well known for wild choices, like dating Marilyn Manson. She arguably has better taste in her hair! This is one of my favorite celebrity hair cuts, as a matter of fact. Not everyone would be brave enough to try this, and not everyone could pull it off so well!

## 16. Jennifer Aniston

![Jennifer Aniston](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/xw/dw5aldzd54c930fbf04e6467227349_500x350.jpg)

Jennifer's finally gotten away from her Friends days! Actually, in recent years, I haven't liked her long hair at all. It's been a little too wild, a little too messy, and a little too unkempt for me. I don't know if that was the look she was going for or not, but I didn't like it. However, I do like her version of the angled bob, with cleaner ends and no Rachel layers!

## 17. Kate Winslet

![Kate Winslet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/gq/i1whbqtv54c930fd11e7d910145975_500x375.jpg)

Kate has just the best hair. I always love the way she looks. She makes it on this list, however, for her current Hollywood glam trend. I love the way she's been wearing her hair in glamorous curls; it really brings her vintage look together!

## 18. Tao Okamoto

![Tao Okamoto](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/dg/ehtljyix54c930fe20f93822494013_500x333.jpg)

Tao is so brave – her new bowl cut could have gone so wrong! This is a hard style to pull off, it's hard to look good in it at all, but she was brave enough to do it and her bravery paid off. It draws so much attention to her exquisite cheekbones!

## 19. Ginnifer Goodwin

_ Photo Credit: TalkMediaNews_

I didn't used to like Ginnifer Goodwin, but lately she's been growing on me. I admit, her new, short haircut is part of the reason. She looks so cute! This is another style I'm kind of dying to try out for myself!

## 20. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nk/qu/nghafqii54c93100a6545531642036_500x375.jpg)

Last but not least, we have the reigning queen of wild celebrity hair cuts: Rihanna! This girl knows how to go bold, but it's her current look that I love best. She's gone back to her signature cherry red color, but now she's wearing long, loose, wild curls, and it is gorgeous!

I absolutely love a lot of these celebrity hair cuts. I think Victoria Beckham, for instance, should just become a hair guru – I don't think I've ever seen her have a bad cut! It can take guts to try some of the most fabulous celebrity hair cuts yourself, but odds are you'll look just as glamorous. You just have to take the plunge! Which of these celebrity hair cuts would you secretly like to try?
