# Sangria Hair is the Magenta Hair Color We Are Seeing Everywhere This Fall ...

Magenta hair, is the fall color we are seeing everywhere and has becoming a cool-girl favorite. In fact, this **sangria is the fall's hottest hair color** trend and I know you're going to love it!

A wonderful red, this gorgeous color is making a statement and is flattering on all skin tones and many hair textures.

This season is a bit darker, moodier and more wine-tinged than traditional magenta, which is why we say it is “sangria hair”.

Worn in waves, sleek and straight, crimped, braided or pulled back, magenta hair is showstopping on the runway, and you can use it too. It's not just for models.

Are you looking for a big change? If so, here we have some different shades of the hair color of this fall season. **Sangria is the fall's hottest hair color.**
